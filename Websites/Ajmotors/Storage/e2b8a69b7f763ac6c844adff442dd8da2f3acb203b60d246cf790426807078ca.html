# 2014 Nissan Juke 15RX Type V
{Stock number. 23819}

The Nissan Juke 15RX Type V is an option for those looking for a small SUV that combines practicality with fun-to-drive characteristics, especially if you're looking for a vehicle that offers a distinctive, eye-catching design.

# Key features
- Push button start: The push-button start allows the driver to start the car without inserting a key into the ignition. Instead, as long as the key fob is inside the vehicle (usually in your pocket or bag), you can press the button to start the engine.

- Auto start-stop: The Auto Start-Stop system is a feature that helps improve fuel efficiency and reduce emissions by automatically shutting down the engine when the vehicle is at a complete stop (e.g., at traffic lights or in heavy traffic) and restarting it when the accelerator is pressed. This system is designed to save fuel and reduce unnecessary idling, contributing to better overall fuel economy.

- Auto retractable mirrors: The main advantage of retractable mirrors is their flexibility and space-saving nature, as they can be hidden or compacted when not required, preventing damage and maintaining aesthetics or functionality.
