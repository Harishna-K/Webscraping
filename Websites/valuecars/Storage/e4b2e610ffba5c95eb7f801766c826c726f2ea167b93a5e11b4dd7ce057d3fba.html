Chain Driven Engine - Push Button Start - Dual Airbags

Low km 2012 Subaru Trezia/Toyota Ractis 1.3i L, 5 door family wagon with proximity key, push button start, climate control, idle stop and has only travelled 69077km's. Looks smart finished in Satin Blue Metallic with driving lights, and has the more desired Black interior with lightly textured inserts. Features an economical chain driven 1300cc VVTi engine and automatic transmission giving very good fuel efficiency.
Finance and Protecta Insurance products are also available for this vehicle.

Pre-purchase inspection can be arranged for out of town purchasers.
