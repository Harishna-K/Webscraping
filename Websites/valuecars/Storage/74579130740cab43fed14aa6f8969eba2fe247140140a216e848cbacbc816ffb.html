Economical - USB/AUX Connectivity - Late Model

$2000 Minimum Trade in on this vehicle, Conditions apply

Looking to save fuel and avoid road user charges? then have a look at this super tidy Toyota Aqua Hybrid, The 2nd facelift of this popular 5 door hatch which is renowned for its fuel efficiency.
This 2018 model comes equipped with Climate control air conditioning, Multi-function steering wheel, Dual airbags, ABS, 5 Shoulder seatbelts, ISOFIX and more!
Looks sharp finished in Classic Silver Metallic with sporty styling and Rear spoiler, Has a stylish Black interior complimented with Blue seat inserts and Features Toyota's DOHC engine with generation 3 Hybrid Synergy Drive in front of a smooth automatic transmission giving very good fuel efficiency, also carries a 4 star UCSR safety rating.

Finance and Protecta Insurance products are also available for this vehicle.

Pre-purchase inspection can be arranged for out of town purchasers.

You can buy with confidence from Value Cars in the knowledge we are a totally New Zealand owned and funded provider of good quality cars with great after sales service for over 30 years.
