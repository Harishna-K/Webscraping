# 2018 Toyota Prius S safety plus
{Stock number. 24080}

The 2018 Toyota Prius S Safety Plus is a trim level of the Toyota Prius hybrid that offers a combination of advanced safety features, efficiency, and modern technology. The "S Safety Plus" trim is primarily available in certain markets (such as in Japan or some other regions) and typically includes various safety enhancements over the base model.

# Key features
- LDW: Lane Departure Warning (LDW) is a system designed to alert drivers when the vehicle unintentionally drifts out of its lane without signaling.

- Pre crush safety: The system continuously scans the road ahead, looking for potential obstacles or vehicles in its path. This is typically done using radar (which can detect objects even in low visibility) and a forward-facing camera (which helps identify pedestrians or lane markings).

- Push button start: Push Button Start is a convenience feature commonly found in modern vehicles, including the 2018 Toyota Prius S Safety Plus. It allows you to start and stop the engine without having to insert a traditional key into the ignition.
