8 Airbags - Plug in Hybrid - Low Kms

Introducing this Spacious Plug-in Hybrid Toyota Prius renowned for there excellent economy and high level of reliability.
This extremely tidy Prius is equipped with Climate control air conditioning, Proximity key with Push button start, 8 airbags, Reverse camera, Heated Seats & Steering wheel and has travelled only 51965km's from new!
Looks very smart finished in Clearwater Blue Metallic complimented by Silver exterior details through the roofline and pillars, chrome exterior accents, Driving lights on 15 inch Factory alloys, And has a stylish Black suede look interior with perforated seat inserts fitted with removable leather look seat covers and complimented by a woodgrain style trim. Features an 1800cc Hybrid Synergy Drive engine, With a Plug-in charging system and an Automatic transmission that gives great fuel efficiency, and also has a 5 star UCSR safety rating.
Finance & Protecta Insurance products are also available for this vehicle.

Pre-purchase inspection can be arranged for out of town purchasers.

You can buy with confidence from Value Cars in the knowledge we are a totally New Zealand owned & funded provider of good quality cars & great after sales service for over 30 years.
