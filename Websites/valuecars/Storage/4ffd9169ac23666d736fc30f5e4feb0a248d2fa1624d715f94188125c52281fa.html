VERY LOW KM - 6 SEATS - TURBO DIESEL - VERSATILE

Very low km 2019 Nissan Caravan NV350 Long DX turbo, flat floor rear wheel drive van with 6 seats, collision avoidance, stability control and has only travelled 18507kms! Looks smart finished in Gun Grey Metallic, and has Grey upholstery with lightly textured seat inserts complimented by a Black dash. Features a 2.5L DOHC VVT engine with automatic transmission with overdrive, also has a 5 star VSRR safety rating!!

This Caravan is a great option if you are in the market for a van, with it's huge load space, twin sliding side doors with the flat floor also. The cabin has seats for 3 with good head and leg room as well as the 3 seat bench in the rear.

Tradies, couriers, weekend warriors or even dirt bikers wanting to take the bikes with needing a trailer, the options are plenty. Don't miss this one, come on in, check it out for yourself.

Buyers looking for a spacious van like a Ford Transit, LDV, Toyota Hiace, Volkswagen Transporter or the likes may well be wise to consider this Nissan Caravan NV350 with the high roof & longer wheelbase.

Finance and Protecta Insurance products are also available for this vehicle.

Pre-purchase inspection can be arranged for out of town purchasers, and we can also ship at a cost of only $250 to NZ main
centres.

You can buy with confidence from Value Cars in the knowledge we are a totally New Zealand owned and funded provider of good quality cars with great after sales service for over 25 years.
