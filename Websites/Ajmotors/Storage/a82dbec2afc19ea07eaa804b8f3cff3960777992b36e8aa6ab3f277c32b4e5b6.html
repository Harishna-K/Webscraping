4X4, 7 Seater, Leather, Sunroof, Bluetooth

Adventure meets comfort in this 2013 Silver Nissan X-Trail! Equipped with 4WD, heated leather front seats, and a sunroof, this SUV is perfect for exploring off-road or cruising through the city. With features like Bluetooth, a reversing camera, and parking sensors, its designed to keep you connected, confident, and comfortable on every journey.

Key Features:

Stylish silver exterior with durable 17-inch alloy wheels.

4WD capability for all-terrain confidence.

Luxurious leather seats with heated front seats for extra comfort.

Reversing camera and parking sensors for stress-free parking.

Bluetooth connectivity for hands-free calls and music streaming.

Cruise control for smooth, relaxed highway driving.

Sunroof for added light and fresh air on your drives.

2 airbags for safety and peace of mind.
