# 2010 Honda Insight Hybrid
{Stock number. 23907}

The 2010 Honda Insight Hybrid is a compact hybrid car that was introduced as a more affordable alternative to other hybrid vehicles like the Toyota Prius.

# Key features
- Apple car play: In summary, Apple CarPlay is a great way to integrate your iPhone with your cars infotainment system, making driving safer and more convenient. Its available on many newer vehicles, but with aftermarket upgrades, older models can enjoy the benefits of CarPlay as well.

- Push button start: The system typically uses encrypted signals between the key fob and the car, making it more difficult for thieves to steal the vehicle by traditional methods, like hot-wiring or duplicating the ignition key.

- Reverse camera: A reverse camera, also known as a backup camera, is a safety feature that provides a view of the area behind a vehicle when it is in reverse. It is typically mounted on the rear of the car and displays the view on the cars infotainment screen or rearview mirror, making it easier for drivers to see obstacles or pedestrians in their path when backing up.
