Fairfield Chair Co.

Add to MyMarket

AMERICAN-MADE & FAMILY-OWNED FOR 103 YEARS Since 1921, we’ve been experts in bench made upholstery. Each piece is handcrafted in the shadows of the Blue Ridge mountains in Lenoir, NC. Today, we offer upholstery and casegoods for the whole home. Our thoughtful collection spans from living to dining to bedroom, modern to classic & in stock to custom. In addition to serving America’s top retail, e-commerce, and to-the-trade clients, we are a leading resource for all categories of upholstery and casegoods within the commercial segment. We are experts in the senior living category and excel within contract, hospitality, country clubs, public spaces and education sectors. To learn more and experience our collection, please visit fairfieldchair.com.

Maxwell Dresser

Maxwell merges clean lines with softly rounded corners, offering a modern yet timeless look. Crafted from oak solids and veneers, the Maxwell Dresser is available in both a Sunlit Oak or Caramel Finish and features seven spacious drawers.

Camille Bar Cabinet

The Camille Bar Cabinet takes a modern approach to traditional design. Crafted from rubberwood solids and topped with marble, it features an interior drawer, a reversible shelf with wine storage, and ample space for entertaining essentials. Finished in Linen with Antique Pewter Striping.

Salvador Cocktail Table

The Salvador tables are crafted in smooth metal and finished in rich gold. Featuring a playful animal print on all surfaces, this light-catching pattern adds a touch of drama and luxury to any aesthetic. The collection consists of a 44" round cocktail table and a 24" high drink table.
