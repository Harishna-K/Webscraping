The 2016 Mazda CX-5 20S is a well-equipped crossover SUV that offers a balance of performance, comfort, and efficiency. Below are the key features of the 2016 Mazda CX-5 20S:

### Engine and Performance:
- **Engine**: 2.0L 4-cylinder engine
- **Transmission**: 6-speed automatic transmission
- **Drive Type**: Front-wheel drive (FWD)

### Interior Features:
- **Seating**: 5-passenger seating with cloth upholstery
- **Infotainment**: 7-inch touchscreen display with Mazda Connect system
- **Climate Control**: Manual air conditioning with pollen filter
- **Steering Wheel**: Tilt and telescopic steering wheel with audio and cruise controls

### Safety Features:
- **ABS**: Anti-lock braking system
- **Traction and Stability Control**: Dynamic Stability Control (DSC) and Traction Control System (TCS)
- **Rearview Camera**: Standard rearview camera for easier parking.

### Technology and Convenience:
- **Keyless Entry**: Keyless entry and push-button start
- **Bluetooth**: Standard Bluetooth connectivity for hands-free calling and audio streaming
- **Cruise Control**: Standard cruise control
- **Power Windows and Locks**: Standard power-operated windows and locks


The 2016 Mazda CX-5 20S provides a comfortable ride, solid fuel economy, and a well-rounded set of features that make it a strong competitor in the compact crossover SUV segment.
