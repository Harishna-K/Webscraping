Herzog Veneers

Add to MyMarket

Herzog Veneers is a sixth-generation, women-owned family business with more than 120 years of expertise in the wood industry. We specialize in luxury wood veneers, offering a hand-curated selection of over 150 species, including natural, dyed, fumed, and recomposed options. Our matched veneer panels provide a seamless, luxurious look that surpasses the quality of laminates. With bespoke lumber selections and custom veneer options, we offer design flexibility for any project. As a Forest Stewardship Council (FSC)-certified company, we prioritize sustainability, making us the preferred choice for architects and interior designers seeking high-quality, eco-friendly, and visually stunning materials. Common Wood Veneer Applications include: • Wall Paneling • Cabinetry & Shelving • Ceilings • Doors • Furniture Design • Feature Walls • Wardrobe & Closets • Reception Desks & Counters • Kitchen Cabinet & Bar Fronts • Custom Built-ins • Decorative Accents & Inserts. Introducing our newest product LithoWood™, Paper-Backed Wood Veneer Sheets, enhanced with digitally printed rare wood veneer patterns. Combining natural wood elegance with cutting-edge digital printing technology, delivering luxury real wood aesthetics with consistency previously only available in laminates. Wood patterns are meticulously selected from an exquisite collection of veneer logs, accumulated over more than 40 years. LithoWood™ is crafted on sustainably certified wood veneer substrates with UV resistant inks.
