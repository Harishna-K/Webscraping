# Key Features

Alloy wheels: These are lighter and stronger wheels than regular steel wheels, often making the car look nicer and improving performance.

Auto headlights: The headlights turn on and off automatically based on the light outside, so you dont have to remember to do it.

Cruise control: This feature allows the car to maintain a steady speed on the highway without you having to keep your foot on the gas pedal.

Keyless entry: You can unlock the car doors without taking the key out of your pocket; just touch a button or pull the door handle.
