Our newly released furnishings include highly sought-after line extensions and fresh collections that juxtapose high design, excellent craftsmanship, and functionality. Villa & House Fall 2024

Explore our showrooms in IHFC 609 & 610.

Watson 4-Drawer and 2-Door Cabinet, Light Mocha Shimmer Weave

The Watson Collection showcases a custom shimmer grasscloth in an elegant grey-brown tone, offering a sleek, modern aesthetic with a bold, masculine appeal. It features two cabinet doors and four spacious drawers on soft-closing glides with custom-cast bronze bar pulls.

Nolan 4-Door Cabinet, Pristine Oak

The Nolan 4-Door Cabinet is expertly crafted from white oak veneer with a sleek, clear lacquer finish. Its bold, curved, and chunky elements draw inspiration from 1970s-80s design. The front showcases a stunning chevron wood grain pattern, adding texture and detail. Resting on recessed feet, the cabinet blends retro charm with modern elegance.
