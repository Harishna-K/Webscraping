TowBar, Cruise Ctrl, Carplay, Canopy

Take on any challenge with this 2017 Silver Ford Ranger! Rugged, reliable, and feature-packed, this ute is equipped with a canopy and towbar, making it perfect for work or adventure. With Android Auto, Apple CarPlay, and a reversing camera, this Ranger combines practicality with modern tech to keep you connected and confident on the road.

Key Features:

Sleek silver exterior paired with durable 17-inch alloy wheels.

6 airbags for maximum safety on the road.

Android Auto and Apple CarPlay for seamless connectivity and entertainment.

Reversing camera and parking sensors for effortless maneuvering.

Cruise control for smooth and relaxed long-distance drives.

Canopy included, offering secure storage for tools, gear, or luggage.

Towbar ready for towing trailers, boats, or caravans with ease.
