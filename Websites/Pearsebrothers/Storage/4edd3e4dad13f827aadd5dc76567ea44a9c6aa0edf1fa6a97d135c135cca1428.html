SEGWAY AT5 S Quad Pro ATV
The AT5 S Quad Pro is the pinnacle of the AT5 S range, designed to exceed expectations and redefine excellence in ATV technology and reliability. Building upon the solid foundation of the AT5 S EPS model, the AT5 Quad Pro takes your farming experience to an entirely new level of dependability and convenience.

Powertrain: Petrol Engine
Displacement: 499cc, 4-Stroke Single-Cylinder, DOHC Engine
Max Torque: 44 Nm
Horsepower: 39 hp
0-60km/h: 4.6 Seconds
Fuel Supply: Bosch Electronic Fuel Injection
Drive System Type: 2WD/4WD/4WD lock
Differential Lock: Front with Fixed Rear Axle
Transmission: CVTech
Smart Electronic Power Steering (EPS): Three Models: Standard / Comfort / Sport on EPS models only
Cooling System: Dual Oil Column Piston Cooling System + Liquid & Oil Heat Exchanger Cooling System
