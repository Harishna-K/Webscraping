# 2013 BMW 3 Series
{Stock number. 24356}


The 2013 BMW 3 Series is part of the F30 generation of the 3 Series, which debuted in 2012 and ran through 2019. The 2013 model year saw significant updates compared to its predecessor, including a redesigned exterior, a more refined interior, and the introduction of new technology features.

# Key features
- Memory seats: The 2013 BMW 3 Series offers memory seats as an available feature, typically included in higher trim levels or as part of optional packages. This feature allows drivers and front passengers to save their preferred seat position settings, making it easier to adjust the seat to the perfect position at the touch of a button.

- Rear fog lights: Rear fog lights are used in adverse weather conditions to help other drivers see your vehicle from behind. They are especially helpful when driving in fog, heavy rain, or snow, where standard tail lights may not be visible due to poor visibility.

- Heated seats: Heated seats are a popular feature in the 2013 BMW 3 Series, providing comfort during cold weather by warming the seat cushions and backrests. This feature is typically available in higher trims or as part of an optional package, such as the Premium Package.
