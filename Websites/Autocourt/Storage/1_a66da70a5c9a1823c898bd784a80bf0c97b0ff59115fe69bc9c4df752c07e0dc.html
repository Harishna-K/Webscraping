A 2001 Toyota Platz X, in dark green, that has travelled 106,750 kilometres.
The Platz is powered by a 150cc twin cam VVT-i motor (with timing chain) with a 4 stage automatic transmission, and has dual airbags, ABS brakes, CD player, power steering, electric windows, central locking, and climate control.
Good car, with good access and seating, roomy interior and boot, (especially with back seat folded down), excellent fuel economy and Toyota's reliability.
