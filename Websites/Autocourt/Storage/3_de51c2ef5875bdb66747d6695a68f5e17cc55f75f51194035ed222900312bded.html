A neat Honda CR-Z Alpha Hybrid coupe in grey, with 16 inch alloy wheels.
Powered by a 1500cc petrol engine with timing chain, with Honda's Integrated Motor Assist (IMA) hybrid-electric system, and CVT transmission, this compact sport coupe has travelled 41,100 kilometres.
The CR-Z is a lively performer but also achieves a 3 1/2 star fuel economy rating, 5.3 litre per 100km, and 4-star VSRR safety rating.
This proves that a hybrid car doesn't have to be boring.
Put a little bit of fun in your life and save money while you are enjoying it.
It comes with CD player, cruise control, electronic stability control, and a rear-view camera.
