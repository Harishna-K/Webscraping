211 Horsepower, Sport, Leather, Turbo, Ltd

Step into luxury and performance with this 2015 Nissan Skyline 200GT-T Type SP. Powered by a responsive 2.0L turbo petrol engine delivering 211 horsepower, this sporty sedan combines exhilarating performance with modern comforts like leather upholstery, heated seats, and radar cruise control. Loaded with advanced safety features, including forward collision mitigation and 360-degree cameras, this Skyline offers peace of mind and sophistication in one dynamic package.

Bluetooth connectivity for seamless hands-free use
360-degree cameras for ultimate parking confidence
