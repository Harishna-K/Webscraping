Branch Home

Add to MyMarket

Branch Home defined by two friends and colleagues, and now partners in design – Amanda Branch Covington and Amit Kochhar branched out and are curating a lifestyle brand piece by piece leaving their mark on the home furnishing world using materials and shapes we all love. The Branch point of view is fresh and in motion. As the name suggests, Branch is about sophisticated collections hand-crafted to create something new, while enjoying the journey of discovery. Amanda generally ignites the design process. She loves to reinvent traditional forms with distinctive finishing touches and subtle detail, inspired by runway fashion, architecture, nature, travel, art, and even day-to-day life. Amit knows how to turn ideas into reality, expertly pairing designs with production resources to bring a design to life. He is the master of juggling and balancing the business wearing many hats…even design—shhh!
