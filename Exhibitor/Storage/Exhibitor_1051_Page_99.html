Liora Manné

Add to MyMarket

Liora Manné's patented Lamontage® process changes the way we interact with textiles. Free of the limitations of traditional woven, knitted or printed textiles, our process produces handmade, fully-customizable, high-performance rugs, wall and ceiling covering, upholstery fabric and decorative art, which are anti-microbial, stain-resistant, bleach-cleanable and easy to maintain. Customize an existing design or provide your own inspiration at no additional cost with a low 24 square foot minimum. The Lamontage® process allows for full flexibility in creativity. Our LUSH collection introduces custom hand-tufted rugs in wool and silk blends. LUSH adds a luxurious and long-lasting element to any space. Our collections invigorate interiors with exceptional artistry and timeless appeal.
