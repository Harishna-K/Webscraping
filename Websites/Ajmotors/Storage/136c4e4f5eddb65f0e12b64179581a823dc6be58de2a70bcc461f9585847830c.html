New Tyres, Bluetooth, Family 7-Seats

Great choice! The 2015 Mazda Premacy is a versatile and stylish 7-seater designed for families who need both space and efficiency. Finished in a stunning light blue, this people mover is packed with practical features like sliding passenger doors for easy access, a reversing camera for effortless parking, and Bluetooth connectivity to keep you connected on the go. With its smooth 2000cc engine and custom 15-inch alloy wheels, the Premacy delivers both comfort and reliability for every journey.

Key Features:

7-Seater Configuration Perfect for families or extra passengers

15-Inch Custom Alloy Wheels Unique and stylish design

Sliding Passenger Doors Convenient access for all passengers

Reversing Camera Easy and safe parking every time

Bluetooth Connectivity Hands-free calls and music streaming

Dual Airbags Added safety for driver and front passenger

Fog Lights Improved visibility in tough conditions

Ignition Knob (Push-Turn Start) Modern and easy keyless start

2000cc Engine Reliable performance with great fuel efficiency

Smooth Automatic Transmission Effortless and comfortable driving
