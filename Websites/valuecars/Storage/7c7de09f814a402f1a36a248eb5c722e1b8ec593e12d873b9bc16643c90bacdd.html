LOW KM - STUNNING COLOUR - CRUISE - 4 AIRBAGS

Low km 2018 facelift model Renault Lutecia (Clio) Intense, 5 door European family hatch with climate, cruise control, part leather, push button start, 4 airbags, reverse camera and has only travelled 31254km's. Looks quite stunning Blue with Black factory body kit like styling side accents, driving lights and 17 inch alloys with Black highlights. Has a stylish Black interior with Black and Grey leather type seat inserts. Features a 1.2L turbo charged DOHC VVT engine and tiptronic transmission with good fuel efficiency. Also has a 4 star VSRR safety rating.

The Renault Clio (Lutecia) is a supermini car, produced by the French automobile manufacturer Renault. It was launched in 1990, and was in its fourth generation in 2012. The Clio has had substantial critical and commercial success, being consistently one of Europe's top-selling cars since its launch, and it is largely credited with restoring Renault's reputation and stature after a difficult second half of the 1980s. The Clio is one of only three cars to have been voted European Car of the Year twice. The Clio is sold as the Renault Lutécia in Japan. Lutecia is derived from the word Lutetia, a former Roman city that is now known as Paris.

The cabin comfortably seats five from a higher seating position for which provides good head and legroom and also aids entry and exit. There is a height adjustable drivers seat to enable the driver to easily find a suitable position for both comfort and good visibility. Split folding rear seats provide versatility of the rear cabin.
