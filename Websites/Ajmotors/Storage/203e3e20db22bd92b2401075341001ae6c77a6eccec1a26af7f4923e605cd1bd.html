4WD, Push Button, Power Seat Adjustments

Great pick! The 2015 Subaru XV is a stylish, capable, and adventure-ready SUV thats perfect for city driving and weekend getaways. Finished in crisp white, this XV comes equipped with Subarus legendary AWD system, ensuring confidence on any road. With 17-inch alloy wheels, fog lights, and paddle shifters, it offers both rugged performance and sporty driving dynamics. Inside, enjoy premium comfort with electric seat adjusters, dual-zone climate control, and push-button startmaking every drive a pleasure.
