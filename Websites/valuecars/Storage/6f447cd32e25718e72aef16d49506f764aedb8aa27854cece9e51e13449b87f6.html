LOW KM 2015 MODEL - 5 SPEED MANUAL - DARK INTERIOR

Low km 2015 third generation 5 speed manual model Mazda Axela four door family sedan with push button start, multi-function steering wheel and has only travelled only 43951kms! Looks sharp as a tack finished in Artic White with sleek wind cheating shape, has the desirable Black interior complimented by textured patterned inserts. Features an economical 1600cc engine with the 5 speed manual transmission with good fuel efficiency, also has a 5 star UCSR safety rating.

This third generation model sits atop of the SkyActiv chassis and is designed to be more aerodynamic than it's predecessor with a drag coefficient (Cd) of 0.26 for the sedan, slightly higher for the hatchback.

Axela has quickly become a very popular mid sized family hatch or sedan, preferred in it's class by many. Offering seating for five with good head and leg room. A higher seating position along with wide opening door spaces allow for ease of entry and exit. A height adjustable drivers seat allows the driver to easily find a position for comfort and good visibility. There is an adequately sized boot as would be expected in a modern family sedan.

Buyers considering the likes of Subaru Impreza, Liberty, Suzuki Baleno, Corolla, Volkswagen Golf, should take a look at the Mazda Axela/Mazda 3 as there are similar features or efficiency comparisons that could be made.

Finance and Protecta insurance products are also available for this vehicle.

Pre-purchase inspection can be arranged for out of town purchasers, and we can also ship at a cost of only $250 to NZ main centres.

You can buy in confidence from Value Cars in the knowledge we are a totally New Zealand owned and funded provider of good quality cars with great after sales service for over 25 years.
