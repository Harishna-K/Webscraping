The 2015 Honda Grace Hybrid EX is a hybrid sedan that offers a blend of comfort, efficiency, and advanced features

### **Performance & Engine:**
- **Powertrain:** 1.5L i-VTEC petrol engine paired with a 1.3 kWh lithium-ion battery and an electric motor.
- **Fuel Economy:** Excellent fuel efficiency due to its hybrid system, typically around 26-30 km/L (depending on the driving conditions).

### **Interior & Comfort:**
- **Seating:** Comfortable seating for up to five people, with fabric upholstery.
- **Climate Control:** Dual-zone automatic climate control.
- **Power-adjustable Seats:** Driver's seat is typically power-adjustable for added comfort.
- **Rear Seats:** 60/40 split-folding rear seats for increased cargo flexibility.

### **Safety Features:**
- **Multi-angle Rearview Camera:** Offers different viewing angles for easier parking.
- **ABS & EBD:** Anti-lock Braking System (ABS) and Electronic Brake-force Distribution (EBD) for enhanced safety.
- **Airbags:** Front, side, and curtain airbags.

### **Technology:**
- **Smart Key Entry:** Keyless entry and push-button start for convenience.
- **Cruise Control:**
- **Bluetooth Hands-free:** For hands-free calling and music streaming.

### **Other Notable Features:**
- **Eco Assist System:** Helps improve fuel economy by providing feedback on driving style.

These features combine to make the 2015 Honda Grace Hybrid EX a well-rounded vehicle with a focus on fuel efficiency, comfort, and safety.
