Amisco Industries Ltd.

Add to MyMarket

Amisco, a Quebec-based Canadian company, takes great pride in designing and producing residential furniture that satisfies the tastes and needs of all. Our top-quality furniture reflects the latest North American design trends, and we are committed to producing pieces our customers will enjoy using day after day. Every item we make is exceptionally comfortable, superbly finished, durable—and backed by worry-free after-sale support. Amisco—head and shoulders above the rest of our industry!

Rachel table & Barbara chair

Elevate your dining space with the Rachel table and Barbara chair. The Rachel table’s versatile "C" shaped base, customizable in various materials and sizes, pairs perfectly with the Barbara chair’s Scandinavian elegance, featuring a curved backrest and padded seat. Together, they bring style, comfort, and sophistication to your home.

Bryana swivel stool

The Bryana swivel stool blends farmhouse, transitional, and contemporary styles. Its curved, upholstered backrest ensures comfort, while the open design adds a touch of sophistication. With a 360-degree swivel seat and sturdy metal base, this stool offers both functionality and elegance.

Amelia table & Beatrice chair

Enhance your space with the Amelia table and Beatrice chair. The Amelia table's elegant pedestal base and hydraulic slide system expand seating effortlessly. Paired with the Beatrice chair's plush seat and sophisticated wood legs, this combo brings style, comfort, and charm to any contemporary setting.
