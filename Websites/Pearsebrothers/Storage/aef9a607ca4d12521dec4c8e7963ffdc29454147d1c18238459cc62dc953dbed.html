Exceeding the minimum quad bike safety standards set by the Australian Government, the AT6 L can withstand tilt angles of up to 33° and comes fitted with an Operator Protection Device for added peace of mind when its time to venture into rough terrain.

Packed with modern technology, the AT6 Ls T-BOX and smartphone app allows you to enhance your operations and embrace the power of the digital world with access to real-time data and functions including remote monitoring, remote control, and safety monitoring through the onboard Smart Commanding System (SCS).
