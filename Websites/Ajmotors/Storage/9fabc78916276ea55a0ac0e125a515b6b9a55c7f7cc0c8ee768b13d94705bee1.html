# 2017 Nissan Note E-power X
{Stock number. 24377}

The 2017 Nissan Note e-Power X is a compact hatchback featuring Nissan's innovative e-Power hybrid system. This system is unique because it uses a petrol engine to charge a battery, which then powers an electric motor to drive the wheelsmaking it essentially an electric vehicle with a range extender.

# Key features
- 360 Degree reverse camera: A 360-degree reverse camera, also known as a 360-degree parking camera system or surround-view camera system, is an advanced safety feature designed to give the driver a bird's-eye view of the area surrounding the vehicle.

- Parking sensors: Parking sensors are a common vehicle feature designed to assist drivers with parking and avoiding obstacles when maneuvering in tight spaces. They work by using ultrasonic or electromagnetic technology to detect objects near the vehicle, alerting the driver to potential hazards. These sensors are typically installed in the front and/or rear bumpers of the vehicle and are integrated into the cars safety system.

- LDW: LDW stands for Lane Departure Warning, a safety feature designed to help prevent accidents by alerting the driver when the vehicle unintentionally drifts out of its lane without signaling. It is part of a broader category of advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS) aimed at improving road safety and preventing collisions.
