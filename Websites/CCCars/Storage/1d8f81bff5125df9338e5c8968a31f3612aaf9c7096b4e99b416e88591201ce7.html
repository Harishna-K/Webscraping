The **2015 Toyota Aqua S Push Start** (also known as the Toyota Prius c in some markets) is a compact hybrid that blends excellent fuel efficiency with a practical design, making it a great choice for urban driving or eco-conscious buyers.

. **Fuel Efficiency**:
- One of the standout features of the Aqua is its **exceptional fuel economy**. With its hybrid powertrain, it delivers some of the best mileage in its class.

**Push Start System**:
- The **Push Start** feature is one of the convenient tech additions to the Aqua S. You dont need to insert a key to start the car simply press the brake pedal and push the start button to get the engine running. This adds an extra layer of convenience, especially for drivers who prefer keyless entry and ignition

**Comfort and Interior Features**:
- **Air Conditioning**: The Aqua comes with automatic climate control for maintaining a comfortable cabin temperature.

**Safety Features**:
**Airbags**: The Aqua comes with dual front airbags,
- **Anti-lock Braking System (ABS)** and **Electronic Brake-force Distribution (EBD)** are included to enhance braking performance and stability.
- **Vehicle Stability Control (VSC)** and **Traction Control** are standard, helping to improve handling and reduce the risk of skidding in slippery conditions.

**Compact and Sporty Look**:
- The **2015 Aqua** has a modern, sporty look with smooth, flowing lines and a compact footprint. Its an attractive hatchback that manages to look sleek and contemporary while maintaining its eco-friendly vibe.
