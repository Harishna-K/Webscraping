A very nice Subaru Forester 2.0i-L AWD, in red with 17 alloy wheels and rear spoiler.
This tidy example has the later 2 litre Boxer engine with timing chain, coupled to CVT transmission, and has travelled 32,400 kilometres.
The Forester has built up a solid reputation over many years as a reliable wagon with some off-road capabilities.
This version has quite a list of factory options including, CD player, climate control, cruise control, EyeSight, Si-Drive and X-Mode.
EyeSight driver assist technology with twin cameras mounted at the top of the windscreen which combine seven features for frontal collision avoidance, lane departure warning, and adaptive cruise control.
The Si-Drive engine performance management system has three modes to enable the engine characteristics to be altered to suit driving conditions, from economic around town, to highway cruising, and when desired, to make full use of the 2.0 litre engine.
X-Mode is an advanced form of traction control to assist in slippery conditions.
This up-market model also has heated front seats which are electric adjustable, power-operated hatch and a rear-view camera.
