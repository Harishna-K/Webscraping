Sleep On Green

Add to MyMarket

Sleep On Green® is an artisan mattress company with a 40-year legacy of handcrafting fine beds. Born out of necessity following their son's tragic motorcycle accident in 2007, Fidenzio and Grazia Bizzotto sought to create an eco-luxury mattress that was not only incredibly comfortable but also restorative. Leveraging their combined 30 years of experience in home furnishing and design, Fidenzio Bizzotto assembled a global team of sleep experts, researchers, and producers to develop the inaugural Sleep On Green® mattress. Their mission: to meticulously craft each piece by hand, ensuring that the detail and precision are evident in every stitch. Sleep On Green® believes that Mother Nature provides the purest, chemical-free ingredients. Natural materials promote better health, improved sleep, environmental sustainability, and mattress longevity. Their materials are plant-based, and for each mattress sold, trees are planted in affected forests worldwide. In partnership with One Tree Planted, they support reforestation efforts in regions impacted by devastating wildfires, such as California and Australia, as well as areas in Florida's Everglades ravaged by deforestation.

Sleep On Green debuts at High Point Market Fall 2024

Sleep On Green, a 40-year-old Italian artisan mattress company, will make their High Point Market debut on Oct 26-30, 2024 for the very first time. Premiering their newest design innovation 'The Dolce Vita' eco-luxury mattress in the Gamma showroom within Casa Italia at 130 W Commerce Ave.
