# 2017 Toyota C-HR G
{Stock number. 24085}

The 2017 Toyota C-HR G is a compact crossover SUV that falls under the "G" trim level, which is typically a higher or mid-range trim offering more features than the base model.

# Key features
- ACC: The ACC feature in the C-HR allows for safer and more relaxed driving, especially in situations like highway cruising or in traffic.

- Pre crush safety: Pre-Collision System (PCS) is designed to work in a variety of driving conditions and helps reduce the risk of accidents, especially in urban environments where sudden stops are common. The inclusion of this system in Toyota Safety Sense-P (TSS-P) is a major step in improving safety and making driving less stressful.

- LDW: LDW stands for Lane Departure Warning, a safety feature commonly found in modern vehicles. It helps alert the driver if their vehicle begins to unintentionally drift out of its lane without the use of a turn signal.
