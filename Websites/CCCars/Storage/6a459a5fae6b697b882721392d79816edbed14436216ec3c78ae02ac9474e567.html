The 2022 Suzuki Swift XG is a popular compact car offering a good balance of style, practicality, and efficiency.

### Engine and Performance:
- **Engine**: 1.2L 4-cylinder Dualjet engine (petrol)
- **Fuel Economy**: 5.0 L/100km

### Interior Features:
- **Upholstery**: Fabric seats for a comfortable and durable interior
- **Dashboard**: Simple, ergonomic layout with easy-to-use controls
- **Air Conditioning**: Manual air conditioning with air filter
- **Steering Wheel**: Adjustable tilt steering wheel (with audio controls on higher trims)
- **Seats**: Split-folding rear seats for added flexibility in cargo space
- **Storage**: Decent boot space and compartments for storage

### Safety Features:
- **ABS (Anti-lock Braking System)**: Standard for improved braking performance
- **EBD (Electronic Brakeforce Distribution)**: Optimizes brake force to each wheel based on load
- **ESP (Electronic Stability Program)**: Helps maintain control in slippery conditions
- **Child Seat Anchors**: ISOFIX points for secure child seat installation
- **Crash Safety**: Designed with Suzuki's Total Effective Control Technology (TECT) body to enhance safety in the event of a crash

### Convenience and Comfort:
- **Power Windows**: All windows powered with auto up/down for the driver's window
- **Central Locking**: Keyless entry and central locking for added convenience
- **Steering**: Electric power steering for ease of handling
- **Trip Computer**: Displays fuel consumption, range, and other useful driving statistics

The **2022 Suzuki Swift XG** is designed to be an affordable, practical compact car with a modern infotainment system, good fuel efficiency, and safety features
