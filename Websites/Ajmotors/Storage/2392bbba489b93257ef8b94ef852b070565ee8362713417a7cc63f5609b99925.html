# 2018 Mazda CX-3 20S PRO ACTIVE S PACKAGE
{Stock number. 23100}

The 2018 Mazda CX-3 20S Pro Active S Package is a compact SUV known for its sporty handling, stylish design, and high-quality interior. The CX-3 is noted for its agile handling, making it fun to drive in urban environments and on winding roads. Its compact size also contributes to good maneuverability.

# Key features
- ACC: a driver assistance feature that automatically adjusts the vehicle's speed to maintain a safe following distance from the car ahead.

- Heads up display: Shows current speed, allowing for easy monitoring without looking down.

- 360 Degree reverse camera: A system provides a birds-eye view of the area surrounding the vehicle, which is especially useful when parking or maneuvering in tight spaces.

If you're looking for more information about the car, feel free to reach out! Whether you have questions about specific features or need assistance with anything else, our team is happy to help.
