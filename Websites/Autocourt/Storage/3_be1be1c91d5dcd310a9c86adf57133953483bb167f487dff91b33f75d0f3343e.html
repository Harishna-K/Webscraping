Very nice 2014 Subaru Outback in dark blue, with 17 inch alloy wheels, that has travelled 49,600 kilometres.
Powered by a later double overhead camshaft motor which develops 127 kW, (with timing chain rather than belt), combined with Subaru's Lineartronic transmission, fuel consumption has been reduced by 6.3 per cent over the earlier models.
The Si-Drive engine performance management system has three modes to enable the engine characteristics to be altered to suit driving conditions, from economic around town, to highway cruising, and when desired, to make full use of the 2.5 litre engine.
The EyeSight driver assist technology with twin cameras mounted at the top of the windscreen combines seven features for frontal collision avoidance, lane departure warning, and adaptive cruise control.
The latest X-Mode traction control system assists in controlling the car in slippery conditions.
It also comes fitted with a CD player, 7 airbags, climate control, heated seats, electric adjusting front seats, smart key starting/locking, rear-view camera, and hands-free tailgate.
The Outback has a 5-star VSRR safety rating.
