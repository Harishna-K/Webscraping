# 2016 Toyota Aqua G GS Cruise Control
{Stock number. 25429}

The 2016 Toyota Aqua G GS is a part of the Aqua (also known as the Prius C in some markets) lineup, offering a compact, fuel-efficient hybrid car that is perfect for urban driving and daily commutes. The G GS trim is one of the higher variants, providing a good balance of comfort, features, and efficiency.

# Key features
- LDW: LDW uses cameras and sensors to monitor the vehicles position relative to lane markings on the road. If the system detects the car moving out of its lane without the use of turn signals, it will alert the driver through visual or auditory warnings (usually a beep or flashing light).

- Push button start: You must have the key fob with you inside the vehicle (usually in your pocket or bag). As long as the key fob is within range (typically a few feet of the car), the system will recognize it.

- Cruise control: To use the cruise control, you first need to be driving at a steady speed (typically above 30 km/h or 20 mph). Once youre at your desired speed, you can engage the system by pressing the cruise control button on the steering wheel (or stalk, depending on the vehicle setup).
