# 2017 Mazda CX-3 20S
{Stock number. 24382}

The 2017 Mazda CX-3 20S is a compact crossover SUV that blends sporty driving dynamics with a stylish design, offering a more refined alternative to many other vehicles in its segment

# Key features
- I-Stop: When the vehicle is at a complete stop and conditions are right (like the engine is warmed up and the climate control doesn't need maximum output), the system automatically shuts off the engine to save fuel.

- Push Button start: The Push Button Start system is a convenient feature available in many modern vehicles, including the 2017 Mazda CX-3. It allows you to start and stop your engine without the need to insert a key into the ignition

- Parking sensors: Parking sensors are a handy feature in many modern cars, including the 2017 Mazda CX-3, that help drivers park safely and avoid obstacles when maneuvering in tight spaces.
