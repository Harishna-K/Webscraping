Made Goods

Add to MyMarket

At Made Goods, we focus on creating special statement pieces for luxury designers and boutiques. We collaborate with artisans from around the world to create our collections, which include mirrors, furniture, lighting, and decorative accessories. Texture and materials are our primary focus, giving our pieces a look and feel that is uniquely Made Goods. We can also customize our line to meet your specific needs. Appointments are required to visit the showroom. Please email press@madegoods.com for appointment information. We look forward to working with you!

Our brands/collections consist of:

  • Blue Pheasant
  • Burton James, Inc.
  • Pigeon & Poodle
  • Thucassi

Archer Buffet

The Archer buffet bestows an intriguing visual to a room with its intricate hand-carved design. Defined patterns of raised mango wood hold slight variations that are to be expected.

Holland Oval Dining Extension Table

Made from walnut or poplar burl and standing atop sturdy, cone-shaped legs, the Holland dining extension table has an inviting presence for any dining room. Seating ranges from 6, 8, or 10 chairs with the extension. The dining table ranges from 72" - 108". Each table comes with two 18" extensions. Slight variations may occur between pieces.

Amara Upholstered Coffee Table

Standing atop tapered legs with elegant rounded corners, the Amara coffee table evokes a refined touch. Poplar burl's naturally occurring irregular grain patterns add character, and stunning cane panels complement an upholstered top cushion. Further customization is offered with one of our luxe upholstery fabrics or COM.
