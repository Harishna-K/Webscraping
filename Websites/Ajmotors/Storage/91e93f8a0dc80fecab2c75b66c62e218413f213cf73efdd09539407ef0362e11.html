# 2012 Subaru Impreza Sports
{Stock number. 23954}

Overall, the 2012 Subaru Impreza Sport is a solid choice for anyone looking for an affordable, reliable, and sporty hatchback or sedan with all-wheel drive.

# Key features
- Eyesight: EyeSight is Subaru's advanced driver-assistance system (ADAS), designed to enhance safety and improve driving experience by using cameras and sensors to monitor the road ahead and provide real-time alerts or intervene when necessary

- Pre crush safety: Pre-Collision Safety System is closely associated with EyeSight, but even vehicles without EyeSight can offer some level of pre-collision protection.

- LDW:
LDW (Lane Departure Warning) is a driver assistance system designed to alert the driver when the vehicle unintentionally drifts out of its lane without using the turn signal. It is a key component of modern vehicle safety systems, helping prevent accidents caused by driver inattention or drowsiness.
