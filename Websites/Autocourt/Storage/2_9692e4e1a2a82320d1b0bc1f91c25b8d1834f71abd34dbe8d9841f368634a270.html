2011 Lexus RX450h Hybrid 2WD, in white with spoiler and 19 inch alloy wheels, that has travelled 42,300 kilometres.
This superb wagon has a 3.5 litre petrol/electric hybrid engine with 6-speed automatic transmission, which delivers 6.6L/100km economy.
As you would expect, the Lexus has a long list of factory options
including luxurious leather seats, (electric adjustable & heated front seats), multiple airbags, CD, cruise control, LH-side & rear-view cameras.
