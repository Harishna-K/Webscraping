Adesso Inc.

Add to MyMarket

Adesso was established in 1994 based on the belief that there was an under-served niche among consumers who sought high-end, contemporary home products at moderate prices. Since then, Adesso has not only revolutionized the home industry with its innovative products, but also gained substantial recognition for its well-designed and well-priced lamps and RTA furniture, quickly establishing itself as an industry leader and consumer favorite. In lighting, products include a growing assortment of LED task lighting, LED torchieres, decorative table lamps, floor lamps and pendants and our newest division, ADS360. . Adesso's furniture and accessory offerings include seating, coffee and accent tables, coat racks, shelving, magazine racks and screens.

Lite Up Play by Kooduu with sound by JBL

Experience the perfect fusion of Danish lamp design and remarkable sound with powerful bass, thanks to SOUND BY JBL. Adorned with exclusive wood and brass details, this stunning portable speaker lamp effortlessly enhances any environment, both indoors and outdoors. The light offers adjustable brightness levels & 8 hours of battery life.

Linis Table Lamp

The Linis Table Lamp exudes a refined charm that complements any decor with a classic antique brass finish. The lamp's white glass shade, which has a glossy exterior and frosted interior, can be adjusted to multiple heights with a telescoping pole. The touch switch on top of the pole allows you to effortlessly adjust the lighting to suit your mood.

Ophelia Floor Lamp

Elevate your home with our stunning Ophelia Floor Lamp. The antique brass finish adds a touch of timeless sophistication, while the tapered pleated white fabric shade gently diffuses light, creating a soft and inviting glow. The chunky natural wood base provides a sturdy and stylish foundation, infusing your space with rustic warmth and character.
