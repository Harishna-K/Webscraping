LOOM Imports

Add to MyMarket

LOOM Imports is a woman-owned handmade textiles company, based in Asheville, NC & Oaxaca, Mexico. We are a mission centered, B-Corp Certified social enterprise. Loom offer's collections of handcrafted wool rugs in addition to custom, made-to-order textiles based on designers, artists & clients' own needs & commissioned designs. LOOM Imports grew out of personal connections to master artisans throughout the world & a deep commitment to sustainable & fair trade. We envision a more conscious future, where consumers are empowered to know how & where their goods are made. We believe that our homes & gathering spaces should be adorned with objects that hold deeper meaning & honor human connection. We believe supporting artisans in remote communities can be a driver for eradicating poverty & for preserving cultural heritage.
