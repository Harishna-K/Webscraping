# 2014 Subaru XV 2.0L 4WD
{Stock number. 24675}

The 2014 Subaru XV 2.0L 4WD, also known as the Subaru Crosstrek in some markets, is a compact crossover SUV. It is powered by a 2.0L 4-cylinder engine, providing a balance between power and fuel efficiency

# Key features
- Eyesight: The 2014 Subaru XV 2.0L 4WD comes equipped with Subaru's EyeSight Driver Assist Technology on certain trim levels. EyeSight is a suite of advanced safety features that uses stereo cameras mounted near the rearview mirror to monitor traffic and the road ahead. This system is designed to enhance the driver's awareness and help prevent accidents.

- LDW: LDW stands for Lane Departure Warning, and it's an important safety feature often found as part of advanced driver-assist systems like Subaru EyeSight.

- Pre crush safety: The Pre-Collision Safety system in the 2014 Subaru XV 2.0L 4WD, which is part of the EyeSight Driver Assist Technology, is designed to help mitigate the effects of a potential collision or, in some cases, prevent one altogether
