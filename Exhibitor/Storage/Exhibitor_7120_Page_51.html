Eastern Accents

Add to MyMarket

Discover the finest in home textiles. Eastern Accents is a trend-leading designer and manufacturer of luxury bedding, curtain panels, decorative accessories, and more home decor. Family-owned and proudly based in Chicago for over thirty years, we pride ourselves in the highest quality American craftsmanship, a leading design team, and unparalleled customer service. From impeccable fine linens and beautiful top-of-bed to intricate decorative pillows and handcrafted upholstered furniture, Eastern Accents is the go-to resource for the very best in soft home decor.

35th Anniversary Party, New Design Center, Platform Beds, Bedding Intros & More from Eastern Accents for Fall 2024.

Eastern Accents is turning 35! The leading home textiles brand, which has its manufacturing base in Chicago, will be celebrating its 35th anniversary at Fall High Point Market 2024, while also unveiling its new Design Center, upholstered platform beds, and 11 new collections of bedding.

Romilly Collection by Eastern Accents

Romilly by Eastern Accents Luxuriate in the glitz and glamor of palatial Romilly. This opulent collection is a modern take on regal style—featuring an embroidered damask drenched in champagne hues. Sparing no pleats, beads, or sparkle, Romilly offers layers of appliqued pillows, shining top-of-bed, and a pleated bed skirt.

Gustav by de ‘Medici by Eastern Accents

Gustav by de ‘Medici by Eastern Accents Explore a world of luxurious inspiration with unabashedly chic Gustav. A digital print on satin pairs vintage safari imagery with traditional Japanese art for a unique aesthetic rich in pattern and color. Accentuate this saturated palette with exquisite matte velvet pillows and 100% organic cotton.

Colt by Niche by Eastern Accents

Colt by Niche by Eastern Accents Streamlined style is getting a polished makeover. An irresistible blue and gray palette and charming geometric motifs make Colt a well-balanced collection with plenty of character. Add an extra pop of pattern with a retro-inspired geometric bed scarf and curtain panel.
