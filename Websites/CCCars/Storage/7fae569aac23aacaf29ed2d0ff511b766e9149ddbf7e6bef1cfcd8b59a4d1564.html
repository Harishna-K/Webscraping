The 2014 Mazda Premacy 20C is a compact minivan, known for its practicality and efficiency.


**Interior and Comfort**:
- **Seating Capacity**: 7 seats with flexible seating arrangements (captain's chairs in the middle row, and a 2+2+3 seating layout).
- **Air Conditioning**: Manual climate control.
- **Power Windows**: Standard electric windows for convenience.
- **Adjustable Steering Wheel**: Tilt-adjustable steering for better driving comfort.

**Safety Features**:
- **Airbags**:
- **Anti-lock Braking System (ABS)**: Helps maintain control during hard braking.
- **Electronic Brake-force Distribution (EBD)**: Optimizes brake force for better stability.
- **Traction Control System (TCS)**: Helps prevent wheel spin during acceleration.
- **Stability Control**: Improves vehicle stability, especially in challenging conditions.

**Convenience and Practicality**:
- **Power Side Mirrors**: Adjustable side mirrors with electrical controls.
- **Foldable Seats**: 2nd and 3rd-row seats can be folded down for expanded cargo space.

**Technology**:
- **Bluetooth Connectivity**: For hands-free calls and audio streaming.

These features make the 2014 Mazda Premacy 20C a practical and family-friendly vehicle with decent fuel efficiency and a comfortable driving experience.
