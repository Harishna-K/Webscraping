Facelift, Push Button, Rev Camera, Hybrid

Looking for a stylish, fuel-efficient, and feature-packed hatchback? This 2018 Black Toyota Aqua is the perfect match! Equipped with advanced safety features like lane departure warning and forward collision warning, plus eco-friendly hybrid technology, this Aqua offers comfort, confidence, and convenience all in one sleek package.

Key Features:

Sleek black exterior paired with 15-inch alloy wheels.

Petrol hybrid engine for incredible fuel efficiency and reduced
emissions.

Advanced safety features, including lane departure warning and
forward collision warning.

Push-button start for modern convenience and effortless ignition.

Reversing camera for stress-free parking and navigation.

Eco and EV modes to maximize fuel savings and minimize your
environmental impact.

Dual airbags for added safety and peace of mind.
