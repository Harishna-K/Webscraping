# 2019 Toyota Corolla Hybrid
{Stock number. 22453}

The 2019 Corolla Hybrid is a solid choice for those looking for a reliable, fuel-efficient sedan with modern technology and safety features. Its particularly appealing for city driving and daily commutes.

# Key features
- Hybrid Synergy Drive (HSD): A Toyota's hybrid technology that combines an internal combustion engine with an electric motor to optimize performance and fuel efficiency.

- LDW/LDA: Monitors lane markings and alerts the driver if the vehicle begins to drift out of its lane without a turn signal being activated.

- Adaptive Cruise Control: Uses radar and cameras to detect the speed and distance of the car in front. If that vehicle slows down, the system will reduce your speed accordingly.

If you're looking for more information about the car, feel free to reach out! Whether you have questions about specific features or need assistance with anything else, our team is happy to help.
