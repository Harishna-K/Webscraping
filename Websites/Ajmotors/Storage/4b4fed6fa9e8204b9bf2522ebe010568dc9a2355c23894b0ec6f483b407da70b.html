1 Owner, TowBar, Cruise Ctrl, Turbo Diesel

Discover a workhorse with reliability, efficiency, and comfort with this 2016 Toyota Hiace. This one-owner vehicle has been meticulously maintained and is powered by a robust 3.0L turbo diesel engine, making it an ideal companion for both business and personal use.

3.0L Turbo Diesel Engine: Delivers excellent torque and power, ideal for hauling and long drives while maintaining impressive fuel efficiency.

Cruise Control: Perfect for highway driving, cruise control helps reduce fatigue and allows for more relaxed journeys.

Reversing Camera: Parking and reversing are a breeze, thanks to the clear, easy-to-use reversing camera that enhances visibility and safety.

Towbar Installed: Ready to handle your towing needs, this Hiace comes with a towbar, making it perfect for trailers, small boats, or additional cargo.

New Warrant of Fitness (WoF): Drive with confidence knowing that this Hiace has passed its latest inspection, ensuring its road-ready and safe.

One-Owner Vehicle: This Hiace has had only one previous owner, contributing to its well-maintained condition and clean history.

Spacious Cargo Area: Known for its roomy interior, the Hiace offers ample space to accommodate tools, equipment, or supplies for work, or even conversion for travel and camping.

Legendary Toyota Reliability: Built to last, the Hiace is famous for its durability and low maintenance costs, making it a smart choice for anyone needing a dependable vehicle.

This 2016 Toyota Hiace is a solid investment in reliability and practicality. Whether you're looking for a dependable business vehicle or a spacious option for adventure, this Hiace is equipped to meet your needs.
