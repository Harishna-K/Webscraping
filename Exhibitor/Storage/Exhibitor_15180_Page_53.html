Eliko Rugs by David Ariel

Add to MyMarket

Eliko Rugs, founded by the dynamic father-son team of David and Ariel, boasts over 45 years of expertise in the rug industry. This renowned duo specializes in curating a diverse collection of handmade rugs, highly coveted by decorators and design connoisseurs. By actively collaborating with industry professionals and keeping abreast of the latest trends, David and Ariel offer personalized service, partnering with clients to find the perfect rug that aligns with their specific color, size, and design preferences. Their exquisite collection ranges from vintage Persian masterpieces to contemporary and tribal Tulu designs, along with a selection of decorative antique rugs, ensuring a unique and captivating selection for every taste.

Eliko Rugs by David Ariel Partners with Celebrated Designer Anne-Marie Barton

Eliko Rugs by David Ariel announces its first-ever licensed collection—developed in partnership with interior designer and media personality Anne-Marie Barton—debuting in the rug company’s High Point Market showroom in October.

Anne-Marie Barton x Eliko Rugs by David Ariel Rug Collection

The Anne-Marie Barton luxury rug collection for Eliko Rugs by David Ariel features hand-knotted weaves of the finest wool, mohair, and cashmere in plush, high-pile expressions. Reflecting both vintage and modern aesthetics, the collection of over 25 signature area rug styles celebrates the art of layering in floor coverings. Handcrafted in India.

Pave

Pave is crafted with 85 percent wool and 15 percent cotton in a rich nutmeg hue. Available in size 5 x 7 feet. (Approximately 150 x 215 cm)

Amalfi

Amalfi can be used as either a rug or a wall hanging with 24" of fringe. Available in 5 x 7 or 3 x 6 made of 100% wool.
