LDN Collections

Add to MyMarket

LDN Collections was born out of a shared passion for beautiful art between friends with a combined 40 years of experience in the industry. Our mission is to work directly with you to deliver unique and inspiring art, framed immaculately with a no-nonsense service, allowing you to spend your time focusing on all other design aspects of your project. We believe that design is constantly evolving with the times and our ever-growing archive of images reflects this. Utilising our industry contacts and relationships with artists and photographers to source art that is not only on trend but often exclusive to us, we will be able to find the perfect piece to complement your décor. Each member of the founding LDN family brings a unique perspective to the table, all having held senior leadership roles in all areas of the industry including consultation, design, creative development and manufacturing, so you can rest assured that you are in safe hands and we will be with you every step of the way.

LDN Art Collections Debut at High Point Market

We're thrilled to be debuting our collection at the High Point Market, giving our customers an opportunity to see first hand the quality and craftsmanship of our work. Our goal is not only to provide exclusive, customizable pieces, but to do so in the spirit of partnership and collaboration.
