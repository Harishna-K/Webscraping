# 2017 Toyota Prius PHEV
{Stock number.25059}

The 2017 Toyota Prius Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicle (PHEV) is a versatile and efficient option for anyone interested in blending traditional gasoline power with electric driving.

# Key features
- LDW:
LDW stands for Lane Departure Warning, which is a safety feature commonly found in modern vehicles, including the 2017 Toyota Prius PHEV. It uses cameras or sensors to monitor lane markings on the road, and if the vehicle unintentionally drifts out of its lane without signaling, it alerts the driver with an audible warning or visual cue.

- Pre crush safety: If the driver does not take action in time, the system can automatically apply the brakes to reduce speed and potentially avoid or mitigate the impact of the collision.

- ACC: ACC takes it a step further by automatically adjusting your speed to maintain a safe distance from the vehicle in front of you. If the car ahead slows down, your Prius will automatically reduce speed to maintain a preset distance (which you can usually adjust). When traffic clears, it will accelerate back up to the set speed.
