# 2012 Toyota Alphard Hybrid SR
{Stock number. 24030}

The 2021 Toyota Alphard Hybrid SR is a luxury minivan designed with a focus on comfort, technology, and fuel efficiency. It's a part of Toyota's Alphard lineup, which is known for its high-end features and spaciousness. The hybrid version combines the luxury of the Alphard with a more eco-friendly hybrid powertrain.

# Key features
- Power sliding door: The power sliding door is a key feature in many modern minivans, including the Toyota Alphard Hybrid SR. It provides enhanced convenience, safety, and ease of access, especially in tight parking spaces.

- Power tailgate: The power tailgate is another convenient feature available on the Toyota Alphard Hybrid SR, designed to enhance ease of use and provide a more premium user experience. It allows you to automatically open and close the rear tailgate with minimal effort, making loading and unloading cargo more convenient.

- Cruise control: Cruise control is a standard feature in the Toyota Alphard Hybrid SR, offering convenience and comfort, particularly during long drives or highway cruising. The system allows the driver to set a constant speed without having to keep their foot on the accelerator, making the driving experience more relaxed and less tiring.
