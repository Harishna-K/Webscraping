CHECK THIS VIBRANT MERCEDES AMG LINE CLA 200 OUT !!!

The 2019 Mercedes-Benz CLA 200 finished in stunning Sun yellow is a blend of luxury, performance, and cutting-edge technology. This model features a 1.4-ltr Direct Injection turbocharged 4-cylinder engine that delivers a robust 120kw and 250Nm of torque , ensuring a spirited driving experience. The AMG version adds sportier elements, including a refined suspension system, aggressive styling cues, and upgraded braking components, making it not only a stylish Sedan but also a capable performer on the road.
