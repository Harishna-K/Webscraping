V6 TURBO DIESEL, 4WD, LOW KMS, NO DEPOSIT FINANCE

Unleash the power of adventure with this 2023 Ford Ranger Wildtrak a rugged yet refined 4WD pickup built to handle anything you throw at it. Powered by a 3.0L V6 turbo diesel, this beast delivers smooth, effortless power whether you're towing, off-roading, or cruising the highway.

Engine: 3.0L V6 turbo diesel robust performance with excellent towing capability.

Off-Road Ready: Full-time 4WD, selectable drive modes, and locking rear differential for ultimate traction.

Interior: Luxurious leather-accented seats with Wildtrak stitching, spacious dual-cab comfort.

Entertainment & Tech: 12-inch touchscreen, SYNC 4 infotainment, Apple CarPlay & Android Auto.

Safety: Adaptive cruise control, lane-keeping assist, 360-degree camera, and AEB.

Utility: Electric roller lid for secure, weatherproof cargo storageperfect for work or weekend getaways.

This Ranger Wildtrak is built for power, luxury, and adventure. Take it for a spin and experience the best in performance and versatility! Book your test drive today.
