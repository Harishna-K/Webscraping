# 2019 Volkswagen Tiguan Allspace Bitdi RL 4M
{Stock number. 23671}

The 2019 Volkswagen Tiguan Allspace Bitdi RL 4M is a spacious and versatile SUV, known for its practicality and performance.

# Key features
- Electric handbrake: Instead of a traditional lever, the EPB uses a button or switch to engage and disengage the parking brake. This can often be done automatically when the vehicle is put into gear or turned off.

- Cruise control: enhances comfort on long trips and can improve fuel efficiency by maintaining a steady speed.

- Apple carplay: CarPlay mirrors your iPhones interface on the vehicles touchscreen, giving you access to apps, navigation, music, messages, and more.

If you're looking for more information about the car, feel free to reach out! Whether you have questions about specific features or need assistance with anything else, our team is happy to help.
