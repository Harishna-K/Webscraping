The 2019 Nissan Note X e-POWER is a compact hatchback that stands out with its innovative e-POWER hybrid system, blending electric vehicle performance with the convenience of a petrol powered range extender

The heart of the Note X e-POWER is its unique powertrain. It features a 1.2-litre petrol engine paired with a 1.5 kWh lithium-ion battery and an electric motor delivering 80 kW (about 107 horsepower) and 254 Nm of torque
Unlike traditional hybrids, the petrol engine doesnt directly drive the wheelsit acts solely as a generator to charge the battery, while the electric motor provides all the propulsion. This setup gives you the instant torque and smooth, quiet acceleration of an electric vehicle, with fuel efficiency. Its a clever compromise that eliminates range anxiety since you refuel with petrol, no external charging required.

On the outside, the 2019 model sports Nissans V-Motion grille with a blue accent highlighting its e-POWER status,
Inside, its practical for its size4.1 meters longwith a spacious cabin offering ample legroom for five passengers and a smartly designed boot.

Tech-wise, its loaded with Nissans Intelligent Mobility features. You get the e-POWER Drive system with three modesNormal, S (sportier response), and ECOplus a one-pedal driving option that lets you accelerate and decelerate using the accelerator, thanks to strong regenerative braking.

The quiet cabin, bolstered by extra sound insulation, makes it feel a class above typical compacts. All in all, the 2019 Note X e-POWER blends efficiency, tech, and practicality into a package thats easy to live with, if you value EV-like driving without the plug-in hassle.
