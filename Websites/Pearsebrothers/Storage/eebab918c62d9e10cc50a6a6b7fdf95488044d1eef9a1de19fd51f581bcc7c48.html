4WD, CANOPY, 20" MAGS, NO DEPOSIT FINANCE

This 2019 Mitsubishi Triton GLX 4WD is the perfect combination of reliability, performance, and style. Whether you need a dependable workhorse or an off-road-ready weekend warrior, this Triton delivers with its powerful 2.4L turbo diesel engine, 4WD capability, and rugged upgrades. Featuring a lockable canopy for secure storage, 20 alloy mags, and flared guards for an aggressive stance, this ute is built to handle any challenge.

2.4L Turbo Diesel Engine Delivers strong torque and fuel efficiency, perfect for towing and heavy loads.
4WD with Low & High Range Ready for off-road adventures, job sites, and tough conditions.
20 Alloy Mags & Flared Guards Custom look with improved stance and road presence.
Lockable Canopy Secure your tools, camping gear, or equipment with ease.
Spacious & Practical Interior Comfortable seating, ample storage, and easy-to-use controls.
Modern Safety Features Equipped with ABS, stability control, multiple airbags, and more.

This Triton GLX 4WD is a solid investment for both work and play, offering rugged capability, upgraded styling, and Mitsubishis legendary reliability.
