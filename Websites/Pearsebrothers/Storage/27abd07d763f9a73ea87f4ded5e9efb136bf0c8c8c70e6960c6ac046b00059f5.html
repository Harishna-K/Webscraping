ONE OWNER, 4X4, CANOPY, NO DEPOSIT FINANCE

Powerful 4WD Performance: The 2015 Holden Colorado LTZ DC PU 4WD is equipped with a 2.8L turbo-diesel engine, delivering impressive power and torque, making it perfect for both on-road cruising and tackling tough off-road terrains with ease.

Exceptional Towing Capacity: With a towing capacity of up to 3.5 tonnes, the Colorado LTZ is built to handle heavy loads, whether you're hauling a trailer, boat, or work equipment, making it a versatile choice for work and play.

Spacious & Comfortable Interior: Offering a refined cabin with leather upholstery, advanced infotainment, and ample room for passengers and cargo, the Colorado LTZ combines comfort with practicality, making long trips or daily commutes a breeze.

Advanced Safety Features: Equipped with a suite of safety technologies, including Stability Control, Rear Parking Sensors, and Traction Control, the Colorado LTZ offers enhanced protection for you and your passengers on every journey.

Stylish & Bold Design: With its rugged exterior design, chrome detailing, and bold stance, the Colorado LTZ delivers a striking presence on the road while maintaining the durability needed for any adventure.

Smart Connectivity: Featuring Bluetooth connectivity and an advanced infotainment system, the Colorado LTZ ensures you stay connected with your favorite apps, music, all at your fingertips.
