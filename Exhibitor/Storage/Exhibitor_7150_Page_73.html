HiEnd Accents

Add to MyMarket

HiEnd Accents creates everyday luxury bedding, linens, and home textiles that transform living spaces. Founded in 2005 in Dallas, Texas, our family-owned company specializes in unique, intentional designs across Updated Classics, Modern Contemporary, Modern Rustic, and Casual Chic styles. Our distinctive collections blend creativity with quality, offering standout pieces that elevate any room. HiEnd Accents is one of four brands in our portfolio, alongside Paseo Road, Indigo Hill, and Saturday Linens. Explore our collections to discover truly original home textiles.

Provence Washed Linen Bedding Set

Inspired by the colorful toile patterns of the 1980s, this shabby chic comforter set features a painterly print of Victorian settlers strolling through a lush nature scene. Its soothing, golden color palette harmonizes beautifully with warm neutrals. Printed on a washed linen blend, this bedding offers a soft linen-like feel with a relaxed drape.

Stella Faux Silk Velvet Waterfall Pillowcase Set

This decorative piece features a cascade of luxurious fabric, creating an elongated train that adds a regal and romantic touch. The waterfall-like design highlights the supple texture and lustrous sheen of the faux silk velvet. Available in a range of neutral and jewel-toned colors, it complements various styles effortlessly.

Stella Faux Silk Double Box Stitch Quilt Set

This exquisitely crafted quilt features plush faux silk velvet, offering a lustrous sheen and a touch so soft it mirrors the finest silk. The lightweight yet durable design ensures comfort throughout the seasons, making it a perfect fit for both warm and cooler months. The double-box stitching adds a sophisticated texture.
