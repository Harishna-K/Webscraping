# 2015 Lexus IS300 Version L
{Stock number. 25111}

The IS300 Version L in 2015 is a more upscale version of the IS300, focusing on luxury, comfort, and a more refined driving experience compared to the base or F Sport trims. It's an excellent choice if you're after a blend of sporty performance with an emphasis on luxury.

# Key features
- Cruise control: It uses radar and cameras (typically located behind the rearview mirror) to monitor traffic in front of you. When a slower vehicle is detected, the system adjusts your speed by braking or reducing throttle. Once the road clears, it speeds back up to your preset speed.

- LDW: LDW stands for Lane Departure Warning. It's a safety feature designed to alert you if the car unintentionally drifts out of its lane without signaling.

- Pre crush safety:
The Pre-Collision System (PCS) in the 2015 Lexus IS300 Version L is part of the suite of advanced safety technologies designed to help avoid or mitigate collisions
