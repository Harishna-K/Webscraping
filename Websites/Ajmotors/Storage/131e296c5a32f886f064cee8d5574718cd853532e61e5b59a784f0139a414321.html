# 2013 Subaru Impreza Sport 2.0l - S eyesight
{Stock number. 24066}

The 2013 Subaru Impreza Sport 2.0L with EyeSight is a solid choice for anyone looking for a compact car with excellent safety features, good fuel efficiency, and the added benefit of AWD, especially for drivers in colder or wetter climates.

# Key features
- Eyesight: is known for being one of the most comprehensive active safety systems in its class. EyeSight uses two cameras mounted on either side of the rearview mirror to monitor the area in front of the vehicle.

- Cruise control: cruise control is a great feature for maintaining speed and reducing driver fatigue, and adaptive cruise control further enhances this by adjusting speed based on traffic conditions. Both systems improve driving comfort and safety, especially on long trips or in highway driving.

- Push button start: Push Button Start (or Keyless Ignition) is a convenient feature in modern vehicles that allows the driver to start and stop the engine without using a traditional mechanical key. Instead of inserting a key into the ignition, the driver can simply press a button (typically located on the dashboard or steering column) to start or stop the engine.
