# 2018 Honda Fit Hybrid F package
{Stock number. 24443}

The 2018 Honda Fit Hybrid F Package is a well-rounded, compact hybrid car with a focus on fuel efficiency, practicality, and technology. It offers a good balance of comfort, modern features, and an efficient hybrid powertrain. It's especially popular in markets where small and fuel-efficient vehicles are highly valued.

# Key features
- Sport mode: In the 2018 Honda Fit Hybrid, Sport Mode is a driving mode designed to enhance the car's performance and driving dynamics. When activated, Sport Mode adjusts various vehicle settings to deliver a more engaging driving experience.

- Push start button: The push-start button in the 2018 Honda Fit Hybrid (and many other modern vehicles) is part of a keyless entry and ignition system that allows you to start and stop the engine without inserting a traditional key into the ignition.

- Cruise control: The Cruise Control system in the 2018 Honda Fit Hybrid allows you to set and maintain a constant speed without having to keep your foot on the accelerator pedal, making it easier and more comfortable to drive on highways or long stretches of road.
