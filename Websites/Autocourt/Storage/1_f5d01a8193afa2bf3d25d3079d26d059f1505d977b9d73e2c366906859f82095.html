It has a 2.8 litre 6 cylinder engine with automatic transmission, and rear wheel drive..
It has heated leather seats, four airbags, electric windows, electric mirrors, air-conditioning, and a power-operated soft top which is also in very good condition.
A good example well worth your inspection.
