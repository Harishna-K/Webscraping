Koncept Inc.

Add to MyMarket

Koncept began when Industrial Designer Peter Ng and his two sons, Kenneth Ng and Edmund Ng, joined forces in 2002 in order to harness years of manufacturing and industrial design experience. Embracing modern, minimalist lines while maintaining rich functionalities, Koncept's designs strike the perfect balance between form and function. The Koncept team has designed a range of award-winning products significantly influencing the LED task lighting industry. Their works have been featured in major press such as TIME Magazine, Oprah Magazine, and Interior Design Magazine. Koncept has earned over 60 international design awards including the Red Dot Design Award, iF Design Award, Best of NeoCon and the Good Design Award. Koncept has not only received acclaim within the design industry but has also made its mark in the world of commercial advertising and entertainment. Their innovative products have been prominently featured in various high-profile platforms, including Chase's "How do you cash back?" commercial, where their lighting solutions illuminated the narrative. In the realm of television, Koncept's creations have graced the set of the popular show "Better Call Saul," seamlessly blending into the show's distinctive aesthetic. Furthermore, the tech giant Apple showcased Koncept's designs at their September 2023 "Wonderlust" Event.

Guy Lantern

The Guy Lantern features a tiltable shade resembling a person, available in matte white, matte black, and brass finishes. Optional accessories are also available. The lantern provides a usage time of at least 10 hours at full charge and over 80 hours at the lowest setting.

Yurei (Table Lamp, Floor Lamp and Pendant)

The Yurei Family includes a table and floor lamp, plus a pendant with 2-in-1 mounting for suspension or ceiling flush. Optional shades are interchangeable. The table and floor lamps feature a built-in USB-C port and a telescopic body for adjustable height.

Z-Bar Titan Floor Lamp

The Z-Bar Titan floor lamp offers 1600 lumens at 2700K, with dimmer functionality, adjustable height up to 84 inches, and a 10ft cord for enhanced reach. Available in Matte Black and Brushed Aluminum, it features a pivoting LED head for versatile lighting, blending functionality, and refined design.
