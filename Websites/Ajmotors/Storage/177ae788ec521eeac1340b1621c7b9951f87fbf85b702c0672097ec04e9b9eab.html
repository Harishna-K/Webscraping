Whether for daily driving or occasional weekend trips, its well-equipped to handle a variety of driving conditions while offering ample space for passengers and luggage.

# Key features
- Apple car play: Apple CarPlay is a feature that allows you to integrate your iPhone with your cars infotainment system for a seamless and safer driving experience. When you connect your iPhone to the car, typically via a USB cable or wirelessly (if supported), Apple CarPlay mirrors your iPhone's interface onto the car's display screen, providing access to a range of apps and features designed for ease of use while driving.

- Cruise control:
Cruise Control is a feature that automatically controls a vehicle's speed, allowing the driver to maintain a constant speed without having to manually press the accelerator pedal. Its especially useful on long highway drives, as it reduces the need to keep your foot on the gas pedal, making driving more comfortable and less tiring.

- Push button start: Push-Button Start is a convenient feature that allows you to start and stop your car without having to use a traditional key. Instead, you simply need to have the key fob inside the vehicle, and you press a button (usually located on the dashboard or near the steering wheel) to start or turn off the engine.
