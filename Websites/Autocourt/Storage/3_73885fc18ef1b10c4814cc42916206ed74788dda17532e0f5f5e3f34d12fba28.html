An immaculate 2012 Toyota Prius S PHV 5 door hatch with only 47,400 kilometres.
The Toyota Prius Plug-In Hybrid is a variant of the popular and proven Prius with a battery pack that provides an extended electric-only driving capacity. This 4.4 kWh Lithium-ion battery enables the Prius to travel up to 26 kilometres on electricity alone before the hybrid petrol system kicks in.
This makes the Prius ideal for that short daily town commute, and the battery can be recharged from a 15 amp household supply in 90 minutes.
The 1.8 litre petrol engine develops 73kW and the electric motor develops 60kW, so performance is quite good while the economy is excellent.
The Prius comes with six airbags, electronic stability control, CD player, heated front seats, rear-view camera, and keyless start.
The Prius PHV has a 5-star economy rating and a 5-star UCSR safety rating.
