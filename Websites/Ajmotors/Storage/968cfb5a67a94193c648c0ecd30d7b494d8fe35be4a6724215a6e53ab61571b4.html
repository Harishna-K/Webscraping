# 2016 Nissan Skyline 4D 350GT HV TYPE P
{Stock number. 23807}


The 2016 Nissan Skyline 4D 350GT HV TYPE P is part of the Skyline lineup, a luxury sedan in Nissans range, known in many regions as the Infiniti Q50. The "HV" in the name stands for "Hybrid Vehicle," indicating it is a hybrid model that combines a petrol engine with an electric motor to offer both better fuel efficiency and more power.

# Key features
- 360 Degree Reverse Camera: It provides a bird's-eye view of the area surrounding the vehicle, making parking and maneuvering in tight spaces much easier and safer.

- Memory seats: When you return to the vehicle, press the corresponding memory button (Memory 1 or Memory 2) to recall your saved seat and mirror position.

- Parking sensors: These sensors help drivers navigate tight parking situations by providing auditory or visual alerts when they get too close to obstacles, such as other vehicles, walls, or curbs.
