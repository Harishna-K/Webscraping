Luxury meets performance with this 2010 Silver Lexus IS 250! Designed to impress, this elegant sedan features a powerful V6 engine, push-button start, and a host of high-tech features like Bluetooth and a reversing camera. Whether its style or practicality youre after, this Lexus delivers.

Top Features Include:

Timeless silver exterior with eye-catching 16-inch custom alloy wheels.

V6 engine for a smooth and powerful driving experience.

Push-button start for effortless starting and stopping.

Electric seat adjusters for maximum driver and passenger comfort.

Reversing camera for stress-free parking and reversing.

Bluetooth connectivity for hands-free calls and music streaming.

Fog lights to ensure clear visibility in adverse weather.
