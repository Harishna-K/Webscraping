The 2019 Toyota Corolla Fielder Hybrid GX Touring is a practical and efficient wagon variant of the popular Corolla lineup, blending Toyota's reliable hybrid technology with the spaciousness of a station wagon.

This model is built on a 1.8-litre hybrid powertrain. The Fielder Hybrid is front-wheel drive and designed for practicality, with a focus on family-friendly utility and low running costs.
Key features typically include:
Exterior: Sleek, aerodynamic wagon body with a sporty yet functional design.
Interior: Spacious cabin with seating for five, offering ample legroom and headroom. The rear seats fold down (often in a 60:40 split) to expand the cargo area, which starts at around 407 litres and can grow significantly with the seats downideal for touring or hauling gear. Cloth upholstery is standard, with a simple yet durable finish.
Technology: A infotainment system, 7-inch display with Bluetooth connectivity, USB ports, and a basic audio setup. Steering wheel controls for audio and cruise control are common.

Safety: Equipped with Toyota Safety Sense models featuring pre-collision warning with autonomous emergency braking, lane departure alert, and automatic high beams. Additional safety kit includes multiple airbags, ABS with electronic brake-force distribution, and stability/traction control.

Hybrid-Specific Features: Regenerative braking to recharge the battery, an ECO driving mode for maximizing fuel economy, and a hybrid system indicator on the dashboard to monitor energy usage.
Comfort and Convenience: Automatic climate control, power windows and mirrors, keyless entry, and a reversing camera.
