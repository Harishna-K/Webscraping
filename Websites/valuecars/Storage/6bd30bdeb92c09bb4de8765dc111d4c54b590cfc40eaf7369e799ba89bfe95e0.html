Airbags - 7 Seater - Alloys

Looking for a roomy wagon that seats 7 that won't break the bank? Then check this Honda Odyssey out!, With a punchy 2.4 litre engine that's also chain driven and the desirable dark interior trim this is one nice wagon!
This Odyssey is equipped with Dual airbags, Climate control air conditioning, 16" Alloys, HID headlights, ECO mode, Multi function steering wheel, Indicator mirrors, Traction Control, ISOFIX Child seat anchors and more!
Finance & Protecta Insurance products are also available for this vehicle.

Pre-purchase inspection can be arranged for out of town purchasers.

You can buy with confidence from Value Cars in the knowledge we are a totally New Zealand owned & funded provider of good quality cars & great after sales service for over 30 years.
