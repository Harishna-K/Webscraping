Napa Home & Garden

Add to MyMarket

Like you, we believe in the beauty of home. We were founded in Napa, California, in 2000, with one simple goal: to help elevate each & every living space. Over the years we have forged relationships with many of the world’s best artisans, allowing us to offer you selections that are curated, crafted and contemporary. From the front hall to the back garden, Napa is there to help make everything beautiful. After 10 years, Napa Home & Garden is back at High Point. We look forward to celebrating old relationships, and discovering new ones! With our gorgeous new indoor-outdoor pillow collection, new teak dining tables, large scale pendant lighting and exquisite Italian ceramics straight from Tuscany, Napa offers you the very best- in more ways than one. Our extensive offerings also include home accents, lighting, furniture, wall art, basketry, tabletop, gardener's pots, cachepots and large scale planters to get things growing. Faux florals and estate trees to stimulate the senses and an array of home decor to exceed the expectations of today's discerning designer. You'll find us in our wide-open showroom in the Suites at Market Square just inside the front entry, you can't miss us! We’re here to bring you the very best in home, garden, floral & candles. Happy Shopping!

FIORI DECORATIVE GRANDE BOWL

Each dogwood flower and leaf detail of the Fiori Decorative Grande Bowl is handmade and applied by Italian artisans in Tuscany, Italy. With classic Italian craftsmanship passed down through generations, each piece is a true original. On a grand scale, it is a dynamic centerpiece for a feature table, kitchen island, or mantel.

MONTEREY GRANDE PENDANT

Hand-woven of natural rattan and designed in a bell-shaped silhouette, this casual yet refined pendant is scaled for the wide-open space. The honey-brown finish is right on time for the warming trend interior designers are looking for. A unique fixture above a kitchen island or dining table adds texture and warm filtered light to any space.

GEMMA STORAGE CONSOLE, SET OF 3

Woven in a thick Kubu rattan with a natural gray finish, this set of three is a gorgeous storage solution for any room. It features a soft close hinge lid for safety, they're super sturdy, and they nest to stow away. Smart, strong, and beautiful, what a combo!
