the 2020 Ford Ranger XLT Double Cab is a solid option for those needing a reliable and capable truck with modern amenities and decent off-road potential. It strikes a balance between being functional for work and fun for recreational use.

# Key features
- Cruise control:
Cruise Control is a feature that allows a vehicle to maintain a set speed without the driver having to keep their foot on the accelerator pedal.

- Key less entry: Keyless entry is a convenient feature in modern vehicles that allows you to unlock and start your car without using a traditional key. Instead, it relies on a wireless signal transmitted from a key fob (or sometimes a smartphone app) to interact with the vehicle.

- Pre crush safety: These systems rely on sensors, cameras, radar, and other technologies to detect potential collisions with vehicles, pedestrians, or obstacles, and can take action to mitigate or avoid the crash altogether.
