The 2016 Honda Shuttle X Hybrid is a compact hybrid wagon that offers a blend of practicality, fuel efficiency, and comfort.

### **Powertrain**
- **Engine:** 1.5-liter Atkinson-cycle 4-cylinder engine.
- **Electric Motor:** Integrated hybrid system combining the engine with an electric motor for improved fuel efficiency.

### **Interior Features**
- **Seating Capacity:** 5 passengers with spacious and versatile seating arrangements.
- **Climate Control:** Automatic climate control for comfort.
- **Cargo Space:** 470 liters of boot space with rear seats up, expanding with the rear seats down for larger items.
- **Smart Storage:** Numerous storage compartments for small items.

### **Performance & Driving Features**
- **Drive Modes:** Hybrid system adjusts between electric and gasoline engine power based on driving conditions.
- **Regenerative Braking:** Recaptures energy during braking to recharge the battery.
- **Smooth Ride:** Suspension and handling designed for a comfortable and stable ride.

### **Convenience Features**
- **Keyless Entry and Start:** For easy entry and starting of the car without removing the key fob.
- **Push-button Start:** Standard across trims for easy operation.
- **Cruise Control:** For maintaining consistent speed on highways.

### **Other Notable Features**
- **Electric Parking Brake:** Provides more space and convenience compared to a traditional handbrake.
- **Energy Flow Display:** Shows how power is being used or regenerated during driving.

This model is tailored for customers looking for a reliable, fuel-efficient, and spacious vehicle with hybrid technology. Its a great option for families or anyone seeking a versatile and eco-friendly car.
