We have learned a thing or two from being in the permanent botanical industry for over 35 years. Having the highest attention to detail, using only the finest quality materials, and constantly giving out superior customer service, now that’s D&W Silks. We source our products from all around the world and assemble in the USA. We take great pride in our artistic vision and hence only use the most realistic replica faux botanicals during our design process. Furthermore, we continuously strive to always offer our customers the best value in the industry. With our quality products, you will be adding comforting, hassle-free artificial plants and trees that will last! You will see and feel the positive difference our products add to any setting. When carrying our collections as part of your business, we have no doubt you and your clients will love our product. Please be sure to visit our website for our full product line and our latest intros.
