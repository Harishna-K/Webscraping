MOBIUS KERAMIKK

Add to MyMarket

"Life is better when you have original art in your home." The core mission of Möbius Keramikk is to provide affordable, accessible art through sculptural tabletop and home decor, such as vases, dinnerware, and serigraphs.

Our brands/collections consist of:

  • Shoppe Object

Penguin Pitcher

A modern porcelain pitcher in classic black and white. It is ideal for display and perfect for iced tea, water, lemonade, or the beverage of your choice. 100% made in the USA and hand designed by Christina Osheim, the geometric color blocking responds to the architectural form of the pitcher.

Dinnerware - dinner plate, salad plate, soup bowl

Classic silhouettes with a modern twist, this dinnerware set is sure to make an impression on your table. Owner and artist, Christina Osheim, considers every angle, whether you stack your place settings, or lay them out, you will notice the close attention to the lines and shapes and how they interact between one another.

Sake Pot and Cups

Crisp, direct, geometric lines characterize this Sake set in classic black and white.  100% made in the USA and hand designed by owner and artist, Christina Osheim, this set is sure to be an instant classic in any home.  Enjoy an intimate drinking experience made all the more special by holding and using these petite works of art.
