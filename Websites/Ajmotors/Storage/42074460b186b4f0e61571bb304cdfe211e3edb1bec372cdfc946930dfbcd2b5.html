# 2019 Subaru Impreza 2.0L Eyesight
{Stock number. 24386}

The 2019 Subaru Impreza 2.0L Eyesight offers a safe, practical, and fuel-efficient option for those who need a compact car with the added benefit of all-wheel drive and advanced driver assistance features. It's especially appealing in regions with challenging weather conditions, thanks to its robust safety systems and AWD capability.

# Key features
- Eyesight:
The Subaru Eyesight Driver Assist Technology is a suite of safety features that uses cameras and sensors to monitor the road and assist the driver in avoiding potential hazards. This system is standard on many Subaru models, including the 2019 Subaru Impreza 2.0L Eyesight, and is designed to enhance overall safety by preventing accidents and improving the driving experience

- ACC: Eyesights adaptive cruise control helps maintain a set speed and a safe distance from the vehicle in front of you. If the car ahead slows down, the system automatically reduces your speed to maintain the set following distance.

- LDW: Using cameras, Eyesight monitors the lane markings on the road. If the vehicle starts to drift out of its lane without the turn signal being used, the system will alert the driver with an audible warning and a visual alert.
