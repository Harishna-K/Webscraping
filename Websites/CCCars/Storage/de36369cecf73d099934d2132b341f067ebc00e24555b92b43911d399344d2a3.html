The 2014 Mazda Axela 20S Touring is a popular compact car with a stylish design and a range of features. The "Axela" is the name used for the Mazda3 in Japan

### **Engine and Performance:**
- **Engine Type:** 2.0-litre 4-cylinder engine.
- **Transmission:** 6-speed automatic transmission (with manual mode).
- **Drive Type:** Front-wheel drive (FWD).

### **Interior Features:**
- **Seating:** Fabric seats, with power-adjustable drivers seat in some versions.
- **Air Conditioning:** Dual-zone automatic climate control for comfort.

### **Safety Features:**
- **Airbags:**
- **ABS (Anti-lock Braking System):** Helps maintain control during hard braking.
- **EBD (Electronic Brakeforce Distribution):** Optimizes brake performance for passenger safety.
- **Traction Control System (TCS):** Helps maintain grip on slippery roads.
- **Stability Control (DSC):** Prevents loss of traction during sharp turns or emergency maneuvers.
- **Rearview Camera:** For easier parking and reversing.

### **Other Features:**
- **Keyless Entry and Push Start:** Convenience for locking, unlocking, and starting the car.
- **Push-Button Start:** Adds to the modern feel of the car.
- **Tilt and Telescopic Steering Wheel:** Adjustable for personalized comfort.

### **Chassis and Suspension:**
- **MacPherson Strut Front Suspension:** Provides good handling and comfort.
- **Multi-Link Rear Suspension:** Enhances stability and driving dynamics.
- **Electric Power Steering (EPS):** Responsive and efficient steering

These features make the 2014 Mazda Axela 20S Touring a practical, well-rounded choice for those seeking a compact car with a balance of performance, style, and comfort.
