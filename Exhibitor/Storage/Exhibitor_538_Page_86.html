Johnston Casuals Furniture Inc

Add to MyMarket

Johnston Casuals produces custom handcrafted contemporary furniture in North Carolina. Our skilled artisans have been crafting a wide range of distinct furniture since 1981. An architectural approach focused on sculptural simplicity separates Johnston Casuals from its competitors. Our facilities stay up to date by using the latest technology and production techniques, including robotics, laser cutting and automated powder-coating.

A New Collaboration with Aspiring Designers

We began a new collaboration with professors and design students at Appalachian State University. From that effort we have added several new unconventional designs to our line and feature them for sell in our newest showroom at 220 Elm, room 330.

2023 Fall Show Room

If you made it to the Fall show or decided to sit this one out, it doesn't matter. You can tour it virtually and see what you missed or what you might have forgotten about!

Labyrinth Rectangle Dining Table

The Labyrinth Rectangle Dining Base has a sturdy geometric design, which forms a unique maze-like structure perfect for both formal and casual dining experiences. Ideal for a minimalist dining room or a sleek office, it exudes an air of sophistication and a fresh take on luxury. Shown in Black Metal Finish

Phoenix Dining Table

The Phoenix Dining Table base is a modern marvel of geometric precision, with angular metal components resembling phoenix wings. Available in square and rectangular sizes, it's ideal for intimate or large gatherings. Topped with glass, the intricate metalwork is visible from every angle, making it a striking centerpiece.

Laurel Dining Table

The Laurel Dining Table is a seamless fusion of natural warmth and modern design. The walnut-finished wooden base features smooth curves linked by understated metal connectors, supporting a robust glass top. This piece brings a touch of organic elegance to the dining experience, designed for longevity and timeless appeal.
