Add to MyMarket

At Loloi, we make rugs, pillows and wall art for the thoughtfully layered home. Visit our expansive showroom (IHFC D-320) to shop this seasons’ newest collections from Loloi and our exclusive collaborations with Amber Lewis, Magnolia Home by Joanna Gaines, Rifle Paper Co., Chris Loves Julia, Brigette Romanek, Carrier and Company, and Angela Rose. And don’t miss our assortment of hundreds of one-of-a-kind rugs, wall art and pillows, each meticulously sourced from around the world. Servicing the Trade exclusively, enjoy the best customer service provided by our sales staff and no minimum on any order placed.
