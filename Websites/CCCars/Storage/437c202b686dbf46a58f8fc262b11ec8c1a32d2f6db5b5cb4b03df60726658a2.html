The **2014 Toyota Aqua G Leather** is a hybrid car that was marketed in Japan, known for its fuel efficiency and compact size.

**Interior Features:**
- **Leather Upholstery**: As the name suggests, the G Leather trim comes with leather seats, providing a more premium feel compared to the cloth upholstery in the base models.
- **Heated Seats**: Front seats may feature heating functionality for added comfort.
- **Climate Control**: Automatic climate control for adjusting interior temperature, ensuring a more comfortable ride.

- **Steering Wheel Controls**: Leather-wrapped steering wheel with audio and cruise control buttons.
**Exterior Features:**
- **Alloy Wheels**: The Aqua G Leather trim comes with upgraded alloy wheels, giving it a sportier appearance.

**Other Features:**
- **Push Start Button**: The G Leather trim often includes a push-to-start ignition system for convenience.
- **Smart Key System**: Allows for keyless entry and start.

Overall, the **2014 Toyota Aqua G Leather** offers a balance of luxury, practicality, and eco-friendliness, with leather-trimmed interiors and a focus on safety and fuel efficiency.
