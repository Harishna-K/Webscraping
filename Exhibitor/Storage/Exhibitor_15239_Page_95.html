Lauren HB Studio

Add to MyMarket

At Lauren HB Studio, we thrive on making beautiful things for beautiful people. We produce pottery, sculpture, and commissions for public and private spaces out of our studio in Lakewood, Ohio. Lauren HB Studio was founded by Lauren Herzak-Bauman in 2013. Trained as a sculptor and educator, and inspired by her vision her entrepreneur spirit, Lauren taught herself how to slipcast pottery at her studio in Lakewood, Ohio and unknowingly launched a pottery business. While interest in her unique geometric forms grew, Lauren continued to pursue her sculpture practice, landing her first public commission at The Westin Hotel in downtown Cleveland. Lauren has stayed true to her belief in the vibrancy of art to instill meaning and transform environments. Lauren is proud to employ a small and lively bunch of beautiful humans that work collaboratively to make pottery and sculpture. Lauren HB Studio pots are sold nationwide. Lauren’s sculptures inhabit both public and private spaces around the country.
