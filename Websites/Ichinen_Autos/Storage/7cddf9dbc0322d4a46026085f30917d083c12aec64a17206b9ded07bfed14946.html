Introducing the 2020 Mercedes-Benz A 45 S AMG Turbo 4MATIC+, the "ultimate hot hatch" that redefines performance and practicality.

At its heart lies a handcrafted 2.0L inline-4 turbo engine, embodying AMG's renowned "One Man, One Engine" philosophy. Delivering an unparalleled 421PSthe, the most powerful level in its class, this masterpiece pairs with a twin-scroll turbo for seamless torque and smooth acceleration.

With no "Eco" mode, it boasts Slippery, Comfort, Sport, Sport+, and Race drive modes for every thrill seeker. Compact yet versatile, its perfect for city drives and dominating the track alike. With an unmatched balance of everyday usability and unparalleled performance, the A 45 S 4MATIC+ sets a new standard in its category.

Discover refined luxury, groundbreaking technology, and precision engineering in this power-packed yet practical marvel. Test drive today!
