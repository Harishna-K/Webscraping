We carry over 60 collections of handmade and machine-made rugs, including 6 indoor/outdoor collections. You will find looks to suit all your lifestyles ranging from casual and contemporary to traditional and classic, along with indoor/outdoor, natural, coastal and kids’ rugs. We are well known for being in the forefront of innovation and design and continues to bring the most unique constructions and latest design and color trends to market. With attention to international design and details, each rug we bring to High Point is made with superb materials and blended into the best styles available. We bring everything from eclectic to classic to the floor.
