# 2012 Mitsubishi Delica D5
{Stock number. 23853}

The 2012 Mitsubishi Delica D5 is a compact MPV (Multi-Purpose Vehicle) that was first introduced in Japan and is known for its versatility, ruggedness, and spacious interior. It's part of the Delica series, which has been popular for many years due to its combination of off-road capability and family-friendly features.

# Key features
- Power sliding door: The 2012 Mitsubishi Delica D5 comes with the option of power sliding doors, which provide greater convenience when entering and exiting the vehicle, particularly in tight spaces or when carrying heavy loads.

- 4WD gear: The Delica D5 typically comes with an all-wheel drive (AWD) or 4WD configuration, depending on the trim level and market. In 4WD versions, the system allows the engine's power to be distributed to all four wheels, improving grip and handling on challenging terrains like gravel, mud, snow, or sand.

- Cruise control: The 2012 Mitsubishi Delica D5 is equipped with cruise control as a standard or optional feature, depending on the trim level. Cruise control is a convenient system that allows the driver to set and maintain a constant speed without needing to keep their foot on the accelerator, making it ideal for long highway drives or extended trips.
