# Key Features

ABS (Anti-lock Braking System): Prevents the wheels from locking up during hard braking, helping you maintain control.

Adaptive Cruise Control: This system automatically adjusts the car's speed to keep a safe distance from the car in front of you, making long drives easier.

Reversing Camera: This camera shows you whats behind the car when youre backing up, making it easier to see obstacles.
