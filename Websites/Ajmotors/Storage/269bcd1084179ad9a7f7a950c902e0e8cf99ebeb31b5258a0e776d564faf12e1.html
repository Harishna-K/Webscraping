# 2016 Toyota Vellfire 2.5X
{Stock number. 20223}

The 2016 Toyota Vellfire 2.5X is a luxury minivan known for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and modern features. It typically comes with a 2.5-litre 4-cylinder engine, offering a good balance of power and fuel efficiency.

# Key features
- Power sliding door: The sliding doors allow for easier access in crowded parking areas, where traditional doors may hit neighbouring vehicles.

- Auto hold: Auto Hold keeps the vehicle stationary after you come to a complete stop without needing to keep your foot on the brake. Once activated, it automatically engages when you stop the vehicle and disengages when you press the accelerator.

- Cruise control: This allows you to set a desired speed, and the vehicle will maintain that speed without you needing to press the accelerator.
