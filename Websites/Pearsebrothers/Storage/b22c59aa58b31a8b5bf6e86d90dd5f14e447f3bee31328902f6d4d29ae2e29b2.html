LOW KMS, 17" MAGS, TURBO, NO DEPOSIT FINANCE

The 2015 Fiat 595 Competizione is a dynamic and stylish hot hatch, designed for driving enthusiasts who crave excitement behind the wheel. With its 1.4-liter turbocharged engine, this compact powerhouse produces an impressive 177 horsepower, delivering quick acceleration and responsive handling.

Performance & Handling
Engine: 1.4L turbocharged petrol engine, 177 hp, 250 Nm torque

Transmission: 5-speed manual or automatic with paddle shifters

Braking: High-performance Brembo brakes for superior stopping power

Suspension: Sport-tuned suspension for enhanced stability and grip

Exhaust: Monza quad-tip exhaust system, delivering an aggressive sound

Interior & Comfort

Seating: Sporty, body-hugging racing seats

Steering: Flat-bottom leather-wrapped steering wheel

Climate Control: Automatic climate control for year-round comfort
Technology & Infotainment

With its blend of performance, Italian craftsmanship, and everyday usability, the Fiat 595 Competizione is perfect for those who want a thrilling yet practical ride. If youre looking for a distinctive hot hatch that stands out on the road, this is the one to drive.
