# 2012 Toyota 86 GT
{Stock number. 24369}

The 2012 Toyota 86 GT, known in some regions as the Scion FR-S or Subaru BRZ, is a lightweight, rear-wheel-drive sports car developed by Toyota in collaboration with Subaru. It's designed to offer an affordable, fun driving experience, focusing on handling and agility rather than outright power.

# Key features
- Sports mode: In Sports Mode, the throttle becomes more responsive, allowing the engine to react more quickly to input from the accelerator. This results in a more immediate response when you press the gas pedal, giving you a sportier driving feel.

- Paddle gear shifters: Paddle shifters allow you to quickly change gears without losing focus on the road, making them perfect for track days, mountain drives, or when you're really pushing the car.

- Push button start: The Push Button Start system in the 2012 Toyota 86 GT is a convenient and modern feature that allows you to start and stop the engine with just the press of a button, eliminating the need for a traditional key to be inserted into an ignition slot.

If you're looking for more information about the car, feel free to reach out! Whether you have questions about specific features or need assistance with anything else, our team is happy to help.
