# 2014 Toyota 86 GT Limited Bodykit
{Stock number. 24961}

The 2014 Toyota 86 GT Limited is a special edition of Toyota's popular sports car, the Toyota 86, which was co-developed with Subaru (where it is sold as the Subaru BRZ). It features a balance of lightweight handling, rear-wheel-drive dynamics, and a sporty, minimalist design aimed at driving enthusiasts.

# Key features
- Paddle gear shifter: The paddle gear shifter, commonly referred to as paddle shifters, is a feature found in many modern performance-oriented cars, including some variants of the 2014 Toyota 86 (though its more common in the automatic versions). These paddles are mounted behind the steering wheel and allow the driver to manually control gear shifts without needing to take their hands off the wheel.

- Push button start:
The push button start is a convenient and modern feature found in many cars, including the 2014 Toyota 86 (particularly in higher trims like the GT Limited). It allows you to start and stop your car without needing to insert a traditional key into the ignition.

- Sport mode: Sport Mode is a driving mode commonly found in performance-oriented cars, like the 2014 Toyota 86, that enhances the vehicle's performance characteristics to provide a more engaging and dynamic driving experience. When activated, it modifies various aspects of the car's systemssuch as the throttle response, transmission behavior, suspension, and steeringmaking the car feel more responsive and focused on performance.
