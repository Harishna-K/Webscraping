Skyline, Leather, V6, Radar Cruise, Sport

Step into style and sophistication with this 2015 Blue Nissan Fuga, the perfect blend of luxury, performance, and efficiency. With its elegant design, advanced hybrid technology, and premium features, this sedan redefines what it means to enjoy your daily drive.

Features include:

Striking 18-inch alloy wheels for a dynamic look.

6 airbags for optimal safety and peace of mind.

Bluetooth connectivity to keep you connected on the go.

360 reversing camera for unparalleled parking convenience.

Plush leather seats offering superior comfort.

Push-button start for a seamless entry and drive experience.

Front heated seats to keep you cozy in colder weather.

Smooth and efficient petrol-hybrid engine for exceptional performance.

Cruise control for stress-free highway journeys.

Stereo converted to English for your convenience.
