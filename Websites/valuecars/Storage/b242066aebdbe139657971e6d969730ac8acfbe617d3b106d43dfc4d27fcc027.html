8 Seater - Hybrid - Cruise Control

Looking for an Economical, Safe vehicle that seats 8? then have a look at this Nissan Serena!
This is the Facelift Model with a chain driven Hybrid 2 litre engine, This vehicle is frugal on fuel and very spacious with plenty of room for 8 passengers, Combine that with Lane Departure Warning, pre Collision Safety, Indicator Mirrors, Dual airbags, Traction control and shoulder belts for all passengers.
Comfort and convenience is taken care of with Front & Rear Air conditioning, 2 Smart keys with button start and Twin remote electric Sliding doors, Cruise Control, Rear Sunblinds + UV Privacy glass and of course a Reverse Camera!
Finance and Protecta Insurance products are also available for this vehicle.

Pre-purchase inspection can be arranged for out of town purchasers, and we can also ship at a cost of only $250 to NZ main
centres.

You can buy with confidence from Value Cars in the knowledge we are a totally New Zealand owned and funded provider of good quality cars with great after sales service for over 25 years.
