Engine Power: The 2016 Ford Ranger XLT is typically equipped with a 3.2L turbocharged diesel engine, offering a great balance of power and fuel efficiency. The 3.2L 5-cylinder engine, produces impressive torque and horsepower, making it ideal for towing.

Towing Capacity: With its robust engine, the Ranger XLT boasts a strong towing capacity of up to 3,500 kg (7,700 lbs), making it an excellent choice for hauling trailers, boats, or other heavy loads.

Transmission: The manual 6-speed transmission offers a more engaging driving experience, giving the driver full control over gear shifts for better performance in various driving conditionswhether on highways or rough terrains.
