Studio A Home

Add to MyMarket

STUDIO A HOME'S unique mix of organic, design-driven accessories, furniture, found objects and textiles is rich in texture and elemental in composition. Cutting-edge design, unexpected materials and handcrafted finishes form the foundation of our product mix. The eclectic blend of textures, classic silhouettes and timeless design transform any interior. STUDIO A HOME is a partner company and harmonious complement to industry leader, Global Views.

Our brands/collections consist of:

  • Aviva Stanoff by Studio A Home
  • Ernst Gruler by Studio A Home
  • New West by Studio A Home
  • The Roger Thomas Collection by Studio A Home

Cinder Bowls-Black Reactive

The Cinder Bowls, handcrafted from Italian ceramic, feature a distinctive black reactive glaze with an organic texture. Ideal as decorative pieces, their unique design and artisanal charm bring sophisticated elegance to any setting.

The Vessel Bowls, made from natural travertine stone, offer both functionality and distinctive appeal. Their versatile design fits effortlessly into any room, while the natural variations in veining and color make each bowl one-of-a-kind. Enjoy the natural beauty of travertine in your space.
