This is a 2014 Subaru XV 2.0i AWD Hybrid; a smart looking vehicle in silver, with 17 inch alloy wheels.
It has the latest generation 2 litre DOHC Boxer motor coupled to a 10kW electric motor, matched to a Lineartronic CV Transmission which delivers very good performance, with 5.9L/100km fuel economy.
It has travelled 67,800 kilometres.
The Subaru XV is proving to be a popular model with increased ground clearance to allow off-road driving whilst retaining the comfort of normal everyday road use.
It features Symmetrical All Wheel Drive, and VDC Vehicle Dynamics Control (which incorporates stability control, traction control, brake distribution etc), and hill-start assistance.
Also featured are CD player, Smart Key starting/locking, climate control, and cruise control
