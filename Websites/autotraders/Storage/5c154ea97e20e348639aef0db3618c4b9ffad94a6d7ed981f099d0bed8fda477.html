How leasing works

Leasing works a bit like a long-term rental. You drive it, but you dont own it.

  1. Choose your car, pick your terms and apply for lease credit online
  2. Pay monthly rental payments for your chosen term length
  3. Drive it, enjoy it, then give it back at the end

When you lease with Auto Trader you get all of this:

  • Full manufacturers warranty
  • Road tax and roadside assistance included
  • No admin fees
  • Free returns within 30 days*
