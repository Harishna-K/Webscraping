Accent Decor

Add to MyMarket

From our floral design roots to the flourishing flower, plant, and home accents business we are today, Accent Decor is passionate about bringing beauty to the world. Alongside local artists, in-house designers, and global creators, we design exclusive, artful pieces to meet the shifting needs of our customers, including floral designers, home and gift retailers, plant shops, event planners, and more. Our goal is always to offer more functionality, versatility, and longevity for new products. And also, we hope you experience the time, talent, and thoughtful care behind every piece once it leaves us and comes to you. We partner with skilled artisans in 14 countries around the world to turn our design sketches into carefully-crafted pieces, from our mouth-blown glass to handmade ceramics. We are headquartered in Chamblee, GA with distribution centers in Georgia and California. 6% of Accent Decor's gross profits go back to local and global communities. When you purchase with Accent Decor you are supporting and meaningfully impacting lives near and far.

Iambic Trough

Bring unique sculptural style, texture, & dimension to home decor with the extra-large ceramic Iambic Trough. Featuring a warm grey reactive glaze ranging from grey to white, deep irregular ribbing from top to base, & a wide opening, this vessel is gorgeous with a statement planting displayed on a large-scale surface.

Exhibit Collection

Display art, vases, plants, & more on an artful surface with the small-scale mango wood Exhibit Column Collection. Featuring stacked squares & rounded shapes, these columns in 2 heights easily stand out on their own but are perfect for highlighting prized home accents.

Santos Collection

Designed with old-world beauty & modern durability, the large-scale Santos Collection offers a timeless charm to home decor. Cyprus-inspired & meticulously handmade from terracotta, these planters feature dimensional horizontal bands on a neutral ground color.
