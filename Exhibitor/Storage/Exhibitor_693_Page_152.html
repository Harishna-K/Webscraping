Soicher-Marin

Add to MyMarket

Why More than any other decorative object in your home, the art on your walls speaks volumes about you; your values, what’s important and how you see the world. Knowing this, we treat every item in our collection as if it were to hang on our own walls. Why? Because art is personal and authenticity matters. No fake artists. No fake names. No imported, mass-produced creations from assembly line workers. How We are a third-generation family business creating works of art for interior designers and the retail customer since 1959. We source our art from dealers and artists from around the globe. Our collection covers the gamut of genres, design styles and applications for home, office and hospitality spaces. Everything we create and produce is done so by people, not machines, one at a time, just for you. We choose environmentally friendly vendors to source renewable wood products, paper and packaging materials in an effort to keep our footprint that much smaller. What Custom framed decorative artwork from our exclusive archive of images printed on archival paper and canvas using water soluble, environmentally safe inks. Made to order, one at a time. Just for you. Proudly made in the USA. Always and forever.
