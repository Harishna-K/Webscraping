The 2015 Mazda Premacy 20S is a versatile minivan that combines practicality with modern features

- **Seating Capacity:** Accommodates up to seven passengers, providing ample space for families and groups.

- **Sliding Doors:** Equipped with power sliding doors on both sides, facilitating easy access in tight parking spaces.

- **Engine and Transmission:** Powered by a 2.0-liter Skyactiv petrol engine paired with an automatic transmission, delivering a balance of performance and fuel efficiency.

- **Safety Features:** Includes airbags, ABS brakes, electronic stability control (ESC), and a 5-star safety rating, ensuring a secure driving experience.

- **Comfort and Convenience:**
- Dual-zone climate control for personalized temperature settings
- Bluetooth connectivity for hands-free calling and audio streaming.
- Reversing camera and parking sensors to assist with parking and maneuvering.

- **Exterior and Interior:**
- Stylish 15-inch alloy wheels enhance the vehicle's appearance.
- Fog lights for improved visibility in adverse weather conditions.
- Leather seats and a modern dashboard design add to the interior's comfort and aesthetics.

- **Additional Features:**
- Push-button start for added convenience.
- Tinted windows for privacy and UV protection.
- Alloy wheels and a sunroof to enhance the vehicle's appeal.

Overall, the 2015 Mazda Premacy 20S offers a comprehensive package of space, safety, and modern amenities, making it a reliable choice for those seeking a family-friendly vehicle in New Zealand.
