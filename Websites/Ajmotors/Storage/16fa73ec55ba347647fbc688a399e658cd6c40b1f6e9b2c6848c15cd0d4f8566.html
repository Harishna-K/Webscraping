# 2017 Subaru Impreza XV 4WD 2.0L Eyesight
{Stock number. 23085}

The 2017 Subaru Impreza XV is well-suited for people who need a versatile vehicle with the ability to handle various road conditions, from city streets to unpaved roads. Its AWD system and Eyesight technology ensure that drivers have enhanced safety and confidence when driving in challenging environments.

# Key features
- Rear fog lights: Rear fog lights are a safety feature designed to improve visibility for other drivers in conditions of low visibility, such as heavy rain, fog, snow, or mist. They are typically located on the rear of the vehicle, often in the rear bumper, and are activated separately from the standard brake or tail lights.

- Eyesight: Subaru EyeSight is an advanced driver assistance system (ADAS) designed to enhance safety and make driving easier by using a combination of cameras and sensors. It was first introduced by Subaru in 2008 and has since been a key feature in many of their models, including the 2017 Subaru Impreza XV.

- Multi function steering wheel: A multi-function steering wheel is a feature that integrates several controls directly on the steering wheel, allowing the driver to operate various functions without taking their hands off the wheel.
