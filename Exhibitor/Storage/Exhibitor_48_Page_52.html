Eilersen

Add to MyMarket

From coach to couch - Founded in 1895, Eilersen has focused on craftsmanship, quality, and innovation. The journey began producing coaches and since the 1930s Eilersen has been making beautiful, high-quality couches based on the same traditional values of excellent craftsmanship and innovation, that founded the company almost 130 years ago. This has made Eilersen a well-known and recognized high-end brand internationally.

Ares – The calm sofa

Ares was the ancient Greek god renowned for his strength and courage. The same can be said for the new sofa of the same name designed by Jens Juul Eilersen. The Ares sofa is strong in its construction and courageous in its simplicity.

Bure -      A landscape in motion

Bure is a sofa with endless possibilities. Each module is a building block that can be assembled into an exquisite landscape. Bure was designed by Nils Juul Eilersen, fourth generation Eilersen, in close collaboration with his father Jens Juul Eilersen.

Nun -     The comfortable armchair

With the Nun armchair, Eilersen adds another armchair to its collection. The chair is designed by Jens Juul Eilersen, which can be seen and felt in both the chair's expression and comfort, as the same familiar qualities that have become synonymous with an Eilersen over time.
