2018 Volkswagen Tiguan Allspace TDI 4X4 HighLine in stunning Metallic Black with chrome trim sitting on 19" Alloys

Experience the pinnacle of luxury and performance with the 2018 Volkswagen Tiguan Allspace TDI 4X4 Highline. Dressed in a captivating Metallic Black exterior with factory chrome accents, it's a symbol of refined elegance. Inside, its generous space and adaptable seating options ensure comfort for every passenger. Effortlessly navigate the road on its sleek 19" Alloys, powered by the dynamic TDI engine for a blend of power and efficiency. Elevate your driving experience with the Tiguan Allspace where style and functionality converge in perfect harmony
