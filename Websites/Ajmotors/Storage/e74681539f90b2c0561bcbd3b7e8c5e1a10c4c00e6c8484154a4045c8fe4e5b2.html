4WD, Sport, Carplay, Part Leather, 7-Airbags

Adventure meets innovation in this 2017 White Subaru XV Crosstrek! Designed for both city driving and off-road adventures, this AWD SUV features Subarus advanced EyeSight safety system, which includes lane departure warning, forward collision warning, and blind-spot monitoring. Inside, enjoy premium leather-accented seats, dual climate control, and seamless smartphone integration with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto. With its 7 airbags, push-button start, and cruise control, the Crosstrek offers a perfect balance of safety, comfort, and performance.

Key Features:

18-inch alloy wheels complementing a stylish white exterior.

AWD capability for superior traction in all conditions.

EyeSight safety system with lane departure and forward collision warning.

7 airbags for ultimate safety and peace of mind.

Apple CarPlay & Android Auto for seamless smartphone
integration.

Blind-spot monitoring to enhance driving confidence.

Cruise control for smooth, effortless highway driving.

Leather-accented seats for a premium cabin feel.

Dual climate control to keep every passenger comfortable.

Push-button start for modern convenience.
