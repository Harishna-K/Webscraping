# 2015 Subaru WRX S4
{Stock number. 25639}

The 2015 Subaru WRX S4 is a high-performance version of Subaru's popular WRX, primarily available in the Japanese market. The "S4" designation signifies a more refined, higher-spec model compared to the standard WRX, with several upgrades in technology, comfort, and performance.

# Key features
- LDW: LDW stands for Lane Departure Warning, which is a driver assistance system designed to help prevent accidental lane departures. It uses cameras or sensors to monitor the vehicle's position relative to lane markings on the road. If the system detects that the vehicle is unintentionally drifting out of its lane without the use of turn signals, it will alert the driver through visual or audible warnings.

- ACC: ACC stands for Adaptive Cruise Control, a feature that is also part of Subaru's EyeSight suite in the 2015 WRX S4. Unlike traditional cruise control, which maintains a constant speed set by the driver, ACC automatically adjusts the vehicles speed to maintain a safe following distance from the car ahead.

- Pre crush safety: Pre-Collision Braking works by using cameras and sensors to detect potential collisions with objects or vehicles in front of the car. If the system identifies an impending collision and determines that the driver is not responding in time to avoid it, the system will automatically apply the brakes to help reduce the severity of the impactor, in some cases, prevent it entirely.
