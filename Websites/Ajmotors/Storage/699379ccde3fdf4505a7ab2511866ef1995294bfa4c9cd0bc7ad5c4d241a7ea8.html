# 2013 Toyota hybrid 1.8L Rev cam
{Stock number. 21703}

The 2013 Toyota Prius, equipped with a 1.8L hybrid engine, is known for its impressive fuel efficiency and reliability. It combines a gasoline engine with an electric motor, providing a smooth driving experience

# Key features
- Alloy wheels: enhance the vehicle's appearance but also contribute to improved performance and fuel efficiency.

- Multifunction steering wheel: Provides convenience and control while driving. Allows you to control audio system, phone calls and helps maintain road focus for safety.

- ABS Braking: Allows for better manoeuvrability during emergency stops, especially on slippery surfaces.
