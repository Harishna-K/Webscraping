The 2015 Toyota Corolla Fielder G, which is a station wagon variant of the Toyota Corolla, comes with several notable features designed for comfort, convenience, and safety.

### Interior Features
- **Seating**: Spacious interior with fabric upholstery, offering seating for up to 5 passengers.
- **Climate Control**: Air conditioning with automatic climate control for a comfortable interior.
- **Power Windows**: Standard power windows for both the front and rear.
- **Keyless Entry**: Remote keyless entry for convenience.

### Performance & Handling
- **Engine**: 1.5L 4-cylinder engine, providing a good balance of power and fuel efficiency.
- **Drive Type**: Front-wheel drive (FWD).

### Safety Features
- **Anti-lock Braking System (ABS)**: Standard ABS for better control and safety during hard braking.
- **Vehicle Stability Control (VSC)**: Helps maintain stability when cornering or during slippery conditions.
- **Traction Control System (TCS)**: Helps prevent wheel slip in slippery conditions.

This model provides a good mix of practicality, comfort, and efficiency, making it a popular choice for families and individuals looking for a reliable and versatile vehicle.
