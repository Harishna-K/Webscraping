LOW KILOMETRES.
This 2002 Nissan Atlas is in tidy condition and has travelled only 35,500 kilometres.
It has electric windows, central locking, air-conditioning and is as easy as driving a car.
It has a 2 litre EFi petrol engine with 5 speed manual transmission.
These light trucks are in great demand, but new regulations will make them more expensive in the next few months.
Ideal for most trades, and requires WOF rather than COF.
The flatdeck with dropsides measures 2850mm x 1600mm.
The truck has a carrying capacity of 1,500kg, a nett weight of 1,460kg with a GVM of 3125kg, which means that it has 12 month WOF instead of COF.
