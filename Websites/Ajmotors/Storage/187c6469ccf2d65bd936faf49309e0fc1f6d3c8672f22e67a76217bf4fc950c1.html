Introducing the 2020 Kia Sportage LX Diesela modern, fuel-efficient SUV that combines dynamic styling with impressive performance. This compact yet spacious vehicle offers a refined interior, exceptional fuel economy, and the reliability Kia is known for.

- **Fuel-Efficient Diesel Engine**
Powered by a 2.0L turbocharged diesel engine
- **6-Speed Automatic Transmission**
Enjoy smooth and responsive handling with the 6-speed automatic transmission

- **Ample Interior Space**
With spacious seating for up to five passengers and a roomy cargo area

- **Advanced Safety Features**
Includes essential safety technologies such as Electronic Stability Control, Hill-Start Assist Control, and x8 airbags.

- **Modern Infotainment System**
Comes with an 8-inch touchscreen display, Apple CarPlay, and Android Auto.

- **Dual-Zone Climate Control**
The dual-zone automatic climate control lets both the driver and front passenger set their own preferred temperatures, ensuring a comfortable ride for everyone.


This 2020 Kia Sportage LX Diesel is an ideal choice for anyone looking for a compact SUV with impressive fuel economy, advanced safety features, and versatile interior space. Its sleek design, dependable diesel engine, and comfortable amenities make it a fantastic choice for both city driving and outdoor adventures.
