2012 Toyota FJ Cruiser with 192,300 kilometres.
A modern version of the old FJ Landcruiser with all modern features.
It is powered by a 4 litre V6 petrol engine with dual variable valve timing, a 5 stage automatic transmission, and part-time 4WD with high/low ratios.
Standard equipment includes six airbags, "VSC" Vehicle Stability Control, TRC Traction Control, Apple Carplay Compatible, Bluetooth, and air-conditioning, running boards, roof rack, plus a rear-view camera.
