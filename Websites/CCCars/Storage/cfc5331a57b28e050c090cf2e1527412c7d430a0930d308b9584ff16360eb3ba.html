The 2012 Subaru Impreza 2.0L AWD (All-Wheel Drive) offers several key features, focusing on performance, safety, and comfort.

### **Performance and Drivetrain:**
- **Engine:** 2.0L 4-cylinder Boxer engine (producing around 148 horsepower and 145 lb-ft of torque).
- **Drivetrain:** Symmetrical All-Wheel Drive (AWD), which provides enhanced traction and stability, especially in adverse weather conditions.
-
### **Interior Features:**
- **Seating Capacity:** 5 passengers.
- **Climate Control:** Air conditioning with manual controls (automatic climate control available in higher trims).
- **Storage:** Foldable rear seats for added cargo space.

### **Safety Features:**
- **Airbags:**
- **ABS (Anti-lock Braking System)** with Electronic Brake-force Distribution (EBD).
- **Traction Control System (TCS)** and **Vehicle Dynamics Control (VDC)** for stability.
- **Crush zones** and reinforced safety cage design for added protection in the event of a collision.

### **Technology and Convenience Features:**
- **Keyless entry**
- **Power windows and locks**
- **Cruise control** and **tilt steering wheel** for added driving comfort.

### **Driving Experience:**
- **Handling:** Known for agile handling, especially with its AWD system that provides good traction in various road conditions.
- **Noise Isolation:** Offers decent cabin noise isolation for a more pleasant driving experience.

Overall, the 2012 Subaru Impreza 2.0L AWD is known for its reliability, fand its all-wheel-drive capability, making it a good option for those living in areas with challenging weather conditions.
