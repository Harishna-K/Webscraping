Stock number: 25031

The 2016 Subaru Impreza Sport HV 2.0i-S EyeSight is a stylish and sporty hybrid hatchback designed for efficiency and safety. It features a 2.0L petrol engine paired with an electric motor, delivering improved fuel economy and responsive performance. Equipped with Subaru's renowned Symmetrical All-Wheel Drive, it ensures excellent handling and stability in various conditions. The EyeSight Driver Assist Technology provides advanced safety features like adaptive cruise control, pre-collision braking, and lane-keeping assistance. With its premium interior, sporty design, and practical versatility, the Impreza Sport Hybrid offers a balanced blend of performance, comfort, and safety.
