The 2014 Nissan X-Trail is equipped with various safety features, including an emergency brake system.

Anti-lock Braking System (ABS): Prevents the wheels from locking up during braking, allowing for better control.

Electronic Brakeforce Distribution (EBD): Optimizes brake force to each wheel, improving stability and control.

Brake Assist: Detects emergency braking situations and applies maximum braking force to reduce stopping distance.

Traction Control System (TCS): Helps maintain traction on slippery surfaces, enhancing stability during braking.

Vehicle Dynamic Control (VDC): Monitors the vehicle's trajectory and adjusts braking and engine power to maintain control.
