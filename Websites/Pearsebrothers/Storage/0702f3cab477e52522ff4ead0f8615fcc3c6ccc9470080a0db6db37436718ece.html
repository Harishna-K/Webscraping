NZ NEW , LOW MILAGE , 1.0 MILD HYBRID , HIGH SPEC

2023 Ford Puma ST-Line 1.0L Mild Hybrid in A sharp grey Hue is a compact SUV designed for both style and efficiency. With only 27,000 km and with the balance of a 5-year unlimited kilometres warranty, you've got peace of mind for the long haul.

The 1.0L EcoBoost Mild Hybrid engine in a striking Metallic Grey is built to be a worry-free investment for long-term ownership. Its 1.0L turbocharged engine, coupled with mild-hybrid technology, produces a modest 92 kW of power, making it quite zippy for city driving while also delivering excellent fuel economy of around 5.3L/100km.

The ST-Line trim offers a sporty and stylish look, perfect for those who want a bit of flair in their daily drive. With sporty touches with distinctive ST-Line body styling and a tuned suspension that enhances handling. Inside, the Puma ST-Line provides an immersive experience with features like the premium sound system, a digital instrument cluster, and Fords SYNC 3 infotainment system. Ford has also prioritized driver assistance, equipping the Puma with options like adaptive cruise control, lane-keep assist, and blind-spot monitoring, making it ideal for safe urban and highway drives.
