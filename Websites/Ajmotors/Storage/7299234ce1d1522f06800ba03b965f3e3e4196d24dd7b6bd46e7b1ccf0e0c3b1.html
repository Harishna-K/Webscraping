# 2013 Toyota Vellfire 3.5 G edition
{Stock number. 23810}

The 2013 Toyota Vellfire 3.5 G Edition is a premium minivan from Toyota, which is a luxurious counterpart to the Toyota Alphard, targeted at the higher-end market. This model was part of the second-generation Vellfire, first introduced in 2008, and continues the trend of offering top-notch features, comfort, and performance.

# Key features
- Power sliding door: The power sliding doors of the Vellfire 3.5 G Edition are motorized and can be opened or closed with the press of a button. This can be done from the drivers seat, the key fob, or manually by pressing a button near the door.

- Cruise control: To activate the cruise control, you typically need to press a "Cruise" button on the steering wheel or stalk (usually located behind the wheel or on the dashboard).

- Power tail gate: The power tailgate allows you to open and close the rear hatch with the push of a button, making loading and unloading much easier. It opens smoothly and automatically without needing you to manually lift or close the door.
