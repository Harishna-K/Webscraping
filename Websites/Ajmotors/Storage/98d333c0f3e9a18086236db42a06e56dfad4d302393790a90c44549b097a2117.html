# 2017 Subaru XV 2.0-L EYESIGHT
{Stock number. 22400}

The 2017 Subaru XV (also known as the Subaru Crosstrek in some markets) equipped with the 2.0-litre engine and Eyesight technology offers a blend of versatility and safety. The Eyesight suite includes features like adaptive cruise control, pre-collision braking, lane departure warning, and lane-keeping assist, enhancing driver confidence and safety.

# Key features

- Eyesight: Subaru's Eyesight is a driver assist system designed to enhance safety and convenience. Key features include, Adaptive cruise control, Pre crush safety, and Lane keeping assist.

- Symmetrical All-Wheel Drive (AWD) is Subaru's signature drivetrain system designed to provide balanced handling and traction. Also known as full time All Wheel Drive.

- Smart Key System: in Subaru vehicles allows for convenient entry and ignition without needing to remove the key fob from your pocket or bag.
