# 2013 Honda Insight Exclusive 1.5 Hybrid
{Stock number. 24719}

The 2013 Honda Insight Exclusive 1.5 Hybrid is a version of Honda's hybrid vehicle, the Insight, which features a combination of a gasoline engine and an electric motor to provide better fuel efficiency compared to conventional vehicles.

# Key features
- Cruise control: The 2013 Honda Insight Exclusive 1.5 Hybrid comes with cruise control as a standard feature. Cruise control is a system that automatically controls the speed of the car, allowing the driver to maintain a constant speed without having to keep their foot on the accelerator.

- The 2013 Honda Insight Exclusive 1.5 Hybrid does not come with paddle shifters. Paddle shifters are typically found in vehicles with automatic transmissions that offer manual shift modes, allowing the driver to manually control the gear changes without using a traditional gear lever.

- Eco mode: When activated, ECO Mode adjusts the car's throttle response, air conditioning settings, and other driving characteristics to optimize fuel economy.
