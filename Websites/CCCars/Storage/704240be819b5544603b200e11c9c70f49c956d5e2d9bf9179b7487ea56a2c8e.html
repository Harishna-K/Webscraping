The 2019 Nissan Note e-POWER is a hybrid compact car that stands out for its unique powertrain, combining the benefits of electric driving with the convenience of a petrol engine.

**Electric Drive Feel**
- Since the wheels are powered by the electric motor, the Nissan Note e-POWER delivers a smooth, quiet, and responsive driving experience similar to a fully electric vehicle. This is ideal for urban environments where the car can easily accelerate and decelerate without the noise and vibration associated with traditional internal combustion engines.

### **Regenerative Braking**
- The e-POWER system includes regenerative braking, which recovers energy during braking and feeds it back into the battery, helping to improve efficiency and extend the driving range.

**Advanced Safety Features**
- The 2019 Nissan Note e-POWER comes equipped with a range of Nissan's safety features,
- **Intelligent Emergency Braking**: Helps prevent collisions by detecting obstacles ahead.
- **Lane Departure Warning**: Alerts drivers if the vehicle unintentionally drifts out of its lane.
- **Around View Monitor**: Provides a bird's-eye view of the car's surroundings for easier parking and maneuvering.

Overall, the 2019 Nissan Note e-POWER offers a compelling combination of efficiency, convenience, and a fun-to-drive electric experience, all while maintaining the practicality of a compact car.
