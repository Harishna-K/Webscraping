Kaleen Rugs, Inc.

Add to MyMarket

For more than 50 years The Kaleen family has built a reputation as a leading manufacturer, importer, and distributor of multi-category resources in today’s floor covering market, working with designers and retailers to provide beautiful top-quality rugs, carpets, and home accessories for homes around the world. Kaleen is headquartered in Mumbai, India, and has three manufacturing units in Bikaner, Mirzapur, and Panipat where yarn rugs and broadloom are produced and shipped, using various international operations, and our United States office and distribution center in Dalton, Georgia. Kaleen’s point of pride and commitment to customer satisfaction reflects the proven success for over 50 years within the textile industry. The strength of Kaleen’s skilled artisans allows for many opportunities no matter the size or complexity of the project. Kaleen’s new and existing manufacturing plants are certified as “GREEN ENERGY,” i.e. solar power and recycling water plants.

Luxe by Kaleen - Casavola Collection (CVL01-80)

The Casavola Collection blends contemporary design with timeless geometric motifs to bring texture and elegance into any space. Available in Ash, Denim, Glacier, Sand, and Spice, each rug provides a minimalist yet graphic appeal, perfect for any space yearning for a touch of effortless sophistication.

Luxe by Kaleen - Jinan Collection (JNN01-83)

The Jinan Collection is a celebration of exquisite artistry, where the intricacy of hand-knotting transforms simplicity into something extraordinary. Each piece offers a quiet sophistication, allowing the texture and subtle nuances to take center stage. Available in Beige, Ecru, White, and Steel, each hue brings a touch of understated luxury.

Luxe by Kaleen - Rhodera Collection (RHD01-03)

The Rhodera Collection draws inspiration from nature’s elegant process of sandstone formation through hand-knotted artistry that mirrors the intricate beauty found in sandstone’s layers of fine detail. Available in Beige, Grey, and Steel, the collection provides a palette that balances harmony and refinement for any interior.
