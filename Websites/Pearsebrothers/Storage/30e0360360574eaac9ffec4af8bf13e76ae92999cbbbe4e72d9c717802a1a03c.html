4WD, LEATHER, 7 SEATS, NO DEPOSIT FINANCE

Powerful & Capable 4WD Performance: Equipped with a 3.5L V6 engine and a robust 4WD system, the 2018 Nissan Pathfinder TI is ready to tackle both urban roads and off-road adventures with ease.

Spacious 7-Seater: Perfect for larger families or groups, with three rows of seating to comfortably accommodate up to seven passengers.

Luxurious Leather Interior: Premium leather upholstery throughout the cabin, adding sophistication and comfort for both short trips and long journeys.

Smooth & Powerful Drive: Powered by a 3.5L engine, offering a smooth, responsive driving experience with ample power for towing and highway driving.

Advanced Safety Features: Includes Nissans Safety Shield technologies, such as blind-spot monitoring, forward collision warning, and emergency braking, ensuring a safe ride for everyone.

User-Friendly Technology: A modern infotainment system with a touchscreen, Bluetooth and navigation to keep you connected and informed on the go.

Impressive Towing Capacity: With a strong towing capacity, the Pathfinder TI is ideal for transporting trailers, boats, or heavy loadsperfect for family vacations or work-related needs.

Spacious Cargo Capacity: Flexible seating and ample trunk space, offering versatility for all your gear, luggage, or sporting equipment.

Comfort for All Passengers: Tri-zone climate control, heated seats, and power-adjustable front seats provide personalized comfort for every passenger.
