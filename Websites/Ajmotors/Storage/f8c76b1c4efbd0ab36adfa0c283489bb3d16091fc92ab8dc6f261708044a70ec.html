# 2013 Nissan Juke 15RX TYPE V
{Stock number. 23818}

The 2013 Nissan Juke 15RX Type V is a quirky, fun-to-drive small SUV that offers distinctive styling, good performance for its size, and a surprising amount of utility. It's ideal for those looking for a unique vehicle that stands out on the road and provides a dynamic driving experience.

# Key features
- Auto start stop: The auto start-stop system (also known as idle-stop or engine stop-start) is a feature designed to improve fuel efficiency and reduce emissions by automatically shutting off the engine when the vehicle comes to a stop and then restarting it when the accelerator is pressed.

- Push button start: Push button start (also known as keyless ignition) is a modern feature found in many vehicles, including the 2013 Nissan Juke, that allows you to start and stop the engine without using a traditional key.

- Retractable mirrors (also known as power-folding mirrors) are a convenient feature found in many vehicles, including the 2013 Nissan Juke, designed to enhance convenience, reduce damage in tight spaces, and improve the overall aerodynamics of the vehicle.
