Excellent example of a 2010 BMW 535i Gran Turismo in Grey Mica with Black Full Leather Sport Interior. Features Include Multi Function Steering Wheel, Panaromic Sunroof, Cruise Control, Heated Front Seats, Dual Zone Climate Control, 2 Keys and Factory Alloy Wheels.
