Mia Melange

Add to MyMarket

Mia Mélange is a proudly South African brand that handcrafts interior décor and lifestyle products, using predominantly 100% cotton rope. Our team of talented artisans ethically handcrafts baskets, bowls, planters, bags, ottomans, and more! We aim to create high quality products, by using the best and most sustainable local materials we can find. Each one of our products is carefully designed and produced with great care and attention to detail. Our designs are inspired by a love for nature, texture, natural materials and ancient crafting techniques. We like to combine all of these to create modern and functional designs. We make all our products in our studio in the Winelands town of Stellenbosch, South Africa.

Mia Melange Karoo Re-rope

At Mia Mélange, we value sustainability and the impact we have on the earth. We created a beautiful collection made with a base of Re-Rope™, a rope that is locally made from recycled South African plastic bottles.
