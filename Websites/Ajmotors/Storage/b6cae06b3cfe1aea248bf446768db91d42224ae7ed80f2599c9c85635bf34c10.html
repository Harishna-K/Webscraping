# 2020 Mercedes Benz A180 1.3P 7AT (NZ New)
{Stock number. 24799}

The 2020 Mercedes-Benz A180 1.3P 7AT is part of the A-Class range and is a compact luxury hatchback offered by Mercedes-Benz. It combines stylish design, advanced technology, and a performance-oriented driving experience, although positioned as a more entry-level option in the Mercedes-Benz lineup.

# Key features
- Key less entry: The 2020 Mercedes-Benz A180 1.3P 7AT comes equipped with Keyless Entry as part of its premium features. This system allows for convenient access to your vehicle without needing to remove the key fob from your pocket or bag.

- Cruise control: The 2020 Mercedes-Benz A180 1.3P 7AT is equipped with Cruise Control, a feature that enhances driving comfort and convenience, particularly on long trips or highway driving.

- Heated seats: The heating controls are generally located on the side of the front seats or integrated into the center console. Youll find a button to activate the heating, often with multiple heat settings (usually Low, Medium, and High).
