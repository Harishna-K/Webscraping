Introducing the 2020 Holden Acadia LTZ-V, a premium 7-seater SUV that seamlessly blends luxury, performance, and versatility.

- **Engine & Performance:** Equipped with a robust 3.6-litre V6 petrol engine producing 231 kW of power and 367 Nm of torque, paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission. The all-wheel-drive (AWD) system ensures confident handling and traction across various terrains.

- **Interior Comfort:** The spacious cabin features full leather upholstery, with heated and ventilated front seats providing year-round comfort. Tri-zone climate control ensures all passengers enjoy a pleasant environment, while the advanced Bose infotainment system with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto keeps everyone connected.

- **Safety Features:** The Acadia LTZ-V is equipped with a comprehensive suite of safety technologies, including Autonomous Emergency Braking, Lane Keep Assist, Blind Spot Monitoring, and Rear Cross Traffic Alert, all contributing to a 5-star ANCAP safety rating.

- **Practicality & Space:** Designed with family adventures in mind, the Acadia offers flexible seating configurations and ample cargo space. The smart-slide second-row seats provide easy access to the third row, accommodating up to seven passengers comfortably.
