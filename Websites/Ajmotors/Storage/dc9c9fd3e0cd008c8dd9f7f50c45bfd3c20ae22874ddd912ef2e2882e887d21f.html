# 2018 Subaru XV 20.L Eyesight 4WD
{Stock number. 23621}

The 2018 Subaru XV 2.0L Eyesight 4WD is a compact crossover SUV that combines Subaru's signature all-wheel-drive (AWD) system with the brand's Eyesight safety technology.

# Key features
- Eyesight technology: The primary benefit of Eyesight is that it increases overall safety by reducing human error in critical driving situations. It offers an extra layer of protection against collisions, even when the driver may not be fully alert or reacting fast enough.

- Lane departure assist: The system continuously scans the road ahead for visible lane boundaries (typically painted lines) and tracks the vehicles position within those lanes.

- Adaptive cruise control: It builds upon traditional cruise control by adding an element of intelligence, using sensors like radar or cameras to monitor traffic and adjust the car's speed accordingly.
