A very tidy 2007 Toyota Blade G hatchback, in silver, with 16 inch alloy wheels and rear spoiler, that has travelled 47,300 kilometres.
The Blade is a luxury version of the Toyota Auris (Corolla) fitted with the 2.4 litre Toyota Camry motor, and very smooth CVT transmission.
To look after the extra power, the car is fitted with stability and traction control, while the passengers are protected with seven airbags amongst other safety components resulting in a 4-star VSSR safety rating.
The Blade also comes with part-leather seats with electric adjustment on driver's seat, "Smart Key" starting/locking, CD player, climate control, and rear-view camera.
Contact us soon to arrange for a test drive of this superb hatchback.
