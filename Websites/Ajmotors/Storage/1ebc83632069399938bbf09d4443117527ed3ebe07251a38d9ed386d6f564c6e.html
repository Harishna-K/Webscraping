Say hello to your next adventure companion! This 2013 Black Nissan Dualis is a standout in every way, blending impressive safety features, a smooth drive, and luxurious touches like heated seats. Whether you're navigating city streets or cruising the open road, this Dualis is designed to make every drive enjoyable.

Highlights at a glance:

18-inch alloy wheels and sleek black finish for a polished exterior.

Safety first with +6 airbags, traction control, and a reversing camera.

Stay connected on the go with Bluetooth connectivity.

Heated front seats add a touch of luxury to chilly mornings.

Robust 2000cc engine offering a smooth and confident ride.

Fog lights to light the way during adverse weather conditions.

Brighten your day with a sunroof and fresh air while you drive.
