NZ NEW, LOW KMS, NEW 20INCH MAGS + TIRES

Striking Blacked-Out Design: The 2021 Ford Ranger XLT features a sleek, blacked-out look that enhances its bold, aggressive styling, making a statement on and off the road.

Versatile Canopy: The rugged canopy adds an extra layer of utility, offering secure storage for all your gear while protecting it from the elementsperfect for work, weekend trips, or outdoor adventures.

Stylish and Practical: The blacked-out canopy complements the vehicle's muscular design, providing both a premium look and extra cargo space without compromising style.

Comfort and Convenience: Featuring a spacious interior with modern tech like Apple CarPlay, Android Auto, and a rearview camera, the Ranger XLT keeps you comfortable and connected during every drive.

Advanced Safety Features: Equipped with adaptive cruise control, lane-keeping assist, and automatic emergency braking, ensuring your safety on any journey.

Low Mileage, Like-New Condition: With a 2021 model year and low mileage, you're getting a near-new vehicle with reduced depreciation and great long-term value.

Ultimate Practicality: The canopy not only offers enhanced storage but also provides a secure, weatherproof solution for transporting tools, gear, or supplies wherever you need to go. Equipped with a lightbar in the back for those late night or early morning adventures!
