Lucas + McKearn

Add to MyMarket

Lucas McKearn is a partnership between Flambeau Lighting and Elstead Lighting which was created in 2016. It is a design focused company dedicated to creating some of the world's more unique interior lighting. The partners are Joe McKearn, who has been the well renowned leader at Flambeau Lighting of New Orleans and Jonathan & Deborah Lucas, the British husband and wife team that run their near 50 year old lighting fixture manufacturing and design company from the country village of Alton in the South of England.

Kristy 5 Light Chandelier

The Kristy 5 Light Chandelier brings a glow to any space. 42" wide; the chandelier height can be adjusted at the top of the fixture with gold piping above the point where the chains meet. Finished in antiqued gold, the fixture's textured frame and Opal white glass add style and elegance to any setting.

Meridian 9 Light Pendant

The Meridian Chandelier is a 21" wide adjustable height, two-tier fixture. The nine lights enclosed accent the radiant style of the design. The body is an antiqued gold leaf and is highlighted by dark grey textured glass discs. When lit, the discs give off a light grey tone. When not illuminated, the discs are a dark grey.

Saucer 4 Light Pendant

The Saucer 20" Pendant is a unique multi-tier adjustable height item finished in Aged Brass. There are lights hidden below the profile of the Aged Brass tier that create an uplight effect. The Opal Glass orb below creates a clean, functional light to illuminate the areas below.
