Sport Turbo, Part Leather, 4WD, Subaru Eyesight

Experience sporty performance and advanced technology with this 2012 Black Subaru Legacy! Featuring a turbocharged engine, AWD capability, and paddle shifters, this Legacy is built for drivers who love control and thrill. Add in luxurious touches like dual climate control, push-button start, and modern tech like Bluetooth and a reversing camera, and this sedan is as stylish as it is powerful.

Key Features:

Bold black exterior paired with sleek 18-inch alloy wheels.

Turbocharged engine delivering thrilling performance and power.

Subarus signature AWD system for superior grip and handling in all conditions.

Paddle shifters for a sporty and engaging driving experience.

8 airbags for enhanced safety and peace of mind.

Push-button start for modern convenience and effortless ignition.

Bluetooth connectivity for hands-free calls and music streaming.

Reversing camera for easy parking and safer maneuvering.

Dual climate control for personalized comfort for both driver and
passengers.

Cruise control for relaxed, smooth highway driving.
