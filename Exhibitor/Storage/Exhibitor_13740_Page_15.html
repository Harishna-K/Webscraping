Add to MyMarket

Fibre by Auskin sources, designs, manufactures & distributes natural fibre products. We source natural fibres from around the globe offering a range of unique natural fibre products & textures for a multitude of industries. Headquartered in Australia, we operate on a global scale purchasing raw material and finished products from around the world. We own & operate tanning, manufacturing and design facilities in China and have sales offices located in all major markets – USA, Europe, Japan, China, Australia and New Zealand. Whether it’s Alpaca wool from South America or Merino woolskin from the depths of the Southern Alps of New Zealand, we provide customers with unique natural fibre products and design solutions.
