Mr. & Mrs. Howard

Add to MyMarket

From designers Jim and Phoebe Howard, this collection encapsulates beautiful design using a fresh blend of traditional and current sensibilities grounded in timeless design theory. The collection incorporates both upholstery and case goods. New Nellie Jane Collection! As an experienced designer, Nellie has noticed a remarkable resurgence of traditionalism and an undeniable yearning for maximalism. In response, she skillfully revived the iconic elements of the golden age of furnishings, such as scallops, box pleats, and gathered skirts, while ensuring a contemporary twist suitable for today’s modern families. Excited to see you there!

MH11304 Hadley Twin Bed - Nellie Jane for Mr. and Mrs. Howard Collection

The Hadley Twin Bed brings a touch of Moroccan-inspired elegance to any bedroom. Available with or without rosettes, the uniquely shaped headboard adds a stylish flair that enlivens any space. This contemporary bed frame is perfect for those seeking a chic and comfortable upholstered bed. Its twin size makes it an ideal choice for smaller rooms.

SWH334C King Swivel Chair - Nellie Jane for Mr. and Mrs. Howard

The King Swivel Chair is a romantic, elegant, yet relaxed swivel chair with curves in all the right places. Dressmaker details such as a pleated corner give this chair a freshly updated traditional design.

MH30362 Osbourne Cabinet

The Osbourne Cabinet, a continental commode with a high gloss spider web sycamore door front, is a wonderful addition to a foyer or living room. The canted tapering legs complete the look of this functional yet striking cabinet.
