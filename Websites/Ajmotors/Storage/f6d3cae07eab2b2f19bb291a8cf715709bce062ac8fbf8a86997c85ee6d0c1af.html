# 2019 Toyota Corolla Fielder HV G
{Stock number. 23813}

"Efficient, versatile, and sleek the 2019 Toyota Corolla Fielder HV G combines hybrid technology with the practicality of a station wagon, offering exceptional fuel economy, advanced safety features, and ample space for every journey."

# Key features
- LDW: Lane Departure Warning (LDW) is a system designed to alert the driver when the vehicle unintentionally drifts out of its lane without the use of turn signals. It uses cameras or sensors to monitor lane markings on the road and detects if the vehicle is veering away from its lane.

- Pre crush safety: Pre-Collision Safety (also known as Pre-Collision System or PCS) is an advanced driver assistance feature designed to help prevent or reduce the severity of a collision. It is part of Toyota's Toyota Safety Sense (TSS) suite of safety technologies, and the 2019 Toyota Corolla Fielder HV G comes equipped with this system.

- Key less entry: Keyless Entry is a convenient feature that allows you to unlock and start your vehicle without needing to take the key out of your pocket or bag. This system uses radio frequency identification (RFID) or proximity sensors to detect when the key fob is nearby, allowing you to unlock and start the vehicle with just a button press or a touch.
