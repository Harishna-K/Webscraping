Malouf

Add to MyMarket

At Malouf, we innovate fresh ways to bring comfort into the home because our main goal is simple: we want to help people sleep better. Sam and Kacie Malouf transformed a two-person operation into a company with over 11,000 product choices. In their journey, Sam and Kacie found a common thread tying consumers together—desire for comfort, identity, and value. We’ve refined modern products, infusing unique foams with natural materials and improving the features and usability of adjustable bed bases. We’ve innovated by creating patents, researching new products, and building a vertically integrated company. In addition to our flagship Malouf™ products, we also offer a family of brands so everyone can create a fully and beautifully designed home: Wellsville™ luxury mattresses, Salt Flat™ home furnishings, and Weekender™ entry-level sleep products. As a Certified B Corporation®, we use business as a force for good. We’re concerned not only with revenue, but also with our corporate social responsibility for society, workers, the community, and the environment. We’ve got a triple bottom line: planet, people, profits.
