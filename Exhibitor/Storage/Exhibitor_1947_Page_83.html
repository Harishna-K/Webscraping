Global rug and textile furnishings company, Jaipur Living, is an innovator in the design world, partnering with talented designers and artisans around the world. With the Brand’s core promise to create memories from the ground up, Jaipur Living empowers, inspires, and connects differing communities, from the artisan to the end consumer, through meaningful and eye-catching design. We believe how we make our products is as important as the items themselves.
