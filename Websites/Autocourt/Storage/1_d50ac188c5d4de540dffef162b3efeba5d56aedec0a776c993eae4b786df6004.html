This is a very tidy Toyota Dyna truck, having travelled only 42,800 kilometres.
A 2002 Dyna with a 2 litre EFi petrol engine and 5 speed manual gearbox, suitable for many trades.
It has a flatdeck with dropsides with which measures 2800mm long by 1600mm wide, and has a carrying capacity of 1250kg.
The tilt cab provides access to the engine etc.
With a GVM of 2995kg, the Dyna can be driven with a car licence and does not need a COF.
