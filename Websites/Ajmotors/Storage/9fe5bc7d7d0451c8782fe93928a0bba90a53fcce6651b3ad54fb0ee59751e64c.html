Facelift, Bluetooth, Push Button, 8-Airbags

Introducing the 2019 Toyota Prius S, a stylish and highly efficient hybrid thats ready to provide a reliable, fuel-saving drive with outstanding safety features. This Prius stands out with a low odometer reading of just 105,000 km, making it a dependable choice with years of efficient performance ahead.

Toyota Safety Sense: Equipped with Toyotas comprehensive suite of advanced safety technologies, including Adaptive Cruise Control for a relaxed highway experience, Lane Departure Warning to help keep you centered on the road, and Forward Collision Mitigation to reduce accident risks by providing automatic braking if a collision seems imminent.

Automatic High Beam: Enhance night driving safety with automatic high beams that adjust to surrounding traffic for optimal visibility without dazzling other drivers.

Bluetooth Connectivity: Stay connected on the go with built-in Bluetooth, allowing hands-free calls and streaming from your favorite devices.

8 Airbags: Designed with passenger safety in mind, this Prius includes 8 strategically placed airbags to provide enhanced protection for all occupants in case of an accident.

Smart Key System: Experience convenience with Toyotas Smart Key, allowing you to lock, unlock, and start the vehicle without removing the key from your pocket or bag.

The 2019 Toyota Prius S is an ideal choice for those seeking a well-rounded hybrid vehicle with an excellent balance of fuel efficiency, advanced safety features, and innovative tech. With its low mileage and Toyotas trusted hybrid technology, this Prius offers long-term value and reliability, ensuring youll enjoy every kilometer with peace of mind.
