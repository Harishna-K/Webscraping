# 2012 Toyota Wish 1.8S
{Stock number. 24894}

The Toyota Wish 1.8S is often appreciated for its reliability, fuel efficiency, and practicality, making it a solid choice for families or individuals needing a versatile vehicle with ample passenger and cargo space.

# Key features
- Push button start:
The 2012 Toyota Wish 1.8S typically comes equipped with a push-button start feature, which is part of the vehicle's keyless entry and ignition system. This feature allows the driver to start the engine without inserting a traditional key into the ignition.

- 7 Seats: The 7-seat configuration makes the Toyota Wish a versatile option for families or for transporting multiple passengers without sacrificing too much cargo space. However, the third row is best suited for shorter trips or smaller passengers due to space limitations compared to the front and second-row seating areas.
