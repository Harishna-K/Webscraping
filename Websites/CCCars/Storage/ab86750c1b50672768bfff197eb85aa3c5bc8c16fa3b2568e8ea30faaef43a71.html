The 2015 Nissan Serena Highway Star S-Hybrid is a mid-sized minivan designed for family comfort, practicality, and fuel efficiency.

### Engine and Hybrid System
- **Hybrid Technology**: Equipped with Nissans Smart Simple Hybrid (S-Hybrid) system, which includes:
- An upgraded ECO motor that functions as both an alternator and an auxiliary motor, assisting the petrol engine during acceleration and cruising.
- Regenerative braking to recharge the battery, enhancing fuel efficiency.
- Idling Stop system to reduce fuel consumption by shutting off the engine when stationary.

### Dimensions and Capacity
- **Size**: 4,770 mm (length) x 1,735 mm (width) x 1,865 mm (height), adhering to Japans compact vehicle class restrictions.
- **Wheelbase**: 2,860 mm, providing a stable ride and ample interior space.
- **Seating Capacity**: 8 seats with flexible configurations for passengers or cargo.
- **Interior Dimensions**: Spacious cabin measuring approximately 3,060 mm (length) x 1,480 mm (width) x 1,380 mm (height).

### Interior Features
- **Comfort and Convenience**:
- Push-button start and keyless entry for easy access.
- Power windows, mirrors, and steering.
- Air conditioning
- Multiple seat configurations for versatility (e.g., foldable seats for extra cargo space).
- Reverse camera
- **Storage**: Ample storage compartments and a 60L fuel tank for long trips.

### Safety Features
- Anti-lock Braking System (ABS) and traction control (in some variants).
- Central locking, smart key, and alarm system.

The 2015 Nissan Serena Highway Star S-Hybrid blends practicality, family-friendly design, and mild hybrid efficiency, making it a popular choice for those needing a versatile and economical minivan.
