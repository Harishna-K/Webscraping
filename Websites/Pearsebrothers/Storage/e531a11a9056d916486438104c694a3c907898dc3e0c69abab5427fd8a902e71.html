NZ NEW, ONE OWNER

Powerful Performance & Efficiency: Equipped with a 2.0L EcoBlue diesel engine, the 2020 Transit CUSTOM SWB delivers an ideal mix of power and fuel efficiency, ensuring smooth driving for both city deliveries and long-haul journeys, while keeping operating costs low.

Advanced Safety Features: Stay safe with features like Autonomous Emergency Braking (AEB), Lane Keep Assist, and Traffic Sign Recognition, giving you peace of mind and enhanced security on the road, even in busy environments.

Smart Technology for Productivity: With Fords SYNC 3 infotainment system, Bluetooth connectivity, and Apple CarPlay/Android Auto integration, you can stay connected, navigate easily, and manage your business on the go with ease.

Durable & Practical Design: Built with tough, durable materials, the Transit CUSTOM SWB is designed to withstand the demands of daily use, while offering a comfortable cabin with ergonomically designed seating to keep you productive throughout the day.

Flexible Storage Solutions: With a variety of configurable storage options and load area features, the Transit CUSTOM SWB allows you to adapt the space to meet your specific business needs, whether you're transporting tools, supplies, or larger items.
