### 2018 Holden Equinox LT 1.6DT SUV

Introducing the 2018 Holden Equinox LT, a versatile and efficient family SUV designed to meet the demands of modern life. With only 75,000 km on the odometer and a full service history, this clean and tidy example is powered by the 1.6-litre turbo diesel engine, offering a blend of performance and fuel economy.

- **Engine & Performance:** The Equinox LT is equipped with a 1.6-litre turbo diesel engine, delivering 100 kW of power at 4000 rpm and 320 Nm of torque between 2000-2250 rpm.

- **Interior Comfort:** The spacious cabin features comfortable seating and modern amenities, including an advanced infotainment system with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto integration, keeping you connected on the go.

- **Safety Features:** The Equinox LT comes equipped with a comprehensive suite of safety technologies, including multiple airbags, stability control, and anti-lock braking systems, contributing to its high safety standards.

- **Practicality & Space:** Designed with family needs in mind, the Equinox offers ample cargo space and flexible seating configurations, making it suitable for both daily commutes and weekend adventures.

This 2018 Holden Equinox LT stands out as a reliable and stylish family SUV, offering a blend of performance, comfort, and safety. Don't miss the opportunity to own this exceptional vehiclecontact us today to arrange a test drive or to obtain more information!
