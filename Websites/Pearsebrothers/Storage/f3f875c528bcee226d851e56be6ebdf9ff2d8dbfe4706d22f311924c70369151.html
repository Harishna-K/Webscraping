NZ NEW LEATHER 7 SEATS

Luxurious & Comfortable Interior: Premium-grade materials throughout, including leather upholstery, providing a refined and comfortable ride for both driver and passengers.

Powerful & Efficient Engine: Equipped with a smooth and capable 2.2L diesel engine option, offering a great balance of power and fuel efficiency for various driving conditions.

Spacious 7-Seater: With three rows of seating, the 2015 Sorento is perfect for large families or those who frequently transport passengers, ensuring everyone rides in comfort.

Advanced Safety Features: Includes advanced safety technologies like blind-spot detection, rearview camera, and park assist, offering peace of mind for all occupants.

User-Friendly Infotainment: Equipped with a touchscreen, Bluetooth, and navigation system, ensuring a modern and connected driving experience.

Smooth & Quiet Ride: Known for its smooth, quiet cabin, the Sorento provides a relaxing driving experience, ideal for both city commutes and long road trips.

Towing Capacity: Capable of towing up to 2,000kg, making it a versatile option for hauling trailers, boats, or heavy loads.

All-Wheel Drive (AWD): The AWD capability ensures confidence in various driving conditions, including wet roads or light off-road adventures.

Stylish Design: Sleek, modern exterior with bold lines and a premium finish, making a strong statement wherever you go.

Great Value for Money: Packed with features typically found in higher-end vehicles, the 2015 Sorento offers excellent value for those seeking luxury, space, and performance at an affordable price.
