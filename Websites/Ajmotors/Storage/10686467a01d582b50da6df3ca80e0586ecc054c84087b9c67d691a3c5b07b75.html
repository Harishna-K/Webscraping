# 2013 Toyota Hiace ZL TD (NZ New)
{Stock number. 24182}

The 2013 Toyota Hiace ZL TD is a reliable and practical vehicle with a focus on work-related needs. Its turbo diesel engine offers excellent fuel economy, while its cargo space and robust build make it a great choice for commercial use. The simplicity of the design makes it straightforward to maintain and operate, which is another reason its so popular among businesses.

# Key features
- Mini bus 11 seats: Ideal for group transport: Whether for school buses, shuttle services, or tour operators, the Hiace 11-seater is a solid, reliable vehicle for carrying passengers.
