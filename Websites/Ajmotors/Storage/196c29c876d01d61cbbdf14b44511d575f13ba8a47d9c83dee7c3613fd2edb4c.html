1 Owner, TowBar, Cruise Ctrl, 7-Airbags, Carplay

Tackle any terrain with confidence in this 2020 White Mitsubishi Triton! Built tough and equipped with advanced features, this ute is perfect for work or adventure. With a towbar, cab space, and a powerful design, the Triton offers both practicality and performance. Add in advanced safety features like lane departure warning and forward collision warning, plus modern conveniences like Bluetooth and a reversing camera, and youve got a vehicle thats ready for anything.

Key Features:

Sleek white exterior paired with durable 16-inch alloy wheels.

7 airbags for enhanced safety on the road.

Lane departure warning and forward collision warning for advanced driver assistance.

Towbar and cab space for work or weekend getaways.

Reversing camera for easy parking and maneuvering.

Bluetooth connectivity for hands-free calls and music streaming.

Cruise control for smooth, effortless highway driving.

Fog lights for improved visibility in low-light conditions.
