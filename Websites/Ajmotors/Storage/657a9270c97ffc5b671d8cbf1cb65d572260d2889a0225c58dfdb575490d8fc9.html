# 2021 Hyundai Santa Fe TM 7ST 4WD (NZ New)
{Stock number. 24229}

The 2021 Hyundai Santa Fe TM 7ST 4WD (NZ New) is a modern, mid-sized SUV that combines style, practicality, and advanced technology. The Santa Fe TM is the fourth-generation model of the Santa Fe, and it was released with several improvements over previous generations, making it a popular choice in markets like New Zealand.

# Key features
- LDW: LDW stands for Lane Departure Warning, which is an advanced driver-assistance system (ADAS) designed to alert the driver when the vehicle unintentionally drifts out of its lane on the road.

- Cruise control: Cruise control is a feature in modern vehicles that allows the driver to set and maintain a constant speed without having to keep their foot on the accelerator.

- Pre crush safety: Pre-Crash Safety, also known as Pre-Collision Safety or Pre-Crash Prevention, refers to a suite of advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS) designed to detect potential collisions with obstacles, vehicles, or pedestrians before they happen.
