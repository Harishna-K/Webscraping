Sport, Skyactiv Sense, 6-Airbags, Bluetooth

Looking for a stylish, reliable hatchback with low mileage and cutting-edge safety features? This 2017 Mazda Demio 13S ticks all the boxes! With just 85,000 km on the clock, its packed with smart technology like forward collision mitigation, blind-spot monitoring, and a reversing camera to keep you safe on the road. Add in push-button start, Bluetooth connectivity, and sleek alloy wheels, and this Demio delivers both comfort and confidence.

Low Mileage: Only 85,000 km, giving you plenty of worry-free driving.
Push-Button Start: Modern convenience for easy entry and ignition.
6 Airbags: Enhanced safety for you and your passengers.
Forward Collision Mitigation: Advanced safety to help prevent accidents.
Blind-Spot Monitoring: Drive with confidence on every lane change.
Reversing Camera: Simplify parking and reversing in tight spaces.
Bluetooth Connectivity: Stay connected and hands-free on the road.
Alloy Wheels: Add a touch of style to every drive.
