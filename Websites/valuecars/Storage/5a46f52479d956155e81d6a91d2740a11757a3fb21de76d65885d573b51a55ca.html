Brand New Van- Auction Grade 5 - NZ Stereo

Calling all Tradies!, Here we have an opportunity to purchase a brand new Nissan van at an excellent price.
This Nissan NV200 comes equipped with Dual Airbags, Twin Sliding doors, NZ Stereo with Bluetooth, Air Conditioning, Fuel saving Idle Stop, Electric windows and Mirrors, Traction Control, Remote Locking and has travelled delivery KMs only.
