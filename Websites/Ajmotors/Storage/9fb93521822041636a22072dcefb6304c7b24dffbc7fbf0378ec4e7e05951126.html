# 2014 Toyota Auris 150X S Package
{Stock number. 24392}


The 2014 Toyota Auris 150X S Package is a trim level of the popular Toyota Auris hatchback, designed for those looking for a balance of sporty features and practicality.

# Key features
- Dual climate control:
The dual climate control feature in the 2014 Toyota Auris 150X S Package adds a level of comfort and convenience, especially for drivers and passengers who have different temperature preferences.

- Push button start: The car comes with a key fob that communicates wirelessly with the vehicle. As long as the fob is inside the car (in your pocket or bag), the car detects it.

- Multi function steering wheel:
The multi-function steering wheel in the 2014 Toyota Auris 150X S Package is a key feature that enhances convenience, safety, and the overall driving experience. It allows the driver to control various vehicle functions without taking their hands off the steering wheel.

If you're looking for more information about the car, feel free to reach out! Whether you have questions about specific features or need assistance with anything else, our team is happy to help.
