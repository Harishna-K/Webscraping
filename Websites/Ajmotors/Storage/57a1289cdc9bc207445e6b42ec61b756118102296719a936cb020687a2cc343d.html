# 2020 Toyota Rav4 GX (NZ New)
{Stock number. 23776}

The 2020 Toyota RAV4 GX (NZ New) is a versatile and well-equipped SUV, offering a good balance of performance, technology, and safety. It's a great choice for anyone looking for a reliable family car with modern features and efficient fuel economy.

# Key features
- LDW: The feature is designed to help drivers stay aware of their position on the road and prevent unintentional lane departures, which can be helpful in preventing accidents, especially during long drives or on highways.

- ACC: The key difference with adaptive cruise control is that it uses radar and cameras to detect the distance from the vehicle ahead. If the system detects that the vehicle is approaching another vehicle too quickly, it will automatically adjust the speed to maintain a safe following distance.

- Pre crush safety: Pre-Collision Safety, also known as Pre-Collision System (PCS), is an advanced safety feature in many modern vehicles, including the 2020 Toyota RAV4 GX.
