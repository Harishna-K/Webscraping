# 2021 MItsubishi Outlander XLS 4WD 7seater
{Stock number. 25636}

The 2021 Mitsubishi Outlander XLS 4WD 7-seater is a solid choice if you're looking for a midsize SUV with three rows of seating. It's equipped with a 2.4L 4-cylinder engine, providing a balance of power and fuel efficiency. The 4WD system gives it some off-road capability, and it has a spacious cabin that can comfortably accommodate seven passengers, which is a plus for larger families.

# Key features
- ACC: The ACC system uses sensors, including a radar and cameras, mounted on the front of the vehicle to detect traffic ahead. The system continually scans the road, adjusting your speed to keep a safe distance from the vehicle in front.

- LDW: If the system senses that you're unintentionally moving out of your lane, it will typically issue an audible warning, sometimes accompanied by a visual alert on the dashboard, to notify you to steer back into your lane.

- Paddle gear shifter: Paddle shifters give you the ability to change gears manually for more precise control over your acceleration and deceleration, which can be useful in situations like merging onto highways or driving on winding roads.
