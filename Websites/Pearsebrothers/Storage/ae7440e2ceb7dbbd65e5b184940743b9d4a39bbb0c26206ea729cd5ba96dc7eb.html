Category Defining Three Wheeler

Welcome to Morgan New Zealand, and presenting to you, the beginning of a new chapter, the highly anticipated Super 3 - Available now for order. Please note, the price listed is the starting price for this model, examples of options are shown in the sample photos as above. Like every Morgan, it is important that the vehicle presents the client with a flexible canvas to impart their own personality upon. The Super 3 offers greater levels of customisation than ever before. Enquire within to discuss specific options in further detail, and start your journey with your new Morgan today.

[Please note that our unit allocation for the Super 3 in 2022 is strictly limited]

All Morgan cars go through an intensive process to achieve the optimum paint finish. In paint prep, the panels are sanded back and receive five layers of primer, before being checked and sanded once again. In the paint shop, they receive a final layer of primer before top coat and lacquer is applied.

The unique side blades fitted to Super 3 manage airflow through the radiators, whilst also allowing numerous luggage mounting options. The side blades can be painted to match the main body colour or finished in a contrasting colour, and they provide a canvas for the application of decals and livery packs available.

The design of the 20-inch front wheels is unique to Super 3. The positive offset allows for the longest possible suspension wishbones, with the brakes mounted far out. This maximises ride quality, handling and stability. To keep Super 3 looking proportionally correct, an all-new tyre has been developed with Avon.

The engine resides beneath the nose cone, positioned to deliver optimal weight distribution. The visual mass sits directly behind the centre line of the front wheels, and is a principal proportion of the Super 3. This feature can be highlighted further through customisation where the colour of the cowl can be tailored on one of five available colours.

Bracing the Ford Dragon engine are structural aluminium castings finished in silver or black. These castings support the engine and suspension packages while also ducting air into the radiators. The mechanical requirements of these castings deliver the character of Super 3s face and are therefore a crucial design element.
