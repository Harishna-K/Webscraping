This is a very tidy 2010 Toyota Prius L hybrid in light blue, with 15 inch alloy wheels that has travelled 42,600 kilometres.
The Prius is a hybrid car with a 1.8 litre petrol engine that develops 73kW combined with an electric motor to provide 100kW of power, and a 1.3kWh Ni-MH battery, which results in 4L/100km 4-star fuel economy.
5-star safety is provided by six airbags, ABS, stability/traction control, and the car comes with CD, climate control, and smart key starting & locking.
Be sure to check out this Hybrid Prius soon.
