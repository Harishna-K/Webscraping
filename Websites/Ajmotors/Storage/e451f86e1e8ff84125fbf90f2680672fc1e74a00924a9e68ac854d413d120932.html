# 2017 Nissan Qashqai TI (NZ New)
{Stock number. 25181}

The 2017 Nissan Qashqai Ti (NZ New) is a mid-size SUV that offers a blend of stylish design, practicality, and advanced features. As the top-tier trim of the Qashqai lineup in New Zealand, the Ti variant comes with a range of premium features, making it a well-rounded option for those seeking a comfortable and tech-savvy vehicle.

# Key features
- Glass roof: The panoramic roof usually includes an electric sunshade that can be closed to block out sunlight if needed, providing shade and preventing the cabin from heating up too much on sunny days.

- Heated seats: There are usually multiple temperature settings, which can be adjusted through buttons located on the center console or integrated into the seat controls.

- Parking sensors: The Qashqai Ti typically features both front and rear parking sensors. These sensors detect objects or obstacles in the vehicle's path when parking or maneuvering in tight spaces.
