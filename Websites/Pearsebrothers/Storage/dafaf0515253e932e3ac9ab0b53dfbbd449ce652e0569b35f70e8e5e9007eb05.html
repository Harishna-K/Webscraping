### **2016 Mazda CX-5 2.5L AWD Low-KM, Stylish & Reliable SUV**

The **2016 Mazda CX-5 2.5L AWD** is the perfect combination of **style, performance, and practicality**. With its **low 78,000km**, this well-maintained SUV is **built for adventure**, offering confidence-inspiring AWD capability, a powerful yet efficient engine, and a premium driving experience.

#### **Key Features:**

**Power & Performance:** 2.5L **Skyactiv petrol engine** delivering **strong acceleration** and smooth highway cruising.

**All-Wheel Drive (AWD):** Mazdas **intelligent AWD system** enhances traction in all road conditions, making it **ideal for city driving and weekend adventures**.

**Smooth Transmission:** Paired with a **6-speed automatic** for effortless driving and **responsive performance**.

**Advanced Technology:** 7 touchscreen infotainment system with **Bluetooth, navigation, and a premium sound system** keeps you connected and entertained.

**Comprehensive Safety:** **Blind-spot monitoring, rear cross-traffic alert, stability control, multiple airbags, and a rear view camera** provide top-tier protection for you and your family.
