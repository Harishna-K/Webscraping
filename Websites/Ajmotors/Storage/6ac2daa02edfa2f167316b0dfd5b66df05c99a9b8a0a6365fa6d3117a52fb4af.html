# 2023 Toyota Highlander LIMITED 2.5PH/4WD/HD
{Stock number. 25184}

The "2023 Toyota Highlander LIMITED 2.5PH/4WD/HD" is a high-end, hybrid-powered, four-wheel-drive SUV that comes with advanced multimedia features and premium amenities.

# Key features
- Pre crush safety:
Pre-crash safety refers to a suite of advanced safety technologies designed to help prevent or mitigate the severity of a collision before it occurs.

- LDW: This system uses cameras and sensors to monitor lane markings on the road. If the vehicle unintentionally drifts out of its lane without the turn signal being activated, the system will alert the driver.

- ACC: ACC stands for Adaptive Cruise Control, an advanced driver assistance system designed to automatically adjust a vehicle's speed to maintain a safe following distance from the vehicle ahead. It is an enhanced version of traditional cruise control, which maintains a constant speed set by the driver.

If you're looking for more information about the car, feel free to reach out! Whether you have questions about specific features or need assistance with anything else, our team is happy to help.
