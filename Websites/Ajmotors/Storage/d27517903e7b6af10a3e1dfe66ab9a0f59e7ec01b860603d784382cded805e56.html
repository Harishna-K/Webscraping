1 Owner, TowBar, Canopy, Cruise Ctrl, Carplay

Get ready to conquer any task with this 2016 White Holden Colorado! Built tough and loaded with modern features, this versatile ute offers a towbar and canopy for work and adventure, plus Apple CarPlay and Android Auto for seamless connectivity. With 11 airbags, a reversing camera, and cruise control, this Colorado combines rugged performance with advanced safety and comfort.

Key Features:

Sleek white exterior paired with durable 17-inch alloy wheels.

11 airbags for exceptional safety for all passengers.

Towbar and canopy for added practicality and secure cargo storage.

Apple CarPlay and Android Auto for seamless smartphone integration.

Reversing camera for confident and hassle-free parking.

Cruise control for smooth, relaxed highway driving.

Traction control for improved stability on all surfaces.

Fog lights for enhanced visibility in challenging weather conditions.
