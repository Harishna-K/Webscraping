8 Airbags - Radar Cruise - Super Low KMs

Here we have a super low km Toyota Prius S PHV Plug-in hybrid is this immaculate condition and is very well equipped.
Optioned with Climate control air conditioning, Smart key with Push button start, Radar Cruise Control, Lane departure warning, Collision prevention, Heated steering wheel, 8 airbags, Bluetooth Handsfree connectivity, Reverse camera & has only travelled 20758km's from new.
Looks sharp finished in Titanium Glow Metallic on 15 inch alloys with hubcaps fitted, & has a stylish Black cloth interior with Crème coloured seat, dash & door inserts. Features an 1800cc Hybrid Synergy Drive engine & also has a Plug-in charging system & with automatic transmission with awesome fuel efficiency.

Finance & Protecta Insurance products are also available for this vehicle.

Pre-purchase inspection can be arranged for out of town purchasers.

You can buy with confidence from Value Cars in the knowledge we are a totally New Zealand owned & funded provider of good quality cars & great after sales service for over 30 years.
