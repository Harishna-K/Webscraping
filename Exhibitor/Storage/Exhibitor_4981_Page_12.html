Armen Living

Add to MyMarket

Style leaders in design, modern-day furniture designer and manufacturer, Armen Living celebrates individuality, vibrant youthfulness, sensual refinement, and expert craftsmanship. Experience a boldly unique and exciting contemporary furniture collection defined by colorful, sleek, classically retro lines that resonate with the modern-day lifestyle of customers who choose to celebrate life through their individual style. “Each piece we design and develop conveys self-expression while resonating with a contemporary chic lifestyle." With a full line of indoor and outdoor furnishings for every room including bar, dining, living, office, bedroom, and outdoor living spaces. The result is a fashion-forward collection that is enhanced by sophisticated urban-retro aesthetics and designed with quality craftsmanship, durable materials and with prices that won't break your budget. As one of the leading resources in the country with over 500 SKUs just in their Barstool Category alone, their designs offer a wide assortment sizes, materials, colors, and finishes and with 50% original designs - you won’t find anywhere else. Open to the trade, our customers consist of retailers, designers, stagers, and hospitality purveyors across the globe. With a distribution center centrally located in Southern California, Armen Living offers exceptional and dependable customer service with expert craftsmanship that is beyond compare, and and at fiscally sensible prices.
