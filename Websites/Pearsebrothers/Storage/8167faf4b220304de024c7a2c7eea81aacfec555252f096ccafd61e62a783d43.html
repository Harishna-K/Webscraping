LOW KMS, STEELIES, FLATDECK, NO DEPOSIT FINANCE

Looking for a no-nonsense, hardworking ute? This 2017 Mazda BT-50 GLX is built to handle the toughest jobs with ease. With low mileage, a practical flat deck, and durable steel wheels, this truck is a powerhouse on and off the road. Well-maintained and ready for its next owner, its the perfect workhorse for tradies, farmers, or weekend adventurers.

Key Features:

Engine: 3.2L turbo diesel, delivering strong torque and efficiency.

Transmission: Smooth automatic offering control and fuel economy.

Interior: Spacious and comfortable cabin with durable cloth seats.

Entertainment & Tech: Bluetooth connectivity, AUX/USB inputs, and a clear infotainment display.

Safety: ABS, traction control, multiple airbags, and stability control for peace of mind.

Utility: Sturdy flat deck for easy loading, steelies for durability, and a tow bar for extra versatility.
