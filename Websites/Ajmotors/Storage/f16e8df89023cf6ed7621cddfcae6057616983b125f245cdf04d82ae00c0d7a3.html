New Tyres, Sport, Low KM, Fog Lights

Compact, efficient, and packed with extras! This 2013 Mazda Demio ticks all the boxes with its ultra-low odometer reading of 43,000 km and excellent fuel economy from its zippy 1300cc petrol engine. It comes equipped with brand-new tyres, a sporty set of alloy wheels, and fog lights for added safety and style.

Features at a glance:
Ultra-low 43,000 km odometer
Reliable 1.3L petrol engine
Sporty alloy wheels
All-new tyres for a smooth drive
Fog lights for safer travels in low visibility
Ideal size for urban driving
Apple CarPlay and Android Auto compatibility for seamless smartphone integration
Reversing camera for effortless parking and maneuvering
Dual airbags for essential safety and peace of mind
