New Moon Rugs

Add to MyMarket

There are few things left in the world that are created as part of a living supply chain, touched by human hands every step of the process. A New Moon™ rug is one of those things. Second generation and family-owned, New Moon set the standard for creative innovation and ethical production in Nepal over 30 years ago. Rooted in fine art, our hand-knotted rugs create a beautiful foundation to any space and will endure for years generations to come. Our Tibetan rug design library ranges from antique reproductions, traditional and transitional, to contemporary and atmospheric — all hand-knotted in Nepal without the use of child labor. Using only Tibetan wool, Chinese silk and natural nettle fibers, our rugs celebrate the legacy of the handcraft by remaining radically old-school in an ever-changing modern world. In addition to rugs, New Moon offers a line of throw pillows that perfectly complement our Southwestern tribal-inspired rug collection, Mesa™. Based in Wilmington, Delaware, New Moon is a founding member of GoodWeave — a global nonprofit that works to end child labor and provide services for working mothers.

New Moon Rugs Debuts 7 New Designs At High Point F/24 Market

New Moon Rugs, a leader in luxury Tibetan rug craftsmanship, proudly announces the launch of its fall 2024 introductions, featuring 7 innovative rug designs that will join their vast collection of fine luxury Tibetan rugs — set to debut at High Point Market in High Point, NC in October.

Download

Plumage, slate

Hand-knotted in our true 100-knot quality using Tibetan wool, Chinese silk, and natural nettle fibers. An artful plumage of abstract feathers weaves together a myriad of rich colors and textures. Featuring shades of taupe, khaki, ivory, brown, charcoal, blue, violet, mauve, paprika, tangerine, gold, maize, eggplant, and stone.

Ember, charcoal

Hand-knotted in our true 100-knot quality using Tibetan wool, Chinese silk, and natural nettle fibers. Ember features smokey tendrils that create wisps of movement across the rug's canvas in tones of black, grey, silver, platinum, and ivory.

Nimbus, silver

Hand-knotted in our true 100-knot quality using Tibetan wool and Chinese silk. Nimbus features crystallized, meandering lines of color and texture that traverse randomly, creating amorphous, billowing shapes. The silver, grey, platinum, and charcoal wool is accented by 100% pure silk that adds depth and shine from every perspective.
