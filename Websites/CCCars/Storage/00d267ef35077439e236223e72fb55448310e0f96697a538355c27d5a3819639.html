The 2014 Honda Fit G F package

### Engine and Performance:
- **Engine**: 1.3L 4-cylinder engine (i-VTEC)
- **Power Output**: Around 100 horsepower

### Interior Features:
- **Seats**: Fabric upholstery, with adjustable seats for optimal comfort
- **Dashboard**: Simple and user-friendly design with easy access to controls
- **Climate Control**: Manual air conditioning with a clean-air filter
- **Steering Wheel**: Tilt steering wheel, with audio and cruise controls (depending on model)
- **Storage**: Versatile cargo space with Magic Seat feature (foldable rear seats for larger items)

### Safety Features:
- **Airbags** for enhanced protection
- **ABS (Anti-lock Braking System)**
- **VSA (Vehicle Stability Assist)**: Helps maintain vehicle stability

### Other Features:
- **Keyless Entry**: Allows for easy access to the car without using a physical key
- **Power Windows & Locks**: Standard for convenience
