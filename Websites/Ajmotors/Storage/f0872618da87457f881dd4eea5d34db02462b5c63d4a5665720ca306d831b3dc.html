The 2020 Mitsubishi Triton is a robust and reliable pickup truck designed for both work and play. With its powerful engine, advanced safety features, and spacious interior, it excels in tough terrains while providing comfort for everyday driving. Explore the Triton at AJ Motors in Henderson!

# Key Features

- Bluetooth : Enables hands-free phone calls and audio streaming.

- Cruise Control : Maintains a constant speed for comfortable highway driving.

- Reversing Camera : Provides a view of the area behind the vehicle to assist in reversing safely.
