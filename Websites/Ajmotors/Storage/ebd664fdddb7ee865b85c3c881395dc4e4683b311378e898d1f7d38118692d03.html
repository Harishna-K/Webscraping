Station Wagon, Bluetooth, 8-Airbags

Great choice! The 2013 Toyota Prius Alpha in stunning red is the perfect blend of efficiency, practicality, and modern technology. With its fuel-saving 1800cc petrol-hybrid engine, youll enjoy outstanding fuel economy without sacrificing performance. This spacious hybrid wagon features 17-inch alloy wheels, fog lights, and a reversing camera, making it stylish and easy to maneuver. Plus, with 8 airbags and push-button start, safety and convenience are built right in. Whether you're commuting or heading on a road trip, the Prius Alpha is designed to go the distance in comfort.

Key Features:

1800cc Petrol-Hybrid Engine Exceptional fuel efficiency and smooth performance

17-Inch Alloy Wheels Sleek and stylish for a modern look

8 Airbags Superior safety for you and your passengers

Fog Lights Better visibility in tough conditions

Push-Button Start Convenient keyless entry and ignition

Reversing Camera Park and maneuver with confidence

Bluetooth Connectivity Hands-free calling and audio streaming

Hybrid Efficiency Low fuel costs and reduced emissions

Spacious Interior More room for passengers and cargo

Toyota Reliability A trusted name in hybrid technology
