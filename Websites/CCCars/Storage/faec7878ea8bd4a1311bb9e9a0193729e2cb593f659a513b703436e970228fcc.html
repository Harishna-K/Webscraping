The **2015 Mazda Demio**, known as the **Mazda2** in some markets, is a subcompact hatchback that offers a blend of practicality, style, and driving enjoyment. The **S trim** of the 2015 Mazda Demio is a higher-level variant, providing a good balance of features and performance.

### Engine and Performance:
- **Engine**: 1.5L SKYACTIV-G Inline-4 engine.
- **Transmission**: 6-speed automatic transmission.
- **Drive Type**: Front-wheel drive (FWD).
- **Suspension**: Independent suspension for both front and rear, offering good handling and ride comfort.

### Interior:
- **Seating Capacity**: 5-passenger seating
- **Upholstery**: Fabric upholstery
- **AM/FM radio** and optional **CD player**.
- **Air Conditioning**:,= Providing comfort in various weather conditions.

### Safety Features:
- **i-ACTIVSENSE Safety Features**
- **ABS (Anti-lock Braking System)**: Prevents wheel lockup during hard braking.
- **Electronic Stability Control (ESC)**: Helps prevent skidding and loss of control.
- **Traction Control System (TCS)**: Improves grip during acceleration, especially on slippery surfaces.
- **Dual Front Airbags**: For driver and front passenger protection in the event of a crash.

### Driving Features:
- **SKYACTIV Technology**: Mazda's fuel-efficient and performance-oriented engineering system that includes the engine, transmission, and chassis.
- **Sporty Handling**: Known for its fun-to-drive dynamics, the Demio offers excellent cornering and stability, making it one of the most enjoyable small hatchbacks to drive.
