The 2014 Nissan Note X-DIGS is a compact hatchback with a focus on efficiency and practicality.

### Interior Features:
- **Seating:** Comfortable seating for 5 passengers with cloth upholstery
- **Air Conditioning:** Standard air conditioning for a comfortable cabin environment
- **Cargo Space:** A relatively spacious boot for its class, with split-folding rear seats for more cargo flexibility

### Exterior Features:
- **Design:** Compact and practical design with a focus on fuel efficiency
- **Side Mirrors:** Electrically adjustable side mirrors

### Safety Features:
- **ABS (Anti-lock Braking System):** Standard for added braking control
- **EBD (Electronic Brakeforce Distribution):** For balanced braking performance
- **ESP (Electronic Stability Program):** Standard for improved stability on slippery surfaces
