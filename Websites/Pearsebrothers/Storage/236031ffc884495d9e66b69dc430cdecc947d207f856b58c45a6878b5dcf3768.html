NZ NEW, ONE OWNER 4WD

Rugged 4WD Capability: The 2019 Mitsubishi Triton VRX 4WD is built for adventure with its powerful 4WD system, delivering exceptional off-road performance to tackle any terrain, from rugged trails to tough work sites.

Powerful 2.4L Turbo Diesel Engine: Equipped with a reliable 2.4L turbo diesel engine, the Triton VRX offers impressive towing capacity and fuel efficiency, making it perfect for hauling heavy loads and long-distance driving.

Modern and Stylish Design: The bold, dynamic design of the Triton VRX includes sleek lines, a striking front grille, and a refined finish, offering a stylish presence both on and off the road.

Enhanced Comfort and Tech: Inside, the Triton VRX features leather upholstery, a touchscreen infotainment system with Apple CarPlay & Android Auto, and advanced climate control for a more comfortable and connected ride.

Loaded with Safety Features: Equipped with Mitsubishis advanced safety suite, including forward collision mitigation, lane departure warning, and rearview camera, to give you peace of mind wherever the road takes you.

Impressive Towing and Payload: With a high towing capacity and robust payload capabilities, the Triton VRX can handle all your heavy-duty tasks, from towing trailers to carrying tools or equipment.

Perfect for Work and Play: Whether you need a dependable workhorse or a reliable vehicle for outdoor adventures, the Triton VRX is designed to handle both with ease and style.

Customization Ready: The Triton VRX provides a solid base for further customization, allowing you to personalize it to fit your specific needs, whether its for utility or aesthetics.
