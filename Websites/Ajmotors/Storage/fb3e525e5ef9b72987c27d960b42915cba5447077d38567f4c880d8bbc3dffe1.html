# 2012 Toyota Prius A G
{Stock number. 24481}

The 2012 Toyota Prius A Gv appears to be a trim level variation of the Toyota Prius A model, likely intended for the Japanese domestic market (JDM). The "Gv" designation may refer to specific features or an even more base-oriented or specialized version of the Prius. However, Toyota sometimes uses these kinds of designations for different markets or specific models and options, especially in Japan.

# Key features
- Cruise control: Cruise control in the 2012 Toyota Prius allows the driver to set a constant speed without having to keep their foot on the accelerator pedal.

- Multi function steering wheel:
The multi-function steering wheel in the 2012 Toyota Prius (including the A Gv trim) is designed to provide the driver with convenient access to a variety of controls without needing to take their hands off the wheel. It combines several essential functions for ease of use and to enhance safety, allowing drivers to manage many aspects of their vehicle without distracting them from the road.

- Push button start: The Push Button Start feature in the 2012 Toyota Prius A Gv (and other Prius models from that year) is part of the vehicle's keyless entry and ignition system, designed to enhance convenience and ease of use.
