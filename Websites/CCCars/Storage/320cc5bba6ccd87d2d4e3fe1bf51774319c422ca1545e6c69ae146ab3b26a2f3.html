The 2020 Toyota Yaris 1.3GX is a compact hatchback that combines practicality with essential features

**Performance:**
- **Engine:** Equipped with a 1.3-litre petrol four-cylinder engine
- **Transmission:** 4-speed automatic transmission with front-wheel drive.

**Dimensions:**
- **Length:** 3,905 mm
- **Height:** 1,510 mm
- **Luggage Capacity:** 347 litres.

**Standard Features:**
- **Air Conditioning:** Manual climate control.
- **Bluetooth Connectivity:**
- **Cruise Control:**
- **Steering Wheel:** Tilt and telescopic adjustment.
- **Rearview Camera:**
- **Seat Adjustments:** Driver's seat height adjustment; 60/40 split-folding rear seats.
- **Safety Features:** Eight airbags, including driver, front passenger, side, and curtain shield airbags.

**Lighting and Mirrors:**
- **Headlamps:** Projector-type headlamps.
- **Fog Lights:** Front fog lights.
- **Side Mirrors:** Electrically adjustable with turn indicators

The 2020 Toyota Yaris 1.3GX offers a blend of essential features suitable for urban commuting and daily use, emphasizing safety, comfort, and practicality.
