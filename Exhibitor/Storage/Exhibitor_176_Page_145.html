Sarreid Ltd

Add to MyMarket

Sarreid Ltd was founded in 1967 and offers its customers a line of furniture and accessories that are known for their distinctive, unique blend of old-world tradition and today's designs. Sarreid Ltd is known as an innovative leader in the home furnishings industry dedicated to achieving excellence by providing beautiful products and unparalleled service. Our goal is to develop and maintain long-term partnerships with our customers.
