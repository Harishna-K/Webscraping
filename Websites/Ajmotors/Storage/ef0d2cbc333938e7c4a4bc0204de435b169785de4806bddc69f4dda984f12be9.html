# 2015 Mazda Axela Sport 20S Touring
{Stock number. 23801}

DescriptionThe 2015 Mazda Axela Sport 20S Touring is a sporty and stylish compact hatchback that is part of the Mazda3 lineup, known for its engaging driving dynamics and sleek design.

# Key features
- Heads up display: The heads-up display (HUD) in the 2015 Mazda Axela Sport 20S Touring is one of the car's notable technological features, contributing to both safety and convenience.

- Paddle gear shifter: The paddle gear shifter in the 2015 Mazda Axela Sport 20S Touring is part of the vehicle's 6-speed automatic transmission and is designed to offer a more engaging and responsive driving experience.

- Cruise control: The cruise control system in the 2015 Mazda Axela Sport 20S Touring is designed to enhance comfort and convenience, particularly on long drives or highway journeys. It allows you to maintain a constant speed without needing to keep your foot on the accelerator pedal.
