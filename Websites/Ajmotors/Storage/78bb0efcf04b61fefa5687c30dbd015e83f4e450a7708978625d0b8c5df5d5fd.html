# 2020 Mitsubishi Triton GLX (NZ New)
{Stock number. 23670}

The 2020 Mitsubishi Triton GLX (NZ New) is a versatile utility vehicle with a 2.4-litre turbo diesel engine, providing a solid mix of power and efficiency.

# Key features
- Apple car play: allows you to connect your iPhone to your vehicle's infotainment system, providing access to apps and services directly through the car's display.

- All terrain tires: All-terrain tires are designed to provide a balance between on-road comfort and off-road capability.

- Cruise control: The driver sets a desired speed, and the system takes over throttle control to maintain that speed.

If you're looking for more information about the car, feel free to reach out! Whether you have questions about specific features or need assistance with anything else, our team is happy to help.
