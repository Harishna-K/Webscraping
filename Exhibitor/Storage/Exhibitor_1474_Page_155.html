Stickley

Add to MyMarket

Stickley is a genuine American icon, a brand that has set the standard for furniture craft for more than 120 years. Enduring tradition, superior craftsmanship, and an unshakeable philosophy of excellence are the reasons Stickley continues to produce America’s finest hardwood furniture and upholstery, all while responding to the needs and trends of today’s homes with updated styles and modern collections. Stickley’s Mission pieces are among the most recognizable and cherished in the history of American design. Beginning in 1900 with his revolutionary Arts and Crafts collection, Gustav Stickley showed that every home deserves furniture that is solid and honest, made with uncompromising craftsmanship and satisfying a real need. Gustav’s brother Leopold founded L. & J.G. Stickley, where for sixty years he established the company’s reputation for elite craftsmanship. In 1974, years after Leopold Stickley’s death, Alfred and Aminy Audi purchased L. & J.G. Stickley, determined to honor Stickley’s dedication to quality. By reissuing the Mission collection in 1989, the Audis tapped into a nearly forgotten era of American furniture history and helped to shape a renaissance of Arts and Crafts. Today, the company’s product offering is as stylistically varied and dynamic as it has ever been. Yet what makes each collection stand apart is the same commitment to honest materials and unparalleled craftsmanship that has always been the Stickley hallmark.

Hudson Valley Spindle Bed

Inspired by Americana, the Hudson Valley Spindle Bed feels fresh and contemporary thanks to the contrast between its Dark Roast walnut finish and hand-turned spindles in Natural Maple. An exaggerated cap rail sits on softened square posts, and the entire headboard displays a subtle bow.

Hudson Valley Round Side Table

The Hudson Valley Round Side Table’s distinctive top is inlaid with bold, quilt-inspired marquetry in walnut, maple, and black. It rests on a three-legged base with a unique triangular stretcher shelf. Crafted of American black walnut, this versatile piece is beautiful alongside a sofa, chair, or bed.

Hudson Valley Settee

The Hudson Valley Settee employs subtle details and a trim profile to bring traditional style into the present. Modest in scale, it features sloped arms, tight weltless upholstery, an elegantly shaped back, and tall, radiused legs—all combining to produce a comfortable accent full of personality.
