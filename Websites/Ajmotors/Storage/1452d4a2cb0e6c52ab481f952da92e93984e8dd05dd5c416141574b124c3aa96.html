# 2019 Suzuki Swift Hybrid RS
{Stock number. 24394}

The 2019 Suzuki Swift Hybrid RS is a compact, fuel-efficient, and environmentally friendly version of the popular Suzuki Swift. It's part of Suzuki's hybrid line-up and brings a blend of efficiency, performance, and practicality.

# Key features
- LDW: If the system detects that the vehicle is unintentionally drifting out of its lane without the use of turn signals, it will alert the driver.

- Pre crush safety: Pre-Crash Safety (also known as Pre-Collision System or PCS) is an advanced driver assistance system (ADAS) designed to detect potential collisions before they occur and take action to either mitigate or prevent the crash.

- Cruise control: Cruise Control is a driving aid that allows a vehicle to maintain a constant speed without the driver needing to keep their foot on the accelerator.
