# 2015 Nissan Juke 15RX
{Stock number. 24368}

The 2015 Nissan Juke 15RX offers a distinctive blend of sporty styling, compact size, and efficiency. While it may not have the most spacious interior, it appeals to those looking for a fun-to-drive urban crossover with unique looks and good fuel economy. Its especially popular among those who want something different from the typical SUV offerings on the market.

# Key features
- Key less entry: The system works through a small key fob that contains a transponder chip. The fob must be within close proximity to the car for the system to recognize it.

- Automatic side mirrors: For automatic folding side mirrors (which automatically fold when locking the car or when it's in a tight space), you typically have to look at higher-end trims or specific features not available on the 15RX trim of the 2015 Nissan Juke.

- Auto start stop: This helps reduce fuel consumption, emissions, and engine idling time, particularly in city driving with frequent stops.
