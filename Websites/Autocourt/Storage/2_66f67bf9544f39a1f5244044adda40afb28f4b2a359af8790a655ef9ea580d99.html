AS NEW!
Latest model Toyota Hiace Commuter, ex Japanese dealer's stock, first registered in Japan in 2019. Has travelled only 418 kilometres.
It is powered by the 1GD-FTV 2.8 litre intercooled twin cam turbo/diesel engine which develops 110kW/148hp, with 6-speed automatic transmission.
This is a high spec Commuter, with 12/14 seats* (enquire for more information), cloth upholstery, lap & diagonal seat belts, climate control, rear heater, individual air vents , privacy glass, fully lined, with easily cleaned floor covering.
Many of these features are not available in the NZ new Hiace 12-seater Minibus.
It comes with power-operated LH side door, driver's airbag, ABS brakes with Electronic Brake Distribution, electric front windows, central locking, electronic stability control/traction control, and a rear-view camera.
Can be used for a multitude of reasons; passenger transport, taxi, club, or even a large family.
A great opportunity to purchase a near-new Minibus, (Coach), at a great price.
