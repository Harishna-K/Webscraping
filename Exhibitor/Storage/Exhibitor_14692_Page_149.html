Showrooms 2220

Add to MyMarket

Showrooms at 2220 is one of the largest antique and design centers in the United States housing prestigious domestic and international antique dealers as well as design professionals in more than 100,000 square feet of renovated industrial space. Interior design professionals can shop from the comfort of their offices through showrooms2220.com or visit the expansive gallery located in Philadelphia.

Italian Oak Three Section Bench Circa 1960

Great Italian oak three-section bench circa 1960. This architectural bench has a great design and is very interesting when viewed from behind, so it would work very well floating in the center of a room. 

Blue Ripple Glass and Resin Geometric Form Mirror in the Manner of Line Vautrin

A blue ripple glass and resin geometric form mirror in the manner of Line Vautrin. This mirror is hand made by an artist using vintage glass, so it is one of a kind.

A Three Piece French Oak Cabinet in the Manner of Charles Dudouyt C 1940

A three-piece French oak cabinet in the manner of Charles Dudouyt C 1940. Brutalist styling is used in the relief design of the front cabinet doors.
