Brand new tyres - 8 Airbags - Amazing Value

Here we have the economical and stylish Toyota Corolla Fielder Aero Tourer these wagons are very popular due to their stylish exterior and supreme economy along with a good dose of proven reliability.
equipped with 8 airbags, Climate control air conditioning, ISOFIX Child seat anchors, UV Privacy glass, 2 Smart keys with Button start, Factory aero, Factory alloys with brand new tyres, Traction Control and so much more!
Looks sharp finished in Blizzard White Pearl and has a stylish Black interior with lightly textured patterned seat inserts also features a 1500cc engine with Hybrid Synergy Drive and an automatic transmission giving great fuel efficiency, also has a 5 star VSRR safety rating.

Finance & Protecta Insurance products are also available for this vehicle.

Pre-purchase inspection can be arranged for out of town purchasers.

You can buy with confidence from Value Cars in the knowledge we are a totally New Zealand owned & funded provider of good quality cars & great after sales service for over 30 years.
