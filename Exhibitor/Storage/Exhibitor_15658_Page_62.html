Franco Ferri Italia

Add to MyMarket

A story of passion, craftsmanship and design that begins in 1956 with Rocco Ferri senior and continues nowadays with Franco Ferri and his son Rocco and daughter Paola in their third generation. Projecting itself towards new and exciting challenges, the brand has always remained faithful to its philosophy: to use and develop precious craftsmanship techniques, high manual skills, combined with new technologies and materials of great value to respond to the demands of modernity and innovation. The challenge of interpreting contemporary design with a recognizable identity, without ever forgetting its roots has been the task of our company. This is how Franco Ferri Italia has become, over the years, synonymous with that all-Italian artisan knowledge applied to upholstery to ensure comfort and value. Franco Ferri Italia is a brand that has always been capable to anticipate the changes in taste, ways of living creating a modern design that is destined to last over time. Technology, international language and at the same time attention to details and finishes reserved for the single piece, all contribute to the search for maximum comfort and aesthetic value. This is the winning key that explains Franco Ferri Italia’s success: Ongoing know-how enhancement, use of the best raw materials, openness to new technologies, beauty and functionality in order to always offer the essence and excellence of an all-Italian design.

Scervino

One of the most iconic models of Franco Ferri Italia. Rapidly become a best seller everywhere for its exclusive design and unparalleled comfort, SCERVINO is a modular seating system that can be customized according to customers' needs.

Hollywood

Model Hollywood by Franco Ferri Italia belongs to the Franco Ferri Italia Collection 2024 / 2025. It comes as a corner configuration with adjustable backrests and feather filling in the armrests, quilted seat and back cushions and metal base. Hollywood is one of the most successful models from the new Collection Franco Ferri Italia 2024/2025.

Model Brando

A new iconic model from the Franco Ferri Collection is born! BRANDO is an open base model with metal legs and quilted back. Loose back cushionsmake the model Brando even more comfortable. Recently presented during Salone del Mobile 2024 in Milan, this new design has been positively welcomed from the International audience.
