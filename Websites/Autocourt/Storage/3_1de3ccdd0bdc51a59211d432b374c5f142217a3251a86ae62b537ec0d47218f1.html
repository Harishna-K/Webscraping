This is an impressive looking Mazda CX-5 in wine red, with 19 inch alloy wheels and rear spoiler.
It has a SKYACTIV 2.2 litre turbo/diesel engine with i-Stop technology, coupled to a 6-speed automatic transmission, which delivers exhilarating performance with 6.3l/100km fuel economy.
The CX-5 has travelled 55,500 kilometres.
Safety is looked after with 6 airbags, ABS with electronic brake distribution, electronic stability/traction control, and rear vehicle monitoring, all adding up to a 4-star UCSR safety rating.
It comes with climate control, cruise control, smart key starting/locking, and a rear-view camera in the mirror.
Altogether a very smart SUV type vehicle with 2 wheel drive for those who don't need 4WD.
