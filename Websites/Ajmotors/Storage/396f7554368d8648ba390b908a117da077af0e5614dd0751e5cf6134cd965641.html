# 2013 Hyundai Veloster 1.6 DCT
{Stock number. 24930}

The 2013 Hyundai Veloster 1.6 DCT (Dual Clutch Transmission) is a unique and sporty compact hatchback that stands out due to its asymmetrical three-door design, with one conventional door on the drivers side and two smaller ones on the passenger side. This model represents Hyundai's attempt to blend style, performance, and practicality.

# Key features
- Push button start:
The push-button start feature in cars like the 2013 Hyundai Veloster 1.6 DCT offers a convenient and modern way to start the vehicle without needing to insert a traditional key into the ignition.

- Sunroof: A sunroof is a popular automotive feature that provides an extra touch of openness and natural light to the car's interior. In the case of the 2013 Hyundai Veloster, it comes with an optional panoramic sunroof (depending on the trim level).

- Cruise control: Cruise control is a convenience feature in modern vehicles, including the 2013 Hyundai Veloster, that allows the driver to set and maintain a constant speed without having to keep their foot on the accelerator pedal. This is especially useful during long highway drives or on stretches of road where you don't need to accelerate or brake constantly.
