Plug in Hybrid - 8 Airbags - Heated Seats

Here we have a superb looking Plug-in Hybrid Toyota Prius, These are excellent urban vehicles because of their ability to run full EV over short trips and the comfort of having the hybrid backup, so no range anxiety here!
Equipped with 8 airbags, Climate Control air conditioning, Heated front seats, ISOFIX Child seat anchors, Indicator Mirrors, Heated Steering wheel, Trip computer, Shoulder belts for all passengers and so much more!
Looks very smart finished in Black Cherry Pearl Metallic complimented by Silver accents on the exterior with chrome look detailing, driving lights and on 17 inch alloys.
The interior has a stylish Black suede look with Blue seat inserts complimented by woodgrain style trim. Features an 1800cc Hybrid Synergy Drive engine, With a Plug-in charging system and a Automatic transmission giving great fuel efficiency, and also has a 5 star UCSR safety rating.
Finance & Protecta Insurance products are also available for this vehicle.

Pre-purchase inspection can be arranged for out of town purchasers.

You can buy with confidence from Value Cars in the knowledge we are a totally New Zealand owned & funded provider of good quality cars & great after sales service for over 30 years.
