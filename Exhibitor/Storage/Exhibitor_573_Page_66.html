Global Views & Studio A Home - High Point Market April 2024

Enjoy walking through our showroom where you'll see hundreds of new introductions.

Silk Pillows-Olive

Our Silk Pillows are handcrafted from 100% olive green satin with a delicate ruched design. Featuring a luxurious down-fill insert, they add a touch of sophistication and comfort to any space.

Lion & Lioness Sculptures-Brass

Enhance your decor with our handcrafted Lion & Lioness Sculptures. Cast in brass with a polished finish and elegantly mounted on a crystal base, these statues bring a touch of regal charm and strength to any setting.

Pleated Bowls-Olive Green

Handmade in the Murano region, these dark olive green glass pieces feature a luxurious mother-of-pearl finish. Crafted using a unique centrifuge technique, each piece is shaped by hand and carefully annealed to ensure durability and prevent cracking. This meticulous process enhances both the beauty and resilience of the glass.
