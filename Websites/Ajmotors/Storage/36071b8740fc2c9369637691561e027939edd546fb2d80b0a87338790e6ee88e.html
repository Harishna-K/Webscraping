# 2018 Honda Vezel HR-V Hybrid Z
{Stock number. 23234}

The 2018 Honda Vezel (also known as the HR-V in some markets) Hybrid Z is a compact crossover SUV that combines practicality with fuel efficiency.

# Key features
- Electric hand brake: Instead of a traditional lever, the EPB is engaged and disengaged with a button, typically located on the centre console.

- Multi function steering wheel: Helps keep your hands on the wheel while accessing essential functions, enhancing safety. Streamlines operation of key features, making it easier to focus on driving.

- Lane departure assist: If the vehicle begins to drift out of its lane without signalling, LDA will provide visual and audible alerts to warn the driver.

If you're looking for more information about the car, feel free to reach out! Whether you have questions about specific features or need assistance with anything else, our team is happy to help.
