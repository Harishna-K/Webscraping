"Tradiotional with a twist' For over a quarter of a century, we have proudly specialized in the design, production, and supply of exceptional eclectic furniture and accessories. Our founder, Steven Shell, brings over 40 years of experience in the furniture industry and has been the visionary behind the unique and desirable product concepts that set our pieces apart. Thanks to his expertise, our furniture has found a place in homes across 22 countries, with more than 750 items being shipped each week. Our commitment to excellence is showcased in our permanent display space in High Point, North Carolina and at the Atlanta gift mart. Our business philosophy is refreshingly simple: give customers everything. We pour our heart and soul into ensuring that every piece we create embodies quality, style, and functionality. INFLUENCES The inspiration for our furnishings comes from a deeply personal and honest place, rooted in the rich and diverse experiences of our founder, London born Steven Shell. His global travels throughout Europe and the East, coupled with his love for eclectic locales in London such as Shoreditch and Brick Lane, imbue our designs with a worldly charm. Additionally, influences from iconic periods like 40’s Hollywood, 50’s Americana, 50’s Côte d’Azur, Victorian, and Gothic design are seamlessly woven into our eclectic collection.

76789 The Farringdon Orangery Cabinet: Tradition Meets Modern Elegance

The Farringdon Orangery cabinet combines timeless elegance with modern functionality. Inspired by Palladian window designs from South France, it features gracefully curved top drawers and solid brass handles. This sophisticated piece merges luxury with practicality, making it a standout in the Farringdon collection.

76785 Astoria Bar: A Modern Marvel with Art Deco Elegance

The Astoria Bar, inspired by Art Deco, blends opulence with functionality. This exquisite piece serves as a stunning centerpiece, offering ample storage for bar essentials and accessories. Available in various finishes, it makes a perfect statement in any room.

76796 Lutyens Dining Chair

The Lutyens dining chair combines the iconic English Arts and Crafts style with modern elegance. Made from solid mahogany, it features a sculpted wooden seat that blends classic design with comfort. This chair adds timeless charm and sophistication to any dining space.
