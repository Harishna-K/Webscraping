DIESEL, MANUAL, REFRIDGERATED, NO DEPOSIT FINANCE

Take your business to the next level with this versatile 2014 Isuzu Elf. This truck is perfect for transporting temperature-sensitive goods, with a reliable box body design and integrated refrigeration unit.

Engine: Powered by a robust 3.0L diesel engine, delivering great fuel efficiency and long-lasting performance.

Transmission: Equipped with a manual transmission, giving you full control for smooth and responsive handling, especially during heavy-duty tasks.

Refrigeration Unit: The built-in refrigerator is perfect for keeping perishable items cool during transport, ensuring that your goods arrive in perfect condition.

This 2014 Isuzu Elf is a perfect combination of efficiency, durability, and practicality. Whether for food distribution or any other temperature-sensitive delivery needs, it is an excellent addition to any fleet. Take advantage of this opportunityschedule a test drive today!
