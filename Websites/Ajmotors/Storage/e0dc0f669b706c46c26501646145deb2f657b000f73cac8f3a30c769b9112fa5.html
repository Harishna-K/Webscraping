# 2012 Toyota Estima AERAS Leather Package
{Stock number. 23830}

The 2012 Toyota Estima AERAS Leather Package is a mid-size multi-purpose vehicle (MPV) produced by Toyota, popular in markets like Japan and some other parts of Asia and Oceania. It's known for its spacious interior, comfort, and practicality, often seen as an excellent family car or a vehicle for those who need a versatile ride for both urban and long-distance driving.

# Key features
- Power sliding door: The power sliding doors on the 2012 Toyota Estima AERAS Leather Package are one of the standout features, adding convenience and ease of use, especially in tight parking spaces or when you have your hands full.

- Push start button: The push button start system in the 2012 Toyota Estima AERAS Leather Package adds a level of convenience and modernity to the driving experience.

- Cruise control: The cruise control system in the 2012 Toyota Estima AERAS Leather Package is a convenient feature designed to make long-distance driving easier and more comfortable. It helps maintain a constant speed without needing to keep your foot on the accelerator pedal, allowing you to focus more on steering and road conditions.
