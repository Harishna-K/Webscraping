# 2018 Subaru Impreza 2.0L AWD Eyesight
{Stock number. 23711}

2018 Subaru Impreza 2.0L AWD with EyeSight: A compact car that blends all-wheel-drive versatility, advanced safety features, and modern tech for a confident and comfortable driving experience.

# Key features
- Eyesight: Subaru EyeSight is an advanced driver-assist technology system that enhances safety by using cameras and sensors to monitor the road ahead. It is available on most Subaru models, including the 2018 Subaru Impreza, and plays a significant role in accident prevention and overall driving convenience.

- LDW: Lane Departure Warning uses cameras mounted on the windshield to monitor lane markings on the road. If the vehicle begins to drift out of its lane without the turn signal being activated, the system will alert the driver with a visual or audible warning.

- ACC: ACC functions similarly to traditional cruise control by maintaining a set speed. However, unlike regular cruise control, Adaptive Cruise Control automatically adjusts the vehicle's speed to maintain a safe following distance from the vehicle ahead.
