Sherrill Furniture Company

Add to MyMarket

WELCOME TO THE WORLD OF SHERRILL We have something for every style, room, budget, and color palette! Whether you’re looking for just one piece, furniture for a whole room, or an entire house, the Sherrill family of brands has you covered. At Sherrill, we blend local handmade craftsmanship from the heart of western North Carolina with modern manufacturing techniques to create high-end, quality furniture full of originality. The Sherrill family of brands consists of Sherrill Furniture, Hickory White, Precedent, Sherrill Occasional, Whittemore-Sherrill leather, and Motioncraft. We also have three designer collections featuring furniture inspired and curated by published, award-winning designers. Mr. & Mrs. Howard for Sherrill Furniture features Jim and Phoebe Howard’s calming, beautiful designs. Lillian August for Hickory White showcases Lillian’s mastery of color, drama and style. Excited to see you there!
