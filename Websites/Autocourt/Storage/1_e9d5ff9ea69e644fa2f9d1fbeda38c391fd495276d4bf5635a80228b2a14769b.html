Neat 2011 Nissan Tiida 5-door hatchback in gold, that has travelled only 54,000 kilometres.
The Tiida has exceptionally easy access and provides a roomy interior. The 1500cc engine (timing chain) combined with smooth CVT transmission delivers good performance and 6.3L/100km fuel economy rating.
It comes with part leather trim, climate control, CD player, airbags, ABS etc, and a 3-star safety rating.
