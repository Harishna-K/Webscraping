Low KMs - Dual Airbags - Reverse Camera

Here we have this outstanding Toyota Aqua Crossover these Hybrid 5 door hatches are taller for better vision, Ground clearance and have full factory aero to complete their exterior look.

Really stands out and looks sharp finished in Classic Silver Metallic and is equipped with Dual airbags, Reverse Camera, ECO/EV Modes, UV Privacy glass, ISOFIX, Roof rails, Factory 16 inch alloys and more!
This Aqua has a Black combination leather interior trim with textured patterned seat inserts complimented by exposed stitching. Features Toyota's 1500cc DOHC engine with generation 3 Hybrid Synergy Drive with an automatic transmission giving good fuel efficiency, also has a four star UCSR safety rating.

Buyers considering the likes of Audi A1, Ford Fiesta, Honda Grace, Jazz, Mazda3 Hybrid, MG HS, ZS, Nissan Note E-Power, Suzuki Ignis, Toyota Prius or similar small CC engine hybrid or EV hatch probably should take a closer look at the ever popular Toyota Aqua.

Finance & Protecta Insurance products are also available for this vehicle.

Pre-purchase inspection can be arranged for out of town purchasers.

You can buy with confidence from Value Cars in the knowledge we are a totally New Zealand owned & funded provider of good quality cars & great after sales service for over 30 years.
