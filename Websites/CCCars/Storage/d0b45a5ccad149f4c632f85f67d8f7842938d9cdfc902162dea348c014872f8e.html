The 2013 Mazda CX-5 20S is a compact SUV known for its sporty handling and fuel efficiency.

### Engine and Performance
- **Engine:** 2.0-liter Skyactiv- four-cylinder
- **Power Output:** Approximately 155 horsepower
- **Transmission:** 6-speed automatic transmission
- **Drivetrain:** Front-wheel drive (with optional all-wheel drive)


### Interior Features
- **Seating Capacity:** 5 passengers
- **Upholstery:** Cloth upholstery
- **Climate Control:** Manual air conditioning with available dual-zone climate control

### Safety Features
- **Standard Safety Equipment:** Antilock brakes, stability control, traction control, and multiple airbags

### Cargo Space
- **Rear Cargo Volume:** Approximately 34.1 cubic feet (expandable with rear seats folded)

This model is appreciated for its engaging driving dynamics and practicality, making it a solid choice in the compact SUV segment.
