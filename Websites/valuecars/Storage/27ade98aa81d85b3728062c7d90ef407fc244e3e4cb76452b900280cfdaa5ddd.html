8 Airbags - Economical - 5 Star Safety Rating

Here we have another fantastic example of the economical and spacious Toyota Prius S.
Optioned with 8 Airbags, Stability programme, Climate control air conditioning, Proximity key with Push button start, ISOFIX Child seat anchors, 5 Shoulder belts, Rear Spoiler, Multi function steering wheel and more!
Looks very smart finished in Black Onyx with rear spoiler on 15 inch Black alloys with wheel covers, and has a stylish Black suede look interior with perforated look seat inserts. Features an 1800cc Hybrid Synergy Drive engine and automatic transmission with good fuel efficiency and also has a 5 star UCSR safety rating.
Finance & Protecta Insurance products are also available for this vehicle.

Pre-purchase inspection can be arranged for out of town purchasers.

You can buy with confidence from Value Cars in the knowledge we are a totally New Zealand owned & funded provider of good quality cars & great after sales service for over 30 years.
