# 2014 Toyota Land Cruiser Prado TX
{Stock number. 24910}


The 2014 Toyota Land Cruiser Prado TX is a mid-size 4x4 SUV that combines luxury with off-road capabilities. Its a part of the Land Cruiser Prado lineup, known for its ruggedness and durability, while also offering a comfortable and well-equipped interior.

# Key features
- Sunroof: A sunroof is a panel on a vehicle's roof that can be opened or removed to allow fresh air and light into the cabin. Its typically made of glass, although it can also be a metal panel in some cases. Sunroofs come in different types, and they add both aesthetic appeal and functionality to a vehicle.

- Cruise control: Cruise control is a system in vehicles that allows the driver to set and maintain a constant speed without having to keep their foot on the accelerator pedal.

- Push button start: A push-button start (also known as keyless start or smart start) is a feature in modern vehicles that allows the driver to start the engine with the push of a button, rather than using a traditional key. This system offers convenience, security, and a more seamless driving experience.
