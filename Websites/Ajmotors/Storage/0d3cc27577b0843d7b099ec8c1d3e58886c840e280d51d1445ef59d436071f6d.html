# 2018 Toyota Prius S Safety Plus
{Stock number. 24371}

This model is a solid option if you prioritize fuel economy, reliability, and advanced safety features in a compact hybrid sedan.

# Key features
- Pre-Collision System: Detects vehicles in front and can automatically apply the brakes to reduce the risk of a collision.

-Lane Departure Alert with Steering Assist: Helps to prevent unintended lane departures by providing steering assistance.

- Dynamic Radar Cruise Control: Adjusts the speed to maintain a safe distance from the vehicle in front.

If you're looking for more information about the car, feel free to reach out! Whether you have questions about specific features or need assistance with anything else, our team is happy to help.
