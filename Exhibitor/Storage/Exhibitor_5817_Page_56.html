EurDecor

Add to MyMarket

EurDecor is a collection of hand-made European accessories, ideal for adding that special something to any interior or exterior. We carry DutZ mouth-blown glass, all of which is hand-crafted in Europe. EurDecor will also be showing Ecri potters-wheel-designed ceramics. These pieces are all imported from Holland and are available in luster finishes including real gold, platinum, and ruby as well as our standard finishes. EurDecor offers something for everyone and is a sight to behold. A must see during your trip to the High Point Market!
