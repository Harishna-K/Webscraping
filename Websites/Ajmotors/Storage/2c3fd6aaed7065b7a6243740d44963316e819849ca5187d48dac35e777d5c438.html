New Tyres, Sport, Bluetooth, 6-Airbags

Great choice! The 2015 Mazda Demio in vibrant blue is a stylish, compact hatchback thats perfect for city driving and beyond. With its 15-inch alloy wheels, sporty design, and fuel-efficient performance, this Demio offers both practicality and fun behind the wheel. Inside, the black and blue interior adds a modern touch, while smart features like push-button start, Bluetooth connectivity, and a reversing camera enhance convenience. Plus, with six airbags and i-Stop technology, you get both safety and efficiency in one sleek package.

Key Features:

Fuel-Efficient Petrol Engine Great performance with impressive economy

15-Inch Alloy Wheels Sporty and stylish design

6 Airbags Enhanced safety for driver and passengers

i-Stop Technology Improves fuel efficiency by reducing idling time

Sport Mode Extra power when you need it

Reversing Camera Makes parking a breeze

Bluetooth Connectivity Hands-free calling and music streaming

Push-Button Start Convenient keyless entry and ignition

Black & Blue Interior A unique and stylish cabin

Compact Hatchback Design Easy to maneuver, perfect for urban driving
