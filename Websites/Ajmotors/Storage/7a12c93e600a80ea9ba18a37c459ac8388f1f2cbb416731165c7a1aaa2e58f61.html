Hybrid Family 7 Seats, 9-Airbags, Sliding Doors

Discover the perfect combination of comfort, space, and efficiency with this 2014 Black Toyota Noah! Designed with families in mind, this 7-seater hybrid offers a smooth and eco-friendly drive with features like EV and Eco mode, sliding back doors, and dual climate control. Add a touch of luxury with a cream interior and front heated seats for a truly enjoyable ride.

Key Features:

Stylish black exterior paired with 15-inch alloy wheels.

Spacious 7-seater layoutperfect for families or group travel.

Hybrid petrol engine with EV and Eco mode for improved fuel
efficiency.

9 airbags for enhanced safety for all passengers.

Push-button start for modern convenience.

Sliding back doors for easy entry and exit.

Cream interior with front heated seats for comfort and elegance.

Reversing camera for stress-free parking and maneuvering.

Cruise control for effortless long-distance drives.

Dual climate control to keep all passengers comfortable.
