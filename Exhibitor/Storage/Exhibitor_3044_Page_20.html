Belle Meade Signature

Add to MyMarket

Luxury casegoods featuring bedroom, dining room & occasional pieces. All of our product line is finished in High Point at our custom finishing facility. We offer 30 hand-rubbed finishes on our entire product line at no upcharge. Our Finishes. Your Signature.

Charlotte Acrylic Bed

The Charlotte acrylic bed features acrylic posts with brushed nickel metal accents, and satin brass accents are available upon request. It is available in all 30 BMS fabrics or COM. The Charlotte is offered in a king or queen size with a lead time of approximately eight weeks. 

Patrick Bunching Tables

The Patrick bunching tables are a fun design for any modern living space. The three tables may be purchased as a group (small, medium, large) or you can break them up and create any combination you desire. They are available in all 30 BMS custom finishes allowing you to create the perfect design for any style room. 6-8 week lead time.

Silas Extension Dining Table

The Silas extension dining table features a combination of Quartered White Oak Solids/Veneers and a Polished Stainless Steel Upright (satin brass upon request). It offers a 24" leaf, which allows for the full extension to 120". Available in all 30 BMS finish options.
