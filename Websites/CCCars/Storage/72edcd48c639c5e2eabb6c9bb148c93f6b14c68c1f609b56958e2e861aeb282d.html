The 2018 Mazda CX-3 20S Proactiv is a well-equipped version of the compact SUV, offering a combination of performance, technology, and safety features.

### **Exterior Features**
- **18-inch Alloy Wheels:** Stylish and sporty design
- **LED Headlights:** Standard LED headlights with signature lighting
-- **Power Folding Side Mirrors:** Provides convenience when parking or in tight spaces

### **Interior Features**
- 7-inch touchscreen display
- **Audio System:** 6-speaker sound system
- **Dual-zone Automatic Climate Control:** For added comfort in different weather conditions
- **Automatic Rain-sensing Wipers:** Activated when moisture is detected on the windshield

### **Safety Features**
- **i-Activsense Safety Technology:**
- **Blind Spot Monitoring (BSM)**
- **Rear Cross Traffic Alert (RCTA)**
- **Lane Departure Warning (LDW)**
Support**
- **Traction Control System (TCS)**
- **Electronic Stability Control (ESC)**
- **Rearview Camera:** Standard for easier parking and reversing
### **Comfort and Convenience**
- **Electric Parking Brake (EPB):** For smoother operation and more space in the cabin
-- **Power windows with auto-up/down function**

### **Additional Features**
- **Advanced Keyless Entry:** Allows for easy access to the vehicle without needing to remove the key fob from your pocket or bag
- **Push-Button Start:** For quick and convenient ignition
- **Split-folding Rear Seats:** For enhanced cargo space when needed

Overall, the 2018 Mazda CX-3 20S Proactiv offers a nice balance of sporty handling, stylish design, and a good suite of modern technology and safety features, making it an attractive option in the compact crossover segment.
