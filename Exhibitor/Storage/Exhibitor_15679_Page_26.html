Brass Hardware

Add to MyMarket

We are a one-stop-shop for custom, modular brass shelving, hardware, and home decor. Our lines of modular shelving units have been an internet success, spreading our reach across the US and globally and we are just getting started! We are excited to take this local Savannah business and expand into the parts of your home where function meets design with existing products and the launch of new products. Our goal is to continue to grow our product lines until every space in your home or business can be a home for one of our products.

The Origin - French Bistro Style Shelving Unit

The Origin is our classic French bistro-style shelving unit, which offers a wide variety of configurations to suit your needs. Whether bound for the bar or basking in books, these brass shelves can support your every endeavor.

Cantilever Shelving Unit Customizer

There is elegance in simplicity, and our Cantilever Shelving Units are just that. A true classic, this cantilever-style bistro shelving extends its arms to receive the items you cherish. The cantilever arms are made to mount to a 1" diameter tube and can hold a 10" deep shelf. 

Tipping Rail

Just because you like a buzz, that doesn’t mean your bottles need to get tipsy, too. Keep them contained with a custom Tipping Rail. This fiddler rail system adds functionality and beauty to your shelving and counter space.
