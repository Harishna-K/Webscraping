NZ NEW 20INCH MAGS

Reliable Performance: Powered by a 2.8L turbo diesel engine, delivering 130kW of power and 450Nm of torqueperfect for tough terrains and heavy-duty tasks.

Tough and Rugged Design: Built for both off-road adventures and urban commutes, with a durable and stylish exterior.

Spacious Interior: Comfortably seats five with premium finishes and features like leather upholstery, a touchscreen infotainment system, and dual-zone climate control.

Advanced Safety Features: Equipped with Toyota Safety Sense, including Lane Departure Alert, Pre-Collision Safety, and Adaptive Cruise Control for enhanced protection.

Towing Capacity: Strong towing capacity of up to 3.5 tons, making it ideal for hauling trailers, boats, or caravans.

Fuel Efficiency: Excellent fuel economy for a vehicle of its class, ensuring cost-effective long-term ownership.

Bluetooth & Connectivity: Stay connected with Bluetooth, USB ports, and a high-quality audio system.

Resale Value: Toyota's renowned reputation for durability and value retention makes the Hilux a smart investment.
