Low KMs - Heated Seats - Reverse Camera

Presenting this bright Toyota Aqua Urban hybrid travelled very low kms and with good specification this is one urban to put on the list.
Optioned with Dual Airbags, Smart Key with push button start, Climate control air conditioning, Black Combination leather interior, Heated front seats, Reverse camera and has travelled only 47789km's.
Really stands out and looks sharp finished in High Voltage Yellow complimented with Copper Bronze body kit accents and roof rails on 16 inch Factory alloys, Inside features a stylish luxury Black part leather type with textured patterned seat inserts complimented by exposed stitching. Features Toyota's DOHC engine with generation 3 Hybrid Synergy Drive with an automatic transmission giving good fuel efficiency, also has a 4 star UCSR safety rating.
Finance & Protecta Insurance products are also available for this vehicle.

Pre-purchase inspection can be arranged for out of town purchasers.

You can buy with confidence from Value Cars in the knowledge we are a totally New Zealand owned & funded provider of good quality cars & great after sales service for over 30 years.
