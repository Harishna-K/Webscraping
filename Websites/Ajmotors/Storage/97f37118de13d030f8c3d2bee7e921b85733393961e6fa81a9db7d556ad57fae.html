# 2017 Subaru Levorg 2.0 STI Sport Eyesight
{Stock number. 24053}

The 2017 Subaru Levorg 2.0 STI Sport Eyesight is a high-performance wagon that blends Subaru's rally-inspired engineering with advanced safety features and a practical family-friendly design. Its part of the Levorg line, a popular series known for combining sporty dynamics with everyday usability.

# Key features
- Eyesight: Eyesight uses stereo cameras mounted at the top of the front windshield to monitor the road ahead and detect obstacles, vehicles, and pedestrians. This system works in conjunction with other active safety features to reduce the risk of collisions and assist the driver during various driving scenarios.

- ACC: Eyesight can maintain a set speed and distance from the vehicle ahead by automatically adjusting the cars speed to match traffic flow. If the car in front slows down, the system will reduce your vehicle's speed, and if it accelerates, the system will bring your vehicle back to the preset speed.

- LDW: The system detects lane markings and will alert the driver if the vehicle unintentionally drifts out of its lane without signaling. It also alerts if the vehicle begins to sway, indicating potential drowsiness or distraction.
