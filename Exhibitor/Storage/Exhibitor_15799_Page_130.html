Pelican Reef North America is a manufacturer and wholesale distributor of Home & Patio furniture for over 40 years. We design and provide our customers with furniture suitable for residential and hospitality use.We offer a wide variety of handcrafted designs in styles ranging from traditional to contemporary. The Pelican Reef Tower Daybed with curtains and cushions

The Pelican Reef Tower Daybed, with curtains and cushions, is your hand crafter outdoor luxury daybed made of aluminum frame with woven rope along the facia. A shaded daybed for any outdoor space, our Tower Daybed allows you to feel the wind and breeze with its design.
