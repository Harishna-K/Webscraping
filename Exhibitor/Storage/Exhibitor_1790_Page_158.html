Surya

Add to MyMarket

Surya is an innovative home furnishings brand that provides beautifully crafted rugs, furniture, textiles, wall decor and lighting to transform any space. Design is in our DNA. We are obsessed with design details, constantly pushing the boundaries of color, pattern, texture and form. We translate the latest apparel and interior design trends into home furnishings that inspire endless design possibilities for every lifestyle. To learn more, visit www.surya.com.

Our brands/collections consist of:

  • Mitchell Gold + Bob Williams

Furbara Accent Table Lamp

The Furbara Accent Table Lamp enhances any space with its stylish design. Featuring a ceramic base with a hand-painted khaki finish and topped with a white linen shade, it offers both charm and practical lighting.

Riviera Handmade Rug

The Riviera Handmade Rug offers luxurious comfort and durability. Hand-knotted from 80% New Zealand wool and 20% cotton, it combines softness with strength, adding a refined touch to any room.

Kaufmann Bench

The Kaufmann Bench combines sophistication with simplicity. Featuring a clean silhouette, it is crafted from hand-painted brown ash wood and upholstered in elegant grey polyester. A stylish addition to any space, it offers both comfort and timeless design.
