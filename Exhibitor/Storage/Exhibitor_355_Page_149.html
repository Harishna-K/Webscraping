Overlook Two-Door Cabinet SER-001

The Overlook Two-Door Cabinet combines mid-century charm with modern functionality, featuring a carved front X motif on its doors and triangular wood hardware that follows the same motif. The rich grain of the wood enhances the allure of its black cerused finish.

Ardley Drawer Chest - VMA-502

The VMA-502 Ardley Drawer Chest also showcases the beauty of burl wood, making it the centerpiece of any bedroom. Its scalloped shape and contrasting finishes—dark grey on the outside and light brown on the drawers—create a dynamic visual appeal. Simple brass-finished knobs complement the design elegantly.

Etta Console Table - VMA-512

VMA-512 Etta Console Table - The Etta Console Table VMA-512 blends modern elegance with timeless craftsmanship, featuring burl veneers with a striking book and butt match pattern on the top. Brass accents, ferrules, and hardware add a touch of elegance to this wood console table, enhancing its visual appeal.
