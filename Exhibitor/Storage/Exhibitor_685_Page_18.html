CASPAR IRON SIDEBOARD

The Caspar Iron Sideboard blends modern luxe design with lasting craftsmanship. Fashioned from solid iron, each of its four doors undergoes a manual acid-wash finishing process, resulting in a distinctive landscape effect that adds character to every piece. Inside, find functionality with four shelves and media cord management.

PENTAK WHITE SERVER

The Pentak White Server brings a blend of modern and coastal vibes to your space. Crafted from white-wash mango wood, it features soft-close door hinges, and matte black iron bar door pulls for added convenience and style. With four doors adorned with open pierced fretwork detailing, this server offers ample storage space.

JASPER ACCENT CHAIR

The Jasper Accent Chair is a bold statement piece upholstered in an exquisite zebra print fabric with black and white stripes that offer a dramatic and contemporary look. The fabric is 100% polyester, ensuring durability and ease of maintenance while providing a touch of modern elegance.
