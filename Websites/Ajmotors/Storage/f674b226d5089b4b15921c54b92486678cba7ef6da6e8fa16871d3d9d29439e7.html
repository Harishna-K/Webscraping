Push Button Start, Bluetooth, Eco City Runner

Looking for a compact car thats practical and packed with features? This 2014 Silver Toyota Spade is the perfect fit! With its sliding passenger door, reversing camera, and push-button start, this hatchback is designed to make your daily drives more convenient. Powered by a 1500cc engine and equipped with traction control and dual airbags, it delivers a safe and efficient driving experience.

Highlights Youll Love:

Polished silver finish with stylish 15-inch wheels.

Sliding passenger door for hassle-free entry and exit.

Hatchback design, perfect for city driving and tight parking spots.

1500cc engine for reliable and efficient performance.

Push-button start for modern convenience.

Reversing camera for safer and easier parking.

Traction control for added stability in all driving conditions.

Dual airbags for enhanced safety for you and your passengers.
