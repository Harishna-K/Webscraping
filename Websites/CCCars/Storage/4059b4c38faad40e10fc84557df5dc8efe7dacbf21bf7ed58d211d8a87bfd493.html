The 2015 Nissan Sylphy is a well-rounded sedan that provides a comfortable ride, practical features, and reasonable efficiency.

### Interior & Comfort:
- **Seating**: High-quality fabric upholstery (higher trims may feature leather).
- **Space**: Ample space for both front and rear passengers, offering good legroom and comfort for its class.
- **Air Conditioning**: Automatic climate control (on higher trims) for comfort.
- **Boot Space**: A decent-sized trunk with about 510 liters of capacity, making it practical for carrying luggage or groceries.

### Safety Features:
- **Anti-lock Braking System (ABS)**: Standard for better braking performance and control.
- **Electronic Stability Control (ESC)**: Helps maintain vehicle stability, particularly in slippery conditions.
- **Rearview Camera**: Available in higher trims for easier parking.
- **Vehicle Dynamic Control (VDC)**: Provides added safety, especially during cornering.
