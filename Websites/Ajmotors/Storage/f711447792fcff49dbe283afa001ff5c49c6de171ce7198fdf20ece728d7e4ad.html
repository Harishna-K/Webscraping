# 2018 Subaru XV 2.0I-L Eyesight AWD
{Stock number. 24700}

The 2018 Subaru XV 2.0I-L Eyesight AWD is part of the second-generation Subaru XV, which is a compact crossover SUV. This model offers a mix of practical features, solid performance, and Subarus renowned all-wheel-drive (AWD) system.

# Key features
- Eyesight: Subaru Eyesight is a suite of advanced driver assistance systems (ADAS) developed by Subaru to enhance safety and assist the driver in various situations. It uses a combination of cameras and sensors to monitor the vehicle's surroundings and provide real-time assistance.

- Pre crush safety: Pre-Crash Safety is a vital feature in modern vehicles, designed to prevent or mitigate accidents before they occur. It involves the use of advanced technologies that can detect potential collisions and activate a series of actions to reduce the severity of the impact or, in some cases, avoid the crash altogether

- LDW: They identify the vehicles position relative to the lanes (usually on highways, where lane markings are more prominent) and track its movement. The system is typically able to differentiate between a vehicle intentionally changing lanes (when a turn signal is used) and unintentional lane departure.
