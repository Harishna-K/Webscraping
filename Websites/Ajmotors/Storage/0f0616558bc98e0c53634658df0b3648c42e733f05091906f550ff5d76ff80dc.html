# 2020 Toyota Corolla GX 1.8 Hybrid /NZ NEW
{Stock number. 25246}

The 2020 Toyota Corolla GX 1.8 Hybrid is a part of Toyota's popular Corolla lineup and features a hybrid powertrain. Being a hybrid, it combines a traditional internal combustion engine (ICE) with an electric motor to offer improved fuel efficiency and reduced emissions compared to conventional gasoline-powered vehicles.

# Key features
- Dual climate control: Both the driver and passenger can select different temperature settings, ensuring comfort for both individuals without compromise.

- ACC: ACC stands for Adaptive Cruise Control, a feature that is commonly included in the 2020 Toyota Corolla GX 1.8 Hybrid, as part of Toyotas suite of advanced safety technologies known as Toyota Safety Sense.

- Pre crush safety:
Pre-Collision System (PCS), also referred to as Pre-Crash Safety, is an advanced safety feature that is typically included in the 2020 Toyota Corolla GX 1.8 Hybrid as part of Toyotas Toyota Safety Sense package. This system is designed to help reduce the risk of a collision by detecting potential obstacles ahead and taking preventative action.
