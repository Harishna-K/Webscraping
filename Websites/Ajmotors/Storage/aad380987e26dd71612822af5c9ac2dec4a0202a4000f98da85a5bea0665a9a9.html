Experience sophistication and performance with this 2013 Black Subaru Legacy, a sedan that perfectly balances style and functionality. Whether you're tackling city streets or venturing off the beaten path, the Legacys turbocharged AWD and 18-inch alloy wheels ensure confidence and comfort every mile.

Turbocharged engine for thrilling performance.
