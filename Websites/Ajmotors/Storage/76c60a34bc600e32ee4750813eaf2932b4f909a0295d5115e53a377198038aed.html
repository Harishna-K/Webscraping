# 2017 Nissan Note E-Power Nismo
{Stock number. 24365}

The 2017 Nissan Note e-Power Nismo is a unique version of Nissans compact Note hatchback, blending electric power with sportier performance. The Nismo edition specifically caters to driving enthusiasts who want a blend of eco-friendliness and a more dynamic driving experience.

# Key features
- 360 Degree reverse camera:
A 360-degree reverse camera is an advanced safety feature that provides a bird's-eye view of the area surrounding a vehicle, typically displayed on the vehicle's infotainment screen.

- Drive mode:
Drive Mode refers to the various driving settings or modes that a vehicle offers to adjust its performance, handling, and driving characteristics based on the drivers preferences, road conditions, or the type of driving experience they want.

- LDW:
LDW stands for Lane Departure Warning. It is an advanced driver assistance system (ADAS) designed to alert the driver when the vehicle unintentionally drifts out of its lane without the use of turn signals. This system is especially useful for preventing accidents caused by drowsy driving, distractions, or simple lapses in concentration.
