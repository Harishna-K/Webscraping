# 2012 Toyota 86 G
{Stock number. 25120}

The 2012 Toyota 86 G is a sporty, compact coupe that was developed as a collaboration between Toyota and Subaru. Known in some markets as the Scion FR-S (in the U.S.) or the Subaru BRZ in others, the Toyota 86 is celebrated for its precise handling, lightweight design, and rear-wheel drive layout. It has become a popular choice among enthusiasts due to its affordability and fun-to-drive nature.

# Key features
- SNOW mode: The Snow Mode on the Toyota 86 (and other vehicles with similar modes) is a driving assistance feature designed to help improve traction and stability when driving on slippery or snowy roads.

- Sport mode: Sport Mode optimizes the vehicle for better handling, quicker acceleration, and a more responsive driving feel, making it ideal for spirited driving on dry, twisty roads or even on the track.

- Tiptronic transmission: It's essentially a semi-automatic system that can be found in many vehicles, including some versions of the Toyota 86, and it can be especially useful for enhancing the driving experience.
