2016C 63AMG 4.0 Bi-Turbo V8 20400

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz C 63 AMG features a 4.0 Bi-Turbo V8. It comes with a 9-speed automatic transmission. The sporty design includes AMG styling, upgraded suspension, and luxurious interior finishes.

# Key Features

- AMG: A high-performance division of Mercedes-Benz that offers enhanced engines, sport-tuned suspensions, and distinctive styling for an elevated driving experience.

- Burmester Speakers: Immersive 3D Sound.Provides high-quality audio for an exceptional listening experience Between 11 and 16 speakers.

- Panoramic Sunroof: A glass panel in the roof of the vehicle that can be opened to let in light and air, enhancing the cabin's ambiance.

- Memory Seats : Recalls and adjusts seating positions for multiple drivers with the push of a button.
