Look no further than the 2013 Toyota Spade at AJ Motors! This charming hatchback offers a perfect balance of fuel efficiency, performance, and style. With its compact size, the Toyota Spade is ideal for navigating city streets and finding parking spots with ease. The interior is surprisingly spacious, offering ample legroom and modern features for a comfortable ride.

# Key Features

- Push Button Start : Allows the engine to be started with a simple button press instead of using a key.

- Bluetooth : Enables hands-free phone calls and audio streaming.

- Keyless Entry : Allows access to the vehicle without inserting a key.

- Power Sliding Door : Opens and closes automatically with the push of a button for easy access to the back seats.
