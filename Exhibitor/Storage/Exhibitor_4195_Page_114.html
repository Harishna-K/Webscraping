Modway Furniture

Add to MyMarket

Modway is a modern furniture company headquartered in New Jersey. We have over 10,000 SKUs stored in our strategically located east and west coast warehouses totaling more than 1.5 million sq ft of dedicated space. We feature an extensive selection of color, size, and style options with no order minimums, full drop-shipping capabilities, real-time inventory counts, and an easy online ordering process. Modway’s selection includes products from every major furniture category including living, dining, bedroom, bathroom, office, and outdoor. We carry an extensive collection of RTA furniture and a wide selection of lighting. Catering to both indoor and outdoor needs, we make it easy for you to find the perfect products. For more information, visit us at www.modway.com or stop by our showroom IHFC: H524.

Monroe Performance Velvet Accent Chair

Upholstered in stain-resistant performance velvet, the Monroe Accent Chair features dense foam padding and sinuous spring support for a sink-in-feel that retains its shape. The low-profile design, round wood feet, and beautifully curved back create an inviting silhouette that beckons you to relax and unwind.

Sunlit Squiggle 4-Arm Pendant Light

The Sunlit Pendant Light features four powder-coated squiggle arms, fabric shades, and wooden accents. This pendant is hardwired, offering a clean installation with its round ceiling canopy and straight metal rod. Sunlit is ETL-listed and requires four E26 25W bulbs (not included).

Solace 4-Piece Modular Corduroy Upholstered Sectional Sofa With Ottoman

Featuring a sturdy frame, dense foam padding, and a sinuous spring support system, the Solace Sectional Sofa grants a premium seating experience. The comfy corduroy upholstery adds a cozy, casual vibe, making this deep sectional sofa a perfect addition to any living space or lounge area.
