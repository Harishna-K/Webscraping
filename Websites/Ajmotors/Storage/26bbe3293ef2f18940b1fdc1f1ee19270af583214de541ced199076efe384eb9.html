2 Owners, 1.4L, Cruise Ctrl, 6-Airbags, Bluetooth

Experience style and practicality with this 2015 White Suzuki Swift GLX AB! This sporty hatchback combines a sleek design with smart features like Bluetooth, cruise control, and traction control. With 6 airbags and a fuel-efficient 1400cc engine, the Swift GLX AB is perfect for city commutes or weekend adventures.

Key Features:

Bright white exterior paired with 14-inch custom alloy wheels for a sporty touch.

1400cc engine for efficient yet lively performance.

Compact hatchback design, ideal for urban driving and easy parking.

6 airbags for enhanced safety on every journey.

Bluetooth connectivity for hands-free calls and music streaming.

Cruise control for smooth, effortless highway drives.

Traction control for added stability in various driving conditions.
