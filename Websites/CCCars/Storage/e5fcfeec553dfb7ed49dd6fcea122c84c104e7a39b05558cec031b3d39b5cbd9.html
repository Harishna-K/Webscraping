The 2017 Mazda Demio, known internationally as the Mazda2, offers a variety of features across its different trims. The "S Touring" variant, often referred to as the "13S Touring" in certain markets, includes a comprehensive set of amenities aimed at enhancing comfort, safety, and driving experience.

**Key Features of the 2017 Mazda Demio 13S Touring:**

- **Safety and Security:**
- Anti-lock Braking System (ABS)
- Electronic Stability Control (ESC)
- Dual front airbags
- Side airbags
- Blind Spot Monitoring System (BSM)
- Reverse Camera

- **Comfort and Convenience:**
- Air conditioning
- Heated front seats
- Power windows
- Power steering
- Keyless entry with push-button start
- Electric mirrors
- Retractable power wing mirrors

- **Additional Features:**
- i-Stop (idling stop system)
- ISOFIX child seat anchor points
- Trip computer
- Immobilizer
