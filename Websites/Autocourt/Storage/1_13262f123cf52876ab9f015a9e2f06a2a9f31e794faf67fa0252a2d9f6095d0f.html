An immaculate new generation Toyota Ractis X hatchback, in silver, that has travelled 45,000 kilometres.
One of the more practical hatchbacks available, the Toyota Ractis follows on from the first generation model and the previous Toyota FunCargo, with very easy access, and a spacious carrying capacity.
The Ractis has a later series 1500cc motor (with timing chain), as fitted to the Vitz, Yaris etc, and smooth CVT transmission which combine to provide peppy performance with 6.2L/100km fuel economy.
It has stability/traction control, CD, climate control, cruise control, and reversing camera.
