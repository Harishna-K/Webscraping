LESS THAN A THOUSAND EXAMPLES MADE AVAILABLE WORLDWIDE !!

For sale: a rare 1994 Mercedes-Benz E 500, a limited-version model that was developed in collaboration with Porsche, and one of the few remaining examples in immaculate condition. This exclusive, limited version of the E-Class ( Less than 1000 produced ) features a 5.0L naturally aspirated V8 engine that produces 322 horsepower and 354 lb-ft of torque, providing impressive acceleration and a refined, powerful driving experience. The engine is paired with a 4-speed automatic transmission, offering smooth and responsive shifts. The Porsche-tuned suspension and sport-oriented chassis ensure exceptional handling and cornering performance, setting this E 500 apart from the standard E-Class. As a limited model, the E 500 was produced in smaller quantities, with unique enhancements including a more dynamic suspension, performance upgrades, and distinctive styling cues. With its pristine exterior, flawless interior, and meticulous maintenance, this E 500 is a true collectors piece, combining Mercedes-Benz luxury and Porsche engineering. Dont miss the opportunity to own one of the rarest and most iconic sedans ever produced. Contact for more details and to schedule a test drive!
