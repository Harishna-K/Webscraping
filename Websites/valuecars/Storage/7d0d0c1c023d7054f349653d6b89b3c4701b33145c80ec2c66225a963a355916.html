8 Airbags - New Tyres - Heated Seats

Here we have a very tidy low KM example of the popular Toyota Corolla Hybrid, These have earned a solid reputation for reliability, fuel efficiency and Safety.
Optioned with Climate control air conditioning, 8 Airbags, Shoulder belts for all passengers, Heated front seats, ISOFIX Child seat anchors, Monsoon shields, Brand New Tyres, ECO/EV Modes and more!.
Finished in Dark blue Metallic with a stylish sand coloured interior with lightly textured patterned seat inserts. Features the proven 1500cc engine with Hybrid Synergy Drive and an automatic transmission that delivers great fuel efficiency and also boasts a 5 star VSRR safety rating.
Finance & Protecta Insurance products are also available for this vehicle.

Pre-purchase inspection can be arranged for out of town purchasers.

You can buy with confidence from Value Cars in the knowledge we are a totally New Zealand owned & funded provider of good quality cars & great after sales service for over 30 years.
