This is an immaculate 2014 Toyota Vitz (Yaris) 5-door hatchback in bright yellow, on smart 15 inch alloy wheels, that has travelled only 20,000 kilometres.
A stand-out looking car that looks like new outside, inside and under the bonnet indicating the care with which it has been looked after.
Powered by a new generation 1329cc engine combined with CVT transmission, this delivers peppy performance with exceptional 4.9L/100km fuel economy.
Safety is also looked after by electronic stability/traction control, along with airbags, ABS braking, CD, and climate control.
An ideal little hatchback with good access, and Toyota's renowned reliability.
