MANUAL, 20" MAGS, TURBO DIESEL, NO DEPOSIT FINANCE

Built to handle the toughest jobs and weekend adventures, this 2016 Isuzu D-Max LS is a workhorse with serious style. Featuring a manual transmission for ultimate driving control and a set of 20 mags, this ute delivers rugged performance with a bold, eye-catching stance. Well-maintained and built for reliability, its the perfect companion for work or play.

Engine: Powerful and efficient 3.0L turbo diesel, delivering strong torque and legendary Isuzu durability.

Transmission: 5-speed manual, giving you full control on and off the road.

Interior: Spacious and comfortable cabin with premium cloth seats and modern convenience features.

Entertainment & Tech: Touchscreen infotainment with Bluetooth connectivity for seamless music streaming and hands-free calls.

Safety: Equipped with ABS, traction control, stability control, and dual front airbags for added security.

Utility: Heavy-duty chassis, high ground clearance, and a tough tray ready for work or recreation.

With its solid reputation for reliability, fuel efficiency, and rugged capability, this Isuzu D-Max LS is built to go the distance.

Come check it out today and experience why the D-Max is a favorite among tradies and adventure seekers alike!
