1 Owner, TowBar, Canopy, Carplay, Cruise Ctrl

Meet the ultimate combination of rugged capability and modern comfort: the 2019 White Ford Ranger! This all-mode ute comes equipped with a towbar, canopy, and heated front seats to tackle tough jobs and long journeys alike. With Android Auto, Apple CarPlay, and advanced safety features like 6 airbags, this Ranger has everything you need for work or play.

Top Features Include:

Bright white exterior paired with strong 17-inch alloy wheels.

Towbar for towing trailers, boats, or heavy equipment.

Canopy for secure and weatherproof storage.

Android Auto and Apple CarPlay for modern smartphone integration.

Cruise control for hassle-free highway driving.

6 airbags to prioritize safety for all passengers.
