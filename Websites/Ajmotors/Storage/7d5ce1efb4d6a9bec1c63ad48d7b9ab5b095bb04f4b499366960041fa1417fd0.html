# 2013 Subaru Impreza G4
{Stock number. 24116}

The 2013 Subaru Impreza G4 is the sedan version of the 2013 Subaru Impreza, which was redesigned to offer better fuel efficiency, a more modern interior, and improved handling while retaining the brands signature all-wheel-drive system.

# Key features
- Eyesight: Introduced by Subaru in 2008, EyeSight uses stereoscopic cameras mounted on either side of the rearview mirror to monitor the road ahead, detecting obstacles and responding to potential hazards. By 2013, EyeSight became available as an option in many Subaru models, including the 2013 Subaru Impreza G4.

- LDW:
LDW stands for Lane Departure Warning, a safety feature found in many modern vehicles, including the 2013 Subaru Impreza G4 (when equipped with Subaru's EyeSight system). LDW is designed to alert the driver if the vehicle unintentionally drifts out of its lane without signaling, helping prevent accidents due to driver inattention or drowsiness.

- ACC: ACC stands for Adaptive Cruise Control, an advanced driver assistance feature that automatically adjusts your vehicles speed to maintain a safe following distance from the car ahead. Unlike traditional cruise control, which maintains a constant speed set by the driver, Adaptive Cruise Control can speed up or slow down your car to keep a safe gap between you and the vehicle in front.
