4WD, Leather, Sunroof, Sport, Radar Cruise

Great choice! The 2018 Subaru Outback in sleek black is the ultimate blend of rugged capability and premium comfort. With Subarus renowned AWD system, 18-inch alloy wheels, and a towbar ready for weekend adventures, this Outback is built for both the city and the great outdoors. Inside, youll find luxurious leather seats, a sunroof, heated front seats, and dual-zone climate controlmaking every drive a first-class experience. Plus, with advanced safety features like lane departure warning and forward collision warning, youll have peace of mind wherever the road takes you.
