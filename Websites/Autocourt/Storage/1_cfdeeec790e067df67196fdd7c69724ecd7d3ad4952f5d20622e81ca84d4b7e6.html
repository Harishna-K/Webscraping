Low kilometres!!!
Immaculate Toyota Vitz 5 door hatchback that has travelled only 28,300 kilometres.
Powered by a 996cc motor with variable valve timing (timing chain) and CVT transmission, this peppy little car achieves a respectable 5.5L/100km fuel economy.
This outstanding hatchback comes with power steering, ABS, CD player, air-conditioning, electric windows, etc and provides easy access.
This is a neat looking car in bright red, with grey interior, that has to be seen.
