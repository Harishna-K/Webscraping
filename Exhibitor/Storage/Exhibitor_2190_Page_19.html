Bedford Collections

Add to MyMarket

We design and manufacture in New Hampshire, as well as import decorative throws and pillows. We have acrylic, wool, cotton and bamboo blends. Don't forget our beautiful faux furs. Bring us your event or company logo for a custom knitted throw or we can embroider it onto one of our beautiful throws. We also knit indoor/outdoor throws (Woven in the USA) that are suitable for the hospitality industry. Stop by and see our line. Appointments preferred but not required. Contact 800-242-1537 or customerservice@bedfordcollections.com to schedule and appointment.

Mod

The new Mod throw is custom knit using recycled cotton yarns, giving a modern take on a retro design and styling. With its mid-century vibe, Mod makes a great accent piece but is also cottony-soft to make for a cozy wrap for those cool nights! Mod is machine washable and measures a comfortable size of 50” x 60”.

Refresh

The Refresh throw is crafted from cotton in a two-by-two twill weave construction woven in our New Hampshire mill. It's beautiful drape and weight make it perfect for year-round comfort. Woven with recycled cotton, Refresh measures 47” x 72” and is machine washable.

Kaleidoscope

The new Kaleidoscope lives up to its name with its brilliant palette of nineteen colors! Woven with recycled cotton in our New Hampshire mill, Kaleidoscope’s basket weave variation adds depth and dimension. It is machine washable and measures 47” x 72”.
