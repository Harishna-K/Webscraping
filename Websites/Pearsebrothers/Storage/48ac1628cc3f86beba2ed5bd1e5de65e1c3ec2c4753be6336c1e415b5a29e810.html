3.0 DIESEL, FULL SERVICE HISTORY, 22" ALLOYS

Our 2021 Range Rover Sport HSE combines power, luxury, and versatility.

UK Parallel Import - been in NZ since 1000km

Full Service History with Armstrongs, 22" alloys

Under the hood, its equipped with a 3.0L inline-6 diesel engine, delivering 296 hp and 650Nm of torque for a smooth, yet dynamic driving experience. The 8-speed automatic transmission and all-wheel drive with Terrain Response 2 prepare you for any journey, whether on smooth roads or off the beaten path. The electronic air suspension adjusts the ride height on demand, ensuring each drive feels composed and effortless.

The exterior makes a bold impression, featuring striking 22-inch alloy wheels, LED headlights with signature daytime running lights, and a panoramic roof that bathes the cabin in natural light. Inside, its all about comfort and elegance with 16-way adjustable heated front seats, heated rear seats, and dual-zone climate control for ultimate relaxation.

On the tech side, the Touch Pro Duo infotainment system takes center stage with dual 10-inch touchscreens, offering seamless control over navigation and entertainment. The Meridian sound system provides an exceptional audio experience, while Apple CarPlay and Android Auto keep you connected on the go.
