The 2016 Honda Fit Shuttle Hybrid Facelift is a practical and efficient wagon designed for versatility. Featuring a spacious interior with flexible seating and cargo options, it adapts to your lifestyle effortlessly. The hybrid powertrain ensures excellent fuel economy, making it eco-friendly. With its modern design and reliability, this vehicle is perfect for families. Discover it at AJ Motors in Henderson!

# Key Features

- Cruise Control : Maintains a constant speed for comfortable highway driving.

- Reversing Camera : Provides a view of the area behind the vehicle to assist in reversing safely.

- Roof Rails : Allows for additional storage of cargo on the roof of the vehicle for trips or transporting equipment.
