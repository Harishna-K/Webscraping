Push Button Start, Rev Camera, Hybrid Family Car

Save on fuel while driving in style with this 2012 Red Toyota Aqua! Perfect for eco-conscious drivers, this compact hatchback is powered by a reliable 1500cc petrol hybrid engine and offers convenient features like EV and Eco modes for maximum efficiency. Inside, enjoy a sleek black and grey interior with heated front seats for added comfort, making every drive enjoyable and economical.

Key Features:

Bold red exterior paired with sporty 15-inch alloy wheels.

1500cc petrol hybrid engine for exceptional fuel efficiency and eco-friendly driving.

EV and Eco modes for smart, efficient driving tailored to your needs.

Heated front seats for enhanced comfort during colder days.

Black and grey interior for a modern and sophisticated cabin feel.

Compact hatchback design for easy parking and city driving.

2 airbags for added safety and peace of mind.
