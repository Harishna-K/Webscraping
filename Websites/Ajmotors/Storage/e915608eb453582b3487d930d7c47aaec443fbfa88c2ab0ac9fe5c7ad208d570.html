Drive in Style, Body Kits, Strut Tower Bar

Discover the thrill of open-air driving with this exceptional 2015 Mazda Roadster convertible.

Low Odometer Reading: With just 113,000 km on the clock, this Roadster offers plenty of life and adventures ahead.

6-Speed Manual Transmission: Enjoy complete control and a more engaging driving experience with the precise 6-speed manual gearbox.

Soft-Top Convertible: Experience the joy of open-top motoring with the easy-to-use soft-top convertible.

Body Kits: Enhance the sporty appearance and aerodynamics of your vehicle with stylish body kits.

Strut Tower Bar: Improve the vehicle's handling and stability with the added rigidity of a strut tower bar.

Bluetooth Connectivity: Stay connected on the move with hands-free calling and audio streaming via Bluetooth.

Reversing Camera: Park and reverse with confidence using the clear visuals provided by the reversing camera.

Push Button Start: Enjoy the convenience of starting your engine with the simple push of a button.

1.5L Petrol Engine: Experience the perfect balance of performance and efficiency with the responsive 1.5L petrol engine.

This 2015 Mazda Roadster is a superb choice for those seeking an exhilarating driving experience combined with the practicality of modern features. Its sporty design, coupled with advanced technology and a low odometer reading, makes it an ideal vehicle for both everyday driving and weekend adventures. Whether you're cruising along the coastline or navigating city streets, this Roadster offers unmatched excitement and style.
