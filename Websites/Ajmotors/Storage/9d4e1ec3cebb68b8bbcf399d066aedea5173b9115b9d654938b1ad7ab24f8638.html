# 2017 Toyota CH-R G Hybrid
{Stock number. 24269}

The 2017 Toyota C-HR G Hybrid is a compact crossover SUV that combines a sleek, futuristic design with hybrid fuel efficiency. It was first introduced as part of Toyota's efforts to expand its hybrid offerings in the subcompact crossover segment.

# Key features
- ACC: ACC stands for Adaptive Cruise Control, a driver assistance feature that automatically adjusts a vehicle's speed to maintain a safe distance from the car ahead. It's an enhancement to the traditional cruise control system, offering more convenience and safety during highway driving or in stop-and-go traffic.

- LDW: LDW stands for Lane Departure Warning, a safety feature designed to alert drivers if their vehicle unintentionally drifts out of its lane without the use of turn signals. It is part of a broader category of driver assistance technologies aimed at improving safety and reducing the risk of accidents due to driver distraction or fatigue.

- Push button start: Push Button Start is a convenient feature that allows drivers to start and stop their vehicle's engine with the push of a button, rather than using a traditional key to turn an ignition switch. This system is often found in modern vehicles, adding a touch of convenience, security, and technology to the driving experience.
