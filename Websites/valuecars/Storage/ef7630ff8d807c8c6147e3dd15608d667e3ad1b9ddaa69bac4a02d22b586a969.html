What other buyers say about their 2015 Toyota aqua

These reviews are from buyers who have purchased a vehicle similar to the one you are viewing.

"1) Very smooth 2) fuel friendly 3) clean car 4) Love toyota brand"
— Amandeep K - 2015 Toyota Aqua Hybrid owner
"I love the push button rather than computer screen dashboard on the car I bought. I'm not a techie person and this works well for me. It took me a little while to get used to starting the car and I sometimes got stuck in park mode but I figured it out after a couple of weeks. I would have liked a key sensor on the doors to avoid having to use the key to open the car, esp when my hands are full, but I realise not all Aquas have this. Overall very happy with the car. Great economy too!"
— Lynette G - 2015 Toyota Aqua X-Urban Hybrid owner
"I am enjoying it very much. I have a difficult, steep driveway which tends to scrape the bottom of cars. Before buying an Aqua, I hired one to see whether it could get in the driveway without scraping. It could. The Aqua is lovely and spacious, and is a lot easier than other cars for my elderly father to get in and out of. It feels safe for my dog."
— CATHERINE S - 2015 Toyota Aqua S-Package Prius C owner
