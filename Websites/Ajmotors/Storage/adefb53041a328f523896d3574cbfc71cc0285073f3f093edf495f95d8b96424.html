# 2015 Suzuki Solio Bandit DJE
{Stock number. 23808}

The 2015 Suzuki Solio Bandit DJE is a compact MPV (Multi-Purpose Vehicle) designed for the Japanese market, where it is often used as a practical and versatile family car or a city runabout. The model is part of Suzuki's Solio range and is notable for its combination of compact dimensions, spacious interior, and efficient performance.

# Key features
- Power sliding door: The 2015 Suzuki Solio Bandit DJE comes equipped with a power sliding door as part of its standard or optional features. This feature is particularly popular in compact MPVs and minivans, offering a combination of convenience and practicality, especially in tight spaces.

- Push button start: The push button start system is another convenient feature available on the 2015 Suzuki Solio Bandit DJE. This modern technology allows the driver to start the vehicle with just the press of a button, as long as the key fob is inside the car.

- Cruise control: The 2015 Suzuki Solio Bandit DJE is equipped with cruise control, which is a standard feature in many modern vehicles. Cruise control allows the driver to set and maintain a constant speed without having to keep their foot on the accelerator pedal, making long drives or highway cruising more comfortable and less tiring.
