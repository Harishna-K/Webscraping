The 2015 Honda Shuttle Hybrid Z is a compact wagon designed for practicality and efficiency, It combines a hybrid powertrain with a spacious interior and a range of features tailored for comfort, convenience, and safety.
### Powertrain and Performance
- **Engine**: 1.5-liter Atkinson cycle DOHC i-VTEC petrol engine
- **Hybrid System**: Sport Hybrid i-DCD (Intelligent Dual-Clutch Drive)

### Exterior Features
- **Design**: Features Hondas Solid Wing Face front grille and headlight design for a sharp, dynamic look.
- **Lighting**: Inline-type LED headlights for improved visibility and a sleek appearance.

### Interior Features
- **Seating**: 5 seats across 5 doors, offering a practical layout for passengers.
- **Cargo Space**with over 500 litres of cargo capacity with rear seats up (exact figures vary slightly by source).
- **Flexibility**: Rear seats can be folded or rolled up for additional cargo space, enhancing versatility.

### Technology and Convenience
- **Key System**: Honda Smart Key system for keyless entry and start.
- **Cruise Control**: Standard on the Hybrid Z trim for easier long-distance driving.
- **Climate Control**: Efficient air conditioning tailored for the compact cabin.

The 2015 Honda Shuttle Hybrid Z stands out for its blend of hybrid efficiency, spaciousness, and modern features, making it a strong choice for those seeking a practical yet advanced compact wagon.
