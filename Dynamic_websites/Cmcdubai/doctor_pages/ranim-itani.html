Biography

Ranim Itani is a Lebanese physiotherapist, who believes in the transformative power of physiotherapy, and committed to providing compassionate care, using evidence-based techniques to maximize individuals’ functional mobility and achieve their rehabilitation goals.

She has a special interest in physiotherapy for orthopedic and post-operative rehabilitation cases.

She completed her degree from University of Sharjah, UAE, in 2020, With a mission to empower her patients to lead healthier, more active lives.
