A neat 2014 Nissan Note X hatchback, in grey that has travelled 24,000 kilometres.
The Note has exceptionally easy access, good seating position, and is powered by a 1200cc supercharged engine which provides peppy performance with 5.3L/100km fuel economy.
It comes with air-conditioning, CD player, electric windows, central locking, stability control, (4-star safety rating) and with the back seats folded down, a large carrying capacity.
Good little car at a good price.
