The 2014 Subaru Impreza 1.6i hatchback comes with a variety of features, offering a blend of practicality, safety, and performance.

### Interior Features:
- **Seats**: 5-passenger capacity with cloth upholstery
- **Climate Control**: Air conditioning with cabin air filter
- **Storage**: 60/40 split-folding rear seats for additional cargo space
- **Steering Wheel**: Tilt and telescopic adjustable steering wheel with audio and phone controls
- **Power Windows & Mirrors**: Standard

### Safety Features:
- **Airbags**: Front, side, and curtain airbags
- **Anti-lock Braking System (ABS)**:
- **Electronic Stability Control (ESC)**: Helps maintain vehicle stability
- **Brake Assist**: Provides additional braking force in emergency situations
- **Traction Control System (TCS)**: Prevents wheel spin during acceleration

### Additional Features:
- **Keyless Entry**: Standard or optional on some trims
- **Cruise Control**: Standard
- **Bluetooth Connectivity**: For hands-free calls and music streaming
- **Power-Adjustable Side Mirrors**: Available

The 2014 Subaru Impreza 1.6i hatchback is known for its durability, solid handling, and all-wheel-drive capability, making it a practical choice for families or those who need a compact vehicle with extra traction in varying weather conditions.
