Baker | McGuire

Add to MyMarket

Baker-McGuire is a heritage furniture company with a rich legacy. For more than a century, the company has established itself as a hallmark of design excellence and uncompromising quality. It is comprised of two sister brands – Baker and McGuire – which specialize in curated collections of fine furniture, lighting and textiles, including collaborations with some of the world’s most distinguished interior designers. Its rich furnishings feature classic sensibility in form, finish and function, designed and crafted domestically in its Hildebran and High Point, North Carolina case goods and upholstery plants as well as factories internationally. Baker and McGuire are distributed worldwide through a network of interior design trade showrooms and luxury home furnishings retailers.

Baker Resort Collection - Fall 2023

Baker | McGuire is proud to debut its Fall 2023 collections: The Baker Resort Collection® and The Baker Resort Collection® for McGuire. The exquisite ensemble of furniture is inspired by coastal vacations and modern luxury living, brought to life through naturalistic materials, clean lines, indulgent textures, and simple yet elegant forms.

Goldmine Accent Table from the Barbara Barry for Baker Collection

Like shimmering precious metals that are newly unearthed, the Goldmine Accent Table captures your attention with its rich coloring and multi-faceted surface. This exquisite table is crafted entirely in honed Honey Onyx.

Noah Mirror from the Baker Luxe Collection

The Noah Mirror is imaginatively designed, taking inspiration from the phases of the moon. This artistic silhouette elevates any space with its unique “third moon” shaping in Pure Brass.

Serene Bed from the Barbara Barry for McGuire Collection

Boasting a curved headboard and footboard in sculpted rattan, the Serene Bed is an elegantly crafted option with a grand form. It features woven square cane mesh insets and round, tapered legs that make the bed appear to float.
