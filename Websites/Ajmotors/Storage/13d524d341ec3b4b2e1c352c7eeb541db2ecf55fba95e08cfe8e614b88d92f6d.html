# 2006 Toyota Hiace 5 Speed manual
{Stock number.23245}

The 2006 Toyota Hiace with a 5-speed manual transmission is known for its reliability and versatility. Its commonly used for commercial purposes but is also popular for personal use, especially for larger families or travel.

# Key features
- Manual transmission: Manual transmissions allow for a more connected driving experience, letting you choose the right gear for various driving conditions.

- Reverse camera: There are many aftermarket reverse camera kits available that come with a camera, monitor, and wiring harness. Look for kits designed for vans or commercial vehicles for better fit and functionality.
