# 2015 Nissan Note NISMO
{Stock number. 24376}

The 2015 Nissan Note NISMO is a sporty variant of the popular Nissan Note compact hatchback. It was designed to offer enhanced performance and styling compared to the standard model, while maintaining its practicality and fuel efficiency.

# Key features
- Nismo seats: The NISMO seats in the 2015 Nissan Note NISMO (and other NISMO models from Nissan) are specifically designed to provide better support, comfort, and a sporty aesthetic compared to standard seats

- Auto start stop: By turning off the engine when idling, the system helps reduce fuel consumption, especially in city driving with frequent stops. This can result in improved fuel economy and lower emissions.

- Key less entry: As long as the key fob is in your pocket, bag, or within range (typically within a few feet), you can simply touch the door handle to unlock the car. Many systems require you to press a button or a sensor on the handle to unlock the doors.

If you're looking for more information about the car, feel free to reach out! Whether you have questions about specific features or need assistance with anything else, our team is happy to help.
