Add to MyMarket

Martin Furniture is a leading manufacturer of Dining, Commercial and Home Office, Media, and Accent categories. With a passion for design excellence and unwavering commitment to quality, we have established ourselves as a trusted name in the global marketplace. Our corporate office is in San Diego, CA along with warehouses in both the US and Asia

Willow Lane Bookcase

Willow Lane is designed to embody a refreshed transitional style while providing functionality and sophistication. Crafted from solid wood, each piece features exquisite crown molding and elegant sconce lighting creating a polished look that effortlessly complements any interior.

Towne Office Collection

The Towne Office collection is clean and simple with slim lines, tapered legs and a modern influence. An ash blonde finish highlights the natural beauty of the wood veneers.

Pomona Office Collection

The Pomona Office Collection is designed for the modern professional. Embodying a clean, contemporary aesthetic featuring a striking charcoal wood grain laminate finish that not only enhances durability but also provides a refined backdrop for your workspace.
