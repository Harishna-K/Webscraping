South + English

Add to MyMarket

With a counter-intuitive mix of provenance and personality, South + English is a home décor collection where opposites attract, and styles converge. For designers who seek unique, S+E's Custom Bespoke lineup may be the perfect fit. Designs are the peak of chic and available in a raft of finish options. The S+E High Point showroom is open by appointment all year-round and hosts a variety of special events. In fact, you can't miss our triple-storefront shop awash in purple on MLK Jr Drive, just off Wrenn. S+E is the brainchild of longtime partners and creative collaborators Palmer Linwood Smith and David Ebbetts – the former an irrepressible live-large Southerner and the latter, a dialed-back, buttoned-up Brit. Honestly, they couldn’t be more different. Therein lies the magic. See you soon. For immediate enquiries, just email howdy@southandenglish.com.

Cotswold Cabinet

Brilliantly crafted, featuring elegant yet simple geometric design. Inspired by the architecture of country homes in the Cotswolds, the Cabinet doors replicate a simple yet modern vibe. Of course, the S+E Bespoke collection is a candidate for endless customization, so if the standard dimensions and specs don’t suit you (or your floor plan).

Brewster Dining Table

Convex or concave, designers love Brewster’s flexible reeded base. Top it off with a choice of four round tabletop sizes, or customize them to your liking by the inch. Choose from a beveled or straight edge on any shape. Round, Oval, Square top, you name it! Available in a variety of bespoke finishes, which are entirely mix and matchable.

Hampton 4-Poster Bed

The 4-posts are hand-lathed turnings in a modern style that are combined with solid oak construction that can be made to your specifications. As shown in our Chatsworth White finish with the golden hooks canopy, the upholstered headboard is a COM (which is always welcome).
