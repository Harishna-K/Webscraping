2019 Lexus RX 450h Version L: The Pinnacle of Luxury SUVs

The Lexus RX 450h Version L combines a 3.5L V6 engine with electric motors, delivering a total system output of 313PS.
Its hybrid powertrain offers exceptional fuel efficiency and quiet performance, while the NAVI·AI-AVS system ensures optimal ride comfort by adjusting to road conditions.

The interior features the standard Version L, showcasing genuine wood and premium leather, exuding sophistication. Details like the leather steering wheel and shift knob enhance its luxurious appeal.

With Active Cornering Assist and precisely tuned EPS, the RX 450h offers linear steering feel and outstanding stability, ensuring precise cornering and confidence.

Perfect for daily use, long drives, or sporty adventures, this premium SUV embodies luxury and functionality at their finest.
