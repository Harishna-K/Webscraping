Feature Highlights

  • AA Appraised

Premium model with FULL Black leather, 19' Alloys

Experience luxury, performance, and cutting-edge hybrid technology with this 2014 Nissan Skyline Hybrid 350GT. This impressive sedan combines a powerful 3.5L V6 hybrid engine with premium features, making it the perfect blend of power, style, and efficiency for any driving enthusiast.

3.5L V6 Hybrid Engine with 306 Horsepower
Delivers exceptional power and smooth acceleration, all while optimizing fuel efficiency through advanced hybrid technology.

19-Inch Alloy Wheels
Stylish, high-performance alloy wheels enhance handling and add a sleek, aggressive stance to the vehicle's profile.

Luxurious Leather Seats
Enjoy premium comfort with leather upholstery that gives the cabin a refined, upscale feel.

Power-Adjustable and Heated Seats
Both front seats offer power adjustments and heating, ensuring comfort and convenience on every journey, regardless of the weather.

Adaptive Cruise Control
Maintain a safe distance from the vehicle ahead with adaptive cruise, which adjusts speed automatically based on traffic flow.

Lane Departure Warning
Stay safe with advanced lane departure warning, which alerts you if the car unintentionally veers out of its lane.

Forward Collision Mitigation
This advanced safety feature detects potential frontal collisions and can apply emergency braking to help reduce the impact.

Bluetooth Connectivity
Effortlessly connect your phone for hands-free calls and streaming, keeping you connected while staying focused on the road.

With its combination of a powerful hybrid engine, a luxurious interior, and advanced safety features, this 2014 Nissan Skyline Hybrid 350GT is ideal for drivers seeking a balanced mix of performance and efficiency without sacrificing comfort or style.
