# 2013 Subaru Legacy B4 AWD 2.0GT DIT Eyesight Premium
{Stock number. 24037}

The 2013 Subaru Legacy B4 AWD 2.0GT DIT Eyesight Premium is a high-performance variant of the Subaru Legacy sedan, known for its advanced technology, all-wheel-drive (AWD) capabilities, and sporty driving dynamics.

# Key features
- Eyesight: Subaru Eyesight is a driver-assist safety technology developed by Subaru that uses two cameras mounted on the front windshield to monitor the road ahead. It is designed to enhance the driver's awareness, prevent accidents, and improve overall safety. Eyesight is available on many Subaru models, including the 2013 Legacy B4 AWD 2.0GT DIT Eyesight Premium.

- Turbo gauge cluster: The Turbo Gauge Cluster in cars, such as the 2013 Subaru Legacy B4 2.0GT DIT, is a performance-oriented feature that helps drivers monitor key metrics related to the engines turbocharged performance.

- Pre crush safety: The Pre-Collision Safety System (also known as Pre-Crash Safety or Pre-Collision Braking) is a critical safety feature in modern vehicles, designed to help prevent accidents or reduce their severity when a collision is imminent.
