# Key features
LDW stands for Lane Departure Warning. It is a driver-assistance system designed to alert the driver if the vehicle unintentionally drifts out of its lane without using turn signals. The system uses cameras or sensors to monitor lane markings on the road and detects if the vehicle is veering off its lane.

- Pre crush safety:
Pre-Collision Safety (often referred to as Pre-Collision System or PCS) is a crucial component of a vehicles Advanced Driver Assistance System (ADAS). It aims to detect potential collisions with other vehicles, pedestrians, or obstacles in the vehicle's path, and take preventative actions to minimize or avoid an accident.

- Cruise control: Cruise Control is an advanced driver-assistance system that automatically controls a vehicles speed, allowing the driver to maintain a consistent speed without needing to keep their foot on the accelerator. Its commonly used on highways or long, straight roads to reduce driver fatigue and improve fuel efficiency.
