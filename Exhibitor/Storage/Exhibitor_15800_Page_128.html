elican Reef has been manufacturing and distributing fine rattan, wicker, and outdoor furniture for over 40 years. Pelican Reef has partnered with Panama Jack ® as their worldwide licensee for outdoor and sunroom furniture. Along with Rainbow Outdoor for contract quality patio furniture. The Pelican Reef furniture groups outdoor furniture is made with the best quality materials in order to offer products that are suitable for high use in residential and commercial environments. Our aluminum frames have a baked on powder- coated finish, and which are then hand woven with Wintech ® or Rehau ® synthetic fibers. Our textiline sling products use commercial quality sling for lasting durability. Additionally our polypropylene (PP) material products are commerial grade and tested by CATAS. The Pelican Reef furniture group is headquartered in Florida, with to the trade showrooms in South Florida, High Point, and Atlanta.
