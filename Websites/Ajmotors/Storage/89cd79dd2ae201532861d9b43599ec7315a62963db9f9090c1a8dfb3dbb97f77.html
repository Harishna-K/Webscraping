4WD, Leather, Digital Dash, TowBar, Radar Cruise

Looking for a sleek, feature-packed SUV that offers style, comfort, and performance? This 2016 Volkswagen Tiguan delivers on all fronts, equipped with premium touches and technology to make every drive a pleasure.

19-Inch Alloy Wheels
Sleek 19-inch alloy wheels enhance stability and provide a sporty look, adding both function and style to your ride.

6 Airbags
With six airbags, this Tiguan offers enhanced safety for both driver and passengers, keeping everyone protected on the road.

Leather Seats
Premium leather seating elevates the interior for added comfort, easy maintenance, and a touch of luxury.

Push Button Start
Start your journey with ease using the push-button start, which adds modern convenience and effortless ignition.

Bluetooth Connectivity
Stay connected and enjoy hands-free calling and audio streaming with integrated Bluetooth, providing both convenience and safety.

Reversing Camera
A reversing camera offers a clear view of whats behind the vehicle, making parking and maneuvering in tight spaces safer and easier.

Cruise Control
Cruise control adds comfort to long drives, helping you maintain a consistent speed and reducing driver fatigue.

Paddle Shifters
For a more engaging driving experience, paddle shifters allow you to shift gears manually, giving you full control over performance when desired.

Auto Hold Feature
The Auto Hold feature provides added convenience by holding the brake automatically at stops, making driving in traffic or on hills stress-free.

This 2016 Volkswagen Tiguan combines German engineering with modern luxury, making it a fantastic option for those who want an SUV thats both practical and refined. With advanced tech features, a comfortable interior, and a design made for smooth handling, its a versatile and enjoyable choice for any driver.
