# 2020 Mitsubishi Outlander XLS (NZ New)
{Stock number. 23758}

The 2020 Mitsubishi Outlander XLS (NZ New) is a mid-range model in the Outlander lineup, offering a mix of comfort, practicality, and features that cater to both urban driving and off-road capabilities. As an "NZ New" model, it means the vehicle was originally supplied and sold in New Zealand, which typically ensures that it's configured to meet local regulations and driving conditions.

# Key features
- Push start button: The push-start button is a common feature in modern vehicles, including the 2020 Mitsubishi Outlander XLS, providing a convenient and keyless way to start your engine.

- Cruise control: If you're using standard cruise control (non-adaptive), it allows you to set and maintain a constant speed without needing to keep your foot on the accelerator pedal.

- Electric hand brake: The Electric Parking Brake (EPB), also known as the electronic handbrake, replaces the traditional mechanical handbrake lever with an electronic switch.

If you're looking for more information about the car, feel free to reach out! Whether you have questions about specific features or need assistance with anything else, our team is happy to help.
