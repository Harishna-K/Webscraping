# 2020 Toyota Rav4 Limited 2.5PH/4WD/Hybrid
{Stock number. 25524}

The 2020 RAV4 Limited Hybrid is a great option for those looking for a balance of efficiency, reliability, and a high level of standard features in an SUV. Its perfect for those who want a practical and eco-friendly vehicle without sacrificing comfort or utility.

# Key features
- ACC: ACC stands for Adaptive Cruise Control, a driver assistance feature designed to improve comfort and safety during long drives or highway cruising. In the context of the 2020 Toyota RAV4 Limited Hybrid, the vehicle is equipped with Toyota Safety Sense 2.0 (TSS 2.0), which includes Adaptive Cruise Control as one of its components

- LDW: Lane Departure Warning uses cameras and sensors to monitor the vehicle's position on the road. It detects lane markings on the road and provides an alert if the vehicle begins to drift out of its lane without signaling. The system will typically issue a visual or audible warning to the driver to help prevent unintentional lane departure.

- Parking sensors:
Parking Sensors are an important driver assistance feature designed to help drivers avoid obstacles when parking or maneuvering in tight spaces. In the 2020 Toyota RAV4 Limited Hybrid, parking sensors are part of the suite of safety and convenience features that assist the driver during low-speed maneuvers.
