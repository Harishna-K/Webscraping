# 2017 Nissan X-Trail TL Diesel 4WD
{Stock number. 24453}

The 2017 Nissan X-Trail TL Diesel 4WD is an excellent choice for those seeking a reliable and comfortable SUV that can handle both city driving and outdoor adventures. It combines practical features like high ground clearance and all-wheel drive with a premium cabin, advanced safety systems, and a fuel-efficient diesel engine, making it a strong contender in the mid-size SUV market.

# Key features
- Sunroof: The panoramic sunroof in the 2017 Nissan X-Trail TL Diesel 4WD is a notable feature that enhances the driving experience by allowing more natural light into the cabin and offering expansive views of the sky.

- Front and rear heated seats: The front and rear heated seats in the 2017 Nissan X-Trail TL Diesel 4WD are an excellent feature designed to enhance comfort during colder weather.

- Cruise control: The 2017 Nissan X-Trail TL comes with a traditional cruise control system that allows you to set and maintain a constant speed once you've reached your desired velocity.

If you're looking for more information about the car, feel free to reach out! Whether you have questions about specific features or need assistance with anything else, our team is happy to help.
