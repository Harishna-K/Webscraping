Elevate your driving experience with this 2012 White Toyota Mark X, a luxury sedan that perfectly balances style, safety, and performance. With premium leather seats, heated front seats, and versatile driving modes including Sport, Snow, and Eco, this Mark X is designed for comfort and control. Add in advanced safety with 9 airbags, Bluetooth connectivity, and a reversing camera, and this sedan is ready for any journey.

Key Features:

Elegant white exterior complemented by sleek 16-inch alloy wheels.

Luxurious leather seats with heated front seats for added comfort.

9 airbags for enhanced safety and peace of mind.

Bluetooth connectivity for hands-free calls and music streaming.

Reversing camera for easy and confident parking.

Fog lights to improve visibility in adverse weather conditions.

Push-button start for easy and modern convenience.

Dual climate control to ensure personalized comfort for all passengers.

Sport, Snow, and Eco modes for adaptable driving performance.
