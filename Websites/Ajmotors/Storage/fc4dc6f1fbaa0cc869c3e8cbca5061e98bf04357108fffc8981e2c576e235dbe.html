Low KM, NZ Stereo, Bluetooth, Push Button Start

Looking for a compact, practical car thats big on convenience? This 2012 Toyota Porte is the perfect choice, with an exceptionally low odometer reading of just 54,000 km. Powered by a reliable 1.5L petrol engine, its loaded with features like push-button start, a reversing camera, and Bluetooth connectivity. With its compact size and thoughtful design, the Porte is perfect for city living or family outings.

Low Mileage: Just 54,000 kmthis car is barely broken in!
Push-Button Start: Enjoy modern convenience every time you drive.
NZ Stereo: Stay entertained with a system tuned for local compatibility.
Bluetooth Connectivity: Hands-free calling and music streaming on the go.
Reversing Camera: Simplify parking and tight maneuvers.
Fog Lights: Improved visibility in adverse weather.
1.5L Petrol Engine: Reliable and efficient for everyday use.
Compact and Spacious: Easy to park, with plenty of interior room.
