The 2013 Nissan Latio G, also known as the Nissan Sunny in some markets, is a compact sedan that comes with a variety of features.

### **Engine and Performance:**
- **Engine Type:** 1.5L 4-cylinder engine
-- **Transmission:** 4-speed automatic

### **Exterior Features:**
- **Body Style:** 4-door sedan
**Side Mirrors:** Electrically adjustable

### **Interior Features:**
- **Seats:** Cloth upholstery (leather in some higher trims)
- **Air Conditioning:** Manual or automatic air conditioning, depending on the trim
- **Steering Wheel:** Tilt and telescoping adjustable steering wheel, with controls for audio and cruise control (depending on trim)
- **Dashboard:** Simple layout with analog dials for speed, fuel, and temperature, along with a basic information display

### **Safety Features:**
- **Airbags:** Front airbags,
- **Anti-lock Braking System (ABS):** Standard
- **Electronic Brakeforce Distribution (EBD):** Standard
- **Brake Assist:** Standard
- **Vehicle Stability Control (VSC):** Available on higher trims

### **Comfort and Convenience:**
- **Power Windows:**
- **Central Locking:**

### **Other Features:**
- **Fuel Tank Capacity:** Approximately 41 liters
- **Boot Space:** Around 510 liters

Overall, the 2013 Nissan Latio G is a solid and reliable compact sedan designed for everyday driving with a focus on comfort and safety.
