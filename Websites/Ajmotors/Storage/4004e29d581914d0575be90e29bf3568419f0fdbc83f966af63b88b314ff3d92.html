# 2014 Nissan X-trail 20X
{Stock number. 24306}

The 2014 Nissan X-Trail 20X is part of the third generation of the Nissan X-Trail, which was produced from 2013 to 2020. The 20X trim is a mid-range version that offers a well-rounded combination of performance, comfort, and practicality, especially for those who need a family-friendly SUV with a bit of off-road capability.

# Key features
- Push button start: Push-Button Start (also known as Keyless Start or Keyless Ignition) is a convenient feature found in many modern vehicles that allows the driver to start the car without using a traditional key to turn the ignition.

- Cruise control: Cruise control is a vehicle feature that allows the driver to set and maintain a constant speed without having to keep their foot on the accelerator pedal. It is especially useful for long-distance driving, like on highways, where maintaining a steady speed can reduce fatigue and make driving more comfortable.

- Heated seats: Heated seats are a popular comfort feature in modern vehicles, designed to keep the driver and passengers warm during cold weather. They work by using electric heating elements embedded in the seat cushion and backrest to generate warmth, providing a cozy experience on chilly days.
