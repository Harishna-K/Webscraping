306 Horsepower, Leather, 19' Alloys, Radar Cruise

Experience the thrill of the 2014 Nissan Skyline, a stylish grey sedan that combines power and luxury with advanced features. This Skyline is equipped with cutting-edge technology and high-performance enhancements, making every drive exciting and enjoyable.

19-Inch Alloy Wheels: Premium 19-inch alloy wheels provide a smooth, stylish, and commanding presence on the road, enhancing both handling and aesthetics.

6 Airbags: Drive confidently with six airbags strategically placed throughout the cabin, offering comprehensive protection for all occupants.

Sunroof: Enjoy an open-air experience with the sunroof, perfect for bringing natural light and fresh air into the cabin for a more enjoyable drive.

Leather Seats: Indulge in comfort with luxurious leather seats, providing both elegance and lasting comfort for every journey.

Bluetooth Connectivity: Stay connected on the go with Bluetooth, enabling hands-free calling and music streaming to keep you focused on the road.

Reversing Camera with 360-Degree View: Park and maneuver with ease using the advanced 360-degree camera, offering a complete view of the vehicles surroundings for added safety and precision.

Cruise Control: Maintain your desired speed on long stretches of highway with cruise control, offering a more relaxed and fuel-efficient journey.

Paddle Shifters: Engage with the Skylines sporty side using the paddle shifters, allowing for manual gear shifts and enhanced control of its responsive engine.

Petrol Hybrid Engine: Benefit from the power of a petrol engine paired with hybrid efficiency, delivering a balance of performance and fuel savings to enhance every drive.

This 2014 Nissan Skyline offers a unique blend of performance, luxury, and cutting-edge features, perfect for drivers who appreciate both style and substance.
