# 2019 Mazda CX-9 LTD 2.5PT/4WD/6AT (NZ New)
{Stock number. 25183}

The Mazda CX-9 is known for its dynamic driving experience, which is less common among other 3-row SUVs. Mazda's G-Vectoring Control Plus technology gives it a sporty and agile feel, even though it's a larger vehicle. The 2.5L turbo engine provides plenty of power, especially for overtaking or tackling hilly terrains.

# Key features
- Sunroof: 2019 Mazda CX-9 Limited does come with a power-operated sunroof as part of its premium features. The sunroof adds a nice touch of luxury and can really enhance the driving experience, especially on clear days or scenic drives. It typically has an auto-tilt and slide function, allowing you to open or close it at the touch of a button, and it has a sunshade that you can adjust when you want to block out some of the sun.

- LDW: It uses cameras and sensors to monitor the vehicle's position on the road. If the system detects that you're unintentionally drifting out of your lane without signaling, it will alert you with a visual and/or audible warning.

- Rain sensing wipers: The system uses a sensor located at the base of the windshield (usually near the rearview mirror) to detect rain. When the sensor detects moisture on the windshield, it automatically activates the wipers and adjusts the speed based on the amount of rain.
