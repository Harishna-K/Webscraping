Bodrum

Add to MyMarket

Since 1994, Bodrum Linens has supplied selected retailers with premier quality Turkish linens, offering exceptional value. Our fabrics are woven on state-of-the-art looms and dyed or printed using only the highest quality materials. Our easy-care placemats are made from high quality, 100% phthalate-free vinyl. All finishing is done by highly skilled artisans who take extra pride in their craft. Delivering a quality product with superior customer service is always our priority, as is building long-lasting relationships with our retailers and customers.

Holly Berry

Featuring vibrant evergreen leaves, luscious red berries, and metallic gold accents, our Holly Berry collection captures the timeless elegance and spirit of the season. Available as napkins, runners, and tablecloths, these pieces are perfect for adding a festive sparkle and joy to your home for the holidays.

Archer Mats and Midas Linens

Sophistication meets shimmering allure! Our Archer placemats will elevate your tablescape with their mesmerizing aesthetic; with their alternating stripes, they create a visually stunning effect! Paired with our new Midas linens that feature metallic threads reminiscent of precious stones. Transform your tablescape into a golden masterpiece!

Stingray Trays and Coasters

Entertain with style! Featuring the same material as our Stingray placemats, these trays and coasters are the ultimate luxury. Coasters come with felt on the bottom to avoid scratches.
