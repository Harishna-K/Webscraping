Flora Collection

The Flora Collection features a light, airy ambiance with a botanically inspired Capiz Shell design. This elegant blend of classic and contemporary styles, reminiscent of a torch, combines natural materials with refined sophistication. Finished in Modern Gold, it showcases wood bead columns and scalloped candle cup details.

Plexus Floor Lamp

The Plexus Floor Lamp is a masterpiece blending an Acacia wood base with a warm brown leather shade. The expansive shade, with offset slits, beautifully diffuses light. Crafted with top-tier materials and meticulous hand stitching, it echoes high-end handbags. Finished in sophisticated Graphite, it embodies elegance and fine craftsmanship.

Coral Collection

The Coral Collection evokes nature's essence with transparent or green glass beads perched on branch-like arms, reminiscent of coral or water droplets. The beads, like raindrops, magnify light without casting a green tint. Inspired by airy forms, the design is light and elegant, finished in Brushed Champagne Gold for a touch of luxury.
