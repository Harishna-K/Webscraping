# 2017 Hyundai Tucson GDI elite 2.0P (NZ New)
{Stock number. 24179}

The 2017 Hyundai Tucson GDI Elite 2.0P (NZ New) is a solid choice for anyone looking for a well-rounded, comfortable, and tech-equipped SUV. It's ideal for urban driving, road trips, and light towing, and the added safety features and infotainment options make it a competitive option in its class.

# Key features
- BSM: Blind Spot Monitoring is a valuable safety feature that enhances overall driver awareness and helps to prevent side-impact collisions, particularly when changing lanes on busy roads or highways.

- Heated seats: Heated seats use electrically powered heating elements built into the seat cushion and backrest. These elements are usually made from a resistive material, which generates heat when an electrical current is passed through them.

- Electric hand brake: An Electric Handbrake (also known as an Electronic Parking Brake, or EPB) is a modern replacement for the traditional mechanical handbrake lever or pedal used to secure a vehicle when stationary.
