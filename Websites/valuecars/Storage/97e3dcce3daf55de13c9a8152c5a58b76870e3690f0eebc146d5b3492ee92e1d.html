POPULAR SOUGHT AFTER ECONOMICAL SPORTS HATCH

Low km Sporty 2016 Generation 4 Subaru Impreza Sport 2WD five door family hatch with multi-function steering wheel, stability control and has only travelled 44278km's. Looks very smart finished in Dark Blue Pearl and has the desirable Black interior with lightly textured seat inserts. Features a 1600cc chain driven flat four boxer style engine with automatic transmission giving good fuel efficiency, also has a 4 star UCSR safety rating.
Finance and Protecta Insurance products are also available for this vehicle.

Pre-purchase inspection can be arranged for out of town purchasers.

You can buy with confidence from Value Cars in the knowledge we are a totally New Zealand owned and funded provider of good quality cars with great after sales service for over 30 years.
