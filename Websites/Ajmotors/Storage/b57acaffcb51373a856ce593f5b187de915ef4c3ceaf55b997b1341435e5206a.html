# 2015 Toyota Estima Aeras G-Package 7 seater
{Stock number. 23523}

The 2015 Toyota Estima Aeras G-Package is known for its reliability, comfort, and family-friendly features, making it a great choice for those in need of a minivan.

# Key features
- Power sliding door: They open with the push of a button, making it easier to enter and exit the vehicle, especially in tight parking spaces.

- Power sliding door: Often comes with a keyless entry system, allowing you to unlock and start the vehicle while keeping the key fob in your pocket or bag.

- Cruise control: Helps maintain a steady speed on highways, reducing the need for constant acceleration and braking.
