Low kilometres.
This is a tidy Toyota Toyoace 150 truck that has travelled only 42,000 kilometres.
It has a 2 litre EFi petrol engine with 5 speed manual gearbox.
The tilt-cab allows access to the engine area, facilitating servicing, while the flat-deck with drop-sides, measures 3100mm X 1600mm, and the truck has a carrying capacity of 1,500kg.
With a GVM of 3285kg, the Toyoace can be driven with a car licence and requires 12-monthly WOF rather than COF.
So easy to drive, with power steering, air-conditioning, electric windows, and central locking, it would be suitable for many trades.
