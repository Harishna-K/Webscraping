The 2015 Toyota Aqua X-Urban is a more rugged, crossover-style variant of the Toyota Aqua (known in some markets as the Toyota Prius C). It features some key updates and upgrades over the regular Aqua.

### **Exterior Features:**
- **Sporty Crossover Design:** The X-Urban variant includes body cladding, a more rugged look with wider wheel arches, and a higher ride height compared to the standard Aqua.
- **Roof Rails:** Functional roof rails that add to the crossover aesthetic and provide utility for carrying extra gear or luggage.
- **Unique Alloy Wheels:** A distinct set of alloy wheels designed to give the X-Urban a more rugged and stylish appearance.

###**Interior Features:**
- **Comfortable Seating:** Upholstered seats that offer comfort for both short and long drives.
- **Increased Cargo Space:** The X-Urban has a practical hatchback design with ample cargo space for its class, and it includes a 60/40 split-folding rear seat for flexibility.

### **Hybrid Powertrain:**
- **1.5L Hybrid Engine:** The 2015 Aqua X-Urban is equipped with a hybrid powertrain that combines a 1.5-litre petrol engine with an electric motor, providing good fuel efficiency and eco-friendly performance.
- **ECO and EV Modes:** The car allows you to switch between different driving modes, such as ECO mode for better fuel efficiency and EV mode for electric-only driving at lower speeds.

This combination of hybrid efficiency, crossover design, and advanced features makes the 2015 Toyota Aqua X-Urban a versatile choice for eco-conscious drivers who need a compact car with extra functionality.
