Toyota Safety Sense, Bluetooth, 8-Airbags

Save fuel without compromising on style or technology with this 2019 Silver Toyota Prius! Equipped with a petrol hybrid engine, this eco-conscious hatchback features EV mode, cruise control, and advanced safety with 8 airbags and a reversing camera. Whether for city commutes or long-distance drives, the Prius delivers comfort, convenience, and sustainability.
