Bergs Potter

Add to MyMarket

Since 1942, Bergs Potter has crafted premium products for plants and people to thrive in harmony. Timeless designs crafted with love the traditional way. All made to last for generations. Bergs Potter makes plant pots and vases designed in Denmark and lovingly crafted at small potteries in Tuscany and Porto. Premium clay and a high-firing process make our terracotta pots strong and durable, yet porous, giving plants optimal growing conditions. Bergs Potter offers a wide range of pots, from the cute little bookshelf pot to the substantial floor pot, all with a watertight saucer, so they can be used both indoors and outdoors. Our glazed range includes a vibrant selection of indoor pots and vases in timeless colours. Bergs Potter, where life grows!

Folk

Folk is a range of plant stakes drawing inspiration from Indigenous art; each stake in the Folk collection is designed as a little figure, adding a playful element to your plant display. Whether nurturing delicate flowers or guiding climbing vines, Folk plant stakes offer steadfast support to help your plants grow strong and flourish.

Atlas Pot

The Atlas Pot in sturdy terracotta rests on the shoulders of its saucer counterpart. This pot, inspired by the mythical Titan Atlas, who carried the heavens on his shoulders, has repeating circular patterns of vertical lines to symbolize his tireless work. As with the Titan, the Atlas pot will last and be resilient when cared for.

Gaia Pot
