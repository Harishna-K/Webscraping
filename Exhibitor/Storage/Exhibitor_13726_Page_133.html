Porter Designs

Add to MyMarket

Porter Designs is a globally sourced lifestyle furniture supplier founded in 2006 by Julie Grant and David Weiss. What began as a vacation to Thailand became a passion for designing and sourcing unique and affordable furniture for today’s eclectic home. The line has grown to 1000+ products featuring a full range of upholstery, top grain leather, motion, accent chairs, occasional, dining and bedroom furniture. Porter Designs is committed to sustainability by partnering with manufacturers and factories using sustainable processes. We partner with organizations that follow social and humanitarian practices, sourcing our materials ethically. This allows us to create high quality, heirloom furnishing solutions that customers can keep longer, reducing the amount of furniture found in the landfill. Designed in the creative hub of Portland, Oregon, Porter Designs is proud to work with talented artisans to produce exceptional furniture. The quality craftsmanship can be seen in the details: rich solid wood case goods, tailored upholstery with reinforced sides and backs, and beautiful top grain leathers. Porter believes every piece of furniture should tell a story. Join us daily at the Centers of High Point on Hamilton #230 from 4 – 6pm to enjoy Northwest craft beers and wines.

Porter Designs strives to improve the impact that home furnishings manufacturing and shipping has on the environment. Their new fashion-forward Good Earth, Furniture with a Conscience upholstery collection is crafted from recycled and sustainable materials and covered with performance fabrics.

Porter Designs HP Fall 2020 Showroom Tour

Here's a tour of our October 2020 showroom at High Point!

Ekta Occasional Table

The Ekta Acacia Solid Wood Coffee and End Tables are a testament to modern craftsmanship. Their softly rounded edges and pedestal base form a silhouette that commands attention, while the wire-brushed and matte walnut-stained solid acacia wood accentuates the natural beauty and distinct grain pattern.

Udo Bed

Udo Bed features the elegance of black leather with the warmth of solid mango wood. The headboard, upholstered in black leather, features a woven basket pattern that adds texture and visual interest. The mango wood frame has a warm brown color and an entire bedroom collection to match.

Albina 2Pc Sectional

The Albina Modern Forest Green Sectional features plush upholstery wrapped in a luxurious forest green fabric, inviting you to sit and relax. Its sleek design, characterized by clean lines and a minimalist silhouette, makes it a standout in any contemporary setting. The sectional is surrounded by an elegant wood trip.
