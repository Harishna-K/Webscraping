NZ NEW, HARDLID, CAMBELT DONE, NO DEPOSIT FINANCE

The 2014 Volkswagen Amarok DC 420 2WD 2.0DT with a hardlid combines rugged capability, practical storage solutions, and great efficiency, making it the perfect choice for anyone needing a versatile, secure, and reliable vehicle.

Powerful & Efficient 2.0L Diesel Engine: The 2.0DT engine delivers 420Nm of torque, providing excellent towing power and impressive fuel efficiency for both work and leisure.

Double-Cab Versatility: Spacious double-cab configuration comfortably seats up to 5 passengers, making it perfect for both business and family use.

Hardlid for Added Security & Practicality: The fitted hardlid ensures that your cargo stays secure, protected from the elements, and out of sight, making it ideal for transporting tools, equipment, or valuable items.

Modern Interior & Technology: Features a comfortable, well-designed cabin with a user-friendly infotainment system, Bluetooth connectivity, air conditioning, and ample storage space.

Stylish & Rugged Design: Bold front grille and high ground clearance give the Amarok a rugged, commanding presence, while the hardlid adds a sleek, functional touch to the rear.

Comprehensive Safety Features: Includes ABS, ESP, airbags, and stability control to ensure both safety and peace of mind on the road.
