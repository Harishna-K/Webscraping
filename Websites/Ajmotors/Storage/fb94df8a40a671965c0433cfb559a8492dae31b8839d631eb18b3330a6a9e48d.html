# 2021 Mitsubishi Outlander VRX 2.P/4WD (NZ New)
{Stock number. 25557}

The 2021 Mitsubishi Outlander VRX 2.4P/4WD (NZ New) is a solid all-rounder in the mid-size SUV segment. It offers strong safety features, a spacious and comfortable interior, advanced technology, and excellent driving dynamicsboth on and off the road. Whether you need it for family use, towing, or occasional off-roading, the Outlander VRX has a good balance of capabilities.

# Key features
- Cruise control: If the vehicle in front slows down, the ACC will reduce the speed of the Outlander to match the pace of traffic. Once the road clears and it detects no vehicles in front, it will accelerate back to your preset speed.

- Heated seats: The heated seats are typically controlled via a button on the center console or the seat itself, depending on the model. There are usually two or three heat settings (low, medium, and high), allowing you to adjust the warmth to your comfort level.

- Sunroof: A panoramic sunroof is a large glass panel that spans a significant portion of the roof of the vehicle. It can often be tilted or fully opened to allow fresh air and sunlight into the cabin. The 2021 Outlander VRX offers a dual-pane sunroof, meaning it has two sectionsone for the front seats and another section for the rear passengers.
