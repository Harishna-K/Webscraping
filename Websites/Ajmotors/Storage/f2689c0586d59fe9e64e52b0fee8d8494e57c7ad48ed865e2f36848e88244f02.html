# 2016 Toyota CH-R G Hybrid
{Stock number. 24702}

The 2016 Toyota C-HR G Hybrid is a compact crossover SUV that was part of Toyota's debut hybrid version of the C-HR lineup. The C-HR (which stands for "Coupe High Rider") was designed to offer a mix of style, efficiency, and utility, and the hybrid model was aimed at those who wanted a more eco-friendly option.

# Key features
- ACC: Adaptive Cruise Control (ACC) is an advanced driver assistance feature that adjusts the vehicle's speed to maintain a safe following distance from the car ahead. This system uses radar and cameras to monitor traffic conditions, and if the vehicle in front slows down, the ACC will automatically reduce the C-HRs speed. If the road clears up, the system will accelerate back up to the preset speed.

- LDW: LDW uses cameras mounted on the vehicle to monitor lane markings on the road. If the system detects that the car is unintentionally drifting out of its lane without signaling, it will alert the driver with either visual or audible warnings.

- Pre crush safety: If the driver doesn't respond quickly enough to the warning, the system can automatically apply the brakes to reduce the severity of the collision or, in some cases, potentially avoid it altogether.
