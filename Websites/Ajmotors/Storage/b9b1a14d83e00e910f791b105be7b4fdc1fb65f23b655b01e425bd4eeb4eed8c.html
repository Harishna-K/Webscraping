# 2013 Subaru Impreza sport 1.6 L
{Stock number. 23831}

The 2013 Subaru Impreza Sport 1.6L is an economical, versatile, and safe option for those seeking a compact vehicle with all-wheel drive, perfect for both urban commuting and tackling varying road conditions. It offers a blend of functionality and fun, especially with the hatchback design for added practicality.

# Key features
- Push start button: The push start button (or keyless start) is a feature that allows you to start the engine of your vehicle without needing to insert a traditional key into the ignition. Instead, the car recognizes a key fob or smart key when it's inside the vehicle and, when you press the start button, the car's systems power on and the engine starts.

- Auto start stop: The Auto Start-Stop system is a fuel-saving feature found in many modern vehicles, including some trims of the 2013 Subaru Impreza Sport (depending on the market and model). The system is designed to automatically turn off the engine when the vehicle is at a complete stop (such as at traffic lights or in heavy traffic) and then restart it when the driver is ready to move again.

- Down hill assist: Downhill Assist Control (DAC) is a feature available on some vehicles, including certain trims of the 2013 Subaru Impreza Sport, designed to assist drivers when descending steep or slippery slopes. It helps maintain a controlled and steady speed when driving downhill, reducing the need for constant brake application, especially on challenging terrain or in slippery conditions.
