# 2019 Subaru Impreza G4 1.6L Eyesight
{Stock number. 24383}

The Subaru Impreza is often praised for its safety, practicality, and value, especially in markets where weather conditions (like snow and rain) are more severe. If you're looking for a compact sedan that combines advanced safety features with solid all-weather performance, the 2019 Subaru Impreza G4 1.6L Eyesight is an excellent choice.

# Key features
- Eyesight: Subaru EyeSight is a suite of advanced safety technologies designed to enhance the driver's awareness and help prevent accidents. It uses a combination of cameras and sensors to monitor the road ahead and assist with various aspects of driving

- LDW: LDW stands for Lane Departure Warning, a key feature found in many modern vehicles, including those equipped with Subaru's EyeSight system. It is a safety technology designed to help prevent unintended lane departures, reducing the risk of accidents caused by driver distraction or fatigue.

- ACC: ACC stands for Adaptive Cruise Control, an advanced driver assistance system designed to maintain a set speed while automatically adjusting to traffic conditions. It is an upgraded version of the traditional Cruise Control system, offering more convenience and safety, especially in heavy traffic and long-distance driving.
