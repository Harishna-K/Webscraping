# 2018 Kia Niro EX 1.6 Hybrid (NZ New)
{Stock number. 23926}

The 2018 Kia Niro EX 1.6 Hybrid is a compact hybrid crossover that combines a gasoline engine with an electric motor to deliver improved fuel efficiency and lower emissions. It's a part of Kias move towards more eco-friendly vehicles, offering a practical and affordable option in the growing hybrid SUV market.

# Key features
- Adaptive Cruise Control: If the system detects that the car in front is slowing down, ACC will automatically reduce your vehicle's speed to maintain a preset following distance (usually set by the driver). Once the road clears or the vehicle ahead accelerates, the system will bring your car back up to the set speed.

- Dual air condition: Dual-zone systems use sensors to monitor the temperature on both sides of the cabin. The system will adjust the airflow and air conditioning settings to maintain the set temperature for each side of the car.


- Apple car play: It allows you to use the apps you already have on your iPhone in a safe, easy-to-use format on your car's infotainment screen.

If you're looking for more information about the car, feel free to reach out! Whether you have questions about specific features or need assistance with anything else, our team is happy to help.
