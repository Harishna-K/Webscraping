# Key features
- LDW: LDW stands for Lane Departure Warning, which is a safety feature available in many modern vehicles, including the 2017 Toyota Camry. This system is designed to help prevent accidents caused by unintentionally drifting out of a lane without signaling.

- ACC: ACC stands for Adaptive Cruise Control, a safety feature that automatically adjusts a vehicle's speed to maintain a safe following distance from the car ahead. It is an enhancement of traditional cruise control, adding the ability to "adapt" to the flow of traffic.

- Pre crush safety: If the driver does not take action (such as applying the brakes), the system can automatically apply the brakes to reduce the severity of the collision or, in some cases, avoid the crash entirely.
