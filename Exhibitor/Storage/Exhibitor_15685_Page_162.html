The Mill USA

Add to MyMarket

100% Swakara Wool Rugs, Blankets, Pillows & Wall-Hangings The Mill USA began with the goal of helping alleviate poverty in southern Namibia, creating jobs and training artisans to handweave rugs & other wool goods. Our rugs are designed in the USA and handwoven in Namibia, Africa. The Mill USA connects two families on different continents. Michelle, a South African with a passion for job creation and a rich family heritage in the textile industry and Kathleen, an American interior designer with a love for Africa, beauty and social impact. "Lay one down & Lift many up!"

The Luca Rug

This uniquely patterned handwoven rug will add a layer of global flair to any space. Perfectly imperfect random stripes and no two knots woven exactly the alike, our Luca rug is truly one-of-a-kind. Made of 100% Swakara wool.

The Tehila Rug

Handwoven by skilled artisans in Namibia, our Tehila rug will make a statement in any space with its bold pattern. The organic texture of this rug adds an additional layer of interest! Hand-woven by artisans in Namibia. Made from 100% Swakara wool.

The Whitney Rug

Our Whitney rug is luxe and unique, handwoven by our skills artisans. The simplicity of the thin spun border framing this rug highlights its beautiful organic texture and will add a layer of interest on any floor. Made from 100% Swakara wool.
