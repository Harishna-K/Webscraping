# 2012 Toyota Sai S
{Stock number. 24032}

The 2012 Toyota Sai S is a mid-sized hybrid sedan that was primarily sold in Japan. It's based on the Toyota Camry, but with a unique design and hybrid technology to offer a more eco-friendly driving experience.

# Key features
- Cruise control: sing cruise control can help improve fuel efficiency, especially on long, straight highways, as it ensures that the vehicle maintains a constant speed and avoids unnecessary acceleration or deceleration.

- Parking sensors: These sensors are designed to help the driver park safely by alerting them to obstacles around the vehicle, especially in tight spaces.

- Key less entry: The 2012 Toyota Sai S comes equipped with keyless entry, a convenient feature that allows the driver to unlock and lock the car without physically using a traditional key.

If you're looking for more information about the car, feel free to reach out! Whether you have questions about specific features or need assistance with anything else, our team is happy to help.
