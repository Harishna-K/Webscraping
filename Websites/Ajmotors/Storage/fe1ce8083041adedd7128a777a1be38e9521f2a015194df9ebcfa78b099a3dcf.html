# 2013 Toyota Corolla Fielder Hybrid G
{Stock number. 23840}

The 2013 Toyota Corolla Fielder Hybrid G is a compact station wagon version of the Toyota Corolla, designed primarily for the Japanese market. It is part of the 11th generation of the Corolla, and the "Fielder" name refers to the wagon variant. The Hybrid G model features a combination of fuel efficiency and practicality, as it incorporates Toyotas hybrid technology, known for its environmental benefits and reduced fuel consumption.

# Key features
- Push start button: The Push Start Button (also known as Push-Button Ignition) is a modern feature found in many vehicles, including the 2013 Toyota Corolla Fielder Hybrid G. It allows you to start and stop the engine without needing to insert a traditional key into the ignition.

- Multi function steering wheel: he multi-function steering wheel in the 2013 Toyota Corolla Fielder Hybrid G (and many other modern cars) is designed to provide the driver with easy access to various functions without taking their hands off the wheel. This feature improves safety, convenience, and driving comfort.

- Heated seats: Heated seats use electrical heating elements embedded in the seat cushions and backrests. These elements are powered by the car's electrical system and can be activated via a switch, typically located on the side of the seat or on the dashboard or center console.
