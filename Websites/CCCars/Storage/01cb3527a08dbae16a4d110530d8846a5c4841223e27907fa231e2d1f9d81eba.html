The 2016 Toyota Corolla Fielder G TSS is a petrol-powered station wagon that combines practicality with advanced safety features. Key specifications include:

- **Engine:** 1.5-liter petrol engine, delivering efficient performance.
- **Transmission:** Automatic transmission for smooth driving.
- **Drive Type:** Front-wheel drive (2WD) configuration.

**Safety Features:**
- **Toyota Safety Sense (TSS):** Includes Pre-Collision Safety System and Lane Departure Alert to enhance driver safety. citeturn0search0

- **Airbags:** Comprehensive airbag system including front & side curtain airbags for driver and passenger protection.
- **Braking System:** Anti-lock Braking System (ABS) for improved vehicle control.

**Exterior Features:**
- **Design:** Sleek station wagon profile with a rear spoiler and alloy wheels.

- **Lighting:** Auto headlights for enhanced visibility.

**Interior Features:**
- **Seating:** Seating capacity for five, with fabric seats.
- **Climate Control:** Air conditioning with climate control for a comfortable cabin environment.

- **Infotainment:** Features include a CD player, AM/FM radio, and Bluetooth connectivity.
- **Windows:** Electric windows with auto-up/down functionality.

**Additional Features:**
- **Reversing Camera:** Assists with parking and reversing maneuvers.
