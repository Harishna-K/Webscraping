We take pride in our legacy of creating distinctive furniture, lighting, and décor easily distinguishable in the marketplace. Family-owned for over 20 years, we strive to cultivate enduring relationships with artisans, suppliers, partners, and creators by fostering an environment of trust, respect, and collaboration. Commitment to innovation, quality craftsmanship, and luxury design remains our focus. Our award-winning team aims to inspire customers to reimagine spaces by providing sustainably sourced designs that showcase the skills of local artisans from around the globe.

Channel Cocktail Table

Channel Cocktail Table encompasses an off-white leather console with channel stitching that cascades seamlessly into its waterfall edge. The Brass stretcher bars that cross and wind gracefully around each leg into a stunning brass sabot add sophistication and class.

Tesoro Box

The Tesoro Box presents an inset slate veneer top wrapped in smoke-grey leather with contrasting topstitching. The box is enhanced with a matte black hand-hammered cast iron handle and finished with a fully lined interior.

Gabarone Mirrror

The Gaborone mirror features a unique contoured hand-wrapped top stitched frame in luxurious dot print hair-on-hide leather. The walnut-finished filet adds a rich, warm touch that complements the printed leather, creating a striking visual contrast. This mirror is a true statement piece that adds dimensionality and luxury to any room.
