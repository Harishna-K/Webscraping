# 2019 Mitsubishi Outlander XLS 4WD 7seater (NZ New)
{Stock number. 25336}

The 2019 Mitsubishi Outlander XLS 4WD 7-seater (NZ New) is a well-rounded, mid-sized SUV that provides ample space, modern features, and a high level of reliability.

# Key features
- Cruise control: Adaptive Cruise Control automatically adjusts the vehicles speed to maintain a safe following distance from the vehicle ahead. Unlike traditional cruise control, which maintains a constant speed, ACC will reduce your speed if there is a slower vehicle in front and accelerate back up to your preset speed once the road clears.

- BSM: Blind Spot Monitoring (BSM) in the 2019 Mitsubishi Outlander XLS 4WD adds significant value in terms of safety and convenience, particularly when changing lanes on highways or driving in traffic. It's a helpful technology that can prevent dangerous accidents by alerting the driver to vehicles in areas that would otherwise be difficult to detect.

- Apple car play: Apple CarPlay is a feature available in the 2019 Mitsubishi Outlander XLS 4WD, designed to integrate your iPhone with the vehicle's infotainment system. This allows you to access various apps and features directly on the car's display, providing a safer and more convenient way to use your phone while driving.
