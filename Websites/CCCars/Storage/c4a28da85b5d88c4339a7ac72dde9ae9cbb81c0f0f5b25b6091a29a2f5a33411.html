G Model with Cruise Control

The 2014 Toyota Ractis G comes with a variety of features that make it a practical and versatile compact car.

**Engine**: Equipped with a 1.5-liter 4-cylinder engine, providing a good balance of power and fuel efficiency.

**Interior Space**: Known for its roomy interior, the Ractis offers flexible seating arrangements, with a versatile rear seat that can be folded down for additional cargo space.

**Safety Features**: Common safety equipment include multiple airbags, ABS with vehicle stability control, and a rearview camera.

**Exterior Design**: Compact size with a distinctive design, making it easy to maneuver in urban settings.

**Fuel Efficiency**: Generally known for good fuel economy, making it a cost-effective option for daily driving.
