Timeless Style, Driver Involvement and Performance

Welcome to Morgan New Zealand, and presenting to you, the brand new 2022 Morgan Plus Four - Available now for order, with your choice of either a six-speed manual, or an eight-speed automatic. Please note, the price listed is the starting price for this model, examples of options are shown in the sample photos as above As with any Morgan, the potential in specification is only limited by your imagination. Enquire within to discuss specific options in further detail, and start your journey with your new Morgan today.

Timeless style, driver involvement and performance. Since its launch in 1950, these characteristics have defined our most popular model. Seven decades on, the new Morgan Plus Four is unveiled, and while the traits that have made it so special remain, the technology which underpins it has been brought firmly into the 21st century.

When a design is as established as that of the Morgan sports car, evolution must come before revolution. Since its launch seven decades ago, the iconic silhouette has become a defining feature of the car, and thats not about to change. Even the details, such as its narrower bodywork a hallmark of Morgans core range of cars and unique wing profiling, have been preserved.

In the automatic variant, 0-62 mph takes just 4.8 seconds (manual model: 5.2 seconds), and the acceleration doesnt stop until the car reaches its top speed of 149 mph. In fact, performance of the all-new Plus Four is on par if not exceeds, in the real world that of the previous generation 3.7-litre Morgan V6 Roadster.

Far lighter and infinitely stiffer than the traditional steel frame chassis used in previous generations of the model, the CX-Generation platform transforms the driving experience of the Plus Four. Of course, the cars balanced and involving feeling remains, but with new levels of response and refinement.
