The 2012 Toyota 86, also known as the Scion FR-S in the U.S. and the Subaru BRZ (a joint project between Toyota and Subaru), is a popular lightweight sports car. It's known for its rear-wheel drive, low center of gravity, and great handling, which makes it a favorite among driving enthusiasts.

# Key features
- Paddle gear shifter: While not as precise as a manual transmission, the paddle shifters offered a more engaging driving experience compared to just letting the automatic transmission decide when to shift.

- Rear wheel drive: While not as precise as a manual transmission, the paddle shifters offered a more engaging driving experience compared to just letting the automatic transmission decide when to shift.

- Sport mode: Sport Mode sharpens the throttle response, making the car more responsive to your inputs. When you press the accelerator, the car will feel more eager and will respond more quickly, which is ideal for spirited driving and quick acceleration.
