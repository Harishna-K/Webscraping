The 2019 **Suzuki Swift RS HV** is a hybrid variant of the popular Suzuki Swift, combining a compact and sporty design with a fuel-efficient hybrid powertrain.

### **Powertrain and Performance:**
- **Engine:** The Swift RS HV features a **1.2L 4-cylinder Dualjet engine** paired with an electric motor.
- **Hybrid System:** The system uses the electric motor for additional torque assist and regenerative braking to recharge the battery, improving fuel efficiency and reducing emissions.

### **Exterior:**
- **Design:** The Swift RS HV has a sporty, compact hatchback design with aggressive styling elements like a honeycomb grille, sporty bumpers, and body-coloured side skirts.

### **Interior Features:**
- **Seating:** Comfortable seating for 5 passengers with high-quality fabric upholstery.
- **Climate Control:** Automatic air conditioning system to maintain a comfortable cabin temperature.
- **Storage:** A good amount of boot space for a compact car, and the rear seats can be folded to further increase cargo capacity.

### **Safety Features:**
- **Suzuki Safety Support:** The car is equipped with advanced safety technologies, including:
- **Lane Departure Warning (LDW):** Alerts the driver if the vehicle unintentionally drifts out of its lane.
- **Pre-Collision System (PCS):** Helps reduce the severity of a collision by applying braking assistance.
**ABS with EBD (Electronic Brakeforce Distribution):** Ensures improved stability during braking.

The **2019 Suzuki Swift RS HV** is an excellent choice for buyers looking for a compact, efficient, and stylish vehicle with a mild-hybrid powertrain that offers both sporty driving dynamics and outstanding fuel economy.
