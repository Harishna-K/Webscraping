# 2013 Toyota 86 GT
{Stock number. 24686}

The 2013 Toyota 86 GT, also known as the Scion FR-S in North America, is a compact sports car developed in collaboration with Subaru, featuring rear-wheel drive and a lightweight, balanced chassis. It is part of the Toyota 86 lineup, which is praised for its driving dynamics and accessibility as a sports car. The "GT" variant is the higher trim level, offering a sportier setup and more features than the base model.

# Key features
- Sport mode:
In the 2013 Toyota 86 GT, the Sport Mode is a feature designed to enhance the car's driving dynamics, offering a more engaging experience for those looking to push the vehicle harder on twisty roads or the track.

- Paddle gear shifters: When you are in "Sport Mode" (if activated), the automatic transmission shifts with higher RPMs, and the paddle shifters provide even more of a performance-oriented experience.

- Push button start:
The push-button start feature in the 2013 Toyota 86 GT adds convenience and modernity to the car, allowing you to start and stop the engine without needing to insert a traditional key into the ignition. Instead, as long as the key fob is inside the vehicle (typically in your pocket or bag), you can start the car with the push of a button.
