# 2013 Honda Stepwagon Spada Z
{Stock number. 23817}

The 2013 Honda Stepwgn Spada Z is a model from Honda's Stepwgn series, which is a minivan offering a blend of versatility, practicality, and a premium feel. The "Spada" is a more sportier trim compared to the regular Stepwgn and is designed with a sleek, stylish appearance and added features for a more refined driving experience.

# Key features
- Power sliding door: The power sliding doors can be operated with the press of a button, either from the drivers side or from inside the cabin. This makes it easier for passengers to get in and out of the vehicle without much effort.

- Push start button: The power sliding doors can be controlled using the key fob. This allows you to open or close the doors remotely, which is especially useful when your hands are full or when youre parked in tight spaces.

- Eco mode: The Eco Mode in the 2013 Honda Stepwgn Spada Z is a feature designed to optimize fuel efficiency by adjusting certain vehicle settings to reduce overall fuel consumption. Its particularly useful for drivers who want to get the most out of each tank of gas, especially when driving in urban areas, on highways, or during long trips.
