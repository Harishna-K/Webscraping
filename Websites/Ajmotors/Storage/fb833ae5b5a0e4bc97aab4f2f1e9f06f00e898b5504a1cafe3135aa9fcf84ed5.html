Drive in Style, PreCollision Warning, Push Button

Compact, stylish, and full of personalitythis 2016 Mitsubishi Mirage is the perfect city car! With a low odometer reading of just 109,000 km and a unique candy blue interior, its as fun to drive as it is to look at. Add in smart features like push-button start, forward collision warning, and fog lights, and this Mirage is ready to keep you safe and stylish on the road.

Fog Lights: Enhanced visibility in challenging conditions.
Compact and Economical: Perfect for city driving and easy parking.
