The Toyota Hilux SR TD DC 2.8D/6AT is a highly versatile and reliable vehicle suitable for both work and recreation, offering impressive towing capacity, off-road prowess, and a comfortable driving experience. Would you like to know more about specific options or comparisons to other vehicles in its class?

# Key features
- LDW: Lane Departure Warning (LDW) is a valuable tool in modern vehicles, helping to prevent accidents related to lane drifting by providing timely alerts to the driver. It is especially beneficial for drivers who travel long distances or who may experience fatigue.

- Cruise control: Cruise Control is a system that automatically controls a vehicle's speed, allowing the driver to maintain a constant speed without needing to keep their foot on the accelerator pedal. In the 2022 Toyota Hilux SR TD DC 2.8D/6AT, the Cruise Control system is likely part of the standard equipment, providing convenience and comfort during long trips or highway driving.

- Pre crush safety:
Pre-Collision Safety (PCS), also known as Pre-Collision System, is an advanced safety feature designed to reduce the severity or even prevent accidents by detecting potential collisions ahead of the vehicle and taking action to avoid or mitigate the impact.
