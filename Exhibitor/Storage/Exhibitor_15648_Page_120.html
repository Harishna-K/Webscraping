Neos & Co.

Add to MyMarket

At the heart of the Neos & Co. brand is a legacy of Greek craftsmanship, where modern design meets practical application. With over two decades of firsthand experience in the furniture industry, we strive to create pieces that rise above trends. All our pieces are made in Greece with a strong focus on sustainability, creating as little impact to the environment as possible. Timeless craftsmanship, innovative design, and a customer centric experience - These pillars encapsulate Neos & Co.'s essence. We accomplish this using cutting edge materials, innovative technologies, and an unparalleled eye for detail. Built with the client in mind, our pieces are made to be used. We uphold the highest standards in manufacturing, ensuring each piece of furniture reflects our love for design and will stand up to day-to-day use. From personalized service to bespoke solutions, we prioritize forging lasting relationships and exceeding expectations at every touchpoint.

Silea Sideboard
