Toyota Safety Sense - Dual Airbags - Economical

$2000 Minimum trade!! ( Conditions apply )

This superb late model, Toyota Vitz Hybrid equipped with Toyota Safety sense is a safe and economical 5 door hatch.
This lovely wee fuel saver is equipped with Dual airbags, Climate control air conditioning, Collision prevention, Lane departure warning, Vehicle stability control, 5 Shoulder belts, ISOFIX child seat anchors, Auto lights, Traction Control, ECO\EV Modes, Multi function steering wheel, UV Privacy glass and more!.
Looks great finished in Platinum silver complimented by a stylish Black interior with subtle patterned seat inserts and features Toyota's 1500cc DOHC chain driven engine with Hybrid Synergy Drive in front of a smooth automatic transmission giving great fuel efficiency.

Buyers considering Audi A1, Ford Fiesta, Holden Barina, Honda Fit, Hyundai i20, Mazda Axela, Skoda Fabia, Subaru Impreza, Suzuki Baleno, Volkswagen Golf or Polo should take a closer look at the Vitz HV as an option.

Finance and Protecta Insurance products are also available for this vehicle.

Pre-purchase inspection can be arranged for out of town purchasers.

You can buy with confidence from Value Cars in the knowledge we are a totally New Zealand owned and funded provider of good quality cars with great after sales service for over 30 years.
