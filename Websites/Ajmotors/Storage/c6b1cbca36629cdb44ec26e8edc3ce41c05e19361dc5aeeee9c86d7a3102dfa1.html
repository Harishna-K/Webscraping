# 2014 BMW 116I 1.6L Petrol
{Stock number. 25118}

2014 BMW 116i 1.6L A compact luxury hatchback offering dynamic performance, sleek design, and a premium driving experience.

# Key features
- Turbo charged: The 2014 BMW 116i 1.6L is equipped with a turbocharged engine. Specifically, it features a 1.6-liter turbocharged 4-cylinder petrol engine that delivers a balance of performance and fuel efficiency. The turbocharged setup allows the 116i to produce around 136 horsepower while maintaining relatively low fuel consumption, making it a sporty yet economical choice in the compact luxury hatchback segment.

- Rear wheel drive:
The 2014 BMW 116i 1.6L is front-wheel drive (FWD), not rear-wheel drive (RWD). While many BMW models, especially those in the 3 Series and above, feature rear-wheel drive, the 1 Series, starting from the 2012 model, adopted front-wheel drive for its smaller, more compact models like the 116i.

- Push button start: the driver can keep the key fob in their pocket or bag and simply press the start/stop button to activate the engine, adding an extra level of ease and sophistication to the driving experience.
