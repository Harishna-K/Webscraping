# 2016 Mercedes-Benz GLA 250
{Stock number. 23847}

The 2016 Mercedes-Benz GLA 250 is a compact luxury crossover SUV that blends sporty performance with elegant design. It sits between the subcompact GLA and the larger GLC in Mercedes' lineup, offering a more nimble and agile driving experience compared to its larger siblings.

# Key features
- Keyless entry: The GLA 250 is equipped with Push-Button Start. To start the vehicle, you dont need to insert the key fob into the ignition. Instead, once inside the car, simply press the brake pedal and push the "Start" button on the dashboard (usually near the steering wheel).

- Safety Features:
Active Brake Assist (automatically applies the brakes in case of an impending collision)
Attention Assist (warns the driver if signs of drowsiness are detected)
Blind Spot Assist and Lane Keeping Assist
Parktronic with Active Parking Assist for easier parking maneuvers
Rearview Camera standard

- Adaptive Cruise Control (ACC) is an advanced driver-assistance system that automatically adjusts the speed of your vehicle to maintain a safe distance from the car ahead, making highway driving more comfortable and less stressful.

If you're looking for more information about the car, feel free to reach out! Whether you have questions about specific features or need assistance with anything else, our team is happy to help.
