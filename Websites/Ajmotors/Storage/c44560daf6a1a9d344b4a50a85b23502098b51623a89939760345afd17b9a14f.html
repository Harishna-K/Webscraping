# 2014 Nissan Note X DIG-SEMA Brake P
{Stock number. 24402

2014 Nissan Note X DIG-SEMA: A compact hatchback with a turbocharged 1.2L engine, offering a perfect balance of fuel efficiency, practicality, and modern design for city driving.

# Key features
- Pre crush safety: The 2014 Nissan Note X DIG-SEMA, like many modern vehicles, is equipped with various safety features designed to reduce the risk of accidents and protect occupants. While the car may not have the advanced pre-crash safety systems seen in more recent models, it does offer certain key safety technologies aimed at preventing or mitigating the effects of a collision.

- LDW: Lane Departure Warning (LDW) is a safety feature that alerts drivers when their vehicle unintentionally drifts out of its lane without using the turn signals. It is designed to help prevent accidents caused by distracted or fatigued driving by providing an audible or visual warning when the vehicle crosses lane markings.

- Push button start: Push Button Start is a convenient feature that allows drivers to start or stop their car with the press of a button, rather than using a traditional key to turn the ignition. This technology adds a modern touch to the driving experience, making it easier to start your car, especially when your keys are in your pocket or bag.
