Sport, Carplay, Leather, Alloys, Push Button

Experience efficiency and style with this 2012 Black Toyota Aqua! Equipped with a hybrid petrol engine and modern features like Bluetooth, a reversing camera, and leather seats, this sporty hatchback is perfect for urban commutes and weekend adventures. Its eco and EV modes help you save on fuel while lowering your carbon footprint.

Highlights Youll Love:

Sporty black exterior with custom 16-inch alloy wheels.

Petrol hybrid engine for outstanding fuel efficiency and eco-friendly
driving.

Leather seats for a premium and comfortable cabin experience.

Bluetooth connectivity for hands-free communication and music streaming.

Reversing camera for added confidence when parking.

Push-button start for quick and easy ignition.

Fog lights for better visibility in low-light conditions.

Eco and EV modes for smarter, more efficient driving.

Compact yet versatile hatchback design, ideal for city driving.
