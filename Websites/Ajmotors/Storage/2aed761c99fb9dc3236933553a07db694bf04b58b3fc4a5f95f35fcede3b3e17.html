# 2017 Subaru Impreza G4
{Stock number. 24704}

The 2017 Subaru Impreza G4 refers to the sedan version of the Subaru Impreza, part of the fifth generation (also known as the "G4" in some regions). This model was introduced with several updates compared to previous versions, offering improved safety, technology, and performance features.

# Key features
- Eyesight: Subaru's EyeSight Driver Assist Technology is a suite of advanced safety features designed to enhance the safety of both the driver and passengers. First introduced in 2008, EyeSight uses cameras and sensors mounted at the top of the front windshield to monitor the road ahead and provide various driver assistance features.

- Adaptive Cruise Control (ACC): This feature maintains a set speed but also adjusts the vehicle's speed to maintain a safe distance from the vehicle ahead. It can slow down and speed up based on traffic conditions, making long highway drives much more convenient.

- Pre-Collision Braking: If the system detects an imminent collision with a vehicle or object in front, it can automatically apply the brakes to help reduce the severity of a collision or avoid it entirely. This is especially useful in sudden stop situations or during low-speed driving.
