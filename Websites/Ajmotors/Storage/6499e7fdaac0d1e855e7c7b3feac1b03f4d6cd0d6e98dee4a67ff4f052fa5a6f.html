4WD, TowBar, HardTop and SoftTop, Cruise Ctrl

Stand out on and off the road with this 2018 Matte Black Jeep Wrangler! Designed for adventure, this 4WD beast is powered by a V6 engine and sits on bold 18-inch custom alloy wheels. Whether you're cruising through the city or tackling rugged terrain, the hard-top sunroof, leather seats, and heated front seats ensure a comfortable ride. With a towbar, reversing camera, and Bluetooth connectivity, this Wrangler is as practical as it is powerful.

Key Features:

Tough matte black exterior with 18-inch custom alloy wheels.

4WD capability for conquering any terrain.

V6 engine delivering strong and reliable performance.

Hard-top sunroof to enjoy open-air freedom.

Leather seats with heated front seats for extra comfort.

Bluetooth connectivity for hands-free calls and music streaming.

Reversing camera for easier parking and maneuvering.

Towbar for hauling gear, trailers, or extra equipment.

Fog lights for improved visibility in low-light conditions.

Cruise control for smooth and effortless highway driving.

4 airbags for added safety and peace of mind.
