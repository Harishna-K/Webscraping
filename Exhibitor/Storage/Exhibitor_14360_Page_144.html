RW Collective "Formerly known as Rockford Wholesale"

Add to MyMarket

The RW Collective is a to-the-trade line featuring products from major brands in the furniture industry, carefully crafted with our retail partners in mind. A profound curiosity and passion for product design motivates us to develop pieces rich with historical and global influences and propels our continued efforts to evolve our product lines. The RW Collective serves our customers as a ONE Home resource with a wide range of offerings for high value and affordable sensibility. With showrooms in major market areas, our goal is to serve as a daily, year-round resource for designers and dealers. Our Atlanta showroom is open seven days a week – it is a significant, well-furnished space that is unbound by market schedules, which serves as a huge advantage for our clientele. We have made a customer service commitment to offering a streamlined ordering process that creates quick turnarounds, consolidated shipping, and higher profitability. Please share with us in this monumental mission and allow our design and customer service team to show you how we can service your immediate—and future—furniture and design needs.

Declan Swivel Chair

Ideal for design enthusiasts and designers seeking a stylish, comfortable, and functional addition to their space. The Declan Swivel Chair is a perfect blend of contemporary elegance and Scandinavian simplicity. Upholstered in luxurious boucle fabric, this chair exudes both comfort and style, making it an ideal addition to any room. 

Sephora Modular Sectional

Designed for both style and functionality, the Sephora Modular Sectional features a cozy bolster pillow on the back side, adding an extra layer of support and sophistication. Crafted with plush, ribbed fabric, this sectional offers an invitingly soft seating experience that makes relaxation effortless.

Massimo 60" Bench

Elevate your space with the Massimo Bench, a standout piece of occasional furniture designed to blend style and functionality seamlessly. Upholstered in luxurious boucle fabric, this bench offers a sophisticated texture that adds depth and elegance to any room.
