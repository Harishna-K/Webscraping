SPORT, PETROL, ECONOMICAL, NO DEPOSIT FINANCE

A perfect blend of fun, efficiency, and reliability, the 2012 Suzuki Swift Sport is a compact hatchback with a sporty edge. This well-maintained example offers nimble handling, a rev-happy engine, and an engaging driveall while remaining fuel-efficient and practical. With a sleek design, its ready for its next adventure.

Engine: 1.6L naturally aspirated petrol engine, delivering 134 hp for a lively, responsive drive.

Entertainment & Tech: Touchscreen infotainment system, Bluetooth connectivity, and premium sound.

Safety: Stability control, ABS, multiple airbags, and a reinforced chassis for peace of mind.

Utility: 5-door practicality with a surprisingly spacious boot for its class.
