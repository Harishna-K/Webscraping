LOW KM - POPULAR FAMILY WAGON - 8 AIRBAGS

Low km 2014 Toyota Corolla Rumion 1.5G family wagon, with proximity key, push button start, climate control, 8 airbags and has only travelled 44559km's! Looks sharp finished in Classic Silver Metallic, and has desirable Black interior with textured/patterned inserts.
