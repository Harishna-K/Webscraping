Drive in Style, New Tyres, Carplay, 18' Alloys

Experience luxury and performance with the 2014 Toyota Crown Hybrid Athlete S, a sophisticated sedan that seamlessly blends power with cutting-edge technology and style. This hybrid offers both efficiency and elegance, making every drive an elevated experience.

Hybrid Powertrain: Combines electric and petrol power for an impressive balance of performance and fuel efficiency, ideal for those seeking luxury with a reduced environmental footprint.

18-Inch Alloy Wheels with New Tyres: Equipped with sleek 18" alloy wheels and brand new tyres, this Crown Athlete S promises enhanced road grip and a stylish profile.

Cruise Control: Maintain your speed effortlessly on long drives with cruise control, adding comfort and convenience to every journey.

Apple CarPlay & Android Auto Compatibility: Stay connected and entertained on the go with seamless smartphone integration, allowing access to maps, music, calls, and messages right from the touchscreen.

9-Airbag Safety System: Comprehensive airbag coverage throughout the cabin provides maximum protection for both the driver and passengers in case of an impact.

Luxurious Interior Design: Step inside to experience plush seating, high-quality materials, and a thoughtfully designed cabin that surrounds you with comfort and elegance.

Advanced Handling and Performance: Known for its smooth handling and responsive drive, the Crown Athlete S offers a sporty, refined driving experience that stands out in its class.

This 2014 Toyota Crown Hybrid Athlete S offers a compelling mix of luxury, advanced technology, and hybrid efficiency. Perfect for those who want a stylish, high-performance sedan with the benefits of a hybrid, this Crown Athlete S stands ready to impress.
