8-Airbags, Cruise Ctrl, Sport Sedan, Push Button

Step into style, comfort, and safety with this 2017 Silver Honda Civic! Designed with advanced features like 8 airbags, a reversing camera, and brake hold, this sleek sedan delivers peace of mind while offering the convenience of Bluetooth, push-button start, and ECON mode. Perfect for both daily commutes and weekend road trips, this Civic is a true standout.

Key Features:

Elegant silver exterior paired with stylish 16-inch alloy wheels.

Safety-first design with 8 airbags and brake hold technology.

Push-button start for a modern and effortless driving experience.

Reversing camera for added convenience when parking.

Bluetooth connectivity for hands-free calls and seamless audio
streaming.

Cruise control for relaxed and efficient highway driving.

ECON mode for enhanced fuel efficiency.

Climate control for personalized comfort no matter the weather.
