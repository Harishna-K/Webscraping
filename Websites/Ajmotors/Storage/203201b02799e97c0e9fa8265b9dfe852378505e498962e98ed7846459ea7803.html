# 2020 Toyota Highlander GX 3.5P/4WD/8AT NZ New
{Stock number. 25252}

The 2020 Toyota Highlander GX 3.5P/4WD/8AT is a solid, versatile SUV offering a reliable engine, excellent safety features, and a comfortable ride for families. While it isnt the most fuel-efficient option in its class, its blend of performance, space, and practicality makes it a well-rounded choice for those who need an all-around capable vehicle. If you prioritize safety, comfort, and towing capability, the Highlander is a great option.

# Key features
- Pre crush safety: The system provides visual and audible warnings to alert the driver when a potential collision is detected. These warnings help the driver take immediate corrective actions, like braking or steering.

- LDW: LDW stands for Lane Departure Warning, and in the 2020 Toyota Highlander GX 3.5P/4WD/8AT, it is part of Toyota Safety Sense 2.0 (TSS 2.0). The Lane Departure Warning system is designed to help the driver stay safely within their lane on the road by providing alerts when unintended lane departure is detected.

- ACC:
ACC stands for Adaptive Cruise Control, and in the 2020 Toyota Highlander GX 3.5P/4WD/8AT, its part of the Toyota Safety Sense 2.0 suite (TSS 2.0). Adaptive Cruise Control is an advanced version of traditional cruise control that helps maintain a set speed and a safe distance from the vehicle ahead.
