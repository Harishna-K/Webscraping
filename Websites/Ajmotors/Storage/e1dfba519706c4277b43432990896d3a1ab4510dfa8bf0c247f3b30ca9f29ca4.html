24144

The 2021 Hyundai Santa Fe TM 7ST 4WD (NZ New) is a great choice if you're looking for a modern SUV that offers a comfortable ride, advanced features, and a solid build. The "TM" series refers to the fourth-generation Santa Fe, which was first introduced in 2018. It has a more refined design and comes packed with the latest technology and safety features.

# Key features
- LDW: It typically works by using cameras or sensors to monitor the lane markings on the road, and if the vehicle starts to veer off without signaling, the system will provide an audible or visual warning to the driver.

- ACC: With ACC, the system can automatically adjust the vehicle's speed to maintain a safe distance from the car ahead. If the vehicle in front slows down, ACC will automatically reduce the speed to match, and once the road clears, it will accelerate back to your set speed. Some systems can even bring the car to a complete stop in heavy traffic and resume driving when traffic starts moving again.

- Pre crush safety: Forward Collision-Avoidance Assist (which includes Pre-Collision Safety) is standard, adding an extra layer of protection for both the driver and passengers. Its especially useful in urban environments where sudden stops and unpredictable traffic can occur.
