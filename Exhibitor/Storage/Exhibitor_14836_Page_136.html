Rainbow Outdoor

Add to MyMarket

Rainbow Outdoor, was established in 1985 in Turkey. The production of Plastic Garden Furniture has started in early 2002 and became a pioneer of the industry over the years. The company kept developing consistently and introduced variety of models in large color preferences inside its facilities which have been built on 50.000 m2 closed area in Gaziantep, south of Turkey. The company is also continuing its RD and renovation works consistently to develop more items every year. Thanks to our high levels of in-stock inventory and high-speed production opportunity, we guarantee fast and on-time delivery as well as ergonomic and aesthetic designs. Over past years, Rainbow Outdoor has tremendously expanded its product range through Rattan, Wedding, Aluminum series. Today, Rainbow Outdoor North America is able to offer large variety group of products that appeal any level of budget without compromising our quality standards. We provide furniture for resorts, motels, hotels, bars, cafes, restaurants, houses, villas, aqua parks, shopping centers, hospitals, schools besides super chain markets, DIY stores. Becoming known worldwide as we supply products in over 62 countries and create values by consistently developing to satisfy our customer needs as best as we can. sales@rainbowoutdoor.com www.rainbowoutdoor.com
