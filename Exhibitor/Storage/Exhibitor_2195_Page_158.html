Madrone Lounge Chair

The eye-catching silhouette of the Madrone Lounge Chair will stun from all angles. The curved lines of the three-legged brown oak wood frame lend a statuesque presence to any room. The low back seat is upholstered in ludlow sesame leather for luxurious comfort.

Boise Nesting Coffee Tables (set of 2)

Free-flowing curves give the Boise Nesting Coffee Tables fluid silhouettes that create the perfect accent in any living space. Crafted from aluminum with antique brass and antique bronze finishes, these versatile tables can be nested together or arranged separately, offering flexibility and style.

Baz Barstool

Bold and stylish, the Baz Barstool is sure to turn heads. Featuring a daring silhouette upholstered in napa black faux leather. Gold stainless steel detailing and channel stitching add depth and visual impact to the overall look of the piece.
