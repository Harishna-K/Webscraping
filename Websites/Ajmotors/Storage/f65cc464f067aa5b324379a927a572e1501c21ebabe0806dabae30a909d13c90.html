# 2014 Toyota Sai G
{Stock number. 2330}

The 2014 Toyota Sai G is a hybrid sedan known for its fuel efficiency and eco-friendly features. It combines a gasoline engine with an electric motor to offer a smooth driving experience and reduced emissions.

# Key features
- Cruise control: You can set your desired speed, and the system will maintain it until you manually change it or disengage it.

- Key less entry: To unlock, just touch the sensor on the door handle. The doors will unlock automatically.

- EV Mode: EV mode is only available if theres sufficient battery charge.

If you're looking for more information about the car, feel free to reach out! Whether you have questions about specific features or need assistance with anything else, our team is happy to help.
