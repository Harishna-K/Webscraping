This is a 2016 Mazda Demio (Mazda2) 5 door hatchback which looks very smart in silver with 15-inch alloy wheels, and has travelled only 39,700 kilometres,
It has the later version of the 1300cc engine with SkyActiv i-stop technology, along with 6 speed automatic transmission which delivers peppy performance with 5L/100km fuel economy.
The Demio has good access and visibility, electronic stability control, climate control, smart key starting/locking, six airbags, and a rear-view camera, with a 4-star safety rating.
A very nice looking car, with nicely co-ordinated interior.
