# 2018 Honda Civic SX (NZ New)
{Stock number. 24798}

The 2018 Honda Civic SX (NZ New) offers a solid combination of stylish design, advanced safety features, and reliable performance. Its a well-rounded compact car suited for those looking for a modern, fuel-efficient vehicle with plenty of tech and comfort. Whether you're commuting in the city or heading on a weekend road trip, the Civic SX delivers a smooth, enjoyable driving experience.

# Key features
- Apple car play: Apple CarPlay is an in-car infotainment system that allows you to seamlessly integrate your iPhone with your car's built-in display. It makes it easier and safer to use certain apps, make calls, send messages, and get directions while driving.

- Smart key: The 2018 Honda Civic SX comes with a smart key system (Honda calls it the "Proximity Key"). This allows for keyless entry and push-button start.

- Cruise control: Cruise control is a system in modern vehicles that automatically controls the speed of the car without the driver needing to keep their foot on the accelerator. This feature is commonly used for highway driving, where maintaining a consistent speed is beneficial for both comfort and fuel efficiency. I
