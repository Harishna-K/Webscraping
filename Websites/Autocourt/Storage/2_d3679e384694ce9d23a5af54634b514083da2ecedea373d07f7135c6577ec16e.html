This is a superb 2017 Toyota C-HR S Hybrid, in grey with 17 inch alloy wheels, that has travelled only 52,000 kilometres
It is powered by a 1.8-litre 73kW petrol engine coupled to 60kW electric motor, the same combination as the hybrid Corolla and Toyota Prius.
This hybrid combination results in a 4-star, 4.2L/100 economy rating along with a 5-star VSRR safety rating.
A very smart looking car, it comes with many features including six airbags, stability/traction control, cruise control, frontal collision mitigation, lane departure warning, and reversing camera.
