4WD LEATHER

Powerful Performance: Powered by a 3.2L turbo diesel engine, delivering 147kW and 441Nm of torque for confident towing and off-road capability.

True 4WD Capability: Features a sophisticated 4WD system with selectable drive modes, ready for any terrainfrom rugged trails to smooth highways.

Luxurious Leather Interior: High-quality leather upholstery provides a premium, comfortable cabin experience for up to seven passengers.

Spacious and Versatile: A generous interior with flexible seating arrangements, ensuring both passenger comfort and ample cargo space.

Advanced Safety Features: Equipped with a range of safety technologies, including Multi-Mode ABS, stability control, and rear-view camera for peace of mind.

Towing Power: A robust towing capacity of up to 3.0 tons, making it ideal for trailers, boats, and caravans.

Infotainment & Connectivity: Enjoy a 7-inch touchscreen, Bluetooth, USB, and premium audio, keeping you connected and entertained on the go.

Climate Control: Dual-zone climate control for personalized comfort in both the front and rear seats.

Proven Durability: Renowned for its toughness, reliability, and low maintenance costs, making it a smart long-term investment.

Off-Road Excellence: Whether it's a weekend adventure or a daily commute, the Pajeros solid 4WD system ensures smooth handling and performance on any surface.
