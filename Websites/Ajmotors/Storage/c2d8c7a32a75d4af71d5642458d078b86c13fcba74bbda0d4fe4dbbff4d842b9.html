# 2019 Honda CR-V Hybrid EX
{Stock number. 23486}

The 2019 Honda CR-V Hybrid EX is the hybrid version of the popular Honda CR-V, offering an eco-friendly alternative to the traditional gas-powered model.

# Key features
- Electronic hand brake: The electronic parking brake is controlled by an electric motor and a set of sensors. When you push the button, the system activates the brake by applying force to the brake pads at the rear wheels.

- Key less entry: A key fob (sometimes called a remote key) or smart key is a small electronic device that communicates wirelessly with your vehicle.
The fob contains a radio transmitter that emits a signal, which the cars receiver picks up when you're close by.

- Honda Sensing is a suite of advanced safety and driver-assistive technologies designed to enhance the safety, convenience, and confidence of drivers and passengers. It is available on most new Honda models, and it helps reduce the likelihood of accidents by using a combination of cameras, radar, and sensors to monitor the vehicle's surroundings and assist the driver in various driving situations.
