Luonto Furniture Inc

Add to MyMarket

Luonto Furniture, a family-owned Finnish company now in its third generation, has been creating exceptional quality furniture since 1964. With a dedication to sustainable materials and innovative manufacturing processes, Luonto minimizes environmental impacts while offering a wide range of functional and stylish products. Hailing from Finland, Luonto Furniture takes pride in its heritage, emphasizing both the quality of its products and the preservation of the environment. The US Stock Program, warehoused in South Carolina, ensures quick access to their unique offerings, while the Made to Order option allows for customization to suit individual preferences. Luonto's functionality-focused category is their most popular and features a diverse selection of motion pieces, including powered king-size sofa sleepers, bunk bed sleeper sofas, and space-saving solutions for smaller areas. As the go-to provider for fine functional furniture, Luonto Furniture invites you to explore their impressive collection at IHFC Design - 1036 during this market. Discover for yourself why Luonto Furniture is a leading choice for those seeking quality, sustainability, and exceptional design.
