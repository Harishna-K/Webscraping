The **2015 Mazda Axela S Touring** is a well-equipped variant of Mazda's popular compact car (known as the **Mazda3** in many markets). The "S Touring" trim comes with a balance of sporty features, advanced technology, and comfort.

### Technology and Convenience:
- **Mazda Connect System**: The in-car entertainment system, which includes Bluetooth, and smartphone integration
- **Keyless Entry**: Smart keyless entry system, making it easy to enter and start the car.
- **Push Button Start**: Standard for easier vehicle startup.
- **Rearview Camera**: Standard rearview camera to assist with parking and reversing.

### Safety Features:
- **i-ACTIVSENSE Safety Features**
- **Blind Spot Monitoring System**: Alerts you to vehicles in your blind spots.
- **Anti-lock Braking System (ABS)**: Helps maintain control during emergency braking.
- **Traction Control System (TCS)**: Prevents wheel spin during acceleration.
- **Electronic Stability Control (ESC)**: Helps maintain vehicle stability in slippery or challenging conditions.
- **Airbags**: Front and side
- **Steering Wheel Controls**: Controls for audio, cruise control, and hands-free phone calls.
- **Leather-Wrapped Steering Wheel and Shift Knob**: Adds a more premium feel to the interior.
- **Cruise Control**: Standard cruise control for long-distance driving convenience.

Overall, the **2015 Mazda Axela S Touring** is a fun-to-drive, fuel-efficient compact sedan with a well-rounded mix of technology, safety features, and comfort. It stands out in its segment for sporty driving dynamics and high-quality interior materials.
