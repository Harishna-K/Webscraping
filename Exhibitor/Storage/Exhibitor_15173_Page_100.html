Loll Designs

Add to MyMarket

Made in Duluth, Minnesota, partially with recycled post-consumer plastic, Loll’s modern outdoor furniture adds a unique and modern aesthetic to outdoor spaces. Founded on principles of efficient and responsible use/ reuse of manufacturing materials, Loll was conceived as a way to repurpose excess skate ramp material from our former company, municipal skatepark creator. Loll began by creating a modern Adirondack chair with the durable skate-proof material that was proven to require no maintenance and hold up in all weather conditions. Appreciate the outdoors with maintenance-free furniture made by Loll Designs. Learn more about Loll’s sustainability efforts at lolldesigns.com/sustainability.

A La Carte

From a food prep space to garden storage, a counter for creating cocktails, to a dining table for backyard barbecues, this customizable collection allows you to create your ideal space a la carte with countless configurations.

Slider Storage Chest

Storing outdoor lounge cushions, blankets, and pillows doesn’t have to be a hassle. Simply place them in the Slider Storage Chest, close it, set your drink on top, and boom: you’ve got a coffee table and a storage solution all in one. Up to eight Lollygagger cushions fit inside.

Keene Collection

The all new Keene Collection is making it's debut at High Point Market this Fall and releasing to the public this November. Keene is Loll's first metal frame with HDPE component family of seating products with a design that is more trim in stature that also provides an accessible seat height and integrates our tried and true poly material.
