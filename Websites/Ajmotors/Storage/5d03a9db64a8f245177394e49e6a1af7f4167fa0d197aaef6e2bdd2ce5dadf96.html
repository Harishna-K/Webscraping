# 2016 Toyota Aqua G GS
{Stock number. 24682}

The Toyota Aqua G GS is designed more for efficiency than outright power. It accelerates smoothly and quietly, with the hybrid system automatically switching between the engine and electric motor based on speed and driving conditions. Its lightweight design also contributes to its overall performance.

# Key features
- Push button start: To start the car, you need to have the key fob on your person (e.g., in your pocket or bag). The car uses a proximity sensor to detect when the key fob is nearby.

- Leather and fabric seats: The side bolsters and outer parts of the seats often feature leatherette (or in some regions, actual leather), which enhances the overall look and feel of the seats, giving them a premium, sporty touch. These materials are easier to clean and maintain compared to full fabric seats.

- Pre crush safety:
The 2016 Toyota Aqua G GS comes equipped with a range of pre-collision safety features as part of Toyota's Toyota Safety Sense C (TSS-C) package, which includes technologies designed to prevent or mitigate collisions. These systems are part of the broader trend of using advanced safety tech to protect occupants and reduce the likelihood of accidents.

If you're looking for more information about the car, feel free to reach out! Whether you have questions about specific features or need assistance with anything else, our team is happy to help.
