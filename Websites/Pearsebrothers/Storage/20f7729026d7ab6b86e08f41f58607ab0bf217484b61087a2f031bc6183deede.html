4WD, PETROL, AUTO, NO DEPOSIT FINANCE

If you're looking for a practical and capable SUV that delivers comfort, safety, and all-weather performance, this 2018 Subaru Forester 2.5L Petrol AWD is a fantastic choice. Known for durability, spaciousness, and off-road capability, its perfect for families, weekend explorers, or daily commuters who value confidence on the road.

Performance & Efficiency
Engine: 2.5L 4-cylinder Boxer engine (126kW / 235Nm) for smooth, reliable power.

Symmetrical AWD: Subarus legendary All-Wheel Drive system ensures superior grip in wet, snowy, or rough terrain.

X-Mode & Hill Descent Control: Enhanced off-road capability for slippery or steep conditions.

Interior & Comfort
Spacious Cabin: Seats five adults comfortably with ample headroom and legroom.

Premium Cloth Upholstery with ergonomic seating for long drives.

Large Cargo Space: Rear seats fold flat for extra storage, perfect for road trips or outdoor gear.

Infotainment & Technology
Touchscreen Infotainment System with intuitive controls.
Steering Wheel Controls for audio, cruise control, and hands-free phone operation.
