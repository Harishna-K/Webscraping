Drive in Style, Bluetooth, Reversing Camera

This eye-catching 2020 Suzuki Ignis, in a vibrant yellow finish, is the perfect compact SUV for those looking for a unique and efficient ride. With its sporty look and practical features, this car offers the right balance of fun, safety, and modern tech for city driving and beyond.

15-Inch Hubcap Wheels
Stylish yet practical hubcap wheels provide a smooth ride while complementing the Ignis' fun and compact design.

Dual Airbags
Equipped with front driver and passenger airbags, this Ignis ensures essential protection in the event of a collision.

Traction Control
Maintain stability and control, especially in slippery conditions, with the advanced traction control system, enhancing safety on all surfaces.

Bluetooth Connectivity
Stay connected hands-free with Bluetooth, allowing for seamless calls and music streaming without distraction.

Reversing Camera
Effortless parking and maneuvering with the reversing camera, providing a clear view of what's behind you for added safety and ease.

1.2L Engine (1200cc)
This fuel-efficient 1.2L engine offers a perfect mix of performance and economy, making it ideal for both city commutes and longer drives.

Low Mileage (38,784 km)
With just 38,784 km on the odometer, this Suzuki Ignis has plenty of life left, offering you peace of mind with its low mileage.

This 2020 Suzuki Ignis is a compact powerhouse, combining smart safety features, fuel efficiency, and a bold aesthetic. Whether you're navigating through city streets or heading out on a weekend trip, this versatile vehicle is designed to meet your needs with style and comfort.
