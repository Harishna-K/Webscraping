Sea & Soul Charts

Add to MyMarket

Crafting Memories: The Art of Sea & Soul Charts Welcome to our world of bespoke coastal home decor, where every piece tells a unique story that celebrates the beauty of craftsmanship, personal connection and the enduring allure of the coast. Sea & Soul Charts crafts beautiful coastal home decor featuring 3D nautical wood maps of lakes, islands and coastlines from around the world. Combining a creative use of new technology along with time honored techniques, our products are handcrafted one at a time – the way it used to be. It is our hope that each chart calls to mind your own cherished family memories made along the shorelines that inspire them. Explore our collection and let the joy and memories of the shore redefine your living spaces.

Coastal Accent Tables

Beautiful Accent Tables for All Your Favorite Coastal Locations! From beloved shorelines to iconic lighthouses, each chart tells a story of maritime adventures. Handcrafted of solid pine, our accent tables are available in 7 frame stains and 2 water color stains. Made in the USA using the timeless values of true craftsmanship.
