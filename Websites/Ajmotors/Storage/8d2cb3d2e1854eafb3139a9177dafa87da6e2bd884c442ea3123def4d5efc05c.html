# 2021 Mazda 6 GSX PTR 2.5/6AT (NZ New)
{Stock number. 23672}

The 2021 Mazda 6 GSX is a stylish and well-equipped sedan, popular in New Zealand. It features a 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission, offering a balance of performance and efficiency.

# Key features
- Adaptive cruise control: It uses sensors to monitor the distance to the vehicle ahead. If that vehicle slows down, the adaptive cruise control will automatically reduce your speed to maintain a safe following distance.

- Dual air conditioning: Each side of the cabin can be adjusted to different temperatures, catering to individual comfort preferences.

- Electric parking brake: Engaging the parking brake is as simple as pressing a button, making it more convenient than pulling a lever.

If you're looking for more information about the car, feel free to reach out! Whether you have questions about specific features or need assistance with anything else, our team is happy to help.
