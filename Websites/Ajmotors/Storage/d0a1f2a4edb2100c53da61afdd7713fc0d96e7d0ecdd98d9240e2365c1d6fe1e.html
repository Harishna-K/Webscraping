360 Reversing Camera, Apple CarPlay, Adaptive Cruise Control

2017Leaf 25167

The 2017 Nissan Leaf FE is an eco-friendly electric vehicle that combines zero emissions with practical design. With its spacious interior, advanced technology, and impressive range, it's perfect for city driving. Discover the future of driving at AJ Motors in Henderson!

# Key Features

- 360 Reversing Camera : Provides a comprehensive view around the vehicle to assist with parking and reversing.

- Apple CarPlay: Allows seamless integration of your iPhone with the car's infotainment system for hands-free navigation, calls, and music control.

- Adaptive Cruise Control: Automatically adjusts the vehicle's speed to maintain a safe following distance from the car in front.

If you're looking for more information about the car, feel free to reach out! Whether you have questions about specific features or need assistance with anything else, our team is happy to help. For more details or to arrange a test drive:
