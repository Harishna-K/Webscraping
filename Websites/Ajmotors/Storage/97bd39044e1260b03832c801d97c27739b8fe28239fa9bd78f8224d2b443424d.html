2.0L, Sport, Bluetooth, 18' Alloys, Push Button

Looking for a sporty hatchback with low mileage and impressive features? This 2013 Mazda Axela Sport is a standout with just 80,000 km on the clock and a powerful yet efficient 2.0L petrol engine. Stylish, practical, and loaded with conveniences like push-button start, Bluetooth connectivity, and a reversing camera, this Axela is ready to elevate your daily drive.

Low Mileage: Only 80,000 kmplenty of life left to enjoy.
Push-Button Start: Modern convenience at your fingertips.
Powerful & Efficient: 2.0L petrol engine for smooth performance.
18-Inch Alloy Wheels: A bold, sporty finish.
Bluetooth Connectivity: Stay hands-free and connected.
Reversing Camera: Easy parking and enhanced visibility.
6 Airbags: Advanced safety for you and your passengers.
Hatchback Versatility: Perfect for city driving or weekend getaways.
