The 2013 Toyota Auris (Corolla) 1.5S
. **Fuel Efficiency**
- The 1.5L engine offers a good balance of power and fuel economy, making it an attractive option for those looking to save on fuel costs.
- Toyotas reputation for reliable fuel-efficient engines adds to its appeal.
**Comfort and Interior Space**
- The cabin offers a well-designed interior with ample space for passengers and luggage.
- Quality materials are used throughout the cabin, ensuring a comfortable driving experience.

. **Safety Features**
- Toyota always places a strong emphasis on safety, and includes:
- Multiple airbags
- ABS with EBD (Electronic Brake-force Distribution)
- Stability and traction control
- ISOFIX child seat anchors

**Smooth Ride & Handling**
- The suspension system ensures a smooth ride, while the responsive steering makes handling relatively easy.

. **Reliability**
- As with most Toyota models, the 2013 Auris 1.5 offers exceptional long-term reliability, with a reputation for low maintenance costs.

. **Practicality**
- A decent-sized boot space for everyday needs, with the option to fold the rear seats to create more cargo room if necessary.
- Rear-seat space is decent, making it a comfortable ride for passengers.

**Stylish Design**
- The 2013 Auris has a sleek, modern design with clean lines and a sporty front end that gives it a youthful, dynamic look.
