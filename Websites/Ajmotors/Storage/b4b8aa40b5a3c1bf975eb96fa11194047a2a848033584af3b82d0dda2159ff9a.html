# 2017 Toyota C-HR G Led Edition
{Stock number. 23822}

The 2017 Toyota C-HR G LED Edition is a compact crossover SUV that stands out for its unique design, advanced technology, and practical features. It's part of Toyota's effort to appeal to a younger, more style-conscious market, offering a bold, futuristic look combined with solid performance and a comfortable driving experience.

# Key features
- Pre crush safety: The pre-collision safety system is a crucial feature in modern vehicles, designed to reduce the risk of accidents by detecting potential collisions before they occur. In the case of the 2017 Toyota C-HR, its part of the Toyota Safety Sense (TSS) suite of active safety technologies.

- Parking sensors: The parking sensors in the 2017 Toyota C-HR are part of the vehicle's comprehensive suite of safety and convenience features designed to assist drivers in parking and avoiding obstacles. These sensors are typically integrated into the front and rear bumpers, helping to detect objects or obstacles in close proximity to the vehicle when maneuvering in tight spaces.

- ACC: Adaptive Cruise Control (ACC) is a key feature of modern vehicles, designed to enhance convenience, comfort, and safety during long drives or highway cruising. In the 2017 Toyota C-HR, it is part of the Toyota Safety Sense (TSS) suite of active safety technologies, which is aimed at reducing driver fatigue and improving safety by maintaining a safe following distance from the vehicle ahead.
