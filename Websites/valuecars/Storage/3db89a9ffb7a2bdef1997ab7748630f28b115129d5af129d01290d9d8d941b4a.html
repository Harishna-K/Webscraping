SOUGHT AFTER HYBRID WAGON - GOOD KMS - 8 AIRBAGS

2014 1800cc Toyota Prius v/Alpha S Hybrid, economical, spacious, 5 door family wagon with climate control, push button start, 8 airbags, reverse camera and has only travelled 80516km's. Looks smart finished in Magnetic Grey Metallic Black with driving lights, rear spoiler and on 16 inch alloys with fitted wheel covers and new tyres, has a stylish Dark Grey suede look interior complimented by Black dash & door inserts. Features an 1800cc Hybrid Synergy Drive engine & automatic transmission with really great fuel efficiency, also has a 5 star UCSR safety rating.

The Toyota Prius v, also named Prius (pronounced as Alpha) in Japan, and Prius+ in Europe, is a full hybrid gasoline-electric compact MPV produced by Toyota Motor Corporation introduced in Japan in May 2011, and it is the first Prius variant to be spun off from the Prius platform. The exterior styling is rounded with an extended roofline that allows the vehicle to retain the triangular silhouette symbolic of Prius. Other differences are the trapezoidal-shaped front with sharp-edged headlamps and wind-slipping aero corners for improved aerodynamics, as well as an enlarged under grille. The rear styling has a specially designed roof spoiler that improves air flow.

Seating is provided for 5 within the cabin with good head and leg space. A higher seating position along with wide opening door spaces ease entry and exit. The rear wagon cargo space can easily be enhanced with folding of the rear seats. The interior cargo space is more than 50% larger than the 2010 Toyota Prius, 5 inches longer and 1 inch wider, providing 34.3 cubic feet (970 L) of cargo space behind the rear seats.

Prius v fuel economy was rated 5.3 L/100 km for city driving and 5.9 L/100 km for highway, and a combined rating of 5.6 L/100 km According to Toyota, the Prius v produces 66 percent less smog-forming emissions than the average new vehicle.
