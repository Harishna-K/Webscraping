FlowDecor was founded in 2006. Our goal was to create a curated line of lighting and decor that was always evolving. We focus on contemporary pieces with unique design. We skip the temporary fads and hope our quality pieces remain in people’s homes for years to come. With strong operational backgrounds and a love for home decorating, this husband & wife team thought they could improve upon some of the issues inherently found in this industry. We offer beautiful designs coupled with great value and outstanding customer service.
