Brand New Tyres - Sporty - 8 Airbags

Value Cars is proud to present this sporty looking Hybrid Prius fitted with the "Modellista" factory option aero and a nice set of 17" aftermarket alloys with brand new tyres!.
This 3rd Gen model is finished in the stunning Black Onyx Mica and features the sought after Black interior trim!
Well equipped with 8 Airbags, Climate control air cond, ISOFIX, 5 shoulder belts, Smart key with Button Start, Multi-function steering wheel, Trip computer and of course impressive fuel economy and a proven record for reliability.

Finance and Protecta Insurance products are also available for this vehicle.

Pre-purchase inspection can be arranged for out of town purchasers.

You can buy with confidence from Value Cars in the knowledge we are a totally New Zealand owned and funded provider of good quality cars with great after sales service for over 30 years.
