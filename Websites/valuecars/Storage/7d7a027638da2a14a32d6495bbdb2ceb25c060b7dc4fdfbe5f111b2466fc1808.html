Low KMs - 4 Airbags - Cruise Control

Low km 2015 Renault Lutecia (Clio) Intense, 5 door European family hatch with climate, cruise control, push button start, 4 airbags, reverse camera, USB and Aux connectivity and has only travelled 39701km's. Really stands out, finished in Candy Red Metallic with Black factory body kit like styling side accents and Black mirrors, with driving lights and has 17 inch alloys with Red highlights to compliment the exterior paint. Has a stylish Black interior with Red inserts in the seats, dash and door panels. Features a 1.2L turbo charged DOHC VVT engine and tiptronic transmission with good fuel efficiency.
The cabin comfortable seats five from a higher seating position which provides good head and legroom and also aids entry and exit. A height adjustable drivers seat enables the driver to easily find a suitable position for both comfort and good visibility. Split folding rear seats provide versatility of the rear cabin.

Finance and Protecta Insurance products are also available for this vehicle.

Pre-purchase inspection can be arranged for out of town purchasers, and we can also ship at a cost of only $250 to NZ main
centres.

You can buy with confidence from Value Cars in the knowledge we are a totally New Zealand owned and funded provider of good quality cars with great after sales service for over 25 years.
