# 2014 Toyota GT86
{Stock number. 24888}

The 2014 Toyota GT86 is a compact, lightweight sports car that emphasizes driving engagement and agility. It was developed alongside the Subaru BRZ and shares much of its design and engineering with its sibling. The GT86 features rear-wheel drive, a low center of gravity, and a well-balanced chassis, making it a favorite among driving enthusiasts.

# Key features
- Fujitsubo exhaust: Fujitsubo is a well-known Japanese manufacturer of high-performance exhaust systems, particularly recognized for producing high-quality, well-engineered parts for a variety of vehicles, including sports cars like the Toyota GT86. Fujitsubo exhaust systems are known for improving engine performance, enhancing exhaust sound, and providing a smooth, durable build.

- Sport mode:
Sport mode in vehicles like the 2014 Toyota GT86 typically refers to a driving mode designed to enhance the car's performance characteristics, providing a more dynamic and responsive driving experience. When you activate Sport mode, several aspects of the car's behavior are adjusted to offer sharper performance, handling, and a more engaging driving feel.

- Heated seats: Heated seats are a comfort feature in many modern cars, including the 2014 Toyota GT86, that provide warmth to the seat cushions and backrests. This feature is particularly useful in colder climates or during winter months. In the 2014 Toyota GT86, heated seats are generally available on higher trim levels or as an option.
