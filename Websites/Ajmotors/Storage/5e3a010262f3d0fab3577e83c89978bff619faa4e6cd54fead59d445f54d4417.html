# 2015 Toyota Sienta Hybrid G
{Stock number. 23812}

The Toyota Sienta Hybrid G uses Toyotas Hybrid Synergy Drive system, which combines a gasoline engine and electric motor to deliver both power and efficiency. This system switches between the electric motor and gasoline engine, depending on driving conditions, providing optimal fuel economy.

# Key features
- Power sliding door: The power sliding doors are one of the standout features of the Sienta, especially for families. They make it much easier to access the rear seats, particularly in tight parking spaces or when theres limited room to open regular swing doors fully.

- Push button start: The push-button start system in the 2015 Toyota Sienta Hybrid G is a modern convenience feature designed to make starting the car quicker and more efficient, without the need for a traditional key ignition.

- Multi function steering wheel: The multi-function steering wheel in the 2015 Toyota Sienta Hybrid G is designed to enhance convenience, allowing drivers to control various functions without taking their hands off the wheel. This feature improves safety, comfort, and ease of use while driving.
