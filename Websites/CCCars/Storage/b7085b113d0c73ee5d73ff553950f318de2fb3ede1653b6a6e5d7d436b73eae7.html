The **2018 Toyota Corolla Fielder 1.5X** is a practical and efficient compact station wagon, designed for those who need more space than a sedan but prefer the size and efficiency of a compact vehicle.

- **Fuel Economy**: The 1.5X is known for its fuel efficiency, offering around **1618 km/L**, depending on driving conditions.

- **Storage and Cargo Space**: The Fielder offers **525 liters of cargo space** with the rear seats up, which is more than enough for grocery runs, luggage, or sports equipment. The rear seats can be folded down to expand the trunk space even further.

**Safety Features**:
- **ABS with EBD**: Anti-lock Braking System (ABS) and Electronic Brake-force Distribution (EBD) to improve braking performance.
- **Vehicle Stability Control (VSC)**: Helps maintain control of the car in slippery conditions or while cornering.
- **Traction Control (TRC)**: Works with VSC to prevent wheel spin when accelerating on slippery surfaces.
- **Child Safety Features**: ISOFIX anchor points for child seats.

**Exterior Design**:
- **Body Style**: The Corolla Fielder 1.5X is a compact station wagon, offering the practicality of a larger vehicle while still being small enough for urban driving.

**Driving Experience**:
- **Ride Comfort**: The Corolla Fielder 1.5X offers a comfortable ride with a suspension system tuned for a smooth driving experience, absorbing bumps well in urban and highway conditions.

- **Eco Mode**: A driving mode that optimizes the engine and transmission for better fuel efficiency, ideal for city driving.
- **Power Windows and Door Locks**: Standard on the 1.5X trim for added convenience.
