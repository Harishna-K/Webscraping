A 2010 Toyota Prius S Touring Hybrid, in silver, on 17" alloy wheels, that has travelled 48,500 kilometres..
The Prius is a hybrid with a 1.8 litre petrol engine that develops 73kW combined with a 60kW electric motor and a 1.3kWh Ni-MH battery, resulting in 4.2L/100km economy.
UCSR 5-star safety is provided by six airbags, ABS, electronic stability control/traction control, and the car comes with CD, climate control, rear-view camera, and smart key starting & locking.
The Prius is a useful sized hatchback, with great carrying capacity with the rear seat folded down.
