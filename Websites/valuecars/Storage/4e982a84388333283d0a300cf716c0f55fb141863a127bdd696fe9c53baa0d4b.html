8 Airbags - G Package - Cruise Control

Presenting this popular Toyota Vanguard G package! The "G" grade delivers a much more luxurious trim level than lesser models in the range.
Powered by the reliable chain driven 2.4 engine and also features a Combination leather trim, Heated front seats, Electric front seats, 8 Airbags, DAC, ABS, Traction control, Dual Zone Climate control, Proximity key with push button Start, Indicator Mirrors, Cruise Control, Reverse Camera, HID Headlights, ISOFIX Child seat anchors and so much more!

Finance & Protecta Insurance products are also available for this vehicle.

Pre-purchase inspection can be arranged for out of town purchasers.

You can buy with confidence from Value Cars in the knowledge we are a totally New Zealand owned & funded provider of good quality cars & great after sales service for over 30 years.
