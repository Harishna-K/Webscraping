LumiSource LLC

Add to MyMarket

LumiSource, LLC has been a leading supplier in the home furnishings industry since 1997, designing stylish and affordable products for every budget. We proudly partner with top national chains, independent retailers, and online stores. Specializing in Mid-Century, Glam, Contemporary, and Transitional designs, we offer a wide range of furniture for living, dining, office, and lighting spaces—adding style, functionality, and versatility to any room. Committed to innovation, our talented in-house design team creates unique, trend-setting products that combine functionality with affordability. We're always evolving and expanding our collections to bring fresh and new designs to life.
