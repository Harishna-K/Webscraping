Tidy 2014 Mitsubishi Outlander 20G 2WD wagon in brown with 18 inch alloy wheels, that has travelled 81,500 kilometres.
This has a 2-litre "MIVEC" petrol engine with CVT transmission, and is front wheel drive.
A practical wagon with two extra seats in the rear that fold down to provide a large carrying capacity.
With 5-star safety in mind, the Outlander has 7 airbags, stability/traction control, and cruise control.
It also comes with CD, climate control, smart key, and rear-view camera.
A very nice wagon.
