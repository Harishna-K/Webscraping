Lavin Rugs

Add to MyMarket

Lavin Rugs, a family-owned company based in Fort Worth, Texas, is rooted in exploring the naturalist spirit and bringing it to life in the form of tangible artistry. As a brand focused on craftsmanship, quality, and philanthropy, Lavin Rugs is committed to blending centuries-old techniques with modern rug designs. The company's latest collections feature contemporary designs inspired by natural landscapes and geometric patterns, combining beauty with durability.

Lavin Rugs Grand Opening At High Point Market

Lavin Rugs, a family-owned company based in Fort Worth, Texas, is proud to announce the grand opening of its new showroom at High Point Market in the IHFC Building suite C-212.

New Brand Partnership Between Lavin Rugs and Tamara Day

Lavin Rugs, an upstart rug manufacturer under the Quorum International Inc. umbrella, has teamed up with celebrity designer Tamara Day. Day’s four new rug collections will debut at High Point Market this October. The collections will include 25 styles, from modern designs to rich earthen palettes.

Sachin Collection | Beige Cream

The Sachin Collection of hand-tufted eco-friendly rugs, created from a blend of wool and Jude, demonstrates our profound commitment to quality and sustainability. The textural geometric design and mixed pile types make this soft stain-resistant rug stand out in any space. This rug is available in two shapes and various sizes.

Vani Collection | Cream Green

The Vani collection of hand-tufted ivory rugs, made from a wool blend, will create an inviting feel to your space. Featuring a global-inspired geometric design with a Moroccan flair, this soft, eco-friendly rug provides natural insulation, is stain-resistant, hypoallergenic, and perfect for adding warmth and style to any room.

Mitra Collection | Grey Ivory

The Mitra collection of indoor rugs, crafted from a wool and cotton blend, will add a pop of geometric elegance to any style. This hand-tufted rug has a modern tonal design and is pet/kid-friendly, stain-resistant, and hypoallergenic. Made from natural, eco-friendly fibers, it offers softness and durability —made for stylish, sustainable living.
