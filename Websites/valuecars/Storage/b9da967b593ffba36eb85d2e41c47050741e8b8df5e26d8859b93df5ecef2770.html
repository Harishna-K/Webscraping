Presenting this popular Toyota Vanguard with Sports Package!
Powered by the reliable chain driven 2.4 engine this high grade S package features Factory aero, Paddleshift Tiptronic and 18" Alloys.
Also featured are 8 Airbags, DAC, ABS, Traction control, Dual Zone Climate control, 2 Proximity keys with push button Start, Indicator Mirrors, Cruise Control, Reverse Camera, HID Headlights, ISOFIX Child seat anchors and so much more!

Finance & Protecta Insurance products are also available for this vehicle.

Pre-purchase inspection can be arranged for out of town purchasers.

You can buy with confidence from Value Cars in the knowledge we are a totally New Zealand owned & funded provider of good quality cars & great after sales service for over 30 years.
