- Pre crush safety:
Pre-Crash Safety is a suite of advanced safety technologies designed to detect an impending collision and take preventive actions to reduce the severity or even avoid the crash altogether. In Toyota vehicles, this is typically part of the Toyota Safety Sense (TSS) package, specifically the Pre-Collision System (PCS).

- ACC: ACC stands for Adaptive Cruise Control. It is an advanced driver assistance system designed to maintain a set speed while also automatically adjusting the vehicles speed to keep a safe distance from the car ahead.
