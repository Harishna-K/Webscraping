# 2020 Holden Colorado LTZ
{Stock number. 22037}

The Colorado LTZ is often praised for its comfortable ride, off-road capabilities, and overall value in the midsize pickup segment.

# Key features
- 4WD: Full-time 4WD with a shift-on-the-fly system, allowing you to switch between 2WD and 4WD while driving.

- LDA: In some systems, LDA can provide gentle steering inputs to help guide the vehicle back into the lane.

- Multi function steering: Easy access to essential controls improves the overall driving experience, making it more enjoyable and user-friendly.
