Tempaper & Co.

Add to MyMarket

At Tempaper & Co. we are dedicated to bringing beauty into your home with a range of innovative wall décor solutions. Since our inception in 2008, we have revolutionized the home décor industry with our signature peel and stick wallpaper, breathing new inspiration into spaces with ease. Along with peel and stick wallpaper, Tempaper & Co. also offers peel and stick wall murals, authentic grasscloth, unpasted wallpaper and artisan area rugs. Embrace our diverse assortment of patterns, textures, and colors, and easily design interiors that are expressive and beautiful. Join us in transforming your space with Tempaper®, where every product is a testament to our commitment to innovation, quality, and style.
