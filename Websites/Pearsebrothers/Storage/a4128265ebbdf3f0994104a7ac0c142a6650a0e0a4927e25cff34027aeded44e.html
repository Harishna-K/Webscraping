4WD, LEATHER, TONNEAU COVER, NO DEPOSIT FINANCE

Conquer the toughest terrains in style with this 2019 Holden Colorado Z71. With low mileage of just 66,000kms, luxurious leather interior, and off-road-ready 4WD capability, this ute is built to impress.

Low Mileage: Only 66,000kms, offering reliability and plenty of life ahead.

Powerful Engine: 2.8L turbo diesel delivers exceptional torque for towing and off-roading.

4WD Capability: Designed to handle any terrain with ease and confidence.

Premium Leather Interior: Comfortable and durable leather upholstery with heated front seats for a touch of luxury.

Advanced Technology: Features a touchscreen infotainment system with Apple CarPlay, Android Auto, and navigation.

Rugged Style: Bold exterior design with Z71 decals, black accents, and sleek alloy wheels.

Utility Ready: Factory-fitted tow bar, sports bar, and ample tray space for work or play.
