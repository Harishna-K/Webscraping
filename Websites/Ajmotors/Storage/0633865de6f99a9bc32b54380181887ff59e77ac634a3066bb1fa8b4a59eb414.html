# 2018 Subaru XV 2.0L Eyesight AWD
{Stock number. 23835}

2018 Subaru XV 2.0L Eyesight AWD offers a solid balance of performance, safety, and practicality, especially for buyers who enjoy outdoor activities or need a car that can handle a variety of weather conditions. Its a well-rounded compact SUV with a focus on reliability and technology.

# Key features
- Eyesight: Subaru's Eyesight Driver Assist Technology is a suite of advanced safety features designed to enhance driving safety by helping to prevent accidents and assist the driver in various situations. Introduced by Subaru, it uses a combination of cameras, sensors, and algorithms to monitor the vehicle's surroundings and provide real-time interventions to assist with driving.

- ACC: Eyesight's Adaptive Cruise Control (ACC) maintains a set speed while also adjusting the vehicles speed based on traffic conditions. If traffic slows down or a vehicle cuts in front of you, the system automatically reduces the cars speed. Once the road clears, the vehicle returns to the preset speed.

- Pre crush safety: Pre-Collision Safety (or Pre-Collision System, sometimes referred to as Pre-Crash Safety) is a key component of modern vehicle safety technologies, and it is designed to help prevent or reduce the severity of a collision.
