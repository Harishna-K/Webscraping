Truett Designs

Add to MyMarket

Truett Designs combines luxury artistry with premium craftsmanship, offering a curated collection of textiles and designer wallpaper. Our collections include elegant pillows, throws, fabrics, and wallpaper, each piece sustainably crafted in England and inspired from one of Truett's paintings. From statement wallpaper to luxurious textiles, our products are designed to bring a timeless, refined touch to any home. We are designer friendly, and have lots of options for customization.

Centerpiece Luxury Throw in Blue Hydrangea

Truett Designs will debut their plush oversized velvet throws, inspired by a hydrangea painting and handcrafted in England. Available in 7 stunning colors, these 55" X 75" machine-washable throws from the Centerpiece Collection combine luxury and practicality for the perfect cozy addition to any home.

The Gardens Luxury Throw Pillow in Zennia

Inspired by a Truett original abstract landscape painting and handcrafted in England, Truett Designs introduces The Gardens Collection luxury throw pillows. This 22" X 22" pillow, available in 6 colors and 5 sizes, features a machine washable cotton/linen cover and feather insert.
