5 Speed Manual - Low KMs - Bluetooth

Low km 2015 third generation rare 5 speed manual model Mazda Axela four door family sedan with push button start, multi-function steering wheel and has only travelled 43550kms! Looks sharp as a tack finished in Artic White with sleek wind cheating shape and has the desirable Black interior complimented by textured patterned inserts. Features an economical 1600cc engine with the 5 speed manual transmission with good fuel efficiency, also has a 5 star UCSR safety rating.
Axela has quickly become a very popular mid sized family sedan, preferred in it's class by many. Offering seating for five with good head and leg room. A higher seating position along with wide opening door spaces allow for ease of entry and exit. A height adjustable drivers seat allows the driver to easily find a position for comfort and good visibility, there is also an adequately sized boot as would be expected in a modern family sedan.
Buyers considering the likes of Subaru Impreza, Liberty, Suzuki Baleno, Corolla, Volkswagen Golf, should take a look at the Mazda Axela/Mazda 3 as there are similar features or efficiency comparisons that could be made.

Finance and Protecta insurance products are also available for this vehicle.

Pre-purchase inspection can be arranged for out of town purchasers.

You can buy in confidence from Value Cars in the knowledge we are a totally New Zealand owned and funded provider of good quality cars with great after sales service for over 30 years.
