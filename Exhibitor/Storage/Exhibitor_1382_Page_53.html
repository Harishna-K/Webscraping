Elements International

Add to MyMarket

Elements International is an energetic and progressive furniture wholesaler that offers every style from traditional to contemporary in bedroom, upholstery, dining, gaming and occasional to retailers across the country. Home to a powerhouse of experts with 30 plus years of experience in the furniture industry, we have the skills necessary to provide our customers with quality, on-trend designs and unmatched customer service. In addition to an extraordinary furniture line up, we are also among first and few wholesalers in the industry to offer mixed container programs, allowing retailers to minimize their costs and maximize their profits. Our competitive prices, exceptional quality, and excellence in customer service are ultimately what make Elements International leaders in the furniture industry.

Panorama Bedroom

Transform your bedroom into a posh retreat with the Panorama bedroom group. This collection features a minimalist design, faux marble case tops, and fluted front panels. The curved headboard and footboard add flair, while flared legs and black knobs complete the look, blending timeless and modern charm.

Panorama 5-Piece Dining Group

Introducing the Panorama 5-Piece Dining Group: this sophisticated collection features a stunning two-tone design with a faux marble tabletop and rich dark woodgrain finish. The fluted table base boasts high-end fashion, while plush ivory seating ensures elegant comfort. This set is perfect for a modern yet timeless and inviting dining space.
