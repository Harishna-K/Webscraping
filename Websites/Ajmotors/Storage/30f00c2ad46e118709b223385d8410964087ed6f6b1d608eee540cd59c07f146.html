# 2017 Honda Vezel Hybrid HV sensing
{Stock number. 24393}

The 2017 Honda Vezel Hybrid HV Sensing is a practical and efficient choice for urban commuting with excellent safety features, a well-appointed interior, and a hybrid powertrain for better fuel efficiency. Its Honda Sensing suite of advanced safety features further enhances its appeal, making it an ideal choice for those looking for a compact and eco-friendly crossover with peace of mind on the road.

# Key features
- LDW: The Honda Sensing system that comes with the Vezel Hybrid HV Sensing adds extra safety by not just warning the driver of lane departure but also incorporating features like Lane Keeping Assist (LKA), which can gently steer the vehicle back into its lane if the driver doesn't react.

- ACC: ACC stands for Adaptive Cruise Control. Its an advanced driver assistance feature that helps maintain a set speed and adjusts the car's speed to keep a safe distance from the vehicle ahead. The 2017 Honda Vezel Hybrid HV Sensing includes this feature as part of the Honda Sensing safety suite, which is designed to enhance driving convenience and safety.

- Pre crush safety: Pre-Collision Safety (sometimes referred to as Pre-Collision Braking or Collision Mitigation in Honda's system) is a critical safety feature designed to help reduce the risk of an accident. It's part of the Honda Sensing suite of driver-assistance technologies available in the 2017 Honda Vezel Hybrid HV Sensing.
