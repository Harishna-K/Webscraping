Theodore Chest - LA30361

The Theodore Chest LA30361 makes a dramatic statement with its modern interpretation of a 19th-century English Gentleman's dresser. Veneered in quartered figured Eucalyptus and finished in our signature Belvedere finish, it exudes luxury. With oversized drawers offering ample storage for clothing or linens, this four-drawer chest is practical, too.

Billie Chair - U185-C1

A 1940s decorator classic, the Billie Chair U185-C1 brings retro style to any room with its compact yet comfortable armless silhouette. Perfectly proportioned and featuring a soft blend-down seat, this small accent chair is ideal for adding vintage charm to your living room or any space needing a stylish accent.

Carrie Dining Chair - U187-D2

With slim proportions and clean lines, the Carrie Chair U187-D2 offers a casual yet sophisticated appeal. Designed for both comfort and durability, this lounge chair features casters for easy maneuverability, making it a versatile addition to any space.
