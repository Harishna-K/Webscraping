The 2015 Mazda Axela (known as the Mazda3 in some markets) 15S Touring is a well-equipped hatchback with a balance of performance, comfort, and technology.

### **Interior Features:**
- **Seating:** Fabric upholstery with 5-passenger seating capacity
- **Bluetooth Connectivity:** For hands-free phone calls and audio streaming
- **Climate Control:** Automatic climate control
- **Steering Wheel:** Leather-wrapped steering wheel with audio and cruise control buttons

### **Safety Features:**
- **Airbags:**
- **Stability Control:** Dynamic Stability Control (DSC)
- **Traction Control:** Traction Control System (TCS)
- **Anti-lock Braking System (ABS)**
- **Brake Assist:** Emergency Brake Assist (EBA)
- **Rearview Camera:** Standard in some markets for improved visibility

### **Comfort and Convenience:**
- **Keyless Entry:** Standard keyless entry and push-button start
- **Power Windows:** Power windows with one-touch up/down feature
- **Power Door Locks:** Power door locks with remote keyless entry
- **Drivers Seat Adjustment:** Manual adjustments for driver seat
- **Cruise Control:** Standard cruise control
- **Push-Button Start:**

### **Handling and Driving Experience:**
- **Chassis:** SKYACTIV technology for improved driving dynamics and fuel efficiency
- **Suspension:** Independent front and rear suspension for better ride quality and handling
- **Steering:** Precise and responsive electric power steering

The 2015 Mazda Axela 15S Touring is a compact car that emphasizes a blend of sporty driving dynamics, fuel efficiency, and comfort with an impressive suite of standard features for its class.
