# 2016 Toyota Prius New Shape
{Stock number. 24839}

The 2016 Toyota Prius, often referred to as the "new shape" due to its updated design, represents the fourth generation of the iconic hybrid vehicle. This version of the Prius showcased a major shift in styling and technology compared to previous models.

# Key features
- Push button start: To start the Prius, you simply press the brake pedal and then push the "Power" button, which is typically located on the dashboard or near the center console. The car will start up without the need to insert a traditional key into the ignition.

- Multi function steering wheel: The multi-function steering wheel in the 2016 Toyota Prius is designed to enhance driver convenience and safety by allowing the driver to control various functions without taking their hands off the wheel.

- LED headlights: LED headlights in the 2016 Toyota Prius offer a combination of better performance, energy efficiency, longer lifespan, and a more modern aesthetic, making them an excellent feature for a hybrid vehicle designed for efficiency and style.

If you're looking for more information about the car, feel free to reach out! Whether you have questions about specific features or need assistance with anything else, our team is happy to help.
