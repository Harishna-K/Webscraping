8 Airbags - Cruise Control - Lane Departure Warning

2016 economical spacious new shape Toyota Prius, This is powered by a 1800cc hybrid engine and is optioned with 2 Proximity keys with Push button start, Collision avoidance, Lane departure warning, Cruise control, Climate air conditioning, ABS, Traction control and 8 airbags!
This very tidy Prius has only travelled 95672km's! and looks sharp finished in Glacier White on 15 inch alloys with new tyres, and has a stylish Black interior.

Finance & Protecta Insurance products are also available for this vehicle.

Pre-purchase inspection can be arranged for out of town purchasers.

You can buy with confidence from Value Cars in the knowledge we are a totally New Zealand owned & funded provider of good quality cars & great after sales service for over 30 years.
