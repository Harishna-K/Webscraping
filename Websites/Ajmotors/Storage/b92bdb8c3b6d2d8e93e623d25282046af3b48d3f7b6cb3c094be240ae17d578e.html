Whether you're commuting through the city or exploring off the beaten path, the Vitara Turbo offers a thrilling drive with efficiency and versatility."

# Key features
- Paddle gear shifter: The paddle gear shifter in the 2022 Suzuki Vitara Turbo (NZ New) offers drivers the ability to manually control gear changes without needing a traditional manual transmission. This feature is typically found in cars equipped with an automatic transmission, and it provides a more engaging and responsive driving experience.

- Pre crush safety: Pre-crash safety systems, also known as pre-collision systems, are an advanced safety feature designed to detect potential collisions and help prevent or mitigate the severity of an accident. In the case of the 2022 Suzuki Vitara Turbo (NZ New), this feature is part of Suzuki's Safety Sense suite, which incorporates various technologies to enhance driver and passenger safety.

- Cruise control: Cruise Control is a popular driver assistance feature that allows the driver to set and maintain a constant speed without having to keep their foot on the accelerator pedal. In the 2022 Suzuki Vitara Turbo, Adaptive Cruise Control (ACC) is included as part of the Suzuki Safety Sense suite, offering an enhanced version of traditional cruise control for improved convenience and safety.
