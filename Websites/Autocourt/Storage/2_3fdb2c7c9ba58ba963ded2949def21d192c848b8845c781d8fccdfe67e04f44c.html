This 2014 Toyota Avensis Li Sportwagon in white, with 17 inch alloy wheels has travelled 52,500 kilometres.
The Avensis is designed and assembled in Europe, and the European road-handling characteristics are quite evident.
The Avensis has a number of safety features including multiple airbags, (inc. driver's knee, side and curtain) electronic stability control, and traction control, resulting in a 5-star VSRR safety rating.
It also has part leather/suede type seats, CD, climate control, cruise control, and a rear-view camera.
A very desirable wagon with a good sized carrying capacity, that would suit a variety of uses, business or private.
