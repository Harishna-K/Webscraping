Galerie Wallcoverings

Add to MyMarket

Galerie Wallcoverings is an international wallpaper designer and distributor. Celebrating over 30 years of business, Galerie is internationally renowned for it's high quality, stylish wallcoverings and excellent customer service. Contact our friendly sales team today at 1-800-985-9151 to find a retailer and wallpaper design that will suit your every need. We encourage the purchase of a wallpaper sample from our website to see and feel any designs you may be interested in purchasing.

Smart Stripes 3 Collection Shirt Stripe Wallpaper Roll

A thin, evenly spaced two tone design in gentle colorways that will suit interiors where you want your walls to have interest but not overpower the overall scheme. A great choice for a bedroom or living room, or anywhere where you want a light, positive influence.

Smart Stripes 3 Collection Breton Stripe Wallpaper Roll

A narrow stripe that has an element of fun about it. The proportions allow the paper to be hung horizontally as well, meaning you can create your own aesthetic and combine with any of the other oversize stripes.

Smart Stripes 3 Collection Awning Stripe Wallpaper Roll

This classic stripe brings a timeless elegance to your home. The smooth texture brings a warm feeling of quality and the vertical lines will bring a formal feel to any room.
