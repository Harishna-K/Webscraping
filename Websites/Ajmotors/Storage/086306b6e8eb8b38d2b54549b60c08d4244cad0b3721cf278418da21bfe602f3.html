# 2014 Porsche Cayenne Diesel 3.0L 4WD
stock number {21866}

The 2014 Porsche Cayenne Diesel has a 3.0L V6 engine producing 240 hp and 406 lb-ft of torque, paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission and 4WD. It offers excellent handling, impressive fuel efficiency, and a spacious, luxurious interior with advanced technology.

# Key Features:
- Downhill Assist: This feature helps maintain a controlled speed when descending steep inclines, enhancing safety and stability.

- Dual Air Conditioning: Allows both the driver and passengers to set their preferred temperature, ensuring comfort for everyone.

- Pre-Crash Safety: This system detects potential collisions and can initiate safety measures, such as tightening seat belts or preparing brakes.

- New Zealand GPS: A navigation system specifically tailored for New Zealand, providing accurate maps and points of interest.

- Multi-Function Steering Wheel: This allows you to control various functions (like audio, phone, and cruise control) without taking your hands off the wheel, improving convenience and safety.

If you're looking for more information about the car, feel free to reach out! Whether you have questions about specific features or need assistance with anything else, our team is happy to help.
