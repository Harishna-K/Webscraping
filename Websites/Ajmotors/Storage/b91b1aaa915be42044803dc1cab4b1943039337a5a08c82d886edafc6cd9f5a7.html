# 2013 Nissan Cube 15X V Selection
{Stock number. 24021}

The Nissan Cube 15X V Selection is an urban car with a unique appeal. It caters to those looking for a compact, stylish car with good practicality and fuel efficiency. The design is very much a love-it-or-hate-it aspect, and those who appreciate its offbeat aesthetic tend to find the Cube to be an excellent, reliable car for everyday use.

# Key features
- Glass roof: The 2013 Nissan Cube 15X V Selection can be equipped with a glass roof (often referred to as a "panoramic roof" or "glass sunroof" depending on the version), which is an attractive option for those seeking an open and airy cabin experience.

- Keyless entry: With keyless entry, you can lock and unlock the vehicle without removing the key fob from your pocket or bag. As long as you have the key fob on your person, you can touch the door handle to unlock the doors, or press a button on the door to lock them.

- Auto start stop: The Auto Start-Stop (or Idle Stop) feature is a technology designed to improve fuel efficiency and reduce emissions by automatically shutting off the engine when the vehicle is idling and restarting it when the accelerator is pressed.

If you're looking for more information about the car, feel free to reach out! Whether you have questions about specific features or need assistance with anything else, our team is happy to help.
