Null Furniture

Add to MyMarket

With the increasing development of apartments, townhomes, condominiums, retirement communities, and smaller homes across the country, the demand for furniture that maximizes space without compromising style has never been greater. That's where we come in! Discover the beauty of small-scale living with Null Furniture, your go-to source for living room, occasional, and accent tables. We're not just a furniture provider; we're your partners in crafting inviting and functional spaces, even when every inch counts. As a family-owned and operated business dating back to the 1970’s, Null Furniture is all about the personal touch. Our dedicated team, consisting of just two full-time employees, handles every aspect of our day-to-day operations. We believe that this "small scale" philosophy allows us to focus on what truly matters: providing you with furniture designed specifically for smaller living spaces, where space is a precious commodity.

Arlington

Crafted for sophistication and practicality, each piece in this stunning collection blends durable materials with elegant design. Featuring poplar solids and catalpa veneer with tops that mimic luxurious travertine stone framed in wood for a timeless appeal. All finished in a rich mocha hue complemented by dark antique brass hardware.

Saddlebrook

Crafted with rubberwood solids, white oak veneer, and high-pressure laminate for lasting beauty. Spice brown hues add warmth, while elegant brown oak tops bring sophistication. Features like easy-glide drawers with antique brass hardware, casters for mobility, and built-in charging stations enhance convenience and organization.
