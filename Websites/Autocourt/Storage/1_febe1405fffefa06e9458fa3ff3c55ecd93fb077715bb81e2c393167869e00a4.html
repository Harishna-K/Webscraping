A 2015 Honda Fit (Jazz) 5 door hatchback that has travelled 62,600 kilometres.
Finished in silver, the Fit has a 1300cc i-VTEC engine with smooth CVT transmission which delivers peppy performance with exceptional 5.0l/100km fuel economy.
The Fit is a popular small hatchback ideal for around town and open road driving, with stability/traction control. Easy access, 6 airbags, comfortable seating, and with the back seats folded down providing a huge carrying capacity are features of the Fit.
A good, very economical car.
