The 2015 Mazda Demio (known as the Mazda2 in many markets) with Skyactiv technology combines sporty performance with impressive fuel efficiency and a stylish design. Heres a look at the key features:

**Skyactiv Technology**:
- **Engine**: The 2015 Mazda Demio features a 1.3L 4-cylinder Skyactiv-G engine, which delivers a balance of power and fuel efficiency. Skyactiv technology focuses on optimizing the engine, transmission, body, and chassis to reduce weight, improve fuel consumption, and increase overall performance.

**Compact and Practical Design**:
- The Demio is compact, making it easy to park and maneuver in tight spaces. Despite its small size, it offers surprising interior space for passengers and cargo, with a practical layout and versatile folding rear seats.

**Interior and Comfort**:
- The cabin features a well-designed, driver-focused layout. While compact, its surprisingly comfortable, with good-quality materials used throughout. The seats are supportive, and the cabin feels spacious for the size of the car.

**Safety Features**:
- The Demio is equipped with essential safety features, including:
- **Anti-lock Braking System (ABS)**
- **Electronic Stability Control (ESC)**
- **Traction Control System (TCS)**
- **Rear-view camera **
- **Isofix child seat anchors** for added safety for families

Overall, the 2015 Mazda Demio with Skyactiv technology offers a great mix of efficiency, fun driving dynamics, and stylish design. Its a solid option for those looking for a practical, fuel-efficient, and enjoyable compact car.
