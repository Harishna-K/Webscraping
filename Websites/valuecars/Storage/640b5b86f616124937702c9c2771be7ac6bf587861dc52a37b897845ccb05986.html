Eyesight Safety System - Radar Cruise Control - Chain Driven Engine

Here we have a good looking example of Subaru's Impreza Sport hatch, This 2.0i-S model has a sportier look thanks to its Factory aero, 17" alloys and a more luxurious combination leather interior.
Also features Dual airbags, Eyesight Safety System, Traction control, Lane Departure Warning, Collision Prevention, Radar cruise control, 5 Shoulder belts, ISOFIX Child seat anchors, Electric drivers seat, Spotlamps, High visibility indicator mirrors, Next Generation chain driven boxer engine, Fuel saving Idle stop, Auto Wipers & Lights, Rear wiper and 2 remote keys.

Finance & Protecta Insurance products are also available for this vehicle.

Pre-purchase inspection can be arranged for out of town purchasers.

You can buy with confidence from Value Cars in the knowledge we are a totally New Zealand owned & funded provider of good quality cars & great after sales service for over 30 years.
