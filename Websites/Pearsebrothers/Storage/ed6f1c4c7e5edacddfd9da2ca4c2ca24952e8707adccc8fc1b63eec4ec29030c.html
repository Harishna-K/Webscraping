10 SEATER, LOW KMS, NO DEPOSIT FINANCE

Effortless Entry & Exit: Featuring an automatic side opening door, making it incredibly easy for passengers to enter and exit, even in tight parking spaces.

Convenient Push Button Start: Say goodbye to traditional keys start your vehicle with ease using the modern push button start for a more seamless driving experience.

Spacious 10-Seater: Comfortably accommodates up to 10 passengers, ideal for family trips, group transport, or commercial use, with plenty of space for everyone.

Powerful 2.7L Petrol Engine: Offers a reliable and efficient 2.7L petrol engine, ensuring smooth acceleration and dependable power for urban and highway driving.

Stylish 18-inch Alloys: Upgraded with 18-inch alloy wheels, adding a sleek, modern touch to the Hiaces rugged look while enhancing road handling and performance.

Eye-Catching Flares: Equipped with stylish flares, giving the Hiace a more dynamic and aggressive profile, setting it apart from other people movers on the road.

Automatic Transmission: The smooth and easy-to-drive automatic transmission ensures effortless cruising on all kinds of roads, making it perfect for both personal and commercial driving.

Advanced Safety Features: Includes a suite of safety features such as airbags, ABS, stability control, and parking sensors to protect both the driver and passengers.

Flexible & Versatile Interior: The flexible seating layout allows for quick adjustments to accommodate passengers or additional cargo, making it ideal for various needs.

Fuel Efficient: Despite its size, the 2.7L engine delivers a balance of performance and fuel efficiency, offering excellent value for long drives or daily commutes.

Renowned Toyota Reliability: Built with Toyotas reputation for durability and longevity, ensuring you get a dependable vehicle with minimal maintenance.
