Leather, Rev Camera, 6-Airbags, 16's Alloys

Discover sophistication and comfort in this 2015 Nissan Sylphy, finished in timeless white. With its refined design, advanced features, and reliable 1.8L engine, this sedan is built to make every drive smooth, safe, and enjoyable.

Key features include:

Stylish 16-inch alloy wheels for a modern look

Powerful 1800cc engine for a seamless driving experience

6 airbags providing all-around safety

Premium leather seats for a touch of luxury

Push-button start for effortless convenience

Dual climate control to keep everyone comfortable

Reversing camera for stress-free parking and maneuvering

Fog lights for enhanced visibility in low-light conditions
