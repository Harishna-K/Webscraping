The 2016 Toyota Aqua S is a hybrid subcompact hatchback, also known as the Toyota Prius C in some markets. It's a highly fuel-efficient car, designed for city driving, and it offers a great balance of practicality and economy.

**Powertrain**:
- **Engine**: 1.5-litre 4-cylinder Atkinson-cycle engine paired with an electric motor, forming a hybrid powertrain.
- **Fuel Economy**: Very impressive, with an estimated fuel economy of around 35-40 km/L (depending on driving conditions),
. **Safety Features**:
Toyota Safety Sense C**: Includes features like pre-collision system, lane departure alert, and automatic high beams.
- **Vehicle Stability Control** and **Traction Control** to improve handling and stability in various driving conditions.
- **ABS and EBD (Electronic Brakeforce Distribution)** for enhanced braking performance.

**Exterior Design**:
- **Compact Size**: The Aqua S is designed for urban environments, being small and maneuverable while offering a higher driving position.

**Driving Experience**:
- **Hybrid Efficiency**: Excellent fuel efficiency is a standout feature, making it an ideal choice for city commuting and long-distance driving.
- **Comfortable Ride**: The suspension is tuned for comfort, and the car handles city streets well with its compact size.

- **Keyless Entry and Start**

- **Eco Mode**: A driving mode that optimizes performance to improve fuel efficiency.
- **Rearview Camera**: A standard feature to aid in parking and visibility.

The Aqua S is well-regarded for being budget-friendly, both in terms of purchase price and ongoing operating costs due to its fuel efficiency.
