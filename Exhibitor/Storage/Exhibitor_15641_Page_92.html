Knock on Wood by Enrique

Add to MyMarket

At Knock On Wood by Enrique, we celebrate the unparalleled beauty of handcrafted wood products. Each piece, with its distinct grain and texture, tells a story of meticulous artistry and design. Our offerings are not just visually stunning—they are functional masterpieces that bring warmth and character to any space. We specialize in transforming raw wood into exquisite, one-of-a-kind items that resonate with the individuality of our clients. From the initial sketch to the final polish, we collaborate closely with our customers to ensure that their vision comes to life in the form of exceptional woodcraft. We take pride in our innovative creations, such as vertical chess sets that challenge spatial thinking, home-sized shuffleboards to gather family and friends for entertainment, and guitar-shaped end-tables that strike a chord with music lovers.

Sapo (Toad) Game

A unique game, made entirely using solid wood called Sapo (Toad), is perfect for playing with family and friends. This game involves tossing coins and can be used outdoors for hours of entertainment.
