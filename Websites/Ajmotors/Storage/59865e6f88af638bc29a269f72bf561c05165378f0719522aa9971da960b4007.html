# 2016 Honda Vezel 1.5L Hybrid
{Stock number. 24685}

The 2016 Honda Vezel 1.5L Hybrid is a compact hybrid crossover SUV that offers an efficient and stylish alternative to traditional gasoline-powered vehicles. It combines Hondas well-known reliability with eco-friendly hybrid technology, making it a popular choice for urban commuters and small families looking for an efficient and practical vehicle.

# Key features
- Electric hand brake: An electric handbrake (also known as an electronic parking brake, or EPB) is an advanced version of the traditional mechanical handbrake used in vehicles. Instead of a manual lever or pedal that you pull or push to engage or release the brake, an electric handbrake uses an electronic switch or button to control the brake system.

- Cruise control: This feature is particularly useful for long highway drives, as it reduces the need to manually adjust the throttle, improving comfort and reducing fatigue.

- Heated seats: Heated seats are a popular comfort feature found in many modern vehicles, particularly in colder climates. They use an electrical heating element embedded in the seat cushion and backrest to warm the seat, making the driving experience more comfortable during cold weather.
