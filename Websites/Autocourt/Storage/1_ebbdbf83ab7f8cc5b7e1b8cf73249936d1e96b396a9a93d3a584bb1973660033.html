BRAND NEW!
Latest model Toyota Hiace Commuter GL, first registered in Japan in December 2023 Has travelled delivery kilometres, only 53 kilometres, (Not 53,000).
It is powered by the 1GD-FTV 2.8 litre intercooled twin cam turbo/diesel engine which develops 110kW/148hp, with a 6 stage automatic transmission.
This is a high spec Commuter GL, with 12/14 seats* (ask for more information), cloth upholstery, lap & diagonal seat belts, air-conditioning, rear heater, individual air vents and drink holders for each seat, privacy glass, opening passenger windows, fully lined and carpeted.
It comes with power steering, driver's airbag, ABS brakes with Electronic Brake Distribution, electric front windows, central locking, frontal collision mitigation, lane departure warning, and electronic stability control/traction control.
Can be used for a multitude of purposes; passenger transport, taxi, club, or even a large family.

* Number of seats depends on usage and in most cases such as taxi, tourist operation etc would have to be reduced to 12.

A great opportunity to purchase a new Minibus, (Coach), at a great price.
