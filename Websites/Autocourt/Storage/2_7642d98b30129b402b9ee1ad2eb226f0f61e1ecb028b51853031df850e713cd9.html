2015 Lexus NX200T i-Package 2WD, in blue, with 18 inch alloy wheels, that has travelled 49,800 kilometres.
This superb wagon has a 2.0-litre turbocharged engine with 6 speed automatic transmission.
As you would expect, the Lexus has a long list of factory options
including luxurious leather seats, (front seats heated and electric adjustable), multiple airbags, adaptive cruise control incorporating frontal impact alert, panoramic sunroof, CD, LH side and rear-view cameras.
