Honda Sensing, Part Leather, 10-Airbags, Bluetooth

Elevate your driving experience with this 2015 Black Honda Jade! Combining sleek design, hybrid efficiency, and advanced safety features, this stylish MPV is perfect for families or professionals. With 10 airbags, lane departure warning, and forward collision warning, safety is a priority, while modern conveniences like push-button start, Bluetooth, and Econ mode make every drive smooth, connected, and economical.

Key Features:

Stunning black exterior complemented by sporty 17-inch alloy wheels.

Hybrid petrol engine for impressive fuel efficiency and eco-friendly performance.

10 airbags for outstanding safety for you and your passengers.

Lane departure warning and forward collision warning for advanced driver assistance.

Push-button start for seamless, keyless convenience.

Bluetooth connectivity for hands-free calls and music streaming.

Reversing camera for effortless parking and maneuvering.

Cruise control for relaxed and efficient highway driving.

Econ mode for maximum fuel savings and eco-conscious driving.
