Rainbow Outdoor

Add to MyMarket

Pelican Reef Inc has brought Rainbow Outdoor furniture to the North American market. Rainbow Outdoor started the production of polypropylene (PP) outdoor furniture in early 2002 and became a pioneer of the poly outdoor industry over the years. Constantly developing and introducing various models in large color ranges has been the goal each year. Thanks to our high levels of in-stock inventory and high-speed production capacity, we guarantee fast and on-time delivery and high-quality commercial-grade outdoor furniture. Over the past years, Rainbow Outdoor has tremendously expanded its product range through Poly wicker, polyresin, aluminum, and steel series. We provide outdoor patio furniture for retailers, designers, resorts, motels, hotels, bars, cafes, restaurants, houses, villas, water parks, shopping centers, hospitals, and schools. Rainbow Outdoor is becoming known worldwide as one of the premium brands of poly outdoor and continues supplying high volumes in over 62 countries with quality, consistency, and value
