New Tyres, 4WD, Leather, Sport, 7 Seats, Bluetooth

Take your driving experience to the next level with this 2015 White Subaru Exiga! Combining comfort and versatility with performance, this 7-seater SUV boasts leather-accented seats, front heated seats, and advanced tech like SI-Drive and dual climate control. With Bluetooth connectivity, a reversing camera, and cruise control, every drive is as smooth and convenient as it is enjoyable.

Key Features:

Elegant white exterior complemented by 17-inch alloy wheels.

Luxurious leather-accented seats with heated front seats for extra
comfort.

Advanced Subaru SI-Drive for customizable performance.

Dual climate control to keep everyone comfortable.

Bluetooth connectivity for hands-free calls and music streaming.

Reversing camera for safer and easier parking.

Cruise control for relaxed long-distance drives.

4 airbags for safety and peace of mind.
