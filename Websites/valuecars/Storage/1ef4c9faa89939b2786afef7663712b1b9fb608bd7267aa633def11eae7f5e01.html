8 Airbags - Facelift Model - Reverse Camera

Here we have a very tidy Facelift example of the popular Toyota Corolla Fielder Hybrid, These wagons have earned a solid reputation for reliability, fuel efficiency and Safety.
Very well optioned with 8 Airbags, Climate Control air conditioning, Reverse camera, Shoulder belts for all passengers, ISOFIX Child seat anchors, UV Privacy glass, Monsoon shields, HID Headlights, ECO/EV Modes and more!.
Looks super crisp finished in White with the rear spoiler, and has a stylish Black interior with lightly textured patterned seat inserts. Features a 1500cc engine with Hybrid Synergy Drive and an automatic transmission giving great fuel efficiency, also has a 5 star VSRR safety rating.
Finance & Protecta Insurance products are also available for this vehicle.

Pre-purchase inspection can be arranged for out of town purchasers.

You can buy with confidence from Value Cars in the knowledge we are a totally New Zealand owned & funded provider of good quality cars & great after sales service for over 30 years.
