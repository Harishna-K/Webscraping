Oliver Gal Artist Co.

Add to MyMarket

Oliver Gal Artist Co. is internationally renowned for creating mesmerizing artistic and lifestyle products that exude sophistication, elevating living spaces and igniting self-expression. Allow us to assist you and explore our exquisite art house collections and discover a wide range of stunning finishes. Together, we will embark on a transformative journey, creating living spaces that truly reflect your unique style. Oliver Gal was founded by two visionary sisters, Ana Sanchez-Gal and Lola Sanchez, with a single-minded mission to offer extraordinary pieces of art that effortlessly enhance their environments. Under the inspired guidance of Ana, Oliver Gal has emerged as America's most celebrated wall art brand, earning numerous accolades while being favored by celebrities. Lola serves as our exceptional artist and creative director, infusing our collections with limitless imagination by seamlessly blending haute couture influences, contemporary trends, and elements drawn from popular culture. Crafted by hand, Oliver Gal exemplifies meticulous attention to detail and an unwavering commitment to excellence in both design and craftsmanship. Our artwork has graced the esteemed pages of prestigious publications while adorning the walls of noteworthy personalities. Welcome to the world of Oliver Gal where art converges with lifestyle; where every creation weaves a captivating narrative.

Oliver Gal Artist Co. Spring 2024

Founded in 2012 by sisters Ana Sánchez-Gal and Lola Sánchez, Oliver Gal has been on a mission to offer museum-quality art at a revolutionary price with an ever-changing collection that allows everyone to dress their walls and home with rich imagery. Adding to the allure, every artwork in this collection is a rare gem, as each piece is hand-made.
