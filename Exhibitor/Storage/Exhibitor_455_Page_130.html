Phillips Collection

Add to MyMarket

Phillips Collection has been defining organic contemporary design with our award-winning furnishings for 40 years. We are a to-the-trade resource for residential and commercial interior designers and furniture dealers internationally. Discover truly unique pieces hand-crafted by artisans in extraordinary materials. We offer furniture, accessories, wall decor, sculpture & lighting. Origins by Phillips Collection comprises reclaimed roots and live edge slabs. Elements by Phillips Collection consists of semi-precious stones sourced from around the world. Come see us at one of our flagship showrooms around the country. We have locations in High Point, Dallas, Irvine, Laguna and Las Vegas. Our vast collection is stocked in a 400,000-square-foot facility in High Point, North Carolina. Every piece a conversation.

Sea Urchin Wall Tiles, Set of 4

Whether clustered together or arranged individually, these pieces transform any space into a sanctuary of coastal charm. Crafted in resin and finished in a white hue with sea urchin textures, these pieces are a mesmerizing collaboration between nature's allure and modern sophistication.

Emerging Face Sculpture, Roman Stone

Our captivating Emerging Face Collection is an homage to Cycladic heritage reimagined with a contemporary twist. Each piece in this collection embodies the rich history of the Cycladic civilization, artfully translated into modern aesthetics. Our signature sculpture, meticulously crafted in our durable outdoor roman stone finish. 

Cast Dancing Sculpture, Pirouette, Faux Bleached

Our popular dancing roots have been transformed into repeatable, lightweight pieces of art. The Cast Dancing Sculptures are equally as eye-catching as the originals, with their coveted ribbon-like forms. Hand-finished in a faux bleach to mimic a real wood grain. These sculptures will add a soft, neutral palette to any modern organic home.
