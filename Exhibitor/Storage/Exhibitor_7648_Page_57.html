Fabelli Italia is your Italian source for high quality bedroom furniture and TV Consoles, dining and occasional furniture, featuring the latest trends and designs. We offer Italian styling made for America. Fabelli specializes in contemporary bedroom furniture that include high gloss Italian lacquers and wood finishes. US quick ship program, direct containers and custom styling for distributors and Top 100 Retailers. 100% Italian.

Adele

Adele combines contemporary sophistication with natural charm: each piece in this collection features beautiful light wood with a unique ribbed design on the front panels, adding texture and visual interest. The fronts are paired with a marvelous lacquer finish that enhances the natural beauty of the wood.

Venezia

Venezia is a masterful blend of modern design and timeless elegance. This collection features wooden lacquered surfaces that enhance the natural beauty of the wood grain, providing a sophisticated yet warm ambiance. The set's versatile design makes it perfect for various interior styles, from modern to transitional.
